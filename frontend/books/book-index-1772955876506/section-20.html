BUNG 18

AN LO NEIH DAN

Miriate hi lo nei a eizawng an ni a, leilet nei sawi tham an awm meuh lo. Kan sawi tawh ang khan tunhma chuan hmun ṭha lai, leilet neih theihna hmunah an awm nual a, leilet nei pawh an tam, mahse tunah chuan vai (benggoli) hovin an nek chhuak deuh vek tawh a, tunah chuan tlangramah an cheng deuh ber a, lui kam a awm, leilet siamna rem a awm ṭhenkhat te chuan leilet an la siam a ni. Atlangpuiin lo nei a eizawng an nih a vangin an lo neih dan kan târ lang dawn a ni.

  1. Lo thlan : Tunhma chuan lo an thlan dawn chuan upa hote thukhawm in an sawi hova, an lo duh nalaite an sawi hova, Mizo te ang a number pawt lem lovin an lo duhna lai an nei mai ṭhin. Atlangpui in mahni lo chul an vat leh tlangpui ṭhin. Hmanlai chuan Mizo in, “Ram ṭheh” kan tih angin bawh bik ram an nei ṭhin a, tunlai chuan chutiang chu a awm tawh meuh lo. Tunlaiah chuan upa ho han ṭhut khawm a ro han rel a awm tawh lova, an duhna lai apiang an vat mai ṭhin a, chuvang chuan lo vah dawn a lo inchhuh buai an awm fo ṭhin reng a ni.
  2. Lo en : An lo vah tur hi mang an chhal hmasa ve ṭhin a, an mangchhalah chuan perh an zep a, an haw san a, zanah an lu-ah sahriak thihin pial takin an sam an khuih a, mang ṭha man tum ran in an mu ṭhin a, an mumang a sangha an lo hmuh leh mithi in ‘Tür kim’ an hmuh chuan mang ṭha mangah an inngai a, buh leh bal a nei turah an inngai ṭhin.

An mumang a bawng leh vawk te an lo talh chuan chumi lo an neih chuan thihna turah an ngai a, an lo en chu an chhuahsan a, lo dang an en leh mai ṭhin. Lo chu an vah tawh hnuah pawh ramhuai awmna ni a an ngaih a lo awm a nih chuan an lo vah tawh pawh chu an chhuahsan leh mai ṭhin.

  1. Lo vah : Theihai a lo par a, phunchawng a lo parvul chuk tawh chuan lo vah hunah an ngai a, lo vah an ṭan nghal tawh mai ṭhin. Lo vah hi February thlaah an ṭan tlangpui a, January thla a lo vat tan pawh an awm bawk.

Lo vah lai hian hmeichhiate pawh an awl chuang lova, a hmei a pa in lo an vat ṭhin. Lo vah chu chetsual palh leh thih palh mai theih a nih a vangin upa in, “Dawila pang lawng pang,” an ti a, chumi awmzia chu, ‘Lo vah chu mahni kuang kuah ang kan ni,” an tihna a ni.

  1. Lo hal : Phunchawng par a lo chul tawh chuan lo hal hunah an ngai a, lo hal turin an inpuahchah tawh mai ṭhin. Meilam phiah an uluk loh vang leh mei ven an fimkhur tawk ṭhin loh a vangin an lo hal hian ram kang a tam thei hle ṭhin. An lo hal ram kang in khaw ram (khaw hmun hlui) a ti kang palh a nih chuan a tikangtu chuan vawk a chawi ngei tur a ni.

An lo halin khua emaw, huan emaw a tikang dawn a nih chuan a kang chu an thelh ṭhin a, mahni duhna hmun hmun a hmun hran hran a lo nei an nih a vangin ram an ti kang nasa thei hle a, khua emaw huan emaw kang thei dinhmun a a ding a nih loh chuan kang ṭhelh sen pawh a ni ṭhin loh avangin ram kang chu an thlir ngawt mai ṭhin. Ram an ti kang chhe thei ṭhin khawp mai.

  1. Mangkhawh : An lo chu a kan ṭhat chuan thlai thlak turin tha tam tak sen a ngai lova, a kan ṭhat loh erawh chuan mangkhawh nan tha sen nasat a ngai thei hle ṭhin. An lo-ah hian atlangpuiin Mizo te angin thlam an sa nghal mai ngai lova, buh seng dawnah thlam an sa chauh ṭhin. Fur lai a hlo an thlawh pawh in a sur a sa hnuaiah siksil khumin chaw an fak mai ṭhin.
  2. Thlai thlak : Mang an khawh zawh a thlai an thlak dawn in an lo khawi laiah emaw ramhuai (lo huai) awmna tur “Turtut” an tih chu an siam hmasa ṭhin a, chu chu lo a an inthawi hun a piang a an inthawihna tur a ni.

Chu turtut-ah chuan khuar an siam a, chu khuarah chuan an inthawina vawk ril an phum ṭhin. Lo a an thlai thlak tur zawng zawng chu chutah chuan tlem te te in an tuh hmasa vek ṭhin. Chumi hmun chu perngo in an hual vek bawk ṭhin. An lo a ramhuai tan a an hmun serh zau zawng hi tap tiat vel a zau a ni. Turtur chu thingbul awmnaah an siam kher tur a ni. Thingbul awmna a rem lo a nih chuan thingbul aiah mau pawh an phun mai ṭhin. Chutia an lo a ramhuai hnen a inthawina hmun serh an siam zawh chuan thlai an thlak in buh an tuh thei chauh ṭhin.

  1. Buh tuh : An buh tuhna hmanrua chu chem. Chhia hi a hmawr lam an tât hriam a, afawng lian leh sei takin an siam a, avawnna lai an ti te deuh a, chu chuan buh an tuh in thlai an thlak ṭhin.

Buh tuh leh hlo thlawh lai hian Mizote angin hma an thên ve ngai lova, an hma tawk tur hma an la a, mahni hma ṭheuhah an thawk chho mawp mawp tawh mai a ni. Hma an chuah chuan an chawl zel a, tui te in in an chawlh chhung chuan mei te an zu ṭhin. Hetia hma an chhuah a an chawlh chhung hian hmeichhiate chuan tuithawl teah thlai chi an pai a, mipa te chawlh chhungin thlai an lo thlak zel ṭhin. Hmeichhia te chu an chawl ve ngai lo a nih chu. An hmanrua in a zir lo a ni bawk a, buhtuhah chuan Mizo te angin an rang ve lo.

  1. Hlo thlawh : Hlo an thlawh hian ṭhenkhat chu an inlawm ve ṭhin a, mahse Mizo te ang chuan nula leh tlangval te an inlawm ve ngai meuh lo. An inlawm a nih pawh in mipa leh mipa, hmeichhia leh hmei chia an inlawm ṭhin a, Mizovin, “Ngalchak thapei,” kan tih ang hi a awm ve ngai meuh lo.

An hlo thlawh hmingte chu

  1. Bawrrama = Hnuhpui.
  2. Bawtuai = Hnuhhram.
  3. Bawtiang = Athual.
  4. Tatrui = Pawhchhiat te an ni.

Hnuhpui an kawih zawh hnu hian an lova ramhuai awmna a an siam ‘Turtut’ ah chuan an inthawih ṭhin a, hetia an inthawih hian hnangin sei takin an tah a, chumi chungah chuan hnang bawk a siam vaṭhu lem chu an dah a, chutiang a vaṭhu lem hnang a an tah chu an lo-ah sih a awm a nih chuan sihah te an dah a, an inchungah pawh an vit bur bawk. Hetiang a an inthawih ṭum hian in a ṭangin darbenthek leh tumphung dangte tum in lo thleng an kal ṭhin.

Hemi ṭum hian inthawihna a an hman tur bawng emaw chu lo thleng an kalpui a, mahse lo a talh lovin inah an rawn hawpui leh a, inah inthawih nan an talh ṭhin. Lova an thlai chin chi hrang hrang hnah chu tlemte te in sawhthing hnah nen inah an rawn hawn a, buh chu a bul chawpin bul khat emaw bul hnih emaw nen an rawn hawn tur a ni. In an rawn thlen chuan an inthawina tur lo a an va ken bawng chu ramhuai hnen a inthawih nan in a an luhkapuiah an talh tawh ang. Chumi a ṭang chuan an chhungkaw zing a mipakhat in Phungchengkawl, Aifiar, Nghavawk leh ramsa seh hlum sa reng reng buhseng hma chu an ei thiang tawh lo a ni. Heng bâkah hian sakhi sa leh saza sa pawh an ei thiang lo bawk.

Mizo te angin hlo thlawh lai hian ṭanpui ngaite inpuih hi a awm ve ngai meuh lova, chuvangin hlo thlo thei lo chuan a lo chu athlawh theih loh nalai chu a mak a ngai mai ṭhin. Ṭhenkhat chu a vah lai a an duham ang huin a thlawh lamah an thlo leh thei ṭhin si lova chuvangin ṭhenkhat chuan an lo chu an mâk duai phah fo ṭhin a ni. An ei leh in haichhuahna ber ram ren nachang an la hre lo hle a ni.

  1. Buh seng : Sawitawh angin a tam zawk chuan buh seng dawnah thlam an sa chauh ṭhin a, thlam an sa zova buh seng an han ṭan dawn chuan an inthawih leh rih phawt a, chumi hnuah buh chu an seng a, an buhseng chu chil mai lovin thlamah an dah khawl ṭeuh phawt ṭhin. An buh seng hun hian ruahtui a la kang loh a vangin ṭhenkhat chuan an buh chu an ti uk a, kum tluan in buhuk remchhe tak an ei phah fo ṭhin. Buh an seng dawn a an inthawih dan chu; Thlamah chuan buhvui leh thlai chi hrang hrang an rawn la khawm a, chung an buhvui leh thlai rawn lak khawmte chu an inthawihna vawk thi chuan an thlawr vek a, chumi hnu chuan buh an seng thei chauh ṭhin a ni. Chutiang a buh seng hma a an inthawina chu, “Chachawm ri” an ti.
  1. Buh chil : An buh seng chu chil nghal mai lovin thlamah an dah khawm a, chutia an dah chu a lo tam viau tawh chuan a ni hran in an chil ṭhin. An buh chil hma chuan an inthawih leh phawt a, ramhuai hnen a an inthawina ar emaw, thi chu an buhvui dah khawmah chuan an thlawr ṭhin.

Buh an seng zawh a an chil zawh vek chuan an lova ramhuai hmun atan a an serh hranah chuan an inthawih leh phawt ṭhin. Chuta an inthawih dan chu; an hmun serh hran a an buh tuh vui chu an la a, an deng a, chu buhfai chu tuiah an chiah a, chhang dip ang main dip takin an deng leh a, chakai leh ar sa ahran ve ve in an chum a, chakai leh arsa an chhumna ah chuan buhden dip chu an thlak ve ve a, chu chu an chhungkua in an ei ṭhin.

  1. Buh chhek : Miriate hian buh an seng zawh hian inah an phur haw nghal mai lova, hun engemaw chen chu thlamah an dah a, an lo-ah chuan thlai dang, la leh hmarhcha te a la awm a vangin chung thlai hmun chu an sam fai leh ṭhin a, chu chu “Kruk la” an ti. Hetia lo a thlai hmun an samfai hian buh chu an phova nitin an buh pho ro sa chu an phur haw ṭhin.
  2. Üa plai : Lo thlai an seng khawm zawh vek a, an buh pawh an phurh zawh vek hnu chuan lo a ramhuai tan a an hmun serh ‘turtut’ ah khan vawk pakhat emaw, ar pakhat in emaw an inthawi leh a nga, chu chu ramhuai hnen a an lo neih man chhiah an pekna ang a ni. Vawk pahnih a an inthawi chuan “Cham plai” an ti.

Hetia lo lam a tih tur zawng zawng an tih zawh hnu hian vawk pa lian ber an talh a nga, awmni kham in hlim takin hun an hmang ṭhin. He an vawk talh hi chu inthawina a ni lova, kum khat chhung a an hnathawh zawng zawng tluang tak a an zawh fel theih a vang leh buh bal pawh ṭha tak a an lo aṭang a an thar chhuah theih a vanga an lawmna ruai a ni.

Hemi ṭum hian hun dang a an lam ngai loh, lam dan dangdai tak in mipa ho te chu an lam ṭhin a, an lam dan chu hmanlai ral in run hun lai a ral an run dan leh an indo ṭhin dan chu an tu leh fa te’n hre ve se tih an duh a vangin awmphaw, chem leh fei te nen an lam ṭhin a, chu an làm hming chu, “Chang tawng plai,” an ti. Kei chuan ‘Sarlamkai’ ka ti mai. Kum khat chhung in hetiang làm hun hi vawi khat chiah an nei a. Hetih hun lai hian ‘Likep plai” an tih pawh in an lam bawk ṭhin. Hetiang lam hi tunlai chuan hmuh tur a awm tawh lova, thiam pawh an awm tawh lo, ‘Hmanlai chuan an lam ṭhin’ ti a an sawi bak chu tunlai mite zingah chuan a hmutu pawh an awm tawh lo.

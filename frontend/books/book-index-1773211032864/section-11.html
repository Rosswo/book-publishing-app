BUNG 9

LALPA’N MIRE RAM MIN HRUAI THLENG TA

Vaṭhuampui a hun engaw chen ka châm leh hnu chuan Pu Lalhmachhuana haw hun awm velah Vaṭhuampui Ṭhalai-ho Pharva lam a rawngbawl a chhuakte rualin Vaṭhuampui chu ni 15 March 1991 chuan Mire ram Bangladesh lam pa nin hlim takin ka chhuak leh ta a. Pharva khua thlen hma Tuikuk leh Takam ho khua BCM Missionary-te awmnaahte chuan riakin Pathian thu kan sawi pah zel a. School sakpui ngaite pawh kan sakpui nghal zel bawk a.

Pharva khua aṭang chuan Bangladesh a sumdawng a kal tur Laithankhuma rualin 22 March 1991 Zirtawp ni chuan Bangladesh lam pa nin kan chhuak a. Ramritlangah kan riak phei a. A tukah Ramritlang aṭang chuan Bangladesh lam pa nin kan chhuak leh a. Rumana khua kan thlen chuan ka kalpui pa Laithankhuma chu Rumana ah a riak a, kei chu, “Rumana khuaah chuan report hmang an awm avangin riak suh, Lungthausih khua-ah riak mai rawh,” min tih avangin Rumana-ah chuan riah duh viau mah ila ka riak ngam lova, Lungthausih khuaah chuan ka tlang a.

Lungthausih khua chu thim dawn tepah hian ka zu thleng a, Lungthausih khaw kharbari chuan an khua a ka riah chu a lo phal ta awzawng lo mai a, “Nangchu India mi Mizo i ni si a, Army an rawn kal thut a, an man che chuan ka phur a rit dawn e mai, Army ho hi an sual si a, arte bawngte min dil a, kan pek loh chuan khaihlumahte saisak vakahte min vau ṭhin a, min hrethiam la, kan khua hi chhuahsan mai rawh,” min ti a.

Chutia kharbari in an khaw chhuahsan tura min ngen takah chuan han riah luih chiah pawh ka ngai bik lova, tlai tawh tak, pafa hmelhai hun vel chuan chau tak chung chuan Darjiling a riak tur chuan Lungthausih khua chu ka chhuahsan leh ta a. Zan thim zingah Lungthausih leh Darjiling inkar ramngaw chhahpui mai ka kal lai chuan ka hmuh phak si lohah hian Ungau hi ka hnung lamahte, ka sir lamahte ka hmalamah te ahram chhen mai a, naupan lai a thingtlanga kan awm lai a nu leh pa te’n min tihṭhaih nan a Ungau an lo hman ṭhin vang nge ni, ka hmul thi a ding sung sung mai a, ka hlau lek lek ṭhin hian ka hria a, athim chhah em avangin ka kawlphe khawnvar lah chuan a en thui thei bawk si lova. Zan dar sawm rik hma deuh chuan Darjiling chu ka va thleng a.

Darjiling-ah zankhat ka riak a, a tuk ni 26 March 1991 chuan Faruk khua chu Lalpa’n min hruai thleng a, Faruk khua-ah chuan RCB Kohhran Upa Santhuang inah ka thleng a, a ni chu pa-ah ka zawng nghal a. Faruk khua hi an District khawpui Bandarban hnaite a awm a ni a, Bawm khua-ah chuan khaw lian pawl tak pawh a ni a, in za dawn an awm ve a ni. Faruk khu-ah chuan ni khat ka cham a, Ni 28 March 1991 chuan Faruk ṭhalai ṭhenkhat Tv, Rokhup, Tv, Nazarit (ka thlen in tlangval) Nl, Nawlsum, Nl, Zingnem te Mire ram a Good Friday hmang turte chu ka zui ve a.

12Mire ram ka thlen tirh lai

Motor in mel 7 kan kal hnu in mei 12 ke in kan kal leh a, Pastor chownglowk awmna Empu khua chu kan thleng ta a. Mire rama kal tur a kan in aṭang a ka chhuah hmasak ber kha Dt 27 March 1990 a ni a. Lalpa’n Mire ram min hruai thlen hun hi Dt 28 March 1991 a a ni a, a tahrik leh a thla en chuan March ni 27-ah Hnahthial a kan in atangin ka chhuak a, March ni 28-ah Mire ram chu ka thleng a, vawiin a chhuak, naktuk a thleng ang mai ka ni a, mahse ka chhuah kum hi 1990 a ni a, ka thlen kum chu 1991 kumah a ni thung. Lalpa min hruai dan hi a mak tih loh rual a ni lo.

Empu khua-ah chuan Pastor Chownglowk te inah ka innghat phawt a, ahnuah thlen in min han sem chuan Pu Menkawh-te inah min thlen tir a. Pastor Chownglowk hi a naupa’n lai a ṭanga Mizo kebul a sei lian a ni a, North East India General (NEIG) Mission School Arthah leh Chorachanpur-ah te Mizo Missionary-te hnen a lehkha zir tawh a nih a vangin Mizo ṭawng pawh a lo thiam hle a.

Empu khua hi kawngpui in a pal tlang khua a ni a, in sawmhnih chauh an ni a, Miria vek an ni. Kristian an lo awm ve nual tawhin Pathian ni tih lohah chuan an lo inkhawm thei meuh lo a, Pathian ni ah pawh inkhawm theih loh chang an lo nei nual bawk. Biakin pawh kawng bulah bawngin tia lekin an lo nei ve tawh a, chu an Biakin pawh chu thli in a tih tluk avangin an sa leh thei tawh lova. An inchei dan lah chu ka tan chuan thil thar hlir a nih avangin mak ka ti thei hle a ni.

Kan thlen zan a kan inkhawm chuan Tv, Rokhupa’n hun ahmang a, inkhawm ban chuan kan zaikhawm a, Mizo lengkhawm zai hi an lo thiam ve nual mai a. Good Friday zing inkhawmah chuan Tv, Nazarita’n thu a sawi a, tlai lamah chuan Pastor Chownglowka’n Lalpa zanriah a buatsaih a, chu chu Mire ram a Lalpa zanriah ka kil hmasak ber ṭum a ni.

Atuk ni 30 March 1991 Inrin ni chuan Pastor Chownglowk te nen Mangtawng khua a Biakin thar hawng tur leh Easter Sunday hmang turin kan kan kal leh a, Empu khua a mite pawh mi engemaw zat an lo kal ve a, chumi ṭum chuan mi 12 laiin Ringtu Baptisma an chang a ni. Ka ṭhiante chuan Mire ṭawng thiam lo mahse Bangla an thiam avangin Mire hote nen chuan an inbe thei a, kei chuan Mizo ṭawng bâk ka thiam ve loh a vangin ka be ve thei lova, an inbe lai chu ka lo thlek tawn chhen ringawt mai a ni.

A tuk thawhṭanah chuan Empu khaw lam pan in kan haw chho leh a, Empu khuah chuan Pastor Chownglowk nen kan inkawm a, Empu khua a ka awm hun a ka in hmun turte min kawhhmuh in Biakin ṭha zawk a sak theih hun a Biakin hmun turte min fan pui a, ka in hmun tur leh Biakin hmun chu a in hlat lova, hmar lam a ṭanga Empu khua luh dawn kawngpui chhak tlang dung thengthaw muam tak mai hi a ni a. Chu lai hmun chu, “Nakinah Missionary te an lo la kal a Biakin te kan la sathei a nih chuan,” tiin thing phekah ‘JEHOVA-JIRE’ ti in ahmun hmingte an lo phuah diam nghe nghe a. Vaṭhuampui khua a Lalpa’n ram leh chawlhna min pek tawh thu min hrilh kha tunah chuan min hruai thleng ta a, ka mit ngei-in ka hmu a, ka ke ngei-in ka rap ta. Lalpa chu fakin awm rawh se, Lalpa chu a thutiamah chuan avahan rinawm tak em! Keini kan rinawm lo ṭhin mai pawh a.

13Ka Pu Lalhmachhuana te nupa

Ka mi hmuh tum Pu Lalhmachhuana chu ka la hmuh loh a vangin ka ṭhiante rualin Faruk khaw lamah chuan ka kal leh rih phawt a, Faruk khua a ni hnih ka cham leh hnuin Pu Lalhmachhuana awmna Bengsuri Hmunthar khuaah chuan ka unaupa thar Tv Lalnazarit nen kan zu kal a, Pu Lalhmachhuana chuan min lo lawmin min lo duat em em mai a, akhuate hnenah chuan, “Hei Mizoram a ka tupa alo thleng a, lo leng rawh u,” tiin a khua a mite chu lengah a sawm a.

Ar a talh a, a hnen a kan cham chhung hian ui pawh a talh deuh reng bawk, kei aimahin ka Pu Lalhmachhuana chu a lawm zawk emaw tih mai turin ahnen ka zu thleng chu a lo lawm thiam zawkmah a ni. A nupui ka Pi pawhin min duat em em mai bawk a, an hnenah hun engemaw chen ka cham a, Bangladesh khulehtui nihna Identity Card pawh awlsam takin min siam sak a. Bangla la thiam rih lo mah ila zalen takin Bangladesh-ah ka awm ve thei dawn ta a ni tih ka hriat in Lalpa kawng engkim a ka ṭhatna tur hlir a min thawh saktu chungah leh ka Pu, Lalhmachhuana chungah lawmthu ka sawi bang thei lo.

Miriate hi khaw khatah in tlem te te in an awm a, khaw khatah hian a tlangpuiin hnam khat chauh an awm a. Entir nan: Mizoramah hian Fanai chu a hlangin khua siam tasela, chutiang bawk chuan Sailo leh hnam dangte pawh anmahni hnam hlangin awm zel ta ang se.

Miriate hian anmahni awmdan hi ṭha tawk an inti em em mai a, hnam dang nen pawh an inpawh tehchiam lova, hnam dang nun dan pawh an zir duh lo hle a vangin an changkang thei lo a ni. Kristian an la tlem em em mai bawk a. Bangladesh sorkar leh NGO te pawhin ṭanpui chu an tum ṭhin na in khaw khatah tlem te te a an awm a vang leh inkal pawhna kawng a ṭhat loh em a vangte in ṭanpui ngaihna an hre vak lo ni in a lang. Hmun ṭhenkhatah chuan Sorkar-in School in ṭha takin a sak sak a, mahse chung Mire khua-ahte chuan khawpui a tangin zirtirtu tumah an awm thei silova, bâkah Miriate hian zirna ngaihsan nachang an la hriat loh em bawk a vangin chung School Building-te chu bawng in leh vawk inah a chang leh ṭhin a ni.

Ka kalna apiangah School kal a ṭhat zia theih tawpin ka sawi ṭhin a, mahse, “Kan pi pute a ṭangin keini chu kawlawm in ei kan zawng ngai lo, chempui-in kan ei tur chu kan thawk chhuak ve tho,” an ti tlat mai a. Mahse Kum sanghnih hnu lam a ṭang chuan zirna ngaihsan nachang an lo hre ṭan ve ta. Nu leh Pa te’n an fate zirna tur zawngin an buai ve tawh khawp mai, mahse an fate zirna a vanga insenso erawh an la harsat rih khawp mai. A free a an fate sikul kal tirna an zawn ṭhin a vangin Mission ṭhenkhat tan chuan Kristian a siam a awlsam phah in Kristian tawhsate pawh ṭhenkhat chu an fa te zirna a free theihna tur a nih chuan Mission hrang hrangah an zuan lawr phah bawk a ni. Chutih lai chuan ṭhenkhat chu fate zirna avang a insengso ngam pawh an awm ve ṭan tawh.

Kum sanghnih hnu lam a ṭang chuan Mire ramah Bangladesh a hnam dang Kristiante a ṭangin Mission hrang hrang an lo lut a. Kan unau Miriate hi an la changkan loh em a vangin lo changkang deuh tawh hnam dang Kristian ṭhenkhat te’n sum siam nan tak a hmangte pawh an awm a, thil hrang hrang tawktarhin Kristian ni turin an thlem a, chutia Kristian a an lâkte chu enkawl ṭha leh mang silovin Kristian hming ti chhe zawnga lo awm tawk lah bo lo. Mission ṭhenkhatte lah an thawktu rawihte Pathian lam hawi lo fe fe an lo awm bawk.

Mission ṭhenkhat-te chuan an thawktute hlawh tlem te te in an pe a, a chang phei chuan hlawh pawh pe mumal lova thawktute harsa tak a dahtu-te pawh an awm bawk. An thawktu te’n thlawhhmate nei in ei zawng pah a an thawh a ngaih avang leh Mire ho hnenah pawisa pukte an ngaih zawk avangin leibate an nei erh urh a, an thawhna hmun ami te’n an ngaihsan theih tur a awm aiin hmusit awm a an awm zawk thin a vangte in chutiang a rawngbawl chuan Miria te ringtu puitling a siam chu thil har tak tur a ni. Chuvangin Mire ramah Lalpa ram a zau hleithei lo mai nilovin achang chuan Kristian-te kan hming a chhiat em em changte pawh a awm ṭhin a ni.

Mire ram a thawktu ṭhenkhat chu Krista hming ti chhetu an a ni a, chutih laiin ṭhenkhat chu ṭhahnemngai tak a thawktu pawh an awm bawk. Tunah chuan Miriate hi Kristian Mission te hian kan ti chawmawlh zo tawh emawni tih mai tur a ni. “Kristian a kan awm chuan enge min pek ang, pawisa engzat nge min pek ang?” ti te in min lo zawt phawng phawngte pawh an awm ṭhin a ni. Chung rilru lam a piangsual tak tawhte Krista hnen a hruai a Kristian puitling leh Kristian ṭha tak a siam chu mihring tak a ngaihtuah chuan a huphurh awm thin khawp mai.

Khawimaw laiah chuan Miriate zingah Kristian sawichhia a Missionary te ânchhia min lawh bawrh bawrh changte pawh kan tawn chang a awm ṭhin. Bangladesh a kan Bawm unau, za a za Kristian ni tawhte chuan, “Miria te hi Lalpa’n a hawisan tawh a ni, an zinga rawngbawlin awmzia a nei tawh lo,” an ti a, mahse a mission ang zawng chuan Mizo Kristian te’n India ram a kan thawh ang chanve, chanve leh tal pawh thawk se chuan Mire ram a an thawhna hian awmzia chu a neih ngei ka ring tlat a ni. Lalpa’n Miria te Chhandamna kulhpui chhunga hruai lut turin min ti miau si. Kan unau Miria te hi Krista hnen a hruai thlen theih loh khawp a Lalpa hawisan hnam chu nise an thlarau bo hruai chhuak tur hian Mire hnam zingah Lalpa’n min tir lo tawp ang tih hi ka ngaih dan chu a ni.

Mitam tak an sakhua a nun hahchawlhna hmu zo lo, Kristian a hahchawlhna beisei a min rawn beltute pawh an awm ve bawk a. Kan unau Miriate hian an thlarau boral tur hruai kir a, Kristian puitling a siam turin belh tlak Mission, thawktu Lalpa mitirh ngei an va mamawh tak em. Krista hnen a an nun hahchawlh beisei, mahse anmahni hmangaih tak leh ṭhahnemngai tak a enkawltu tur an hmuh si loh avang a nun tuihal tam takte hi an va lainat awm teh lul em.

Kan unau Miria te hi thlarau lama puitling tur chuan; tuna kan Kristian-te pawh achung a kan sawi takte ang khian naupang, puitling tura kan enkawl ṭhin angin Miria te Ktistian puitling a siam tur chuan thil namai a ni lova, Field-ah ram lâk tawngpawng duham ngawt hleih theih a nih loh a vangin kei chuan kan enkawl phak loh leh kan vil sen loh khawp a bial zauh hi thil ṭha ber niin ka hre lo a ni. Tuna ringtu kan neihte hi ringtu puitling ni tur a theihtawp a kan beih a ngai takmeuh a ni.

Taksa leh thlarauvah chhanchhuah an ngaih zia han hmuh meuh chuan ka lainat ngawih ngawih a. Mire ramah hian Lalpa rawngbawl hna thawh ava han ṭul bawrh bawrh tak em, Lalpa ram a lo zau thuai thuai a, taksa leh thlarauah hma an sawn thuai thuai ka duh a, mahse Office ah hna thawh tur ka lo ngah ve bawk si nen, ka indaih ngang lo a ni. Kan unau (Mizo hnahthlak) Miriate hian keini Mizo te nen hian hnam inunau kan ni tih an hre tawh a, tin, tunah rih chuan Mizo te hi min la ngaisang rih bawk.

Tin, Mizo te hi Mire ram a rawng lo bawl hmasatu-te kan ni a, tun hun hnuhnungah pawh hian kan unau Miria-te taksa leh thlarau a chhanchhuah hna thawk turin Lalpa’n Mizo te hnenah mawhphurhna min pe niin ka hria a. Engtik niah emaw tal chuan chu kan mawhphurhna chu kan hlen ngei chu a la ngai dawn a ni. Miriate hian taksa leh thlarauvah hmangaih taka enkawltu an mamawh em em a ni.

Mire ram a Mission thawk tawhte leh thawk mekte hi ka theih ang angin han tar lang ila, mahse Record mumal a lo awm loh a vangin an chanchin chipchiar tak leh kimchang tak chuan ka ziak thei dawn lova pawi ka ti khawp mai. Heng ka ziak lante pawh hi Pastor Chownglowk-a hnen a ṭanga ka hriat leh keimah in ka chhuina aṭanga ka ziak a ni a, tihsual palhte a awm a nih chuan ngaihhnathiamna nen a adik zawk min lo hrilh hre turin chhiartu duhtak ka ngen che a ni.

NEIG Missionary-te sulhnu la awmchhun, an thing thei phun, Theihai leh Te’ngtere kungte

Hengte hi Mire ramthim lo su hmasatu-te an ni a. Khatih hun lai khan awmchilh lem lova rawngbawl a lo tlawh kual ṭhin mi engemawzat an awm pawh a rinawm. Hengmite hian an Lalpa tan an nun hlan chungin Mire ram thim chhah tak mai chu an lo su tawh a, mi sawm (9) lai in Mire ramah lu an lo phum tawh nghe nghe a ni. Khatih hun lai khan inkalpawhna kawng mumal a awm lova, Miria te pawh an la mawl chhah zual em em a, Pathian thu ngaihthlak pawh an duh lova.

Lalpa rawngbawltute kha Pathian thu hril a an zin in thlenna pawh an hnawn chang a tam ṭhin a, khawlaiah Bible kengin Pathian thu an tlangau pui ṭhin a, Miria te lah chuan lo ngaihthlak a lo pawm a hnek in, “Lusei ui â ho saw lehkhabu kengin ui â ang mai in khawlaiah an bauh an bauh mai,” an lo ti ṭhin a ni.

Missionary te chuan Miria te entawn a tan thei te an phun ṭhin a, chung an thei phunte chu, “Hei hi Lusei thei phun a nih hi,” ti in an sat chhe ṭhin a. Mahse, Missionary te chuan beidawng duh lovin an zirtir lui zel a, tunah pawh khatih hun lai a an awmna ‘Mission veng’ an tih, Tun a Ngaringchah khaw bulah chuan an thing phun, Theihai leh Te’ngtere kungte chu upa tawh tak hmuh tur a la awm a ni.

Chutianga Missionary te’n dodalna kâr a chhel tak a an lo zirtir chu Mire Kristian hnasa te’n Pathian thu bakah huan thlai neih dante an lo inzirtir chhawng ve zel a, tunah chuan Mire hnam pumpuiin huan thlai neih nachang an lo hre ve ta a ni. Tunah chuan ṭhenkhat chuan huan thlaite chu hausakpui tak tak pawh an lo awm ta a ni. Thlarau lamah pawh Miria te’n malsawmna tam tak an lo dawn phah a, Bible Johana bu mal leh hla 75 Mire ṭawngin an lehlin sak a, Mizo a aw b bu chu zirtir ve in chung Johana bu mal leh Hla bu te pawh chu Mizo a aw b hmangin an lo chhut sak a. Thil hlu tak an lo hnuchhiah sak a ni.

Chutih hun lai a an awmna ‘Mission veng’ an tih a ṭang chuan an in sem darh a, Mire ram hmun tinah chanchinṭha an hril darh ta a, mahse Miria te chuan chanchinṭha chu an lo do tlat avangin ram an la thei lo em em mai a, mahse Mire hnam Kristian a awm chhunte chu lehkha an zir tir in Churochanpur-ah lehkha zir leh Pathian thu zir in an lo tir a, chung zing a ṭhenkhat te chu an ṭangkai khawp mai.

Mire hnam zing a Kristian hmasa berte chu Klawnglai leh Menkrawi te unau an ni. Chumi hnu chuan Angka leh a nau Chownglowk te, Laikowi, Singtai, Menpiaw leh Tumsek-i te Kristian-ah an rawn inpe ve leh a ni. Hengte hian lehkha an zir nghal vek a, ṭhenkhat chu sikul kal kher lova Missionary te in a zir mai te pawh an awm.

Ṭhenkhat chu Mire ram a Mission sikul-ah an kal a, Mire ram a Mission sikul a awm tawh loh hnuah Bawm ram Pankhiang khuaah leh Lunginkhar-ahte sikul an kal a, chuta ṭang chuan Churachanpur ahte an kal a ni. N.E.I.G Missionary te chu Miria te’n an do em a vang leh Kristian na in hma a sawn theih loh em a vangin Bawm ramah an insawn ta vek a, “Israel te’n an hawl a an paih hnu kha Van Lal Reng ro pialral lunghlu ka char ta,” ti a hla siamtu in a lo siam ang khan kan unau Bawm hnam te’n Chahchinṭha chu an lo dinchhuah pui ta hlauh zawk a ni.

Pi Thanghmuni thlanlung Ngaringchah a mi

Kum 1944 Janary thla chuan an lo awm tawhna Upper Ngaringchah khuaah chuan an hmangaih leh an ngaih em em Miria te kan tur leh an hnen a Pathian thu sawi leh turin Evan Zakhuma chuan a fate pahnih la naupang tak (Afate hian an Pa, an awmna khaw hlui lama kal tur an ngen tlat a vangin) Sangliana leh Rinliana hruai in a zu kal leh a, an khaw mite chuan an lo lawm em em a, Evan, Zakhuma chuan Mire ho chu duhsak tak leh duhthawh takin ni hnih chhung Pathian thu a hrilh a, a fate erawh chu an ṭhiante nen an tual chai in dâi velahte an lo tlâ a.

A ni thum ni chuan khaw dangte fang zel a Pathian thu sawi zel a tum lai-in Evan Zakhuma chu a khua a sik a, khawsikpui alo ni a, a khawsik chu a zual zel a, a fapa naupangte, Sangliana hnenah chuan, “Ka ek a chhuak, min hruai teh,” a ti a, daipawnah a va dailen chuan a ek chu a tui hle mai a, chu chu Mire hovin an hmuh chuan, “Tuihri a vei,” an ti a, ramhnuaiah an tlanbo san ta vek mai a, tlanche ve thei lo tar ho chauh chu inah chuan an awma, nakinah chuan Evan Zakhuma chuan afa pa Sangliana chu a ko va, “Pathian in min kir tir leh thut a nih loh chuan ka kalsan dawn che u a ni, i nu ka va ngai ve le! Ka kalsan che u a nih pawh in i pute lo bel ang che u,” ti in a fapa chu a chah a, chawhnu lam chuan a fapa Sangliana hnenah chuan, “Sangpui, min tung rawh, ka thaw a tawp dawn ta,” a ti a.

A fapa in a han tun chuan a chat thla chu a ni ta der mai a, chumi khua a putarte chuan, “A pang a daih hma in pawnah ṭhangteah (Thil phona) a ruang hi dah a ngai ang, chuti lo chu inchawi a ngai ang,” an ti a.

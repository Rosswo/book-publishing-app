Thlahtute chhuan chhui dan tlangpuiah chuan kum 25 zel chhuan khat a chhiar a ni a. Ṭhatinkuala a ṭanga Pu Hemphunga thleng hian chhuan 11 a ni a. Kum 275 chhung Fânai ho hi an Lal tihna a ni. Ṭhatinkuala a ṭang a Chhunruanga thleng hi Burma Chin Hill a an awm lai in an Lal a. Chhuan 7 a ni. Burma Chin Hill-ah kum 165 an Lal tihna a nih chu. Rorehlova chin hi Mizoram a an awm hnu a Lal-te an ni a, Mizoramah hian chhuan 4 an Lal a ni. Kum 100 chhung Mizoramah hian an Lal hman tihna a nih chu. Kum 1900 hma lam kha chuan Mizoram-ah hian mihring an la tlem in an la thawl hle a. Khua pawh a la tlem hle a ni. Chi leh chi, khua leh khua inrun an chin a vangin khaw khat a in tlem te te in an awm ngam lo a ni.

Hetiang hi kan inthlah chhawn dan chu a ni a, A hming Maka, Remruattua remruat dan hi a mak khawp mai. Min thlahtute zingah pawh ṭhenkhat chu an hming kha hnam hmingin a kal nghal a, ṭhenkhat-te erawh chu hnam hmingin a kal ve chuang lem lo. Ka fate hian hmanlai Mizo Lal te an lal lai aṭang a Lal thlah kal zel kan ni tih an lo hriat a, midangte a innghat lova Lalpa rin chhan chung leh Lal thlah kan ni tih inhria a midangte tana chhenfak awma an awm zawk beisei in heng min thlahtute hi ka han ziak lang a ni.

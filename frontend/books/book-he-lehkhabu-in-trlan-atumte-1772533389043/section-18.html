Cyclostyle a chanchinbu ka her lai

Chu Cyclostyle hmang chuan Magazine ang ve deuh hian ka chhuah ve ta a, chuta ka thu chhuah chu; Chanchin thar tlem, Hriselna lam, Pathian thu tlem leh Bible chang zawnawlna thupui nei in. Ka Cyclostyle chu thing a siam a nih a vangin arei hnu chuan achàng a, anawr kâi kim hleithei tawh lova, mahse Lalpa’n mite thinlungah hna a thawk a, Lunglei Chanmari Kohhran BMP-te chuan ka dil ni miah lovin Cyclostyle ṭha leh man tam tak mai chu a thlawn in min pe leh ta a. Chu chuan ka hna chu ka chhunzawm leh thei ta a ni.

Chu khawl Lalpa’n min pek, ka kut a hlantu Lunglei Chanmari Kohhran BMP-te chuan chutiang khawl lian leh rit tak mai ramdang Bangladesh a ka keng thla tur chu ramriah buaina ka tawh palh min hlauhthawn pui em em mai a. Mahse kei chuan, Lalpa hruaina in tluang takin Bangladesh Mire ram chu ka phur thleng a ni. Ka khawl thar chu ka hmang ṭangkai em em mai a. Magazine pawh fel fai leh ṭha takin ka chhuah thei ta a ni. Weekly, Monthly leh Quarterly pawh ni lem lovin ‘Hman chang changly’ in ka chhuah ve ṭhin a. Mire Kristian te’n chhiar nuan an ti ve khawp mai. Chu ka Cyclostiyle chu kan khawmpui nikhuate hian kan hmang tangkai thei ṭhin khawp mai.

Mahmingi pasal fanaute leh ka fanu Mazuali nen

Nula Lalhmingsangi (Mahmingi) chuan engemaw changa zai pahin kan eitur a lo buaipui reng bawk. Pathian in zaimite a awmpui a, zan a rei tulh tulh a an aw lah aṭha tulh tulh mai bawk

Chumi zan chuan mawitak leh ṭhatakin Mire hla, lam har tak tak chu Cassette 5 ngawt kan thun khat vek nia! Hla zir chawp kan la ni mah mah. Chuta ṭang chuan ka ṭhiante ho nen chuan ṭhian ni mai lovin UNAU-ah Lalpa’n min siam a, Lawmkima Kawlni leh a farnu fa, Lalhmingsangi (Mahmingi) te phei chu Lalpa’n chhungkhatah min siam a, tun thleng hian Lungleiah chuan Mahmingite in chu ka inpui berah ka hmang ta hial a ni. Lalhmingsangi (Mahmingi) phei chuan ka fanu Lalhmangaihzualzeli (Mazuali) leh ka fapa Mika Lalmuanawma (Avala) te chu an sikul kal theih tirh a tangin a fa ang mai in a enkawlin a buaipui a ni.

Apasal neih hnu pawhin an enkawl chhunzawm zel a, apasal neih zan chuan ka fanu Mazuali chuan a ni hnen a mut a ngen tlat mai a, a pi te’n amuthilh hnuah an pawm hrang thei hram a, khami zan kha chu buaithlak tak a ni. Mahmingi chu a nulat lai aṭangin ka fate avangin chhangchhe nu ang maiin a awm phah a, a pasal neih hnu pawh chuan kan pafa thum chuan min la chhawm ta zel a ni. Apasalte chhung hi miṭha tak chhung an lo ni hlauh mai a, keini pafa ho pawh in (Mizoram a ka chhoh changin) kan in ang main ka fate Hostel aṭang a an rawn chhuah chuan kan awm a ni.

Ka fanu Mazuali leh ka fapa Avala chu pawl sawm an nih thleng chu Chawngfianga Memoprial School (CMS) neitu U Hmingtea’n (F.Rohmingliana) an Hostel a dah in Monthly fee min awl vek a, U Hmingte a chungah hian lawm thu sawi dan pawh ka thiam lo a ni. Lalpa’n kawng engkimin kan ṭhatna tur hlirin min kaihruai a, ka fate an têt lai a ṭha tak a sikul an kal theihna turin hmangaih tak a lo enkawl turte min pe zel hi ka va han lawm ṭhin tak em.

Hmun dang dang a Kristian-te hla ka zirtir hman lohna hmun a mite pawh in he Cassette hmang hian anmahni in hla an lo zir thiam thei a, Kohhran kan phuisui phah hle a ni. Tlaivar thak a hah tak a min zai saktute leh sum tam tak min sen saktu BCM chungah Mire Kristiante hmingin lawmthu ka sawi mawlh mawlh e.

3.2.2. Lalmuanpuii’n (Puitei) Mire hla Cassette a siam : Kum 2004 khan nau nei turin Mizoram ah kan rawn chhova, nau kan neih zawh chuan Bangladesh a Mire ramah kan rawn haw thla leh a, Office ah leh Field ka buai theih em a vangin ka nau Lalmuanpuii (Puitei) chu nau min awmpui turin ka hruai thla a, ka nau Puitei hian ka Nu a chhun a, zai amisa riau mai a. Ka Nu hi a vanglai chuan Fanai tlang dungah nà ná nâ chuan zai thiam leh aw ṭha a hmingthang ve kha a ni a.

Puitei chu nauawm pahin a zai ṭhin a, chutih lai a kan in chu kawngpui hnuai chiah a awm hi a ni a, chutia Puitei zai chu kawngpuia kalte chuan an hriat in Radio a zai emaw ti in Rdio ngaih thlak tum chuan an rawn kal ṭhin a, Puitei nauawm zai mai a lo ni ṭhin si a. Kum tawp lam MCC khawmpui chuan Puitei chu solo turin an ngen nasa ta mai a, a ni lah chuan, “Mire hla ka sa thiam lo, Biakin a zai chu ka duh lo,” a ti bur mai bawk si a.

Kan thlem kan thlem fe hnu chuan inkhawmpui tawp lam chuan a Solo ve ta nge nge a, chutia a zai chuan Mire upa ho leh ṭhalai ho chuan, “I Tapy ah khan Puitei zai kha min Record sak rawh,” an ti a, kei chuan, “Hla Record natur Cassette ruak pawh ka nei lo,” ka tih pawh chuan, Battery leina tur pawisa min pe a, min nawr lui ta tlat mai a.

Chutia hnial ngaihna awm lo khawp a min ngen tak a vang chuan kan ṭhenawm pa in sak,(Tun a kan thenawm) a sak zawh tawh si, an la luah hma, in ruakah ka tingtang-te leh kan Keyboard te, tawngkhat pawh tling manglo chuan Mire hla Cassette khat chu kan thun ta a. Kum thar 2005 kumtir lam chuan ka nau Puitei chu a haw a lo hun tawh si a, a haw dawn chuan Empu khua ṭhalaite chuan, “Mizoram inthlen hunah ṭhatakin hla Cassette min siamsak ngei ngei dawn nia,” an ti a.

Kum 2005 January thla chuan Mizoram lam pa nin Puitei te nen chuan Mizoram-ah kan chhova, Chittagong kan thlen chuan Puitei chu a hritlang hle mai a, Tlabung kan thlen chuan a khua a sik titih deuh tawh a, Lunglei kan thlen chhoh phei chuan a khua a sik hluah mai a. Kei chu Mire hla Cassatte siamna a sum min thawh sakturin ka ngamtlak Pu R.Thanmawia Venghlun te nupa ka bia a, an nin a sum senso min tum sak thei dawn a, Sound treck min siam sak turin Pu C.Lalzarmawia kan be leh a, Pu C.Lalzarmawia chuan Sound treck 5 min siam sak hnu chuan Sound treck chu ka la a, Clucose te ka lei a, ka farnu Lalmuanpuii (Puitei) damlo tlun hluah hluah mai chu ka ṭawngṭai a, a khawsik pawh chu chawplehchihin a reh a, “Puite, tho la Clucose kha in la chaw ei bawk la intiharh in hla chu zir rawh le,” ka ti a.

Puitei chuan, “A-u, ka la chak lo in ka tha ala khur a sin, bâkah Mire hla ka sa ngai bawk si lova, an ṭawng a har bawk si” a ti a, kei chuan, “Puite, Lalpa’n Mire hla sa turin a ti dam tawh che a, ṭan lak chu i tih ve tur a ni,” ka ti a, Clucose te a in a chaw a ei zawh chuan hla chu a zir ve ta nghal a. A hla sak ngai loh Mire hla sak chu harsa a ti hle mai a, kei chuan a sak dik loh laite chu ka lo hrilh in a sa ṭha leh zel a, kar khat chhung lek chuan Mire hla 14 lai mai chu kan Record thei ta a, Chu chu Copy tam tak a siamin Mire ramah chuan ka keng thla ta a. Empu khua a Puitei Mire Cassatte Relist lai

Mire ram ka thlen chuan Mire ṭhalaite chu an lawm hle a, ropui takin kan Relist a, chumi Cassatte chu Mizoram a chhohpui leh in Pu R.Thanmawia (Pu Mawia) te nupa ṭanpuina bawk in Music Vedio kan siam leh ta a, Puitei chu Bualpui ‘H’ ah a awm daih a vangin ka han ko hman tawh si lova Lunglei a Moria ka unau nu Lalhlimpuii (Mahlimi) chu hla sawm lai ka shooting tir a, Lunglei zaithiam; Pency B.Lalthanglia ni, Bethsy Lalrinsangi, K.Lalthangzela, Jenet Remsangpuii leh Pi Mamua ni (Pu C.Lalzarmawia nupui) te’n hla pakhat ṭheuh min sak sak bawk a. Heta an hla sak hi tunlai hla ka thlan khawm a ni.

Ahnuah ka nau Lalmuanpuii (Puitei) zai bawk Mire hla bawkin Chrismas hla leh lengkhawm zai kan Record leh in Music Vedio kan siam leh a, a vaiin Mire hla Music Vedio hla 42 mawi tak takin kan siam a ni. Kan awmna velah chuan Banhla te hi phun nachang chu an hre ve na in a hmuna siam lovin kawng kamah leh an duh duhna laiah an phun ngawt zel a, atuk hunah aleitu vai an tuk tir a, atuktu vai lah chuan aneitu zawk Banhla man pe lova midang a pek changte a lo awm ṭhin a, banhla bu ru a inpuh reng rengin an buai ṭhin a, a hmun siam tura ka tih lah chuan tumahin min bengkhawn duh bar pawh an awm si lova, keiman ahmun chu ka siam ta a, chuta ṭang chuan zawi zawi in a hmun siam nachang an hre tan ve ta a ni.

8. Anmahni ke a ding thei tura buatsaih : Ramthim a Lalpa rawng kan bawlna reng rengah hian Mission in a tum ber chu kan thawhna hnamte anmahni ke a ding thei tura buatsaih hi a ni a, amârawh chu an ke a dingthei tura kan beihnaah hian Field a dang ang bawk in an ke a ding thei tur pawhin an mamawh a dang ṭheuh ngei ang. Mahni ke a ding thei tura hun duh rei zawng pawh a inang lo viau ngei ang. Kan enkawl dan azir pawh a ni thei bawk.

Kan unau Miriate hi tunhma aṭanga lal nei lova khaw te tak te te a, a hnam hnam a mi tlem te te a lo awm ṭhin an nih a vangin Kristian Vanram Kawngzawh a, “Mitin mahni thu thu a awm tur,” tih ang deuh kha an ni a, mitin mai hian khuahkhirhna hi an ngithei lo em em mai a, chuvang chuan Kristiante pawh rinna kawnga puitlin lam pan an harsat phah hle a ni. Chubakah khawsak harsa tak takte an ni a, chumai ala ni lo, changkanna lamah hma an sawn mawh em em mai bawk a, an nunphungah thil tih ho dial dial peih lo mi an ni a, khaw khatah pawh in tam an awm thei lova, khaw te tak te te in an awm ṭhin a, khawkhat hotu Kharbari an nei ve takna in chung an khaw hotu kharbarite pawh chuan khaw chhung enkawl leh rorelna-ah awmzia an nei em em hran chuang lem lo.

An hnam zingah hian ‘Mithiam’ ti a han sawi tur an awm meuh rih lova, sorkar leh Politic lamah pawh hruaitu fel leh rintlak an la awm meuh lo bawk, Kristian-te zingah lekphei chuan kan Lal Isua’n tehkhin thu a a sawi, Buhchi theh thu ang khan chanchinṭha a theh a theh phuar a, a ṭo chhunte pawh enkawl zui leh si lova kalsan leh ta te, ṭhen khat chu sum siam nan a hmangtu chanchinṭha hriltu der ho te’n an lo ti khawbaw zo tawh bawk nen, Pathian thu sawi hawt si lova, “Kristian a in awm chuan mi pakhatah cheng sanghnih ṭheuh kan pe ang che u/sikul,tuikhur kan pe ang che u,” ti a lo thlem tawk lah bo lo.

An hnam kalphung leh miziaah pawh chanchinṭha hril a harsat tawh tehlul nen, heng chanchinṭha hriltu derte avang pawh hian Mire ramah chanchinṭha hril a hautak takmeuh a ni. Chu bakah Bangladesh Mosolman ram a awm an nih a vangin Mizo Kristian te’n duh angin kan luhchilh tei bawk si lo va, kan unau Mire Kristian-te mahni ke a ding thei tur chuan hun kan la duh rei deuh hmel khawp mai.

Mahse, Lal Isuan Luka 19:40-ah, “A ni erawh chuan, “Ka hrilh a che u, hengte hi an ngawih reng chuan lungte tal an au chhuak tur a ni,” a ti a, eng ang pawhin har mahse, Lalpa rinchhan a beidawng mai lova kan beih zel chuan lungte auchhuah hun nghak kher lova keini hi Mire hnam zinga chanchinṭha hril a kan au chhuah zel chuan kan thawhrimnate chu Lalpa’n mal a sawm ngei ka beisei tlat a ni. Mire Kristian te mahni ke a ding thei tura buatsaih tur chuan beidawng mai lova kawng thui tak kan kal a la ngai ngei dawn a, kei ni thawktu leh Mission pawh Mire hnam tan hian hma kan lo lak tawh laklawh a vangin an tan a uikawm viau a rem dawn lo vin anmahni ke a an din theih nan kan inhuam thui viau pawh a ngai ngei dawn a ni.

Hetiang hi an dinhmun a la nih miau avangin anmahni ke a ding thei tura buahsaih tur chuan anmahni hnam zinga mi ngei hruaitu ṭha chher chhuah sak a ni lothei lo a ni. Field dang angin Mire hnam zingah hian Missionary kan in tir lut zung zung thei lova, achhan chu Bangladesh Muslim County a awm an nih a vangin Missionary a in tirh luh theih loh hrim hrim a ni. A luh dan kawng chu arammi nihna neih leh Bangla (Bengali ṭawng) thiam a ni.

Heng zawng zawngte avang hian Mire Kristiate hi mahni ke a ding thei turin kan pui thei lo tihna a ni lova, Lalpa rinchhan a Mission in hma kan lâk a, an kut leh ke a ding thei tura aṭul anga tih sak kan inhuam chuan hun duh rei deuh mahse, engtihah emaw chuan anmahni kut leh ke in an la ding ngei ang. Mahse, kan beidawn san emaw, Mire hnam tan a kan uikawm viau emaw, an hnamah hruaitu chher chhuah kan hlau/duh lo tlat emaw a nih chuan kan unau Mire hnam hi engtik khawtikah mah taksa leh thlarauah an ding chhuak ve ngai dawn lo a ni, chuan an thisen mawh kan phur ngei bawk ang.

Tunah hian Baptist Church of Maruchah hian Assembly kan nei tawh a, Centralization System kan hmang ve bawk a, kum 2013 Assembly aṭang phei kha chuan thilpek lamah kan harh ve hle in ka hria a, kan khua Empu a kan Mission in 32 lek, thingtlang lo nei a eizawng, rethei te te te’n tunhma chuan kum khatah sing ruk/sing sarih te kan sawma pakhat a tlin chuan kan lawm ve em em ṭhin kha a ni a, kum 2013 hnu lam kha chuan Biakin ṭha leh keimahni tawk a ropui ve tak kan sak mek lai pawh in kan sawma pakhat tling khawm chu nuai chuang fe a ni ve thei tawh a ni. Baptist Church of Maruchah hmun atan Baptist Church of Mizoram in Dingte khaw bulah ram tha tak mai leh tui pawh thatna Ekar 5-lai a zau kan lei sak tawh bawk, hemi thla kan lakna lai hi a ni.

Kan unau Mire Kristian te hi mahni ke a ding thei tura buatsaih tur chuan keini Mire ram a Lalpa rawngbawl a hma la mekte hian kan sawi tak ang khian, Hruaitu ṭha chher chhuah sakte, Kohhran kalphung mumal taka kaihhruaite, Treining chi hrang hrang pekte, School Upgrade sakte, Clinic tih phusui zelte, Rinna kawng a hma an sawn ve theihna a tan a an mamawh hrang hrang phuhruk sakte, sawi tur a tam mai; kan tihsak an la ngai a ni. Galatia 3:3 ah chuan, “Heti emin in â maw? Thlarauva tan tawh siin tisain in zo dawn em ni?” a ti a, kei ni pawh Thlarauvin kan tan tawh a, tisa chuan tawpsan mai i tum lo ang u aw.

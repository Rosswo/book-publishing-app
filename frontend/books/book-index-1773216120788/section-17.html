BUNG 16

MIRIA-TE KHAWVEL

Tunah chuan Miria-te khawvelah lo lut dawn tawh ila: Kan unau Miria-te hi keini Mizo, Bawm leh Pang-te chuan ‘Miria’ kan ti a, Vai (Bengoli) chuan ‘Murung’ kan ti a, Mawk ho chuan ‘Mrow’ an ti a, Sap chuan ‘Mru’ an ti a, Burma Arakanese chuan ‘Miu’ an ti a, anmahni Mire hnam te chuan hnam dang zing a an hnam hming an ziak dan chu ‘Mro/Mru’ ti in an ziak thung.

Anmahni Miria leh Miria-ah chuan Maruchah (Mruchowh ti a ziak pawh an awm) ti in an insawi ṭhin. “Maruchah” tih awmzia chu ‘Mihring fa’ tihna a ni a, ‘Maru’ tih chu ‘Mihring’ tihna a ni a, ‘Chah’ tih chu ‘Fa/tĕ’ tihna a ni. Mahse ‘Maruchah’ tih chu, “Mihring fa” tihna ni mai lovin anmahni hnam bik sawina a ni. Entirnan: Mizo hi; Mi = Mihring. Zo = hmun sang/tlangsang hmun vawt sawina a nih lai in tlang ram a cheng hnam dang. Entirnan- Butan, Nepal leh tlangram a mi zawng zawngte hi Mizo kan ti lova, anmahni pawhin Mizo an inti bawk hek lo. Chutiang chiah chuan Mihringfa zang zawng hi Miria an ni lova, Maruchah chu hnam hlawm khat, anmahni hnam bik insawina a ni.

Miria te pawh hi hmanlai chuan Burma-ah an lo awm tawh ṭhin a, tunah pawh Burma Arakan State chhung Akyab District-ah ṭhahnem tak an la awm bawk a ni. Anmahni chuan Burma a an awm hma hian khawi hmunah nge an awm a, khawi hmun aṭangin nge Burma-ah chuan an lo awm tih an sawi thei tawh lo a ni. Historian ṭhenkhat chuan Miria te hi Mongalian thlah ni in an sawi.

Burma Paltwabial, tunhma a Northern Arakan an tihah chuan kum 1901 vel khan mi 2500 vel an lo awm tawh a ni. Hetih lai hian Kolodine lui fintu Mi lui kam velah an cheng ber a, Bangladesh Chittagong Hill Tract-ah pawh kum 1901 chhiarpui khan 10,540 lai an lo awm tawh a ni. Hmanlai a Arakan Hill Tract ti a an sawi ṭhin; tun a Paletwa bial velah pawh khian an lo cheng tawh ṭhin a, mahse, chuta ṭang chuan Khumi hnam te’n an nawr chhuak ni in an sawi.

Mr Hodgson-a chuan Khumi, khiang leh Miria (Miu) te hi Chittagong Hill Tracts luah hmasatu te ni in a ring. Kan unau Miria-te hi keini Mizo-te ai chuan an lo thlangtla hmasa fe zawk ni in a lang; Pu B.Lalthangliana’n India, Burma leh Bangladesh a Mizo-te chanchin a ziakna phek 214-na a a sawi dan chuan, “Kum 1650 – 1700 vel khan Mizo ho te’n Mizoram hi an rawn luah deuh vek tawh a, tlem a zawng chauh Lusei zing a Hualngo-te chu Chin State-ah an la chambang a ni,” a ti. Kan unau Miria te’n Bangladesh Chittagong Hill Tracts an rawn thlen thlak hun Bangladesh Sorkar Magazine Pakhik Palabodol 1997 September ni 7 a chhuak, phek 29-na in a tarlan dan chuan kum 1418 daih tawh kha a ni.

Mizo upate chuan, “Mizo-te Burma a ṭanga kan thlang tlak khan tum khatah a rual a kal vek theih a nih loh a vangin chhuak hmasa leh chhuak hnuhnungte kan awm a, chhuak hnuhnung turte chuan chhuak hmasa turte hnenah ti hian thu an chah a, ‘Nakin a in hnung zui a thlang kan rawn tlak ve hunah in hnu rawn chhui a awlsam nan in kalna a piangah sulhnu in lo hnuchhiah zel dawn nia,’ ti in thu an chah a.

Thlangtla hmasa te chuan an thu inchah ang chuan an kalna a piangah thingpil thel in sulhnu an lo hnuchhiah zel a, chu an thing thelah chuan Muk thing hi an lo thel a, Muk thing chu hnai nei a ni a, thel aṭang a rei vak lovah an thelna hnuhma chu rei tak thel tawhna ang mai in a dum vek mai a. Tichuan thlangtla hnuhnung zawkte chuan an unau thlangtla hmasate sulhnu chu chhui zelin thlang an tla ve ta a.

An unau thlangtla hmasate Muk thing thel an han hmuh chuan a lo dum vek tawh si a, an unau thlangtla hmasate chu an thleng hla hle tawh tura an ngaih a vangin an duhna lam lamah an kal ta a. Unau za pawh chu awmtlang thei tawh lovin an inbo zo ta a. Aṭhen Mizoram-ah, aṭhen in Mizoram kal pelin India ramah, aṭhen Burma leh Bangladesh-ah te kan lo awm darh ta a ni,” an ti.

Kan pi pu-te chuan kan unau thlang lo tla hmasa a Muk thing lo theltu te chu aeng hnam ber nge tih an sawi silova, keini thangtharte hian, ‘Atu a te hian nge Muk thing chu lo thel ang?’ tih hi kan in rinsiak angai ta a ni. Ka zawh theih chinte ka han zawh kual ngial leh Mizo History bu hrang hrang ka chhiar hnu pawhin kan unau thlang lo tla hmasa a Muk thing lo theltu te chu ka hrechhuak thei lova, Pu Malsawma Kawlni in Zofate chenna chin map a siamah chuan, “Kan unau thlangtla hmasa a Muk thing lo theltu te chu Sian ho ni in a lang,” ati a. A ni pawhin, “An ni” a ti chiah bik lo a ni.

Kan unau thlangtla hmasa a Muk thing lo theltu te chu Sian ho chu an ni thei lo, a chhan chu Burma-ah Sian State chu Burma ram chhak lam Chiana nen a an inrina lam a awm a ni a, an State pawh hi a zim chhe lo khawp mai District 52 lai a awm bawk. Mire History ka chhuina leh Mire upate ka kawmna a ṭangin kan unau thlangtla hmasa a Muk thing theltu te chu Miriate hi an ni tih ka hre thei a ni.

Bung hmasa lam a kan sawi tawh ang khan, kan unau Miriate zinga rawng ka bawl ṭantirh khan Miria te hi hnam dang nun dan leh tihdan lâk a, zir hi an harsat hle a ni tih ka hriat avangin ka theih ang ang chuan anmahni ang a awm hram hram hi ka tum a, chu bâkah Mizo hnahthlak an ni ve a ni tih hi hriat tir ngei ka tum bawk a, Mizo te nen kan mizia pawh a inan na lai a tam khawp mai. Mizo nihna aṭang nilova Maruchah (Miria) nihna aṭang a an zinga rawngbawl chu ka tih ngei ngei ngai ni in ka hria a ni.

Mizo leh Miria te hi hnam inunau kan nih zia hrilhfiah nan kan mize inan-na laite, kan ṭawng inan na laite, kan khawsak phung inanna lai leh kum tam tak liam ta a Burma aṭang a thlang kan lo tlak dan te, Chhinlung chhuak an nih ve zia leh an ni Mire hnamah chuan ‘Simlung’ hnam an awm lehnghal a, Mizo hlaphuahtu in, “Kawngres PC Unian a lem chuang lo, Chhinlung chhuak Zofate kan ni,” tih hlate sak sak in thlang kan tlak lai a, thlangtla hmasa te’n Muk thing an lo thel a vanga inbo ta kan nih thu te ka han sawi chuan ngaihnawm an ti thei hle ṭhin a.

Pu Malsawma Kawlni in, Mizo fa te chenna chin map asiam chu ka thusawi ti chiangtu a tan ka entir zel bawk a; a ṭangkai khawp mai. Mahse kan awmna hi rambuai a nih a vangin ka kawl reng ngam ta lova ui chung chungin kan kawmthlang kawrah ka paih ta a ni. He map hlu tak mai hi ahunlai a Mizoram a fiamthu thiam hmingthang Lalbiakzuala Hauhnar min pek a ni. Chutia a thu leh hla leh map te nen a Miria te leh Mizo te chu hnam inunau kan nih zia ka han sawi chuan ngaihnawm ti em em hian an lo ngaithla ṭhin a. Chutia kan hnam History leh Zofa te kan thlangtlak dante ka han sawi a Muk thing thel thu ka han sawi chuan ngaihnawm ti tak leh ngawi reng a ka thu sawi an ngaihthlak lai chuan ben har zawk ang mai hian an lo inrawlh thuai a, an thlangtlak dan leh Muk thing theltu an nih dante chu phur fahran hian an lo sawi ve var a.

An sawi dan chuan, “Burma a ṭanga kan thlangtlak dawn in (An thlangtlak ṭanna chiah hi an sawi thei tawh lova) keini Mire hnam hi thlangtla hmasa ber kan ni a, kan unau, kan hnung a thlang rawn tla ve turte chuan, ‘In hnu rawn chhui zelin nakinah hmun khatah kan awm ho leh a nga, in kalna kan hriat theih nan in kalna apiangah sulhnu lo hnuchhiah zel ula, ram ṭha, awmna atan a hmun ṭha in thlen hunah kan rawn thlen ve hun a in sak a kan buai a ngaih loh nan in min lo sak kep dawn nia,’ an ti a.

Kan unau te thu min chah ang chuan thlang kan tlakna kawngah chuan thing pil te thelin sulhnu kan nei zel a, kan thing thelah chuan Muk thing hi kan lo thei palh a, Muk thing chu thel aṭang a rei vak lovah a dum nghal vek a vangin kan unau thlangtla hnuhnungte chuan kan Muk thing thel, rei tak thelna hnu ang mai a dum vek tawh chu an han hmuh chuan kan thleng hla lutuk tawh tura an ngaih a vangin kawng dang lamah min pen bo san ta vek a ni ang, tun thleng hian min rawn fin thei tawh lo a ni .

Mahse kan thlahtute a ṭanga thuchah a nih a vangin in erawh chu kan la sak kêp reng a, chu chu ‘In zawm (kim tawm)’ kan ti a, chu in zawm chu kan in nen a inzawm in kan sa a, tap pawh kan siam a, tunhma chuan luah lohvin kan dah reng a, tunah erawh chuan kan unau te’n min rawn fin theih tak loh a vangin kan luah ve tawh a, chaw eina hmun, inkawm khawmna hmun leh mikhual riahna hmunah kan hmang tawh a ni. Mizo te chu kan unau thlangtla hnuhnung, Muk thing kan thel avang a peng bota, in kan lo sak kepte ngei chu in lo nih chu,” an ti a ni.

61Mire in

Keini Mizo te ai chuan kan unau Miria te hi an haihawt lo zawk a ni maithei, thil chhut ngai chi ni lovin thu inchah hriat reng theina lamah chuan kan tluk lo ni in a lang, a chhan chu keini Mizo te chuan in min lo sak kêp tur a thu kan inchah kan hriat ve tawh loh a vangin. An ni chuan an thlangtlak tirh a thlangtla hnuhnung zawk te’n an tan a in lo sak kep tur a an thu lo chah chu vawng rengin tun thleng hian in an la sak kêp reng a, chu chu a ni ‘In zawm’ (Kim tawm) an tih chu.

Unau kum rei tak inhmu tawh lo leh inhre tawh lo in Miria leh Lusei te chu kan han inhmuh chuan kan lo inhai nasa hle tawh hi a lo ni a, kan khawsakna ram leh tlang a lo dangin kan boruak hip a lo inang lo nasa tawh mai si a, kan ṭawng pawh a lo dang nual tawh bawk nen; mahse unau pianghmun, chhul khat chhuak pawh kan awmna ram leh hmun, kum za tam a lo dang tawh a nih chuan chutiang a khawsak phung, ṭawng, mizia leh nun phung dan leh hrai nasa tak a a lo danglam pawh chu awm lo a ni hran lo reng a ni.

Chutia Miria leh keini Lusei(Mizo) te chu unau, chhul khat chhuak kan ni tih kan han inhriat meuh chuan kan hlim tlangin kan inbiak dante chu a lo danglam in inkungkaihna thuk tak, tuma phelh leh rual loh hmangaihna a phuar belhbawmin kan lo awm ta, a va ropui in a va lawmawm tak em.

Kan unau Miria te hi Bangladesh Chittagong Hill Tracts phai ram hmun ṭha chin thlengin an lo inzar pharh a. Mahse, tunah chuan vai hovin tlang ram lamah an nawr chhuak leh deuh vek tawh a ni. Mire ram a ka zuk thlen thlak hlim khan Alikodom phai lamah mi engemaw zat chu an lo la awm a, mahse chung an awmna hmun ṭha te chu tunah chuan vai hovin an luah zo deuhthaw tawh a ni.

Miria te Mizia leh nunphung

Miria te hi Juda-te ang a dan ngaina tak an ni a, nupui pasal an inneih laite hian akhirh thei ang berin dan an siam emaw tihturin dan te hi an tikhirh thei hle mai a. Upate pawh an dan hriat leh an dan hman pawh ainang vek lova, an inneih lai a an dan hman chu hmeichhe lam upate dan hriat hi an hmang tlangpui ṭhin. An lo neih, in sak dan, khaw siam chungchang, inthawina leh ruai buatsaih leh kawng hrawng hrangah dan a tam em em mai a. Mire tlangval sam zial tur

Mahse, Miria te hi dan ngaina viau e tilo chuan zalenna hi an ngaipawimawh leh hle lawi bawk si a. Chaw ei laite hian an kut in mi hma a mi pawh nise an duhzawng sahrang leh chawhmeh chu kut a zen a hmet a dapin an duh duh chu la nalh nalh mahse an tan chuan thil mawi lo a ni hran lo. Naupang pawh; ‘mitin mahni thu thu a awm tur,’ ti ang hrim in nu leh pa leh aia upate khuahkhirhna hnuaiah an awm lo ni hian a hriat a, ruai siamna leh huau huaunaahte an kal duh leh duh loh chu naupangte pawh ni se anmahni thu thu a ni mai a.

Naupang puaklai te pawh zial an zu khu hlat hlat ka hmu fo ṭhin. Aia upate pawh an hming pumin an ko mai ṭhin a, Pastor Chownglowk kum sawmsarih chuang fe tawh pawh naupang te te pawh in Chownglowk, ti in an ko mai ṭhin. Naupangte,(anmahni fa te pawh) tirh an har em em a, han tir ila, “Ka peih lo,” an tih tawh chuan an nu leh pa leh aia upa pawhin tihngaihna vak an hre tawh chuang lo.

Sa hi an ngaina em em mai a, an hnathawk kal tur emaw, an hnathawk lai pawh ni se, sa kâp emaw, awk emaw an awm a nih chuan an hnathawh lai ṭhulh ṭhakin sa chan tur chuan chem. kengin an thawk chhuak chiam mai ṭhin. Sa a nih tawh phawt chuan a te emaw, a lian emaw, sa hrisel emaw, hrisel lo emaw, a ma thi pawh ni se ei loh an nei meuh lo ama ni tih turin an sa ei loh chu a tlem khawp mai, an hnam bik in an ei serh (ei thiang lo) sa te a nih ngawt loh chuan.

Nula leh tlangvalte hi an zalen hle a, ruai leh huau huau hi an lawm hle bawk kan tih tawh kha. Khaw dangah pawh ni se, eng hunah pawh huau huau awmnaah chuan an kal mai ṭhin. Nu leh pa te pawh in khap an tum chuang lem lo

62Mire naupang mei zu lai

Ṭawng zamawh buru pui pui sawi pawh hi a hmei a pa in an uar em em a, an inkoh dan pawh zahmawh ngialngan tak tak leh hming ni a mawi lo tak taka inkoh an ching khawp mai. Ran ek, entirnan; uiek, sanghar ek, pangang, leh zawng chhu, tite a inkoh pawh an ching hle bawk. An nun danah hian ‘hmasawn tumna an nei miah lo ama ni chu aw’ tih mai tur hian a tu a te pawh hian hmasawnna lam kawng an zawh lo em em a, midang nun changkang zawkte han zir tumna pawh an nei lo ama ni chu tih mai turin nun dan ṭha leh changkang an zir duh lo em em a ni. Bawlhhlawh leh ṭawp an ngaithei hle bawk. Inbual erawh an peih khawp mai, an inbual uluk lo mai pawh a.

An nun dan hi kawng engkimah hian an inthlahdah em em vek ni mai hian a hriat a, thianghlimna leh inchhungkhur khawsak vawn fel, khaw chhung leh tuikhur tih thianghlimna lamah pawh zirtir an harsa em em a ni, kan zirtir tam ve tawh viau nain tun thleng hian ekin nei sawi tham an la awm meuh lo. Mire hnam ang a inthlahdah leh hmasawn tum lo hnam hi hnam dang zingah hian an awm ve angem aw tih mai a awl ṭhin khawp mai. Hei vang hian Mire hnam zing a Kristiante pawhin taksa leh thglarau lamah hma an sawn theih loh phah in Kristian lo te tan anmahni hnam Kristian te aṭang a zir tur an hmuh loh a vangin Kristian pawh kan punchak loh phah ni in ka hre ṭhin. Mahse, tun hnai aṭangin tlem tlem in Kristian te chu Kristian lote ai chuan thianghlina lamah hma an sawn ve deuh tawh.

Mire hnamte hi hnam huaisen tak an nilova, an inhlau tawn ṭhum emaw tih mai tur a ni. An khaw hotu Kharbari te pawh hi khawchhung rorelnaah an ṭangkai tur angin an ṭangkai ṭhin lo hle a. An intihthiam loh te pawh hian han inhnial vek emaw, insual emaw ai chuan aruk a indawi emaw, tura inhrai emaw an ching zawk ṭhin a ni. An lo leh huan thlaite pawh an indawi chhiat sak fo ṭhin bawk. Thil tih tur emaw, thu ṭha han hrilh hian “aw, ni e,” han tih mai hi chu an awlsam em em em a, an tihpuitlin loh pawh in pawi ti awm pawhin an lang lem lo.

Khaw khatah in tlem te te a awm an nih a vang tepawh a ni mahna; inlungrual an thiam lo em em mai a, a lian a ṭanga a te ber thlengin mitin mahni thu thu in an awm ṭheuh emaw tih mai tur an ni. An hnam nunah hian inngaihdamna hi a awm meuh lo, thil an tihsual palh emaw te hian pawisa chawi tir emaw vawk chhun (Chawi) tir emaw a ni deuh zel ṭhin. Ramhuai hi an hlau em em bawk.

Kawng hi an ngaipawimawh lo hle mai a, ṭhenkhat chuan vantlang kalkawngah tak thlam te an sa a, chutiang a kal tlang tur chuan an thlam luhkapui lawn phawt a, chuta ṭanga thing pum te tak te a rahbi an tuk ve hlek chu zawh thlak leh a ngai a, a thlanglamah chuan a sang thei si a, buaithlak thei tak a ni. Khawchhungah pawh ṭhenkhat chuan vantlang kalkawngah tak in an sa a, an luhkapui (Luhkapuiah hian zanah chuan an zung mai ṭhin a, naupangte chu an e bawk ṭhin) hnuailamah te kal a ngai ṭhin. An luhkapui lah chu bawhhlawh tinreng an paihna a ni si a, a kal a hreawm thei ṭhin khawp mai. Khaw kar kawng pawh an lo neihna a danglam angin kumtin deuhthaw hian khaw karkawng ṭhenkhat chu a danglam ṭhin a, an lo neihna lamah khualzin kawng pawh an siam chawp mai ṭhin. Khaw ṭhenkhat khaw kawng chu, “Nikum/hman kumah ka kal tawh a an khaw kawng chu ka hria a lawm,” tih ngawt theih pawh a ni ṭhin lo.

64Mire nu tui chawi lai

Hmeichhia te dinhmun : An mipa te an zalen em em laiin an hmeichhiate thung chu an zalen lo hle mai a, chhuatthlang a mipate ṭhutna a ṭhut ve chu sawi loh; mipa ṭhute hmaah pawh an va kal pawh thiang lo ang hial a ni. Tuikhur a an pawnfen an suk pawhin an pawnfen chu êm chhungah an phur ngai lova, an em phurhna hnam leh êm karah chuan an zep a, tichuan an phur haw a, an pawnfen suk chu an zâr pawhin ualau takah an zar ve tur a nilova, dithlirarfem hulhliapah a zârna an siam a, chutah chuan an zâr mai ṭhin. An pasal te pa leh an pasal te u ṭhutna hma, hnai deuhah an kal tur a nilo.

Pasal nei tawhte chu zing varṭianah an thova, tui an chawi a, tuichawi an rawn haw hnu chuan eirawng an bawl a, eirawngbawl an peih hnu chuan an chhungkua in chaw an ei a, ramṭang chin tawh chu an feh leh nghal ṭhin. Miriate hi an feh chhuak hi an zing thei hle a. an feh haw erawh chu an tlai ṭhin hle thung. Nu ṭhen khatin min hrilh dan chuan, “Kan khawlai a ui ek leh vawk ek te hi kan hmu ngai meuh ṭhin lo,” an ti.

65Mire nu in an puanpui athui zawm lai

An lo feh an rawn haw chuan mipate chu an inbual a, chaw an ei a, ṭhiante nen inkawm in in dangahte an leng ṭhin. Hmeichhiate vethung chu an feh haw chu hah hle mahse an em an nghat a, buh an deng nghal a, chhung rual deuh phei chu an buh den tur a tam si a, an buh den chu bati eng hnuaiah an thlei leh nghal vek bawk a, chutia an buh den an thlei leh vek hnu chuan an inbual a, chaw an ei ve chauh ṭhin. Chaw an ei kham chuan an mut hma chu la an kai leh ṭhin.

66Miria ten ruai an theh lai

Hmeichhiate chu chaw an ei chuan an khup pahnih a ṭhingṭhi in an kut vei lam chuan in nem khalhin hnungzang zawl dawrhin chaw an ei tlangpui. Naupawmte chu an ṭhuthluang mai ṭhin. Hetiang a hmeichhia in chaw an eichhan chu an pawnfen a tawi em a vangin ṭhutlan palh an hlauh vang ni berin a lang. Mipa te erawh chu ṭhutungin chaw an ei tlangpui. Hnathawh an hah bawk a, a hmei a pa in an chaw lak a ṭha thei hle a, hmeichhiate chu chaw an kâ a khat ṭulh chunga ṭawng tuam tuam an ching hle bawk. Lalpa’n a venga, an chaw lâk a ṭha ṭhin bawk a, an taksa a ṭha in an hrisel viau tho a ni. In inhnaih te te an ni a, an hmeichhiate chu han inlènna hun pawh a awm lo a ni bawk a, an eirawngbawl lai hian in dang leh in dang a ṭanga inbiak hi an ching viau bawk.

67Mire hmeichhia buh deng lai

Miriate hian mikhual chaw ei koh hi an ching hle a, mikhual chaw ei a an sawm hian buh balah malsawm dawnnaah an ngai a, chawhmeh nei lo hle mahse hmarhcha rawt nen chauh pawh mikhualte chu chaw ei an sawm tho ṭhin a, rawngbawl a kan kal vel changte hian tuk khatah vawi sawm ai tam chaw kan ei chang a awm ṭhin. Chaw hmeh a lo ṭhat chang phei chuan ei tlem a har ṭhin si a, kan puar lutuk hi nileng a, pum zut a mut hle hle mai awl tak a ni. Tin, mikhual chaw ei a an koh hian mikhual te chu an mahni chhungkaw ei hma in an ei hmasak tir ṭhin.

69Mire infiamna Taket (Insuk paih)

Ho viau mahse kan nun dan inan lohna tihlanna pakhat a tan; hmeichhiate chu ding chungin an zung ṭhin a, mipa te erawh ṭhu chungin an zung ve thung tlat. An pianphung, pumrua leh vun rawngah chuan keni Mizo-te nen kan inang hle a, atlangpui in an mitko a chhah deuh in an mit hmul a tam deuh tlangpui. Hnam hmelchhe tak an nilova, tun ai hi chuan han changkang deuh sela chuan hmelṭha tak tak pawh an awm ve nual ngei ang.

Infiamna (games) an uar vak lova, an hunpui (Festival) nikhua a Mizo in, “Insuk nawr” kan tih ang deuh khan, “Taket” an ti a, suk in emaw, mau in emaw intihtluk/inthehtluk an tum ṭhin, chuta chak te chu an hming a thang ve hle ṭhin. Hei bâk hi chu infiamna mumal tak an nei meuh lo. Tunlaiah chuan hnam dang a ṭanga an zir, hmun dang a sikul kal te’n an rawn chin chhuah infiamna an nei ve nual tawh. An se chhun, bawng chhun nikhua hian hla hmanga inhnial/inel an ching hle a, achak zawk chu an hming athang ve hle thin.

An thil chin ṭha te :

  1. Mikhual chaw ei sawm : Hei hi chu kan sawi tawh a vangin sawi zau tawh lo mai ila.
  2. Damlo zual menpui : Miria te pawh hi Mizo te ang bawk a khaw khat a dam lo zual an awm chuan an inngaihsak a ni tihlan nan inlenpui an uar hle a, tlaivar a ngaih chuan an tlaivar pui bawk ṭhin. An titha khawp mai.
  3. Khawhar in riahpui : Khawhar in riahpui hi an uar ve hle a, mahse Mizo te angin riahpui chin tur bithliah a awm lem lova, nula leh tlangval te’n an riahpui ṭhin.
  1. Tuithawl chhin : Miriate hian tuikhur a tui an chawiin tuithawl in tui an chawi ṭhin a, an tuithawl kâ chu changel hnah khawrin an chin thlap ṭhin a, thuithawl a bawlhhlawh tla tur a veng a, a ṭha khawp mai.
  2. An tel inpek : Mizote ang bawkin ṭhenawm khawvengte chu chawhmeh, antui an insuah in anhnah/chawhmeh an inpe in inngamtlak takin an inban tawn ṭhin.
  3. Chhungkhat inngaihven : Miria te hi chhungkhat leh chi lehkuang inngaihven kawngah hian an fakawm hle a, khawi lo vah chhuahna-ah te pawh ni se hmelhriat loh an biak dawn in an tu, makpa, mo, fa, unau, ṭhian etc…tur nge an nih an inzawt hmasa in chuta ṭang chuan koh dan tur pawh an inhre mai ṭhin. Chhungkhat an in tlawh ṭhin dan chu bung dangah kan la ziak leh ang.
  4. Inkungkaihna ngaihpawimawh : Miriate hian inkungkaihna hi an ngaipawimawh em em a, vawn nun reng an tum ṭhin. An nun aṭang hian keini tunlai chhan a Mizo te hian zir tur kan ngah hle in ka hria. Bung dangah an inkungkaihna nghet turin enge an tih kan la chhiar zel ang.
  5. Ruai siam nikhuaah an tangrual : Mi in ruai a siam dawn chuan fatu tur leh che vel tur a ruat kher a ngai lova, khaw chhungmi te chuan tuma hrilh ngai miah lovin an tihtur ṭheuh chu tlin takin an ti suau suau mai a, an entawn tlak takzet a ni.
  6. Mo in a pasal nu leh pa te a zah thiam : Mo in a pasal nu leh pate a zah thiam em a vangin in khatah mo 4/5 te an awm dual dual a, mo an ngah leh an in chu an sakzawm tam a ni mai a, an pasal nu leh pa te hi ṭhenkhat chuan ahran in chaw ei an siam sak in an kutte hial pawh mo ṭhenkhat chuan an sialsak ṭhin. Mo ṭhenkhat chu pasal nu leh pate tan a tawrhhlelhawm tak takte pawh an awm ve bawk.
  7. Ruk ruk a awm meuh lo : Mire khuaah chuan thil bo sawi tur a awm ngai meuh lo, rukru te an lo awm a nih chuan khua aṭangin an hnawtchhuak hmiah zel ṭhin a, chuvangin Mire khua ah chuan ruk ruk a awm meuh lo.
  8. Inbual an peih : Bangladesh hi ram lum a ni bawk a, Miria te hian inbual an peih em em a, ṭhenkhatte chu nikhatah vawi thumte an inbual ṭhin. An inbual hian a hmei a pa in tuikhurah leh hnathawhna hmunah pawh ni se saruak vekin an inbual ṭhin a, sahbawn an hmang ngai meuh lo, an inbual dawn chauh emaw tih lai in an khel an beng thawk a, a inbual zo der ṭhin, inbual an sam khawp mai. Tunlai ah chuan sahbawn an hmang ve tawh.
  9. Pakkitun : An ruai siam reng rengin ruaiṭheh hma chiahin khua a upate chu vawk thithun leh zu an pe hmasa ziah ṭhin a, hei hi upate zahna tih lanna a ni. Vawk thithun-ah hian vawk thi bâkah sawhthing, aieng, chi, hmarhcha leh sa ṭhenkhat an thun tel ṭhin a, a tui thei ṭhin khawp mai.
  10. Awi u pa : Nu leh Pa te chawimawina hi atheite chuan ruai ropui takin an siam ṭhin a, bawng leh vawk leh ran dangte pawh an talh nual thin bawk. Chutih lai chuan an Nu leh Pa te chaw an ei dawn chuan an kut an sil sak a, a nu leh pa chawimawitu chuan a nu leh pa chu an puanpui thar phah chungah an ṭhut tir a, a nu chu puan thar a sin tir ang a, a pa chu diarvar thar tawng sarih a khim tir bawk ang a, “Ka Nu, ka Pa, ka sèn tek aṭangin min enkawl a, ka dam lohin min enkawlin ka zun ka ek in thian vek a, min enkawlna leh min hmangaihna hi ka rul seng lo che a, hei hi ka lawm thu sawina-ah min pawm sak dawn nia etc…” ti in sa leh buhchangrum in puar tak in a nu leh a pa chu a hrai ṭhin.

Heng ka han tarlan bâkah pawh hian an dan ṭha leh thil chin dan ṭha tak tak tam tak a la awm a, kan ziak lang vek seng lovang a, bung dang kan chhiar zelna aṭangte pawhin kan hre thei bawk ang.

  2. Menrüm Deng : A ni hi unaupa a ka neih a ni a, Computer program lam a mi thiam tak a ni a, a chhe siam pawh a thiam bawk. Ka Leptop achhiat leh a buai chang pawhin a ni hian min siamsak ziah ṭhin a, a part thlak angaih changte hian a ma pawisa sengin min thlak sak ṭhin. Krama sakhaw tichhuaktu Menleh-a’n Mire hawrawp (Alphabet) asiam pawh Computer ah fonts pawh a man siam a ni a.

Aram leh a hnam hi a vei em em a, Malumghat America Baptist Mission hnuaiah Pathian rawngbawlna lam leh Computer chhe siam leh thil dang dang thawkin a awm a, mahse aduh ang a sap hovin an hnam zinga rawngbawlna an kalpui theih loh a vangin chu a hna chu a bansan a. Chumi hnu chuan Computer dawr te siamin hun a hmang leh a, “Keini Mire hnam hi heti ringawt a kan awm chuan vai ho in kan hmun leh ramte min lak sak zel si a, bâkah kan hnam nunphung reng reng hi a chhe lutuk a, kan hnam hruaitu (Leader’s) te lah hian mahni ṭanghma bâk an ngaihtuah bawk si lova, Politics hmang pawhin kan hnam hi siamṭhat theih a ni lo, tharum hmang chauh lo chuan, ka thusawi lah hi kan hnam Leader te, atu a mah hian min bengkhawn duh si lova, kan ram tan ka thih hmasak phawt a ngai a ni,” ti in an hnam a vei zia min hrilh ṭhin a, kei chuan, “I thil tum chu a ṭha maithei, mahse a la hun rih lo….” Ti a ka hrilhfiah chiam pawh in an hman chhe lutuk tawh a veina chuan a tur chhuak ta nge nge a.

Kum 2010 chuan mi tlemte nen an ram leh hnam tan an rammu ta nge nge a, Unifrom leh ralthuamte pawh khawi khawi a ṭangin nge an neih ka hriatpui ve lo na in an changtlung chak hle mai a, apawlte pawh an tam ve zel bawk a. Achang changin phone hmangin min rawn be ṭhin a, min biak loh chuan kei chuan ka be ve thei lo.

Ka unaupa Menrüm leh a naute chêttlatna Thanchhi leh Alikodom lamah chuan heng; ruk ruk, pawisa khelh zu ruih, suamhmang rual chêttlat ṭhin leh Opiam ruih te chu an rikrapna a vangin a awmtawh meuh lova, Thanchi lam a kan Evangilist te pawhin an awmna lam a mite an fel tawh zia thu te min rawn hrilh fo ṭhin. Engtin tin emaw a an sum hmuh a ṭang hian mirethei fa School kalna nei lo leh Collage kalna neilo naupangte an ṭanpui ṭhin bawk a, anmahni a vanga School leh Collage kal theih phah naupang engemaw zat an awm phah a ni.

Menrüm chuan an hnam dana, nupui man sang lutuk leh nupui pasal neih a thil ṭul lova insenso vak vakte leh dan ṭha lo tak tak atahtawl a tihban zelte an tum a, ralthuam nen a ti an nih a vangin mipuite pawhin hlauhna a vangin an ban ve thei mai a nga, nakinah chuan an hnam tan a thil ṭha zawk a nih a vangin chutiang dan ṭha chu mipui te’n an hman chhunzawm zel theih a beisei a ni.

Mahse, Mire hnam a an Leader te hian an haw em em a, an huat em em theihna tur chhan ni a lang chu Menrüm hian an hnam ṭhanmawhbawk, Leader mahni hmasial tak tak te hi dahṭha tura a la Target an rin tlat vang a ni thei. Chuvangin a tih boral dan hi an zawng reng a.

Menrüm chu tih boral tumin an phiarru reng a, tichuan a hmangaih leh a vei em em, a chipui, Miriate ngei chu amah that turin a awmna hmunah chuan an va intir a, a ni chuan rinhlelhna nei miah lovin a lo kawm a, chutia an inkawm lai tak chuan chempui in an sat ta vak mai a, Menrüm chuan a thi dawn tih a inhriat chuan lei ṭhamin Pathian hnenah a ṭawngṭai a, a chipui a hmangaih em em Mire hnam tan a nunna hla nin, a chipuite kut ngeiah Mire hnam pasalṭha chu kum 2012 April ni 5 khan a fam ta a ni. A ni hi Mire hman tan a Martar awmchhun a la ni. Achipui Miria te hian engtikah tak ngaihsan leh zah nachang la hria ang maw. Ka unaupa Menrüm hian a nupui leh afanu pakhat leh a fapa pakhat, la naupang tak te a kalsan a ni.

  1. Menlung : A ni hi Burma a Miria a ni a, Krama sakhaw hmuchhuaktu Menleh-a’n Bangladesh chhuahsan a Burma a a kal khan Menlung hian a zirtirna hi pawm in Krama sakhua chu a zui ve a. Burma a Mire hnam Krama sakhaw zuitu zingah chuan Menleh chuan sakhaw hruaituah a ruat a, Menleh-a a awm tak loh hnu chuan a ni chu Menleh-a aiawhtu hialah an ngai a ni.

Burma a Miria te chu Krama sakhaw zuitu an ni deuh vek bawk a, Menleh-a’n, “Ka kal bo a ṭanga kum ngana ah ka rawn let leh ang,” atih a vangin a rawn let leh hun tur awm a an ngaihah chuan Menlung chuan, “Menleh arawn kal hunah khawvelah indopui a awm dawn a, chutih hunah chuan tar te chu in tlanche thei ve thei lovang, tun a in lo thih hian Menleh-a hnen in thleng nghal a ni mai,” ti in tar engemawzat a that a, chu vang chuan Mire hnam zingah chuan Menlung-a hming hre lo an awm meuh lo a ni. A chanchin hi Krama sakhaw chanchinah ka ziak lan tho a vangin kimchang tak chuan ziak rih lo mai ila.

  1. Menleh Saula : A ni hi Alikodawm lam a awm a ni a, Miria leh Chakma hel ho inbei lai khan Chakma hel hovin a ke an kah hliam sak a, chuta ṭang chuan a ke a bai a, chuta ṭang chuan “Menleh kebai-a” ti in an ko ta a ni.

  1. Patui Rekchowh : A ni hi ahun lai chuan pachak hmingthang a ni a, buh tawng ( tawng 1 chu tin hrut vel a ni ang) 12 hi zo takin a phur thei a, abanruh hi pakhat chauh ni in an sawi.
  2. Lawngngan : A ni hi a pa lian in a chak em em a, a vanglai chuan taket (Insukpaih siak) ah tumah hnehtu an awm lo ni in an sawi.
  3. Ngau : A ni hi a hunlai chuan suaksual leh fin vervekah a hming a thang hle bawk.
  4. Zaithiam a hmingthangte : Mire zinga zaithiam kan tihte hian hla ruahṭham loh an sa tlangpui a, an hla kalphung hi ṭahhlachham ang deuh a nih a vangin tawpintai a nei lo emaw tih mai turin an sak peih chen chen a ni mai a, anphuah chawp zel a. Zaithiam han tih hian hla phuah tiam a ni tlangpui. Zaithiam hi chu an awm nual a. Parau-a, Nguiin-a, Kangking-a leh Chamrung-i te hi a lar zualte an ni. Atlangpui in Mizo te angin tlangah an awm tlangpui a, lui kam a awm te pawh an awm ve bawk. Khaw khatah hian hmun rem lai deuhah hian ‘Daingkhaing’ an sa ṭhin a, Daingkhaing chu in a bang neilovin a kangin an sa a, chutah chuan chhunah pa hovin hnang hlai leh tah nante, chawlh hahdam, ti ti nante an hmang ṭhin.

Mire hnamah hian Miria ni si; nun phung inanglo leh ṭawng thleng pawh a dang tak tak hnam 8 lai an awm a, chungte chu : 1. Anawk. 2. Sungmow. 3. Dopreng. 4. Tamsa. 5. Rengmitchowh. 6. Rengma. 7. Dawmrawng. 8. Uise te an ni a. Heng hnam hrang hrang ho zingah hian Anawk ho hi an tam ber a, an ṭawng pawh a darh zau ber in mithiam pawh an tam deuh ber bawk. Chanchinṭha pawh an dawng hmasa in Kristian pawh an tam ber a, chuvangin Mire Zirtan bu, Bible Hla bu leh lehkhabu zawng zawngah Anawk ṭawng hi Mire National Language atan ka hmang ta a ni.

Anawk ho hi Mire tlangpuiah leh Sangha (Songgo ti a lam tur) lui hnar lamahte leh hmun hran hranah an awm. Sungmow ho hi Sangha lui hnar lamah an awm deuh ber. Dopreng, Tamsa, Rengmitchowh, Rengma, Dawmrawng leh Uise te hi Matamuhuri lui kam vel, Marancha hung leh Alikodom lamah an khawsa deuh ber a, Burma ah pawh an awm nual bawk. Heng ho hi an awmna hmun inang mahse, an khua erawh an mahni hnam hlang deuh zelin an awm a ni. Ran hi an mamawhna a tam em avangin an vulh tam thei hle a, Mizo te angin chuktuak huan neih an uar vak lo. In ban zawn, In sak ṭan lai leh in chung chih lai chuan an inṭanpui ṭhin, an inlawm a ni mah mah zawk, anmahni pawhin an insak hunah midang an la mamawh dawn a vang in.

70Bumang Raja (Circle Chiff)

Khaw khatah hian khaw hotu, roreltu Kharbari an nei zel a, chung kharbarite chuan an khaw chhung a thu buai hla buai a awm chuan a rel ṭhin. Khaw chhunga mi hnawksak chu hnawhchhuah theihna pawh a nei a ni. Kharbari chungah hian ram ṭhen khat a thuneitu Headman a awm a, khawchhunga mi te’n an kharbari hnenah chhiah an pe a, Kharbari chuan Headman hnenah a khua a mite chhiah pek chu a pe ve leh a, Headman chuan a chunga roreltu Bumang Raja (Circle Chiff) hnenah a pe ve leh chhawng a, chutiang bawk chuan rorelna-ah pawh Kharmari chuan akhaw chhunga thubuai a rem theih loh chu Headman hnenah a thlen anga, Headman in a la rem thei lo fo a nih chuan Bumangraja (Circle Chiff) hnenah a thlen leh ang. Hnam thil thubuaiah chuan Bumangraja (Circle Chiff) hi a sang ber a ni.

An Headman te hian kan sawi tawh ang khan hnam hmangaihna an nei lo em em vek ama ni tih mai turin thing leh maute leh lui a lungte thlengin vai ho hnenah an hralh nasa em em a, Kharbari ṭhenkhat te pawhin chutiang bawkin an khaw reserve a thingte pawh vai ho hnenah an hralh ṭhin a, khua a mi te’n tuman an sawisel ngam bawk si lo. An ram chu a chereuh chak khawp mai.

Nula leh Tlangval inrim dan:

Khawvelah hian hnam hrang hrang hian hnam dan kan nei hrang thlih thliah a, chutiang a kan hnam dân a dan ang zelin kan nula leh tlangvalte inrim dan pawh a dang thei hle bawk. Kan unau Miria te nula leh tlangval inrim dan pawh hi keini Mizo nula tlangval inrim dan nen chuan a danglam khawp mai.

71Mire tlangval nula rim lai

Miriate hian hnam inthliarna an tina hle mai a, chuvang chuan Miria ni lo hnam dang nupui, pasal a nei pawh an tlem rih khawp mai. Anmahni pawh innei thei lo hnam an tam hle a, aṭhen chu nupuiah chauh an neih theih, pasal a an neih theih loh hnam te an awm bawk. Achhan chu Entirnan: Fanai nula kha Sailo tlangval hnenah pasal nei tase, Sailo te chuan Fanaite chu an Pu te an lo nih tawh a, chu vang chuan an makpa te lam hmeichhia chuan an pute lam tlangval chu pasalah an nei thiang tawh lo a ni. Fanai leh Sailo hnam te chu an in pu tawn in an in makpa tawn thei lo a ni.

Hei bâkah hian chhechham a ṭhian insiam hnamte, unau a insiam hnamte, hi nupui pasalah an innei thiang lova, mahse ‘Chia mai lek sawi’ an tih, Bawngmei then hmanga chhechham a ṭhian/unau a insiamte chu chhechham a ṭhian an in siamna chu an sut leh thei a, an sut leh a nih chuan an innei leh thei a ni.

Chhechham a ṭhian leh unai a an insiam hian ṭhian emaw unau emaw chhechhamtu te chu an chipui zawng zawng nen a huam tel vek zel a ni. Chhechham a unau a an insiam hian chhechham a unau/ṭhian a insiamtute chu an thih hnu a kum rei tak a lo vei chuan athar a chhechham a ṭhian/unau a siam leh loh chuan a hma a an chhechhamna khan awmzia a nei tawh lo a ni an ti.

Kan sawi tawh ang khan Miria te hi khaw khatah hnam khat deuh zel an awm a, chung hnam khatte chu inlaichin hnai tak angin an in en vek a, khaw dangah pawh an nu leh pa, pi leh pu te’n chhecham a ṭhian/unau a an siam nupui pasal a an neih theih loh leh an hnampui/farnu te’n pasal an va neihna khua/hnam nupui a neih chu a thian leh bawk siloh a vangin favang awllen lai hian nupui zawng leh nu la rim satliah pawhin nupui a an neih theihte hnam/khua ah chuan nula an va rim ṭhin a.

Mire nula tlangval inrim hun hi favang awllen lai a ni a, zanlai aṭang a khawvar thleng a ni a, khaw danga nula rim ṭhin an nih a vangin an nula rimna tur khua chu a hla deuh a nih chuan an khua aṭangin tlangvalte chu mi 2-5 te pawh tlai lamah nula rim turin an chhuak ṭhin a, khaw hnai a nih chuan zanahte pawh an chhuak ṭhin bawk (Khaw ṭhenkhat hi chu inhnaih te te a ni). An nula rimna tur khua chu an thleng hma a nih chuan nula an va rimna tur khua, tute inah emaw an mu a, zanlaiah nula rim tur chuan an thawk chhuak chauh ṭhin.

72Mire nula in a thi thil sa a keng

Khaw khat a nula ṭhanthi nei chin tawh chu mahni inah an riak ngai lova, an khua a in lian deuhahte an riak khawm ṭhin a, zanriah eikhamah an la kaih tur kengin an riah khawmna inah chuan an kal nghal mai ṭhin a, an mut bu chu zo la kaih hraw angreng tak a tah, rit leh thap angreng tak, sei tak tak a an tah, azung chep chat lova pum hlun mai, pathum ṭhui zawm hi a ni a, pakhat an phah a, pakhat an in vurh a, an lukham atan chuan thing bung emaw, raw pum phel phawk emaw an kham ṭhin a, an puan pakhat zar chu ‘Tingtung’ an ti a, asir hmawr tawpah pakhat an ṭawn a, a lehlam sir hmawr tawpah an ṭawn leh a, an mutna zawn ṭheuh a, Thosilen ang a hmang turin an zar fur mai a, nula mu tlar ho mut pindan a ang zawkmah.

73Mire nula

Chu chu thosi lak a an inven na a an inkhuhna tur a ni a, mahse a chhah em a vangin khaw lum lai chuan puan pawh an sin peih lova, an awmna hi a lum em em ṭhin bawk si a vangin inhkhuh peih a lawi chu an awm lem lova, an puan zar chu ke hlankâi phak lek a hniam a nih a vangin ṭhenkhat mutchhe deuh chuan an ke an hlangkai fur ṭhin a, pawnfen tawi leh khawng angreng tak, zo la kaih aṭanga tah chhah leh thap tak, fenphel lehnghal nen a mu mai an nih a vangin nula riak khawm zan mulai chu mipa tan chuan a enchi loh thei ṭhin khawp mai.

Chuta nulate riak khawm chu favang awllên lai hian an tlangval te’n an rawn rim ṭhin a, tlangval nula rimte chuan nula riak khawm zinga an duh duh chu an kaithovin an rim ṭhin. Tlangvalin rim tura an kaihthawh nulate chu tho peih lo viau mahse an khaw tlangvalte hmakhawngai in an tho ngei ṭhin, a chhan chu an tho lo a nih chuan an khaw tlangvalte chu anmahni rawn rimtu te khua a nula an va rim ve hunin an nula kaihthawh tumte chu an thawh duh loh ve dawn a vangin a ni.

Achang chuan mikhual tlangvalte chu khawchhung tlangvalte chuan an khaw nulate chu an rimpui ve ṭhin a, chutia mikhual tlangval an khaw nulate an rimpui chuan nula riah khawmna-ah chuan khawchhung tlangvalte chu an lut hmasa a, meite an chhem eng a, chumi hnuin khawchhung tlangvalte chuan an haw san leh mai ṭhin.

74Mire nula

Hetia tlangvalin nula an rim hian nula leh tlangval ṭhen khatte chu an inkuahin an inpawm chial a, ṭhenkhatte chu tlangval kapkalakah nula chu ahnunglam tlangval chhawnin a ṭhu a, tlangval chuan a kuah a, tlangval pakhat hian Rina Plung (Rawchhem) hi a lo tum a, tlaivar ṭhakin hla a inchhawn reng chungin thim zingah an inrim tlaivar thak ṭhin a, khua a var hnu chuan tlangval chuan nula tui chawite zui in chumi hnu chuan thing phur emaw, hnah la in emaw an inzui chhuak leh ṭhin. Hmanlai ti ila; Mire ram a ka zuk awm tirh aṭang a hun rei fe chhung chu an nulate hian pawnfen tawite, fenphel tih loh chu thawmhnaw an inbel meuh lova, mipate lah hrenpereng an hreng tawp a, kawr ha abar pawh an la awm bawk si lova, chutia saruak deuhthaw a nula leh tlangvak inkuah chial a hla thu thlum tak taka zan thim zinga tlaivar thak a an han inlem chuan hmeichhiat mipatna lamah pawh chetsual an awlsam hle reng a, an inrimna hmun a hmeichhiat mipatna an hmang thei lo a nih pawhin atuk zinga an thuichawi, thing phurh leh hnah lakna hmunah ṭhenkhat chuan hmeichhiat mipatna an hmang ṭhin. Sawnpaite pawh an tam reng a, mahse ramhmul damdawi fa tihthlakna pawh rinâi daihin an lo hria in an lo hmang tam a ni.

Nula hmelṭha deuhte chu rimtu an neih reng a vangin an meng rim hle ṭhin a, Tlangvalte pawh nula an rim tlaivar ṭhin a vangin chhun a an hnathawhna-ah te chuan an mut a chhuak thei hle ṭhin a, an lovat, thing thlak muthlu a thing kung a ṭanga tla thla tawp an sawite pawh ka hre ṭhin.

An hunpui Sechhun leh thildangahte pawh hmeichhiat mipatna hi an ngai thupui viau lo ama ni chu tih mai tur hian hetih hun, favang awllen a an inrim hun lai hi chuan an hmang nasa bik a, mahse an inrim hun lai nilova tlangvalin nula a va rim chuan nula chuan a rimtu tlangval chu, “Nang chu Hawiraw (Anṭam) par hun lai i hre ve lo a ni maw,” an lo tih chuan tlangval chu zak takin a chhuah nal nal angai tawh ṭhin a ni. (An inrim hunah lo chuan an inrim ngai lo)

75Mire nula lam turte

Tlangvalin hetia nula an va rim hi an ngaihzawngte a ni kher lem lova; tin, Mire nula leh tlangvalte nunah hian keini Mizote anga inngaihzawn a keipui sa hauh taka inhauh hi a awm meuh lo. Nula chuan sawn a lo pai a nih chuan a fapai chu an nu leh pa te in a hrin chu an duh lo a, an nu leh pa te pawhin an fanu sawn pai chu a rai lai ngei a pasal neih tir a, a pasalte in a fa va hrin tir chu an duhdan a nih a vangin an fanu chu a rai lai a rawn nei duhtu an awm mai loh chuan an fanu ni (Nula pa farnu) fapa tlangval a awm chuan an chah ang a, an fanu midang rài chu nei turin an ti a nga, afarnu fapa chuan a pu fanu midangin an rai tawh chu nupuiah a lo nei mai ṭhin, a nei duh lo tlat a nih chuan Tk 70 leh zu um khat an chawi tir ang.(Miria leh Bawm chu an ni-te fapa nen an inneih thin vang a ni)

Mire mipate hi an ṭha hle a, anmahni rài nilo mahse, râi a awm tih an hriat chuan nupui atan an iâi lo mai nilovin rai thei tih chiangsa a nih a vang leh ṭhenkhat chu an man a lo tlem deuh bawk a vangin nula râi chu an ăwl rei hman ngai meuh lo, neitu an hmu vat ṭhin. Fa an lo nei a nih pawhin an pasalte chuan anmahni fa ang main an en a, an fa a ni lo tih a hriat ngai loh a ni. Hmeichhia te erawh chu fahrawn chungah sual tak tak sawi tur an tam mai.

Tlangvalin nupui neih hun tawh ni a a inhria a, nupui neih a duh tawh a nih chuan favang awllen lai chuan khaw hrang hrangah nupui zawngin a vak kual a nga, zan khatah khaw hnih khaw thum pawh a kal thei a ni. Hei hi kan khaw (Tuna ka awmna Empu) tlangvalin a nupui zawn dan min hrilh aṭang a ka hriat a ni.

76Mire nula leh tlangvalte

Ṭhenkhat chu nula nupui a an duh zawng an hmuh chuan nula chu thil (Ṭhi emaw) an pe a nga, nula pawhin a thil a pe ve bawk a nga, anei turin tha tharin, ni/zan dangah tlangval chuan aṭhian mi 5 – 8 hruai in an va kal leh anga nupui tur chu an ru haw nghal tawh mai ang. An inneih dan chu bung dangah kan la sawi leh ang.

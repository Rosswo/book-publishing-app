LALPA’N THIL ROPUI TAK MIN TIHSAK.

Lalpa’n ka tan a thil ropui tak min tih sak zozaite hi ziak vek dawn ila lehkhapuan leh pentui in min daih seng dawn lova, ziak tam hle in inhre mah ila ziah kim sen rual lah a ni dawn bawk hek lo. Ka nu pum chhung a ka awm lai a ṭanga tun thleng hian Lalpa’n thil ropui tak min tih sak mawlh mawlh reng hi a ni rawngawt mai.

Lalpa’n thil ropui tak min tih sak zawng zawngah hian lawmthu sawi zel dawn ni ila, thil dang sawina hun awm lek lovin ka damchhung zawng hi Lalpa hnen a lawmthu sawi nan ka hman zawh vek a ngai hial ngei ang. Chuvangin lawmthu pawh ka sawi vek seng lova, ka nun hi Lalpa-ah a lawm em em reng ringawt mai a ni. Hei hi alo nih dawn hi, hla siamtuin, “Aremruat a in nghat ringtute nun zawngah chuan vuina tur reng hi a lo awm love,” a lo tih ni. Vuina tur awm chu sawi loh ‘Lawmna tur hlir’ a lo ni zawk a ni. Lalpa’n ka tan a thil ropui tak min tih sak zozaite zing a tlemte han tarlan ka duhte chu hengte hi a ni:

  1. Lalpa’n a rawng ka bawl ve min phal sak a, he khawvelah ngei pawh hian min chawi mawi a ni :

Kei, khawvel thiamna leh zirna lamah chuan chhuan tur pakhatmah nei lo hi, tunlai khawvel thiamna leh finna hmachhuanna khawvelah hian BCM Kohhran dan lo ang in (‘Dan lo ang’ ka tih nachhan chu tunlaiah chuan Missonary tur chuan an thiamna in a tlinte chauh lak ṭhin a nih vang a ni a, kei chu ka Academy Certificate in Missionary tur chuan a tlin ve loh hrim hrim vang a ni) Lalpa zarah Baptist Church Of Mizoram (BCM) Missionary ka ni tlat si. BCM danah tunlai chhanah Pastor (B.D) zir chhuak tawhte tan pawh Pastor nih a har em em lai in kei chu Lalpa zarah BCM hotute hriatpuina thlapin Baptist Church of Maruchah (BCM) Assembly-ah Pastor Ordain ka ni in Rev (Reverence) nihna pek ka ni tlat bawk.

Ni 25 February 2018 khan rin loh leh beisei phak ngai reng reng loh in Carson Theological College & Seminary chuan Bachelor Of Ministry (B.min) Digree Award min hlan ve tehtlat mai a, Lalpa khawngaina a vang liau liau a i. Higth School pawh rap pha ve ngai lo kha ka Graduate ve ta reng mai a lo ni. Lalpa hi chu a mite tan hian a va han thilphal tehlul em.

Ni 18 August 2018 khan ka thawhna hnam ngei, Baptist Church Of Maruchah Ṭhai te chuan ropui takin min chawimawi in, ka phu loh thu ziak hlu leh ropui tak mai min hlan bawk a.

Thiamna nei lo leh mi mawl tak mai hi Lalpa’n a rawng ka bawl ve a phal a, a rawng ka bawlna tur a nih a vangin finna, thiamna leh hmanraw hrang hrang, ka mamawh ang zela min pete, ka rawngbawlna a mi leh mi chi hrang hrangte pawh hian min hmangaih a min ngaina em em maite hi ka lawm em em a, Lalpa’n he leiah hian van nun min tem tir ni ber in ka hria a, a ropui ka ti in ka hlim em em a ni.

Kei ang duang tan a thil theih loh leh beisei ngam rual loh chu lei leh vana thu neihna zawng zawng neitu, ka Lalpa leh ka Pathian chuan he khawvela chawimawina sang tak mai, Doctor of Ministry (Doctorate Digree) Award chu 25th September 2024 khan Dayspring International University U.S.A te kaltlangin min hlan a, ‘Ava mak em em ve’ tih mai loh chu sawi tur pawh ka hre lo. Ka lawm em em rualin ka hming bul a ‘Rev. Dr’ tih ahan chuang ve turte chu ka inhmeh lo riau in ka hria a, mahse Lalpa’n kan phu reng vang ni miah lovin chawimawi a duhte chu khawvel hmuhah ropui lo mah ila hetiang hian a chawimawi ṭhin a lo ni. Lalpa’n thil ropui tak min tih sak vang chauh a ni. “Lalpa, hetiang a chawimawina ropui leh sang hi ka phu lo asin,” tibak sawi ngaihna vak pawh ka hre lo. Ka tan chuan a mak em atin ni. Ka tan Lalpa hi a va ṭha em.

Lalpa ruahmanna mak : Kei mi tling lo tak mai Lalpa’n Mire ram a a rawngbawl tura min lo thlang hi ngun tak a ka ngaihtuah hian Pathian thil ruahman lawkna a lo ni tih ka hriat hian a mak ka ti bang thei lo. Mire ram rawngbawlna a kei leh ka unaute leh ka Pu (ka Nu nuṭa) min lo hman dan hi a mak ka tih em a vangin ka tar lang ve hrim hrim duh tlat a ni.

Kum 2005, 2006 khan kan unau zing a naupang ber Lalmuanpuii’n (Puitei) Lalpa tan a mi ṭhahnemngai te thilpek hmangin Mire hla Audio leh Music Vedio Casstte thum lai min siam sak a, Lalmuanpuii (Puitei) zai chu Mire ramah Lalpa rawngbawl nan a ṭangkai em em mai a, kan unau Miria te’n an ngaithla duh em em a, an Mobile ah ka thun sak a, Kristiante hunpuiah phei chuan khaw tin ah Mike (Aurinna) in an zai tir vut vut reng mai a ni. Puitei hla an ngaihthlak a ṭanga Kristian a rawn inpe tate pawh an awm hial a ni.

Kum 2012 khan Maruchah Christian Felloship (MCF) Conference-ah Speaker ni tura kan sawm angin Mizoram a Thutak puangtute Team Deputy Spreker J.Lawmsanga (Ka Pu Mama, ka nu te unau zing a mipa a naupang ber) te an rawn kal a, chumi ṭum a ka Pumama te rawngbawlna chuan tun thlengin Miria te’n an la hlawkpui em em a ni.

Chumi ṭum a kan MCF khawmpui a ṭang chuan Mire Kristian te’n Solfa zir duhna thinlung an neih phahin anmahni Solfa, hla leh Musice zirtirtu, ngenngawl takin min ngenna zarah ka nau Sangtea (J.Vanlalsanga) chu Mire ram a rawng bawl tura koh thlak a nih phah a ni.Hemi kum vek hian ka nau (Ka nu chhang chiah ka Pu Lalduha fapa) J.Vanlalsanga,(Sangtea) chu Mire ramah a rawn chhuk a, bung hmasa lam a kan sawi tawh ang khan a ni hi zai, Musice leh Solfa te a thiam mai bâkah kutthemthiamna talent hrang hrang a neih ṭhat em a vangin Mire ram rawngbawlna-ah kawng hrang hrangin kan ṭangkaipui takmeuh a ni.

2013 kum chuan Mizoram a Evangelist Thutak puangtute Team Speaker Evan Upa K.Dengdailova (Ka Nu u, ka Nu Kungi fapa) chu kan BCM Assembly-ah Speaker tura kan sawm angin a rawn kal thei a, Hemi ṭum hian Mire ram a thil thleng ngai lo; thlarau lam harhna a thleng a, a bik takin ṭhalaite zingah a thleng nasa bik. Kan ṭhalaite chu Pathian fakin ṭap zawih zawihin tlaivar ṭhakin an zaiin an lam a ni. Kum 2013 a ka unaupa Denga Speaker a kan hman in a rahchhuahte chu, hemi hma hi chuan Empu khua in kum khat thawhlawm sing ruk, sing sarih chauh pe thei ṭhin khan, Biakin sak nan a kan hman bâkah Budget ah cheng nuai khat chuang kan pek theih phah a, Kohhran in thilpek ah hma kan sawn phahin kan ṭhan len phah hle a ni tih a chiang em em a ni.

Chu chauh a la ni lo, ṭhalai te thlarau lam a harhna an chan a zarah Mission hrang hrang a mite pawn hlim tak leh venthawn nei miah lova Lalpa an fak ho ṭhin theih nan ṭhalai pual bik a pawl din ṭul a hriatna ka nei a, kan Assembly rorel zawh chuan All Christian Kürthümmi Powchak (ACKP) kan din sak a, chutah chuan Mission hrang hrang a Mire ṭhalai Kristian zawng zawng chu an awm a, kum tin khawmpui an nei ve ṭhin a, an pawl hming pawh hnamdang hriat awlsam zawk nan Mruchow Christian Yuoth Association (MCYA) ti in an thlak leh ta a ni. MCYA chanchin chu bung hmasa lamah kan sawi tawh bawk a, sawi tam tawh lo mai ang.

Heng a tangte hian Lalpa’n Mire ram a kei min lo hruai hi Miria te tan kei leh ka Nu lam a ka chhungte hmangin thil tum leh ruahman fel tak a lo nei ngei a ni tih a lang chiang hle in ka hria. Lalpa’n keini angte a ram rawngbawlna a hmanrua a min han hmang ve duh hi kan tan chuan hei ai a chawimawina hlu leh ropui hi a awm leh thei pawh in ka ring tawh lo. Lalpa, ka lawm takmeuh a ni, kei leh ka chhungte i rawngbawlna hnamrua a min hmang ve duh hi.

  1. Se kawlh ka hau zal :

Ṭum khat chu kan khua a ṭanga chhuah lam Numlai Headman khua-ah Pathian thu hrilin ka kal a, Sêpre lui kam a Singchowng khua ka zu thlen chuan, Singchowng khua a mite chuan, “Lui lamah chuan kal suh, Se pa kawlh a awm a, tumah lui lamah kan kal ngam lo, se kawlh chuan mi a um ṭhin a, hnathawk leh chawhmeh zawng pawhin tumah kan kal ngam tawh lo,” an lo ti a.

Numlai Headman khua a kal tur chuan Sêpre lui dung chu zawh thlak ngei a ngai si a, Kei chuan, “Kei chu ka hlau ve lo, Se kawlh chuan min hlau zawk ang, Chungnung ber a tirhkoh ka ni a lawm,” ka ti a. Ka kal thla ta a, ka kal lam chuan chu se kawlh chu ka hmu lova, tluang takin ka kal a, ka rawn haw lam chuan lui kawr chep lai tak maiah chuan lui kam tuiin a chim loh chinah chuan Se pa lian fu mai, aki kual hmawr lam eng vuh tawh mai hi ka lam hawi zawng hian a lo bawk run mai a.

Ka rilru chuan, “He sial hi a ni ngei ang ka kal lam a Singchowng khua a mi te’n ‘Se pa kawlh’ an tih kha,” ka ti rilru a, ka tiang chuan ka han vai a, ka han hnawt a, a lu alei luk luk a, a tho duh reng reng lo mai a, hêlna awm hek lo, a lam pan pah chuan ka chho zel a, ka tiang a vai pah chuan ka hnawt a, ka hnaih hle tawh chuan a thova, min rawn phin tum niawm tak hian a lu a kun hniam a, a hnar a phit a, a lu ti lei buk buk pah chuan a ke hmalam chuan lei aphiat sawuh sawuh mai a, chutia lei leh van lalber tirhkoh zah lo tak a chu Se chal in a min han cho vel chu ka thin a rim ta em em mai a.

“Ehehe….I va han zak lo em em ve, nang ran mai mai in kei Pathian anpui a siam mihring, Chungnung ber a tirhkoh leh nghal hi min cho ngam ve a ni maw, rawn kal teh. I hmui noah tak khan ka lo fik sak hmawk teh ang che,” ka ti a, ka tiang chu ka paih a, ka chemte pai sei zer zawr chu ka han phawi a. “Nangin a zah lo taka khatia mi tih tur ka ni hleinem, khanglang che hian,” ti a hau bawrh bawrh pah chuan ka han pan hnai a, a bul hnai ka va thlen chuan min en run run a, zawi muang hian a inher a, a kal chho ta hnu hnu a, kei chuan a hnung a zui chho pah chuan zilh ti tih hian ka hau chho pah bawrh bawrh a, a ngaithla peih tawh lo a ni ang, luite-ah min pen san ta a ni.

Ramhuai an tlanchhia : Kan khua a ṭanga chhim lam a khaw pakhat Uâklu/Chini khua an tihah hian Mire in sawm leh panga vel hi an awm a, chu khuaah chuan Kristian in li vel hi an awm ve a, a khât tawk hian ka va tlawh ve reng ṭhin a, ṭum khat chu keimahin va tlawh leh ka tum a, mahse ka Pa Mensowm-a hian, “Uâklu khua-ah kan nu nuṭate an awm a, tlawh ka duhna a rei tawh a, hun ka neih loh avangin ka tlawh thei lova, i rualin ka lo kal ve ange,” a ti a, kan pahnih chuan kan kal ta a, Uâklu khua thlen hma lawk a Prangmawm khua-ah chuan reilote kan chawl a, kan kal leh ta a, Prangmawm khua aṭang chuan Uâklu khua chu a hla tawh lova.

Uâklu kan zu thlen chuan kan Kristian member pakhat; an khua a ka kal apiang a ka thlenna ṭhin, Pu Patui te inah chuan kan va thleng a, mi an lo tam hle mai a, Pu Patui chu an in kil lamah chuan alo ṭhu kun chal mai a, a ban chu puan var hian an lo tuam bur mai a, an tuamnaah chuan thi a lo la kai nuai mai a, “Engatinge?” ti a kan han zawh chuan, a bul vel a mite chuan, “Akawchhungah ramhuai a lut ṭhin a, akawchhung a ramhuai a luh a piangin thisen hi a duh ṭhin a, vawk thisente, ar thisente a dawt ṭhin a, tunṭum chu kan chelh bet tlat a, mahse a talchhuak thut a, chemte a la a. Vawk leh ar a man mai theih loh avangin a ban a inzai a, ama thisen a dâwt ta a, Prangmawm khua in lo thlen khan, “Khi, Lur a rawn kal, tunah Prangmawm khua a lo thleng tawh, rei lovah a rawn thleng mai ang, tlan chhuah vat a ngai,” an ti a, chuta ṭang chuan ramhuai hovin an chhuahsan a, in rawn thlen hun kan lo nghak a nih hi, tunah chuan a harh fim leh tawh a ni,” an lo ti a.

Ka bag ka nghat a, ka ṭawngṭai nghal a, chuta ṭang chuan ramhuai chuan a rawn ti buai tawh ngai lo. Pu Patuia hi Kristian-ah a awmna chu a rei ve tawh a, mahse mi awmherh mi a ni ve a, thil mawi lo tak tak, Kristian tih a mawi lo tak tak a tih ṭhin a vang hian a buaithlak ve hle ṭhin a, mahse tunah chuan a fel ve tawh khawp mai.

Uaklu khu a ka kal apiang hian Krama leh Budish hote hi ka Pathian thu sawi ngaithla tur hian an rawn kal khawm thin a, ka kezungpui khawihin pathian chibai buk tak hian bungbu kawt kawtin chibai min buk ṭhin a, “Hetiang hian chibai mi buk suh u,” ka tih pawhin an sim duh chuang lova, Pathian thu erawh an awi duh chuang lem hlei lova, Kristian tlemte awm ho lah ṭawngṭai dante, hla te ka zirtir a, “Han tawngtai ve teh u,/han zai ve teh u,” ka tih hian, “Keini chu kan ṭawngṭai/kan zai thiam ve lova,” an ti zel a, an khua ka kal zin leh ka zirtir tam tawh tehlul nen, ka thil zirtirte chu thiam tumna thinlung reng an neih loh avangin ka thin a rim a, “Hei hi hre rawh u, vawi tam tak ka rawn kal tawh a, min lo lawm em em na in Pathian in ring duh chuang si lova, Kristian ho pawh hmasawn tumna in nei bawk silova, in khua-ah hian vawikhatmah ka rawn kal leh ngai tawh lovang,” ka ti a, an khua chu ka chhuansan ta a. Chumi ta tanga rei lovah chuan an khua chu an intibuai a, an pem darh ta vek mai a, tunah chuan an khua chu a ram ta der mai a ni.

Lalpa’n chhuancham a ka awm aphal lo : Mire ram ka luh hma, ramri vel a kan la tangkhàn lai, kum 1990 khan ka kalpuite’n min kir san vek a, min tirtu kan Mission Board te ngei pawh in min ṭilh san vek bawk a, Lalpa hruaina in engemaw ti ti in Mire ram chu ka thleng a, Mire ram ka thlen hnuah CFB Mission in thla khatah Tk 1200 in min ṭanpui ve a, chu pawh chu min pek loh thla a awm nual ṭhin a, min han pek ve pawh hian min pek loh thla kha chu theihnghilh a ni zel bawk si a, chutih lai chuan bufai bag khatah (Kuintal khat) man chu Tk 800 a la ni a.

Rawngbawl a ka zin reng a ngai a, in a ka awm lah in Mire hlabu, Bible leh lehkhabu dang dang Mire ṭawng lehlin hna leh ziak hna ka thawh reng a ngai bawk si a, sawhthing hmun leh banhla hmunte siam in lo zimte pawh ka siam bawk a, mahse duhangin chutiang hna chu ka thawk ṭha hman bawk si lova. Ka in tur pawh keima sak a ngai bawk a.

Miriate lah chuan ahming a Mizoram a ṭanga an hnam zinga Missionary han ni ve tafam chu an dam loh in damdawi min rawn dil ve ṭhin a, chung hun a tan chuan damdawi ka inchhawp a ngai ve reng ṭhin a, “Tukin chhuan tur kan nei lo, za nin chhuan tur kan nei lo,” ti a chhuanchham rawn tlan lut an awm ve zauh zauh ṭhin bawk a, chungte chu kut ruak a han chhuah tir leh chi an ni bik si lova, keini pawh chhuanchham lek lek a kan awm chang a awm ṭhin a.

Chawhmeh leina chu sawi loh fate lawm U Buki leh U Thlangdir-te nupa zawng chhang leh chithlum han leina tur takngial pawh kan nei ṭhin lova. Ṭum khat chu kan fairel bel a ruak ta der mai a, buhfai han lei mai natur pawisa lah chengkhat pawh kan nei si lo. Ka nupui hnenah chuan, “Ka pa (Pastor Chownglowk) te buhfai 3 kg lo pûk la, buhfai lei turin Bandarban-ah ka zu kal ang e,” ka ti a, Bandarban-ah chuan ka kal a, mail 24, (37 km) chu ke in mail 12 ka kal a, mail 12 a ṭang chuan Trick remchangah chuangin Bandarban ka thlen chuan buhfai zawrhna vai (Bangoli) buhfai dawrah chuan buhfai leina cheng khat pawh nei lo chuan Lalpa min pek hun nghak chuan ka ṭhu ràn mai a.

Rei pawh ka ṭhu hman lo, Ruma a kan U Lalbuki pasal U Thlangdira

a lo kal a, “E…Lurte, heta ka hmu hlauh che chu a va lawmawm em, tunṭum kan sawm a pakhat chu i u Buki nen nangmah pek che a lang tlat mai a, nangmah pe tur che in ka lo kal a nih hi, hei cheng zariat hi la rawh,” min ti a, lawm takin ka la a, lawm thute ka sawi luam hnu chuan buhfai ka lei a, ka Puzawnte khua Faruk a hla lo bawk a, Faruk-ah ka riak a, a tukah chuan buhfai tukkhum tawngin ka phur a, hlim takin ka haw leh ta a ni. Ka buhfai leina tur an sawm a pakhat min rawn petu U Thlangdira te chu Bandarban aṭang a chhim chhaklam mel 24 vetho Ruma Bazar a awm an ni.

Ṭum khat pawh chhuan kan chham leh dawn tlat mai a, pawisa chengkhat pawh nei lo bawk chuan buhfai lei turin Bandarban-ah ka kal leh a, Bandarban bazar thlen hma, Hafesguna vengah hian ka Pi Lalzovi te hi an awm a, ka hah deuh bawk a, ka Pi Lalzovi te inah chuan tui te in a chawlh hahdam tumin ka va kal a, ka Pi Lalzovi chuan min lo hmuh chuan, “E Lura, lo kal teh, enteh hei niminah Kuwait a i u Babyi’n (a fanu) pawisa a rawn thawn che a,” ti in cheng sangkhat min lo pe a, lawm takin ka la a, Pathian hnenah leh ka Pi Lalzovi hnenah chuan lawm thu ka sawi a, chumi pawisa chuan buhfai ka lei a, hlawhtling takin ka haw leh ta a ni.

Ka Pi Lalzovi hi Pu Taia nupui a ni a, Pu Taia chu tunhma Bangladesh ‘E’ Pakistan a nih lai a Police Officer a ni a, Bangladesh a lo pian hnu a Bangladesh Police Officer lo nita a ni a, a pasal Pu Taia chu a thi tawh a, Bandarban a an inah chuan a nupui leh a fate an la awm a, U Baby-i pawh hi an fanu Kuwait a Nurs hna thawk a awm a ni. Ka Pi Lalzovi hian min duat in min hmangaih em em a vangin a fate pawh hian anmahni unau ang maiin min en ve a ni. Afate chu Bangladesh ah pawh milian Officer an ni deuh vek hlawm a ni.

Kan van Pa hi chuan min va thlahthlam ngai lo tak em, amah ring ngam chiah lotu te pawh an mamawh a pe ṭhin a ni a, amah a innghat leh amah ringngamtu te tan lekphei chuan zamna tur reng hi a lo awm lo a ni. (Sâm 121:1,2)

Ka chhuk ka chhohna-ah Lalpa’n min veng : Ka fanu Mazuali leh a nau chiah Avala chu an naupan tet aṭangin Mizoram a sikul kal an nih a vangin an chhuti rei deuh hi chuan kan awmna Bangladesh Empu khua a an rawn haw zel a ngai a. Thlasik lai leh khaw lum em em laite hian ka fate hruai leh thlahin ka chhuk ka chhoh reng a ngai a, kawng a thui tham em a vangin kan hah thei ṭhin khawp mai. Mahse, Lalpa hruaina leh awmpuina in tunah chuan an nula tlangval ve ta.

Kum 2000 June thla a ṭanga kum 2013 thleng khan Mire Field a Staff hlawh zawng zawng leh kan Budget hi thla hnih thla thum danah keimahin Mizoram BCM Office-ah ka la ṭhin a, achang chuan vawikhatah nuai hnih chuangte ka lâk chang a awm a, ka ramri kân dan lah chu dan lo ang ngawtin ka kal a ngai bawk ṭhin si a. Pawisa chu ramriah Chakma ho hnenah Bangla pawisa in ka thleng hmasak vek a ngai bawk a.

Mission pawisa tam tak mai pai chuan Chakma inah te leh vai inahte ka riah angai fo mai bawk a, Chutih lai chuan tunlai angin Internet kan la hmang lovin E-mail pawh kan la hman loh a vangin Headquarter lehkha pawh hi a ken in ka keng ṭhin a. Chutiang a Bangladesh leh Mizoram ka kârtawn a ka chhuk ka chhohnaah chuan engemaw chang chuan ramri-ah chuan min check nasa thei hle ṭhin a,

Ramriah Mission lehkha pawimawh emaw, Mission pawisa tam tham tak mai min han man sak maise chuan emaw, Misualin min khawng thlu/chemte emaw silai in emaw min vau in ka pawisa pai min chhuh sak vek mai sela chuan (Chakma ho hnen a pawisa thleng ṭhin ka nih a vangin pawisa tam tak ka pai ṭhin tih Chakma ho thenkhat chuan an hre deuh fur mai si a) ka tan a pawi em em dawn a, ka dam khaw chhuak a nih pawh in Field lam leh Headquarter lamin ka thusawi chu; min rawn hmuh kher dawn loh avangin an awi kher lo thei bawk a, min ring ngam tawh dawn lova, ‘mi rinawm lo’ ka lo ni tawh mai dawn a ni. Chu mai nilovin kum tam tak ka tâng thei bawk a; a nihloh leh ka thih phah thei bawk.

Chutiang chu lo thleng palh tase chuan kan Field tan pawh thil pawi tak a thleng ngei ngei dawn a ni. Engemaw chang a ka bag zawng zawng leh ka file leh lehkhabu karte leh taksa te nen lam a min check changte chuan, “Lalpa, min checktu te mit hi ka thil pawimawh hmu lo turin ti del ang che,” ti a rilru a ka ṭawngṭai chang a awm ṭhin, Lalpa’n ka ṭawngṭaina achhang a, an en reng leh an khawih reng chung pawhin ka thil chu hmu lo turin Lalpa’n an mit a tih del sak in, an hriatna atih chawmawlh sak a, vawikhat mah min man ngai lo a ni. Chutiang chuan kum 13 lai kan Field a Staff hlawh leh Budget la a ka chhuk ka chhohna ah Lalpa’n misual, min suamtu tur lakah leh min checktu sipai lakah min veng tlat reng a ni.

Ka Lalpa leh ka Pathian hian eng tik lai mahin min thlahthlam ngai lova, kawng engkimah min hliahkhuhin min veng reng ṭhin zawk a ni, engemaw chang chuan han zam ve deuh chang pawh ka nei ṭhin a, mahse Lalpa’n ka chak lohna te hriain min thla muan a, akut chak thiltihei chu min hmuh tir leh ṭhin. Lalpa, nang chu ka phaw leh ka inhumhimna i ni kumkhua ang.

8. Thihna kotlang a ṭangin Lalpa’n min chhan chhuak : Kum 2014 kha nita in kahria, Mizoram a ka fapa School chhuti rawn haw tur hruai turin Mizoram-ah ka kal a, kan chhoh lamah Horina kan thlen chuan Horina a ṭanga Rangamati lam Vai (Bengoli) khua Busawnchhawra, Horina a ṭanga Rangamati lam hla vak lo ami, vai sangha mantu, mi panga lai mai Chakma hel hovin an that a, chumi chanchin chu kan hre hman a, mahse engmah buaina ala chhuak rih lova, tluang takin Mizoram chu kan thleng a.

Lunglei kan thlen tawh hnu chuan Busawnchhawra a vai ho thinrim chuan an khua a sangha mantu mi panga Chakma hovin an thah phuba lâk nan tlangmi tawhphawt chu an thah theih theih thah an lo tum a, Horina leh a chhehvel a Chakma leh Mawk ho te chu ramhnuai lamah an lo tlanchhe vek mai a. Keini chu hun engemaw chen Lunglei a kan cham hnuin Bangladesh Laizo khua a kan u, U Laltawna kan zu phone a, a ni chuan, “Lo kal ula keiman ka kalpui ang che u,” a ti a.

Keini chu kan ṭhian, Bawm leh Pang ṭhenkhat te nen chuan kan chhuk a, Laizo khuaah chuan kan riak a, Laizo khua aṭang chuan kan u Laltawna chuan min hruai a, Horina Bazar-ah kan riak a, zingah Lons in Rangamati lam pan chuan kan chhuak ta a, Horina thlang lawk, Busawnchhawra khua km khat vel a kan pelh tawh hnu chuan kan chùan-na Lons chu vaukam lam a ding turin a chhunga mi ṭhenkhat chuan an ti a.

Kan han din chuan vaukamah chuan vai thinrim meng sen rum mai talhtumte, chemte leirawhchante kengin mi tam tak an lo awm ṭeuh mai a, Lons chhung a Busawnchhawra khua lo chuangvete chuan keini tlangmi zawng chu chhuak turin min nawr chhen mai a. Lawng a thawkte leh mi ṭhenkhatte chuan, “Chhuak miah suh u, in chhuah chuan in thi vek ang, hmanni a Chakma hovin an khua a sangha mantu mi panga an thahte phuba lâk nan tlangmi an thah theih apiang thah an tum a ni,” an ti a.Lons chhunga Busawnchhawra khua lo awmte chuan lohtheihloh in min nawr chhuak ta a. Lui kam a mipui thinrim inhrosa kan han hmuh chuan ka ṭhiante chu an hlau em em mai a, ka fapa pawh chuan a hlau ve mai ang tih ka hlauh avangin, ka fapa hnenah chuan, “Aval, hlau reng reng suh, keini chu Lalpa rawngbawltu kan ni a, arawng kan bawlna Field-ah a nih loh chuan mi kut tuarin kan thi lovang, Lalpa ram tan a ṭangkai tur lo chuan Lalpa’n kan thih aphal lo a nia, heng ho inhrosa hi chu Pathian tan chuan saipui hma a fanghmir inhrosa ang lek an ni, an hlauhawm lo lutuk tlat, bâkah thi hman pawh kan la ni hlei nem,” ti in ka thlamuan mawlh lawlh a. Chu chu ka rilru tak pawh a ni.

Kan chhukna chu thing zaina awmna hmun a ni a, kan chhuak chiah chu chuta thing zai tum Bangladesh Army pahnih hi an lo awm a, chumite chuan min vengin Lons a chùan tir leh min tum a, mahse vai mipui thinrim chuan Lons chu a kal lo a nih chuan tihchhiatah an vau a vangin Lons pawh chu duhlo chung chung chuan a her liam ve mai a ngai a. Mahse, Army pahnih chuan an hnung a dahin an kut an phar ve ve a, pakhat zawk chuan Warless a keng a, Vai ho thinrim chuan min khawih luih ṭal an tum a vangin Army pakhat zawk chuan an Camp a Army dangte chu a kova, rei lovah Army sawm leh pasarih silai ṭha pui pui keng chu an lo thleng a, chungte chuan min din hual ta lat a.

Busawnchhawra khaw lam pan chuan min chhohpui a, an khua a Board gad-ah chuan min din khawm tir a, chhak deuh feah chuan silai nen chuan min veng a, Board remchangah Horina lamah chuan min chhohpui leh ta a, Horina ah chuan Vai Hotel-ah min riah tir a, zankhua in min veng bawk a, atukah ke a kalna kawngah kan kal a, Chakma khua a kan riah hnu in him takin Rangamati chu kan thleng leh ta a ni.

Busawnchhawra khua ho chuan an khua a min thah chu an duh loh a vangin an khaw thlang lam, an khua aṭang a 1 km vel a hlaah Lons chu an lo din tir hi a lo ni a, taltum, chem leh hmanraw chi hrang hrang nen lui kam a awmte chu an lo inhrosa nasa em em tawh a, mahse an rin loh takin chutah chuan Lalpa’n min chhanhim turin Bangladesh Army mi pahnih a lo dah diam in ralthuam ṭha tak tak keng mi sawm leh pasarih lai a rawn tirh belh leh bawk a vangin Busawnchhawra khua amite, tlangmite thisen chak a tui hal huam huamte lak aṭang chuan Lalpa’n min chhanhim ta a ni. Ka nunna hi tu kutah mah awm lovin ka Lalpa leh ka Pathian, ka Pa van a mi kutah chauh a awm a vangin hlauhna lo thleng thut mahse ka chiai ve lo a ni. Lalpa ka Pathian chu fakin awm kumkhua rawh se.

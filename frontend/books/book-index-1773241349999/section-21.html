BUNG 22

AN SILHFEN LEH AN INCHEINATE

Miriate hi hnam chapo ve tak, hnam dang tih dan a ṭha zawk a ni tih pawh hria se la duh mang miah lo hnam an ni a, chuvang chuan an chhehvel a hnam dangte ngaihtuah chuan an la hniam em em a ni. Mahse Mire ram a Kristian na a lo chak deuh tak hnu hi chuan kawng tam takin hma an sawn tawh a, mahse heta kan sawi tur hi chu Mire ram a ka awmtirh lai leh Mire hnam zinga Kristian na in hmun a la chan loh lai a an silhfen leh an incheinate a ni ka rawn tarlang dawn a ni.

An mutbu leh an thuamhnaw hi anmahni la thàr a ṭang a an kaih a an tah a ni tlangpui a, hmeichhiate chuan hmanlai Mizo hmeichhiate ang bawkin la deh hi an peih em em mai a, hmeichhia ni si la deh thiam lo chu mi ngaihsan an hlawh lo a ni. Nula te pawhin an mut hma chu la an kai ṭhin a, lo a an fehnaah pawh la kaih tur an keng a, an chhun chawlh laite pawhin ṭhenkhat chuan la an kai ṭhin. Ṭhalaite an han inchei tak tak chu thawmhnaw an mamawh vak lo na in thil dang mamawh leh inbel neuh neuh an ngah ve hle thung.

An incheinate chu hengte hi a ni :

  1. Lu-ah :
    1. Samzial : Hmeichhi

leh mipa te’n an sam an zial a, hmeichhia chuan an tukkhumah an sam an zial a, mipa chuan an chhipah emaw, an bek ding lam deuhah emaw an sam an zial ṭhin.

    1. Kâp : Hriaupui hi a mawng lamah tlêp in an ṭawn a

la mawi in an chei leh a, chu chu nula te’n an samzialah an vit lut ṭhin.

    1. Nabek surut : Mau, rua, Alluminium in emaw, samkhuih lianpui kual vùn mai hian an siam a, nula leh tlangval te’n an samzialah inchei nan an hmang ṭhin.
    2. Surut :

Samkhuih hi an ngina em em a, ahnung lam tlang hi an chei a, chu chu khawi hmunah pawh kal se an samzialah a hmei a pa in an tawn reng ṭhin.

    1. Hnah leh pangpar : Nula te chuan hnah leh pangpar hi an samzialah hian an tawn deuh reng ṭhin a, an hunpui lai chuan lehkhamawi leh pawisa te, darthlalang te pawh an samzialah an tawn bawk ṭhin.
    1. Taibawia : Ṭhi chikhat a ni a, arawng sen emaw a hring emaw tam tak an thil ṭeuh a, an lu-ah an khim ṭhin.
    2. Kapsaw : Hei pawh hi an ṭhi ang chi a an siam a ni a, a mum hring fim te tak te te , khap khat zen zawn a sei in an thil ṭeuh a, a bul lamah an ṭawn khawm a, an sam zial a vih luh turin dawhkilh ang in an siam a, chu chu an samzial a vit lut in hnung lamah a uai hnuang ṭhin.
    1. Kowlüchowh : Silver tangka khat hi a laiah an dah a, asirah a hual chhuakin pawisa 25/50 thir an char (welding) vek a, chu chu nula leh tlangval te’n an samzialah an tawn ṭhin.
    2. Tangka dân : Silver tangka khat hi a laiah an samzial a vih luhna tur a kuang an welding a, chu chu nula leh tlangval te’n an samzialah an tawn ṭhin.
    3. Vakul chang : Nula ṭhenkhat chuan an hunpui a an inchei chuan an samzialah vakul chang an tawn ṭhin.
    4. Maipau : Bawng/sial an chhun a an lam lai te hian tlangval te’n halzit an hal a, a ṭek mawi lai takin nula lam lai samzialah an va tawn ṭhin.
    5. Si lumem : Pawisa thir hi a thilna an siam a, an lu vet chhuak thei turin an thil ṭeuh a, chu chu nulate’n an luah khim in inchei nan an hmang ṭhin.
    1. Keng ring lumem : Thi chikhat rawng sen tam tak an thil ṭeuh a, nula te’n an lu-ah khim in inchei nan an hmang ṭhin.
    2. Derkeng : Ṭhi amum eng an thil chu Keng ring lumem sir tuak veve ah an khim ṭhin.
    3. Napawng (Diar) : Hna an thawhna leh khual an zinnaah chuan an khim ngei ngei ṭhin. Diar var hi an inchawimawi nan leh an induhsakna tih lan nan an hmang bawk ṭhin, tawng nga ai a tawi lo a ni tlangpui.
    4. Chemte : Mipate chuan Mizo angin chemte hi an ngaina hle a, khual an zin chuan an samzialah emaw, an diarah emaw a kawm an siam a, an vit lut ṭhin.
  1. Bengah :
    1. Ramseng : Bengbeh Silver a siam, Mizo paikawng ang deuh a arh, a mawng lam pawh beng a atàn theih nan a arh hlek, hmeichhia te’n an beh ṭhin. Ṭhenkhat chuan an bengbeh chhungah chuan an vaihlo ṭhial tur emaw, pawisa emaw an dah ṭhin.
    1. Tai ramseng : Silver emaw, dar emaw a siam kual pan thler mai hi mipa in an beh ṭhin.
    1. Napung ramseng : Hei pawh hi a bial, pan thler, rangkachak a siam mipa beh chi a ni. Mihausa deuh chauh in an beh ṭhin.
    2. Powtu : La hmul sen chi, an ti dip a, abawk rual takin an siam a, alaiah a hring leh a eng sen an dah a, abehna tur a kŭang an siam a, hmeichhia leh mipa in an beh ṭhin.
    3. Ramseng saw : Mipa te’n bial pante an beh a an khai chhawn leh ṭhin a ni.
    4. Pin : Putar bengbeh nei ve lo te’n pin hi an beh ve mai ṭhin.
  1. Hmaiah : Nula leh tlangval an han inchei tak tak chu an hmaiah Powder paw tawk tak mai in an inhnawih a. Tunhma chuan an ha hi an ti dum vek a, an tihdumna hmanrua chu mau no ro an phel phawk a, chu chu an hal a, a alh lai chuan chem chhia phekah an khup thawk a, chumi khu ur chu inṭing dum khâk tlat ang mai hian a awm a, chumi chuan an ha chu an ti dum ṭhin a, an damchhungin a lo dum tawh mai ṭhin a ni.

An ha an tih dumna chhan chu ha var ai chuan ha dum hi mawi an tih vang zawk a ni. Ha var an hmuh chuan, “Ui ha ang mai a var ṭhuh,” an ti ṭhin a ni. Ha tih dum an bansan hnu hian ha leh hmui mai duh tawk lovin lei leh ka chhung thleng in an ti sen ve thung a, chutia an tihsen na hmanruai chu puan tih senna rawng hi a ni. Chumai chu la duhtawk lovin an khabe, hmui hnuai lamahte an ti sen leh ṭhin, ṭhenkhat chuan an biangahte leh an chalahte pawh an ti sen bawk. An hmui hnuai chiah-ah + tiang hian a sen in an nulate chuan an thai ṭhin.

  1. Nghawngah :
    1. Kengrawt (Ṭhi sen).
    2. Keng buk (Ṭhi phiar mum).
    3. Langke chah (Ṭhi phiar phek).
    4. Mangku keng (Sarzuk mu thi).Hei hi nau chhartu te’n an nau char hlawh a an lak ṭhin a ni. An ṭhiah chuan an ngaihsan ber pawl a ni a, mahse a awrh nuamlo ber a ni ang. Nula te chuan an hunawl ah chuan ṭhi hi an thil deuh reng ṭhin.
  2. Bân-ah :
    1. Kuarma kuaise (Vainu banbun/ngun) :
    2. Tai kuaise : Silver a siam, lian deuh, akualah a thlur ang deuh a awm.
    3. Bawtraw kuaise : Apawn lam a mum a, a mum laiah a rek deuhin a awm leh.
    4. Kuaise : Banbun pangngai, Alluminium a an chhun chawp a ni.
    5. Harkawi : Hmeichhia in an kiu chung chiah a an bun a ni a, Silver hmang a siam a ni a, an bun tirh chuan an ban puam lai tawt angreng takin thir inchi khat vel a hlaiin a hren tlat a vangin chaw bâr tur a kut han tawm pawh an harsat phah ṭhin a ni.
    6. Langpat : An ṭhi ang chi, aphek a phiar tlangval in an bampuam laiah an bun ṭhin.
    7. Rutbüt : Zungbun Silver tangka 25 a siam. An ṭhi ang chi pawh zungbun a tan mawi tak in an phiar a, an bun bawk ṭhin.
  3. Taksa-ah :
    1. Kowrma : Ṭhenkhat chuan an hnathawh lai in anmahni la kaih a ṭanga tah an ha ṭhin a, a hmei a pa in kawr hak ai chuan hak loh hi nuam ti zawk an tam hle.
    2. Tawnzau : Tawnzau, ahring emaw, a sen emaw, apawl emaw, lai pawh khuh ṭha mang lovin tawt ṭingṭeng in an ha ṭhin.
    3. Uancha-khup : Hmeichhia te’n a chang chuan puanhlap, asen emaw, a hring emaw, a pawl emaw an kokiah suih in an archang pai mai ṭhin.
    4. Tangka leng : Silver tangka khat leh darkhuang lem te tak te, ke a an bun ri chheng chheng theih chi ‘Te’ngeng’ sei deuh angte pawh chu a thilna kua an siam a, chu chu fual angreng takin an siam a, nula te’n incheina hlu leh ngaihsan awm takah an ngai ṭhin. Atlangpuiin mihausa fanu te deuh chauh in an pai thei ṭhin a ni.
  4. Kawngh/mawng bawrah :
    1. Tapungh dawng : Hei hi naupuakpuan hrenpereng a ni a, anmahni lakaih a ṭanga an tah a ni. An nau puakpuan nen a inang a, mahse hrenpereng hi chu a sei fe zawk a ni. Tunlaiah chuan vai puan pan an hmang tawh a, a zangkhai fe tawh a ni. Mipa in an hren thin a ni.

Uanklai towng (Fèn phel) : Mizo hmarâm ai hi chuan a chhing zawk a, hnung lamah mawng zawnah tak hian a zia a awm a, an zeh lai hian vawk hmul an zep tel a, mahse an tah zawhah vawk hmul chu an pawt phawi leh vek a, chhung lamah chuan a zehna a lang miah lo a ni.

Mire nu nau pawm lai

Hmeichhe pawnfen hi a chhin em a vangin ṭhut fimkhur reng a ngai a, ṭhut lan an hlauh a an fimkhur a vangin an ṭhut dawn leh an din dawnin an ṭhingṭhit ṭhin a vangin an hmeichhiate khup chu a khak deuh tlul ṭhin a ni. A rawng chu a dum a ni.

A pum a, mahse an ven chuan athuah in an veng a, an mal veilam chu a lang deuh vek ṭhin, An hrenna laiah pin in emaw an chilh a, a nihloh chuan an kawnghren in an hreng bet mai ṭhin.

    1. Lawnggi : Mipa ṭhenkhat chuan vai in an ven ṭhin Lawnggi an veng ve ṭhin.
    2. Ruakawm : Silver zai phiar mum, 2 -10 thleng hmeichhe kawnghren a ni a, aman pawh a to khawp mai.
    3. Si kekawm : Pawisa thir, pawisa 25 a thilna an siam a, tam tak an thil a, chu chu an kawngah an hreng ṭhin.
    4. Taiuih : Silver thir kil nei a siàm, lian vak lo chu

hmeichhia te’n an mawng bawrah an hreng ṭhin.

    1. Langke : An ṭhi ang chi phiar phek chu nula leh tlangval in an kawngah an hreng ṭhin.
    2. Langke buk : An ṭhi ang chi phiar mum chu nula leh tlangval te’n an kawngah an hreng ṭhin.
    3. Chiakaidawng (Kekawrtlawn) : Kekawrtlawn an hak tirh hian an sawisel nasa em em a, kekawrtlawn hate diariam nan kekawrtlawn chu, “Bawng chhu hrenpereng” an ti a, chumi hming chu a pu hlen ta a ni.
  1. Ke-ah :
    1. Ro : Naupang in an bun ṭhin Te’ngeng.
    2. Khawkkhen : Nula in an lamna leh an incheina a an ngalrek a an bun ṭhin a, silver a siam te a ni a a kawrawng a, a kawrawngah chuan pên zawng a ri chhawng chhawng thei turin a chhungah thir mum dah a ni.
    1. Khawk ro : Hei pawh hi nula in a lam dawn a a bun ṭhin bawk a ni a, Khawkkhen ai chuan a lian deuh a, khawkkhen nen hian an bun tlang ṭhin.

Miriate hian an fate hi an tet lai aṭangin an nghawngah leh an kawngah la hrual emaw tal chu an awrh tir in an hren tir ṭhin a ni. Mipa te chuan an ram kal in an hrenperengah an chempui hriam tak chu an pai ṭhin a, engemaw chang a kawng nal a an tluk palh chang in an chempui pai chuan an khel a ât duai chang pawh a awm ṭhin a, mahse chutiang a mipa khel a chempui in a ahna ser lianpui pui awm te chu mipa diktak nihna ni in an ngai.

BUNG 23

THU LEH HLA

  1. Thu lam :

Kan unau Miriate hi thu leh hla an thiam in ṭhiante nen a han inkawm a ti ti hi an peih in an thian em em mai a, Field fang a ka kal chang leh patling hote nen a kan inkawm changte hian kan ti ti tlaivar chang leh kan tlaivar thelh changte a awm fo ṭhin reng a ni. An khua a mikhual lo kalte hi khual ṭha tih an hriat chuan an khaw pa ho chu mikhual thlennaah chuan leng khawm in hlim takin an ti ti ho ṭhin.

Mire ho hian thil han sawi fiah dan leh remchang tak a tehkhin thu han hman mai turte hi an hre thei em em mai a, upa ho han ti ti hi chu a ngaihnawm thei ṭhin khawp mai. Amaherawh chu ṭawngkam an thiam in ṭawng bumngrua an ngah kan tih lai hian anmahni Mire ṭawng ngau ngau hi chu ṭawng hausa tak a ni hran lo.

Anni hi alunung chuan ṭhahnem viau mahse Bangladesh a tlangmite zingah hnam hnufual ber an nih miau avangin anmahni ai a hnam fing zawk leh hnam tam zawkte ṭawng chu an awmna hmun a zirin an lak a ngai ṭhin a, chuvangin an chhehvel hnamte ṭawng pawh an la tam khawp mai.

Tin, Mire hnamah pawh hian; bung hmasa lam a kan sawi tawh ang khan hnam hlawm hrang hrang, ṭawng hrang hmang an la awm lehzel a, ṭhen khat chu inzul deuh viau mahse ṭhenkhat erawh chu anmahni pawh an inpawh hleithei lova. Chuvangin kan unau Miria te hi anmahni Mire hnam zingah tal chuan ṭawngpui (National languge) a hman bik an neih ṭul in ka hria a, chu an ṭawngpui (National languge)-ah chuan Anawk ṭawng chu ka hmang ta a.

Anawk zing aṭang hian Kristian pawh an tam in an ṭawng hi Mire ṭawng hrang hrang zingah chuan a ṭha ber a ka hriat vanga hmang ka ni a, Literature zawng zawngah hian Anawk ṭawng vek kan hman tak a vangin tunah chuan Lalpa ṭanpuina zarah Anawk ṭawng chu Mire hnam ṭawngpui (National languge) a ni deuhthaw hial tawh a ni.

Kan unau Miria te hi thlang tla hmasa a muk thing lo theltu te kha an nihna chu lo dahṭha ta ila, an ṭawng a ṭang ringawt pawh hian Mizo hnahthlak anni tih chiang riau in ka hrai a, an mizia leh dan leh hraite heti em em a Mizo te nen kan danglam nachhan chu kum za engemaw zat chhung chu kan khawsakna (Environment) a danglam em em vangte pawh a ni thei a ni. Mizo ṭawng leh Mire ṭawng thumal leh a awmzia inang chiah leh inang thuak a lo tam em em mai hi kum tam tak kalta khan eng tik hun laiah emaw kha chuan hmun khatah kan lo awm in thlahtu bul hmun khat kan ni tih a chiang hle in ka hria a ni.

Ka hringnun leh Miriate khawvel bu chhuak hmasa-ah kha chuan Mire ṭawng leh Mizo ṭawng inan chiahna leh inan thuakna tam tak ka dah a, mahse tunah chuan a ṭul lutuk a ka hriat loh a vangin ka dah ta lo a ni.

  1. Hla lam :

98Mire pa in Rina plung a tum lai

Miria te hian hla hi an ngaina ve hle mai a, ram a an kal laite, khaw dang a nula rim a an kal kawngah te hian an zai veng veng ṭhin. Nula leh tlangval inrimte hi hla hmangin an in kawm ṭhin a, achang chuan hla thu hmangte pawhin an inhnial bawk ṭhin. Achang chuan thawnthute pawh hi hla angmai in a sakin an sa bawk ṭhin.

An hla hi Bawm leh Pangte mithi ṭah ang mai hi a ni a, a chham in an chham zuah zuah a, an chham tawp lamah chuan a hniam in, “òw” tlawk angreng tak mai hian an ti help help ṭhin, chutia an tih tlawk lai chuan an khabe te hi an ti sawi kual vel ṭhin bawk. An zai laite hian an aw an thian sek a, an han zai tui tak tak chuan an mit te an chhing diar diar ṭhin.

An hla thu hi ziak a dah hleihtheih a ni meuh lova, a chhan chu atlangpui in an phuah chawp zel a, an hla chuan tawp hun a nei hran lova, an sak peih chhung a ni mai a, an zai chu lo record in lo ziak ta ila, kan hla ziak chu a satu chu ahma a a sak ang chiah a sa leh tur in ti ila, a sak hmasak ang chiah chuan a sa leh thei kher tawh lovang, achhan chu an phuahchawp zel a nih a vang a ni.

99Nula Chamle zai lai

An zaithiamte chu an bawng chhun nikhua leh an ruai nikhuate hian an zai tir ṭhin a, tape in an lo recoad a, chu chu upa lam leh nula tlangval te’n an tleipui thei khawp mai. An zaithiam a zai lai hian an rimawi, Rina plung leh Tingte’ng te an lo tum rem ṭhin. An hla thu kalphung hi tehkhin thu deuh vek a ni in Mawk ṭawng an hmang tam hle a, chuvangin anmahni Miria te ngei pawh in an hla thu chu an hre thiam vek lo, an hla thu hre tur chuan hla lam a tui mi nih a ngai a ni.

Hla phuah thiam leh zaimi aw ṭha te chu, “Meng sara” an ti a. Chung an meng sara a Mire ram dung leh vanga lar zual deuh te chu mipaah chuan – Parau a ni a, hmeichhia ah chuan Chamrung te an ni. Hemite pahnih lo pawh hi lar ve tak tak chu an awm tho bawk.

An hla thu hi nula leh tlangvalte inlemna leh an vanduai zia an chham chhuahna a ni tlangpui a, Hla thu hmang hian an infak in an insawichhe bawk ṭhin. Hla thu hmang hian an bialnu, bialpa te’n neih an intiam a an neih leh si loh te hian hla thu hmang in anchhiate an inlawh chiam bawk ṭhin. An hlaah hian an bialnu, bialpa te fakna lam aimah in an vanduai zia an chham chhuahna hi a tam mah zawk a ni.

Mire hnam in hla an neih hmasak ber chu doral hma a zam lo tur leh huaisen tak a hmelmate lu la a an hmelma nghawng fik hmawk hmawk tura an infuihna hla a ni a, chu chu Burma a an awm lai a ral run hun lai a an phuah a ni. Kei ni Mizo hla hmasa ber ni a an sawi chu, “Heta ṭangin khatami kha a lang. Khata ṭangin hetami hi a lang,” tih mai a ni a, hla hmasa ber rau rauah chuan keini ai chuan an hla hmasa ber chu a zia fe zawk ni in a lang.

Mire hla hmasa ber chu hei hi a ni:

Sampik tun en üah,

Ang tum en sam pik üah.

Paw pe mi lawtla iang,

Sarra sawi lu sawi len;

Sawi nawk kham pren,

Tungping tauing.

‘üah’ tih hi tunlai a ‘u-owa’ (Bawihte) tihna a ni.

Miriate hian khawvel siamtu chu Phivawk leh Thloh (Sava) hi ni in an ngai a, phai ram hi Phivawk siam a ni a, tlang ram hi Thloh siam ni in an ngai a ni. Phivawk leh Thloh chuan khawvel an siam dawn in Pathian hnenah lei chi an dil a, Pathian chuan lei chi chu a lo pe a, chumi lei chi-ah chuan uirena chi leh sualna chi hrang hrang a lo tel a vangin nula leh tlangval in ngaihna sual leh uirena sual te lo awmah an ngai a ni. Chu chu hla in heti hian an phuah a.

Rum Paksarua, nawn bawn sown iowm,

Reng mau uala, chowrum dowi kowi;

Tawi ku tawi piang, tawng krawng bawn liu,

Ka la krawng bawn liu khe dow,

Sata krow leng tum dawng kowi,

Re la pangkhung mang kuan kai iang leng,

Chang tui prang io, praing tiak khiam lawm,

Thup pe i krawng bawn liu.

Putma liu tawng krawng bawn liu,

Rai lawng bawn liu tawi, akrawng bawn kung;

Iawtma liu tawi, krawng bawn kung,

Iang liu tawi, krawng bawn kung;

Kang lawng ow, chi chang lawng,

Chowng lawng ow,chi chawng lawng lai dow,

Hang kow bu maw mang,

Alai tama mrikrowng chowng kown kowh,

I chimp u tang, i chimp u tang,

Ari tantaw, chakramang pren lai mowr tasow, a u ow.

Mizo ṭawng a he hlathu awmzia tlangpui chu : “Phivawk in lei a siam dawn in lei chi Pathian hnenah a dil a, Pathian in Phivawk hnenah chuan lei chi a pe ta a; a lei chi pekah chuan thing leh mau leh thlai chi tinreng a tel bakah sualna chi tinreng pawh a tel bawk a, chuvang in pi leh pute chhan a ṭang tawhin he khawvelah hian sualna chitin reng a lo awm tawh a ni. Chu vang in ka ‘d’ Bawihte, i rilru ti hreawm ma tache, khawvel pian tirh a ta tawh sualte chu a lo awm tawh a, mi tih loh sual kan ti a ni bik hleinem. Kan pi leh pute lo tih ṭhin ang kha kan ti ve a ni mai a lawm,” ti a tlangvalin a nula chetsualpui a hnemna hla a ni.

Nula leh tlangval inhmangaihna hla pakhat lo tar lang leh ila, he hla thu aṭang hian nula leh tlangval te’n inhmangaihna an lak lungrun ve zia kan hre thei awm e.

Sing khung tara naiawk paing pau,

Nang ampen pawr pen sin khaw lak,

Dawng dang rinit tuk chin ngawi kle dowi,

Powng sai a u powng pat tut kowi.

Mizo ṭawng a a hla thu awmzia chu : “Maipawl chhungah nauban par a chuai thei lo ang in ka thinlung chhungah hian chuai ni reng i nei ngai lo,” an tihna a ni.

Nu in a fa a hmanghaih zia hla an aphuah hian nu hmangaihna a ti chiang riau in ka hria a, tehkhin thu an hman thiam zia leh an hla thu hi tekkhin thu deuh vek a ni tih pawh a lang chiang hle bawk a ni.

Powngtai dow be howng dawnia dow luk kowi,

Howi lek lu lowh, simlung muirau;

Klai sakkhawt bow, ngechowh ow,

Pe ram sang kungh klik di sang lai.

Kuiai chowh lai bow owi,

Be le lak kowi phung lah chawi pawr lowk rüa.

Aia kow cham rap kir sang tui,

Kir iowm dowi lowi khe kowh,

Rüm dawng kua roh thap hapma rau,

Rüm dawng kuaima thawr thap leng leng hawm;

Paw a lawngta powng chawi pawr plawng rau,

Nawng nau nip sak u, plawng kaw rau dowi tak,

Pe bow nge chawi iow,

Hang kow plawng rau bow.

Mizo ṭawng a ahla thu tlangpui chu, “Ramhnuai ngaw dur khupah hian khuaibel in bu a siam a, khuai pakhat chuan an no chawm nan lui a mi tui a zu chawi a, chutia a tui chawi an bu lam a akir leh tur chiah chu vakul hian a lo man hlauh mai a. Chu khuaibel chu vakul lak aṭang a chhuah tum chuan a tal ve nasa mai a. Atal nasat em a vang chuan a thla te a ṭet pheng phung mai a.

Chu khuaibel chuan vakul in a chukna aimah chuan a note tuihal dang ro, dang char a an awm tur chuan ati na zawk a, chuvang chuan na pawh hre chang lovin a theih tawkin chhuah tum chuan a tal fat fat reng a. Chutiang bawk chuan ka hrai bungdawntuaite, lungphang reng reng suh, kei pawhin khawibel in a note a hmangaih angin ka hmangaih che a sin,” ti a chhuat a an chhemthei hrut ri kawrh kawrh chung chuan thenbawm a a nau then mek chu a awi ṭhin a ni.

Tin, mi zaidam, thinnel leh thilphalte hian Pathian malsawmna an dawng a, an dam chhungin ei leh bar an nghei ngai lo, tih hla ṭha tak mai tehkhin thu hmang a an phuah hi han tarlang leh ila.

Kun kaw te’ng thum rum dawng uahar,

Achap suthawng mowik sadaua nowngia uowi,

Plap iua nowngia dow klap thgum,

Kun kaw te’ng thum rum dawng uahar,

Iaw reng dow, piaw mang piaw rui dow,

Thum chak, asang singram, plawng lowng tow,

Plau tawi chowng, kun kaw chak,

Uasaing dow, pro cho rum dawng kua ro,

Sing dawng dow luk hown ta mü dow,

Owi li asung sing ram plowng lawng.

Ow plan uang tawi khun kun kaw chak khai,

Niiawng chepaw rum dawng dow uahar,

Tow tawl lawr nu la lawklek bowr bow,

Lawklek lai khai chahra daka preingma.

Taing bow kla kun kaw rau dowi,

Aluk phung luk kowi klen rui sak chowng.

Mizo ṭawng a ahla thu awmzia tlangpui chu : Vazar rual zingah chuan sava chi hrang hrang leh sakei te pawh an tel ṭhin a, Vazar ho chu an rilru a ṭhat em a vangin chung ho te chuan nuam an ti thei hle ṭhin a. Vazar ho chaw thai chhuah sa chu chung lam aṭangin vakulte chuan an lo laksak leh daih ṭhin a, mahse vazar ho chuan thinrim miah lovin, “In hlei teh tak e,” ti in an nui mai mai ṭhin a. Mahse, vazar ho chu Pathian in chawm in mal a sawm a, an dam chhung in chaw an nghei ngai chuang reng reng lo.

“Hei hi hla a ang hleinem,” kan ti pawh a ni mai thei a, ahla thuah hian an ṭawng tual leng atel meuh lova, Miriate tan ngei pawh zaimi nive lo tan chuan hriat thiam mai mai chi a nilova, mahse an zaimi te chuan tui takin an sa ve ṭhin a ni. Hla hlun ve deuhte pawh an nei ve nual bawk.

Hmanlai chuan bawng an chhunte hian bawng chhun a sak chi hrim hrim hla an nei a, chu chu an bawng chhun hun chauh in an sa ṭhin a, aṭhen chu zing lam a sak chi te, tlai lam a sak chite a ni a, mahse tunlai mite chuan a hla hi an thiam tawh lo a ni. Chuvangin an bawng chhunnaah pawh a thluk ringawt in an sa tawh mai a ni. Chu chu han tarlang leh ila :

Zan a sak chi :

Krawng sa ri ü, ri ü. Uai tam ba ü, ba ü.

(Lei hi a nung reng a, a awm reng bawk a ni)

Nam sa ri ü, ri ü, Iawng tam ba ü, ba ü.

(Kan damna turin inthawina kan hlan, kan ra nin kan nunna kan hum)

Sangku bür hown pe, Chim ü, chim ü.

(An tumphung rimawi Plung fakna a ni)

Tür ram talek ü, Ram ta lek ü.

(Thangṭung kau thle nghial nghial angin thle nghial nghial ru)

Kimma rui sing kawng ü, kawng ü, Kawng sing iang ü, iang ü.

(Ramhnuai a hrui chikhat, “Kimma ruising” an tih chuan bawng kai ru)

Awma dam ti kown na ü, Pa rowpsowi lowi, rowp sowi lowi.

(Lui sangha an leng dun rial rial angin kan bawng chhun tur an ding din rial e)

Krawng bawm sai ü, Sai rit ret ü, ret ü,

Ua di sai ü, Rawng phua tawm ü, tawm ü.

(Nula leh tlangval lam te fakna leh fuihna)

Zing lam a sak chi :

Ua di ü, ua da òw,

Ua di sai che rawng phua tawm ü, tawm ü,

Ua da sai che rawng phua tawm ü, tawm ü.

(Hei hi Phung nupa an bawng chhunna a rawn tel tura sawmna hla)

Aw kaw rum rum, pau lang ü,

Aw kaw rum rum pau lang ü, pau ü,

Ning ria chen lang pau ü, pau ü.

(Kumpui sul lo vei in lui kam pangparte pawh a hun teah an lo par leh ta)

Ning khum iua pau lang ü,

Ning khum iua ri pau lang pau ü.

(Iumiua thing chu khel lovin a par ṭhin ang hian kum pawh a ngai in a lo her leh ṭhin).

Aw kaw chin che hai mün ü,

Aw kaw chin che hai bai iow,

Aw kaw chin che hai mün ü,

Aw kaw chin che hai mün òw ü.

(Lui kam khamrangah kum tin Chinrangin bu a chhep ṭhin a, no a nei ṭhin. Chutiangin kum chu a ngai in a lo inher leh ṭhin).

Kim rengia ma, kim rengia ma,

Pa chowng plawng via sang ü,

Kim rengia ma, kim rengia ma,

Pa chowng singku hua mang ow ü.

(In neitu {Bawng chhuntu} chu dam reng sela, thing buk lian tak angin awm sela, thing lian leh lungpui ang mai a nghetin lo awm rawh se).

Chiama kowng ü, ba ting tüa ü, tüa ü,

Nama kowng ü, ba ting tüa ü, tüa ü.

(Ṭhal a bawng/sial an chhun hlumna hla).

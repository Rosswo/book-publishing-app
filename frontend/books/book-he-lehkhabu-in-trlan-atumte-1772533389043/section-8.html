BUNG 5

MISSIONARY ATAN LALPAN MIN KO

Kan khua a ka phak tawk a Lalpa rawng ka bawl lai leh ka Nu ṭanpui a hlim tak a hun ka hman lai chuan Hnahthial aṭangin ka pa Hminga chu kan khuah chuan a lo kal a, ka pa Hminga chuan, “Hnahthial-ah rawn haw leh tawh mai rawh, hetianga thingtlanga lo hnathawk ringawta i awm chuan nakin zelah i tan a ṭha lovang; Bus permission sorkar-ah kan dil mek a, kan hmuh ngei pawh ka ring, kan hmu a nih chuan nangman i enkawl dawn nia,” ati a. Ka pa Hminga thusawi chu ngun tak a ka ngaihtuahin dik ka ti angreng hle mai a, lo neih a ei ka lo zawng a nih chuan nupui ka lo nei a nih pawh in chutiang a nupui fanaute chawm chu ka tlin reng ka ring lova, “Tunṭum a tan chauh ka pa Hminga thusawi hi zawm leh rih phawt ila, ka tihsual pawhin, ka tihfuh pawhin,” ti in thutlukna chu ka siam leh ta a.

Kum 1988 chuan Hnahthial-ah chuan ka chhuk leh ta a. Ka pa Hminga’n a sawi ang chuan Bus permission pawh chu an lo dil ngei a, kan hmu ngei bawk a, mahse han lei mainatur sum kan neih remchan mai loh a vangin hemi kum hi chuan hnathawh mumal tak ka la nei rih lova.

Kan Kohhran (Hnahthial Bazar Kohhran) chu Pathian Thlarau-ah kan hlim hle mai a, ṭhalai leh tleirawl an hlim zual a, chung ṭhalai hlim ho kaihhruai hna chu U Valtea (Tv Zorammawia) te nen kan thawk a. Kan Unit Ṭ.K.P. te’n, “Kan Kohhran a ṭang hian Missionary kan la chhuak ve mih lova Unit dangah chuan Missionary chhuak an awm nual tawh a, chuvangin kan Unit a ṭanga Missionary chhuak kan awm ve theih nan Pathian hnenah ṭawngṭai in i dil ang u, zing ṭawngṭai inkhawm kan neih ṭhin bâkah; ṭhalai pualin tlai ṭawngṭai inkhawm i siam ang u,” kan ti a. Kum hnih ṭawngṭai in kan Unit a ṭanga Missionary kan chhuah ve theih nan kan dil hnu pawh chuan chhan-na kan la hmu thei lova, a la reh vung vung mai a.

Kum 1990 March thla (a ni chiah ka hre thei ta lo) tir lam chuan Ambassador For Christ Team Speaker Upa K.Lawmsanga chuan Hnahthial Tarpho veng Salem Kohhranah rawngbawlna a nei a, an Kohhran a ṭanga Missionary chhuak Tv, Thangzela’n a rawn ṭawiawm bawk a. Missionary Tv, Thangzela report ngaihthlak beisei pahin ka han inkhawm ve a. Missionary chuan inkhawmnaah chuan report a pe lova, inkhawm banah an Kohhran Upa R.Lalmawia (Pu Mawitea) inah inpawlhona kan nei a, chutah chuan Missionary chuan report a pe a, ngaihnawm ka ti em em mai a.

Upa K.Lawmsanga te nupa

Pu Lawmsanga chuan Mire ram a Missionary an intirh dan leh an Missionary an la luh theih loh dante a sawi bawk a. An mi tirh pahnih an ban tak dante a sawi zel a, Mire ram a thih ngam a inpe an la zawn thute a sawi in Mire hovin an lo auh dante hetiang hian a sawi a, “Miria, Pu Singchownga’n lehkha min rawn thawn a, ‘Pu Lawm, Mire ram a ṭanga in haw tak hnu khan Mire hnam zingah Kristian kan pung nasa em em mai a, meialh ang maiin Mire ram chu a tuam duai duai mai a, amârawh chu Kristian kan nih dan chu hria la chuan i rilru a na ngawt ang.

Mire tlangval pakhat, a hming Menleha’n sakhaw thar a siam chhuak a, chu a sakhaw siam chhuah chu ‘Kramadi’ an ti a. Kristian sakhaw zirtirna zulzui in a zirtir a, amah chu Isua angin a insawi a, amah ringtute chu nakinah a la chhandam dawn niin a zirtir a, chu chu Mire ho chuan sakhaw dik a ngai in mitam takin chu sakhua chu an zui a, min rawn pui lo a nih chuan Miriate thlarau chu kawng diklo lamah kan peng vek dawn a ni. Khawngaih takin keini in unau Miria te thlarau boral mekte hi min rawn chhanchhuak hram rawh u,’ tiin min rawn auva, ka tan chuan Pawla’n ‘Makedonia ramah lo kai la min pui rawh (Tirh 16:9) ti a kohna a hriat aiin a nep chuang in ka hre bik lova, kum thum Mire ramah Volunteert in rawng ka lo bawl tawh a, ram buai vang leh ka hriselna a ṭhat loh a vangin kan rawn haw a ni a, tunah keimah kal thei lo mah ila ka ai kal tur zawn chu ka mawhphurhna a ni tih hria in…” ti in a sawi zel a.

Mire rama Missionary intir turin Ambassador For Christ Mission Board an din thute, an mi tirh mi pathum Bangladesh ramri a Chakma hel hovin an lo man a an bag leh an thil neih zawng zawng an lo chhuhsak vek a, an kal leh a nih chuan kahhlumah an lo vau thute leh chung an mitirhte chu beidawng taka an rawn kir leh thute a sawi bawk a.

Chutia Pu Lawmsanga’n a sawi mek lai chuan Miriate chu ka lo khawngaih êm èm mai a, an mitirhte chuan han bei leh hram sela, han lut thei hram se, Miriate thlarau bo chu chhanchhuak thuai thuai se tihte ka lo ngaihtuah a. Keima kal turah chuan ka ngai lo hrim hrim a. Ka thiamnate a tlem bakah Missionary rawngbawl hna hi chu ka thawh ve phakah ka ngai lo hrim hrim a, a tum pawh vawi khatmah ka la tum chhin ngai lo hrim hrim a ni.

Chutia Pu Lawmsanga’n Mire ram a an mitirh chanchin leh Miriate chanchin a sawi lai chuan lainatna nen mitkhap lo lêk a ka lo thlir doh doh lai chuan putar pakhat hian zawhna nghawng nei thui tak mai hi a rawn zawt tlat mai a, chu a zawhna chu: “Mire ram a Missionary hna thawk tur chuan zirna lamah Qualification bithliah chin in nei em?” tih hi a ni a, chu putar zawhna chu Pu Lawmsanga chuan tihian a chhang a, “Zirna lamah Qualification bithliah chin kan nei lo, Mire ram a Missionary hna thawk tur chuan Thih ngam a inpete chu an tling a ni mai,” ti in.

An Missionary enkawl dan pawh hetiang hian fel takin an siam:

  1. A.F.C. Missionary te hian Kohhran, Pawl hovin Missionary an rawihte angin hlawh an nei ve lova, ramtinah Pathian chanchinṭha hril turin koh a ni tih a inhria in a thawk ang; an lawman beitham tak erawh chu A.F.C. Mission Board in a lo pe ve ṭhin dawn a ni.
  2. Missionary-te hian an thawhna Kohhran hnai berah an ringthar lakte chu an hlan zel tur a ni.
  3. A.F.C. Missionary-te hian Kohhran, Pawl hrang hrang din an tum ngai tur a ni lo.
  4. Missionary te hian an awmna/thawhna ram mite dinhmun leh an nihna a awm in chu chu an nihna tur a ni tih an hre reng tur a ni.
  5. Missionary te hian ṭul leh pawimawh an neih apiang chu Mission Board Committee-ah an thlen ṭhin tur a ni.
  6. A.F.C. Missionary te hi Committee in tul atih hun apiangah a chawlh tir thei ang. Entir nan, Kohhran tan a nun dan ṭhalo leh mawi lo an tih in.
  7. Nupui pasal neih chungchangah a ruaitute phalna an la hmasa tur a ni.
  8. Missionary te’n kum khat chhunga an hnathawh dan leh an hmalak tum dan Committee-ah an report ṭhin tur a ni.
  9. Furlough thu: Missionary te hi thil ṭul bikna a awm loh chuan kum hnih (2) danah an Furlough ṭhin ang.
  10. An chhuti/Furlough hun chhung chu thla hnih a ni ang.

Hetia Pu Lawmsanga’n an hma lâk tawh dan leh an hma lâk zel tum dan a han sawi hian ka pianthar lai a Lalpa hnen a ka lo inhlan tawh dan kha ka hrechhuak zawk mai a, “Lalpa, min hmangaih a vanga hreawm tam tak tuarin i fapa Isua Krista in min tlan chhuah a vangin kei pawh i tan a nung turin ka thlarau, ka nuinna leh ka taksa i hnenah, i duh a i hman atan ka rawn inhlan e. I duh angin min hmang ve ang che,” tiin Lalpa tan ka lo inhlan tawh a.

Kei mi tling lo tak mai hi a rawngbawl turin a thlarauin min thuam a, Mizoram chhungah leh Burma ramahte pawh a chhandamna thu ka lo tlangaupui ve tawh ṭhin a. Hetia kum hnih dawn lai mai, “Lalpa, khawi lamah nge mi tirh leh ang?” ti a Lalpa aw ka lo ngaihchan ṭhin chu chù Putar in Pu Lawmsanga zawhna a zawh Pu Lawmsanga’n a chhanna chu Lalpa’n Mire ram a kal tura min kohna a ni tih min hrilhtu awm ang mai hian ka thinlungah chiang tak mai hian hriatna ka nei ta tlat mai a. Ka mangangin ka zam chhe nasa ta mai a, chhuanlam ka zawng ruai ta mai a.

Hun kha lehchen Lalpa min lo hruai tawhna leh Pathian chak, engkim tithei a a nihna kawng tam takin ka chung ngeiah leh ka mithmuh ngeiah a rawn lantir tawh zawng zawngte chu hai derin Mire ram kher kherah chuan ka kal duh loh avangin chhuanlam tam tak ka nei a. Bangladesh a awm hote ṭawng ka thiam si lo, ram dang a kal turin sapṭawng lah ka thiam bawk silo. Chubâkah Pu Lawma te mitirh pawh Mire ram a lut thei lovin an rawn kir leh a nia, kei tehlul ka luh theih bikna tur a awm lo.

Tin, kal lo tum tehreng pawh ni ila, kal ka tum thu ka chhungte ka hrilh hún hùn chu Mire ram a kal tum ting lo tura min tih hun a ni dawn tih pawh ka hriat sa a ni a. Tin, midang, Mire ram a kal ka tum a ni tih lo hretu apiangte’n midang kal theih lohna hmun a kei tehlul midang an lut thei lo tih hre reng chunga kal tura ka han inpe ve chu min lawmpui ai chuan an hmusitna leh nuih tizatu mai ka ni dawn tih ka hre reng bawk a, hlauhna leh zahna in ka thauah min man a ni ber mai.

Lalpa’n ka rin ai hla, hmun thim, hlauhawm leh zahna ruam kal tlang kher ngaihna hmun a min han ko mai chu, ka pianthar tirh a, “Lalpa i duhna hmun a piangah min tir rawh,” ti kher kher a ka lo inhlan kha ka inchhir rum rum mai a. Lalpa min kohna lah chu midangte hriatpui ve lohvin ka thinlungah a ri ring tulh tulh mai bawk si a. “Lalpa, i hnenah ka kâ ka lo âng tawh tak nâ in Mire ramah kher kher chuan min tir lo hram rawh, kei naupang leh mi chaklo tan chuan ka paltlang tur hmabâk a ko lutuk deuh emmai,” tiin ka ṭawngṭai mawlh mawlh a, mahse Lalpan, “Michak lo chuan ‘ka chak’ tirawh se, chaktak leh huai takin awm rawh, ka awmpui zel dawn che a lawm, tih te leh ‘Tun hun anga i ngawih mai mai chuan nang leh i pa chhungte hi kan boral maiang” tih thu hmangte in min chhang zel si a.

Lalpa’n min koh hun a ka chhan ngam loh chuan Jona dinhmun chu ka dinhmun a ni ve dawn tih ka hre bawk si a. A hnenah engmah sawi tur leh chan pawh ka nei dawn lovin Lalpa lakah engmah beisei tur ka nei tawh dawn miah lo tih pawh ka inhre chiang a. Mahse Lalpa’n Mire ram a kal tura min kohna chu ka chhang ngam thlawt bawk silo.

Hetih hun lai hian kan Kohhran chu kan hlim em em mai a, Bible chhiar khaidiat-te, chawnghei ṭawngṭai tlaivarte kan nei reng ṭhin bawk a. Mahse kei chu chuta ṭang chuan Pathian ka hnial tlat avangin ka ṭawngṭai thei tawh lova, Pathian fak hla pawh ka sa thei bawk hek lo. Lalpa min kohna hnial reng chung si a a hming fak a zai leh ṭawngṭaite chu ka thei lo a ni. Tu hriatpui loh in ka nun chu a hreawm em em mai a, ka tihtheih chhun chu Bible chhiar chauh a ni.

Bible ka han chhiar lah chuan Lalpa min tirhna ngawt ka chhiar fuh leh ṭhin bawk si a, ka tan chuan Baible chhiar pawh huphurhawm tak a ni. Tirhkohte 5:41,42 thu ka han chhiar chuan Tirhkoh te’n Lalpa tirhna a chanchinṭha an hrilhna a man leh roreltute hma a an hruai a an vuak a chanchinṭha hril tawh lo tur a an tih tlat pawhin huaisen tak a an tawrh dante, Lalpa Isua hming avanga tuar tlak a ruat an nih a vanga an lawm zawk ziate ka han hriat chuan Lalpa’n a hming avanga kei mitling lo ber mai tuar ve tlaka min ruat chu mitam tak chan phak loh Van LAL duhsakna ka dawng a ni tih ka hriat chuan vannei ka inti in lawmawm ka ti bawk si. Tirhkohte huaisen zia ka hriat chuan ka ngaisang in ropui ka ti hle a.

Bible ka chhiar zel chuan Tirhkoh Pawla chu Lalpa Isua hming avang a phuar chang huam lova thih pawh a huam ziate (Tirh 21:10 – 16) ka hre zel a. Lalpa hmaah chuan ding tlak reng ka awm lo hian ka inhria a, ka zak em em mai a. Mire ram a kal tur a inpek erawh ka la tum chuang reng reng lo. Michhuanawm ka nih tawh loh a hnuhmanah midangte hmuhsit a awm tura ka inngaih tlatnate, ka chhungte hnenah han sawi mah ila an remti dawn chuang lova ka hriatnate, mualpho riau dawna ka inhriat tlatnate chuan Lalpa min kohna ka dal tir tlat mai a.

Mahse, Lalpa’n Mire ram a a rawng bawl tura min kohna chu midangte hriatpui lohvin Jeremia’n, “A ni chu ka lam lovang a, a hmingin thu ka sawi tawh hek lovang’ ti mah ila; ka ruh chhung a mei kang inkhung ta ang mai hian ka thinlung chhungah hian a awm si ṭhin a, ka pai hle hle zo lova, ka chelh ngang lova ni,” (Jer 20:9) a atih ang mai khan kei pawn Lalpa min kohna chu ngawih bopui mai chu har ka ti em em a, I Kor 15:10 a Tirhkoh Pawlan, “Nimahsela Pathian khawngaihna avangin ka nìh níh ka ni ta,” tih ka han chhiar chuan Lalpa min kohna chu chhang lo thei ka nih tak loh a vangin, Lalpa rinchhan in, Lalpa hming avang chuan ka nìh níh nìh inhuam in Lalpa ṭanpuina dil a ṭawngṭai chungin ka chhungte hnenah chuan ka han sawi ta rawih a.

Ka pa Hminga chuan min chhan dan a ka lo rin lawk ang ngei in, “E!!! nang chu i kal thei lovang, Pu Lawmsanga-te pawh an kal tawh a, an rawn kir leh a, taksa leh thlarauva mi puitling tak tak mi engemaw zat Mire ramah hian an lo kal tawh a, mahse a rei berah kum thum, kum li bâk an awm thei lo, an rawn kir leh zel ṭhin. Ram dang a ni a, nang chu Mizo ṭawng bâk eng ṭawng mah thiam i nei bawk silo. Bâkah, hei tunah pawh Pu Lawmsanga-te mitirhte pawhin Mire ram chu sawi loh Bangladesh pawh lut thei lovin an rawn kir leh a ni a, nangpawh kal mahla lut thei lovin i rawn kir leh a nga, nangmah i hming chhiat bâkah keini i nu leh pa leh i chhungte tan pawh a zahthlak dawn emmai, kal chu tum duh ting lo mai rawh,” min ti a.

Chu chu Pathian min kohna min hriatpui ve lotu leh ka hmangâitute min chhan dantur a ni in min chhan dan tur a ka lo rin lawk dan pawh a ni reng a. Ka pa Hminga chu han chhan leh dantur pawh hre lo chuan a hmaah chuan ka ding zum reng mai a.

Ka pa Hminga’n chutia min chhan avang chuan Lalpa min kohna chu a nêp phah chuang si lova, nêp a hnêk in ka thinlung chhungah chuan Lalpa min kohna chu a ring tual tual mai si a. Ka chaw ei pawh a tui lova, zanah lah ka muhil thei bawk hek lo. Nikhat chu ka pa Hminga chu Lungleiah kal a tum a, a kal hma ni chuan a tukah Lungleiah a kal dawn a ni tih ka hriat chuan lehkha ka ziak a, Lalpa’n Mire ram a kal tura min koh dante ka ziak a, a tuk zingah chuan ka pa Hminga Lunglei kal rualpui chu Lunglei an thlen ve leh a ka pa Hminga lehkha chu pe turin a kutah chuan ka pe ta a.

Ka pa Hminga Lunglei aṭanga a rawn haw chuan keiman sawi hmasak ngai tawh lovin aman min zawt anga, chutih hunah chuan chiang takin Lalpa min koh dan leh Mire ram a kal tura chuti taka min kotu chuan a hmangaih a fa hi Mire ram thleng lova hming chhia leh mualpho tangrang a ka awm chu a phal ka rin loh thu leh min hruai thlen ngei ka rin thu sawi tur chuan ka lo inbuatsaih a.

Ka pa Hminga chu Lungleiah chuan zankhat a zu riak a, atukah chuan a lo haw leh a, a thianpa chuan ka lehkha chu a lo pe ngei a, a rawn haw chuan kei chu varanda-ah ka lo awm a. Ka pa Hminga chuan inchhungah min ko lut a, “Mire ram a kal chu i la tum reng ami?” tiin min zawt a. Kei chuan Lalpa min kohna ka hriat dante leh Lalpa min kohna chhang lo thei ka nih loh ziate sawi in, “Lalpa’n Mire ram a awmhlen turin min kova, ka kal a nih chuan ka rawn haw tawh lovang, thil ṭul dang engemaw avang chuan ka rawn haw zawk chu a ni maithei a, Mire ramah lut thei lovin Lalpa’n martar ve tlak a min ngai a Bangladesh ramri a chakma hel ho kut a ka thi a nih pawh in ka thisen in Mire ram a kal tur kei ai fel leh ṭha Lalpa hnenah a dil chhuak tho thovang. Mahse, kei chu martar tlakah Lalpa’n min ngaih ka ring lo,” ka ti a.

Lalpa’n ka pa Hminga thinlung chu a thlak sak a, “Chutia Pathian kohna i dawng tak tak a nih chuan Pathian fa i ni a, Pathian in a tirhna hmunah chuan i kal tur a lawm, Pathian kohna che chu keini lo hnial theih a ni hleinem,” a ti dam ta diai mai a. A thlun phelh ang huai hi ni in ka inhria a, hlim leh lawm em em in A.F.C. Mission Board Chaiman Pu Lawmsanga hnenah chuan ka kal ta vang vanga, Mire ram a kal tura ka inpek thu ka zu hrilh a.

Atuk dt 24 March 1990 zing karah chuan Pu V.P. Vuana inah A.F.C. Mission Board Committee hmaah chuan ka va inlan ve ta a. Anni chuan ka beisei loh dan deuh mai in min lo chhang a, “Tunṭum atan chuan mithar kan la tawh rih lovang, kan lâk tum zât kan lo la fel tawh a, kan tir liam tawh nghe nghe a ni, i tlai deuh hlek a ni,” ti in min lo chhanga. Ka rilru in, “Lalpa remruat dan hi hriat thiam har ka va ti tak em!!! Min kotu aw ka hriat kha Lalpa aw ngei a ni si a,” ti in ka ngaihtuah a. Chutiang vel ngaihtuak a ka la din reng lai chuan. An zinga pakhat, Pu Sawma chuan, “Apianthar dan leh Mire rama kal tura Lalpa kohna a dawn dante zawt ve hrim hrim phawt ila a ṭha lawm ni,” a rawn ti a. Chutia ka la din reng lai chuan an mi zawh duhte min zawt ta a, min zawhna chu ṭhatakin ka chhang a, Pu Sawma vek chuan, “Kan duh tawk lo la tawh pawh ni ila chutia Lalpa kohna dawngtu keinin lo hnawl chu kan tisual palh a nge, i la ve mai ang u,” a ti a. Tumah hnialkalhtu an awm tak lohvah chuan min la ve ta hram a.

Min ṭanpuina an sawi angina thla that lawmman Rs 800 min pai a, ka kalpui turte chuan Lawngtlai an thlen tawh thu leh Lawngtlai a ka va umphak mai an rin thute min hrilh a. Hlim tak in ka haw ve ta a, In ka thlen chuan ka chhungte chu thil awmdante ka han hrilh a, Lalpa min kohna chhang a Mire ram a ka damchhung a ama rawngbawl tur chuan ka inbuatsaih ṭan ve ta a.

Ka kal hma chuan ka ṭhian, ka rawngbawlpui te’n Mire ram a ka kal tur thu chu an lo hria a, min rawn thlah liam liam a, Kan Unit Ṭ.K.P te pawh in, “Missionary dil a kan ṭawngṭaina Lalpa’n min chhanna i ni e,” ti in min rawn thlah bawk a. Mimal leh pawl hming pawhin duhsakna thilpek te pawh min rawn pe nual bawk a. Chutia, ‘Missionary’min han tih te chuan ka zak lutuk chu ka hmulthite hi a ding sung sung hian ka hria a. Kei in a Missionary hming ka han pu ve chu phu lo ka inti in ka insit em em mai a, ka lawm bawk si.

Ka lo rin angin min hmusit hmel pawh ka hmu lova, min lawmpuitu zawk hlir an ni tih ka hria a, “Lalpa, a vamak ve, ka rin angin ka chungah a thleng lo tlat mai, mi thinlung hi i lo tih danglam sak vek a ni maw?” ka ti hial a. Ka rin loh leh ka beisei loh em em Salem Kohhran K.N.P te pawhin min rawn thlah in thilpek hlu tak takte min rawn pe a. Kan zaipawl, Bible Society zaipawl pawhin min rawn thlahin duhsakna thil pek ṭha tak tak min pe bawk a. Ka chung a Lalpa ṭhat zia hi chu mak ka vahan ti tak em!!! Lalpa fak a zai leh ṭawngṭai te chu nuam ka ti leh ta a. Lalpa min lainattu chu fakin awm rawh se. Ka kal hma niah chuan U Valtea nen ka thil mamawh turte lei in kan bazar a. Lalpa min hruai dante ka ngaihtuahin ka lawm veng veng ṭhin.

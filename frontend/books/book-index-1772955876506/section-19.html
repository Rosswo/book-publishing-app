BUNG 17

AN HUN PAWIMAWH LEH AN NI PAWIMAWHTE

Miria te hian hun pawimawh leh ni pawimawh an nei nual a, chung an hun pawimawh zinga atam zawk chu favang hun lai hian a ni deuh ber a, favang lai chuan ruai ṭheh a zing khawp mai. Kan sawi tawh ang khan Miriate hian huau huau hi an ngaina ve hle mai a, chung an thil tihpùiah chuan khaw khatah mi an tam em em lem lo bawk a, a khua a ruaiṭheh a ni deuh zel ṭhin.

Chung an ruai siamah pawh chuan dan leh hrai pawh an ngah thei ṭhin khawp mai. An dan leh hrai zawng zawng hi han ziak kim vek sen chi pawh a nih loh avangin a pawimawh zual chauh kan ziak lang dawn a ni.

Ruai an ṭheh hian ruai ei tura an mi sawmte, khua ami, in khat a pa ber emaw, khua a mite zawng zawng emaw chaw ei tura an sawmte an rawn thlen kim vek hma chu tumahin chaw an ei ngai lova, (Khaw dang dang tihdan erawh ain ang vek lem lo) inneih nikhua leh ruai ṭhenkhatah chuan a an ruai ṭheh dawn a zu in ho chu an zu in zawhte nghah a ngai ṭhin a, ṭhen khat chu chaw ei tur a an mi kohte an rawn tlen har a vanga kan nghah rei tawh a naklaiin zu in ho zu in zawh nghah a ngai leh ṭhin chu ril a lo ṭam tawh phei chuan a ninawm duh ṭhin khawp mai. Kan han ei theih meuh chuan kan chaw leh sa te chu a dai vek tawh ṭhin a, bawngsa phei chu a lo khal hman hial tawh ṭhin.

Chaw ei a an insawm hma hian inchhungah chaw leh sa chu ei mai theih turin an lo peih diam tawh ṭhin a vangin lo kil hmasatu tan chuan riltam tak chung a hma a chaw leh sa inchhawp, ei lawk theih si loh chuan rilṭàm a ti zual in a ninawm duh khawp mai. Chaw kan han ei theih tawh chuan intihsiak ang mai hian chaw ei hi an chakin an rang em em thung.

An hun pawimawh leh ni pawimawhte chu hengte hi an ni:

  1. Nupui pasal neih :

    2. Pakla purkung ni mang (Chhun lawi I).
    3. Pakla purkung ni mang (Chhun lawi II).
    4. Sutawng (Kutsuih).
    5. Nupui man pek.
    6. Numnâp (Nemngheh).
    7. Nuksak (Sullet)
    8. Thilchhawm phurh.
  2. Mithi sawngbawl dan :
    1. Awmlai thi.
    2. Sar thi.
    3. Raicheh.
    4. Hlamzuih.
    5. Ruang lam.
  3. Sa kah leh awh.
  4. Bawng/Sial chhun:
    1. Uar iak (Zan riak).
    2. Linglang (Lehlan).
  5. Chhechham a ṭhian/unau a insiam:
  6. Awi U Pa (Nu leh Pa chawimawina ruai).
  7. Chamleng (Chhungkhat inkan).
    1. Makpa in a Pu (A nupui pa) a kan na.
    2. Nuksak (Sullet).
    3. Taawi (Damlo kan)
  8. Fate beng verh.
  9. Chamung pawk pawi (Thlai thar ruai).
  10. Cha mawi (Buh thar lawman ruai).
  11. Changkran pawi (Kum thar ruai).
  12. Kua khang (Khaw hung/serh).

    2. Siadawng khang (Fur khaw hung/serh).
  13. Nupui pasal neih :

Nupui pasal neih hi an hun pui zingah chuan an ngaih pawimawh pawl a nih a vangin a tlinte chuan an tiropui thei hle ṭhin. Mihausa ṭhenkhat chuan an fapa naupangtĕ in nupui neih tir tepawh an awm a, chung naupang tĕ a nupui an neih tir ṭhenkhatte chu an nupui te’n an thlim mu ṭhin a (an nupuite chu nula puitling an ni ṭhin). He dan hi a ṭha lo khawp mai, achhan chu an fapa naupang tĕ chu an lo puitlin chuan an nupui chu hna an thawk hah nen; an lo lan upat tawh tawh ṭhin a vangin anmahni rualpui nupui neihsan ta te pawh an awm ṭhin reng a ni.

Bung hmasa lamah khan an nupui zawn dan chu kan sawi nual tawh a kimchang zawk deuh in sawi leh ta ila.

    1. Uar mang (Zan lawi):

Tlangval in nupui a zawn chuan a ṭhiante a hruai a, a nupui tura a duh a hmuh chuan atam zawk chu an ṭhiante’n an biak sak a, an mahni a biate pawh an awm bawk. Nula ṭhenkhat chuan amah nupui tura rawn beiseitu ber hmel pawh hmu lo an awm. Nula chuan pasal neih hun a inti ve tawh in pasal neih a lo rilruk ve tawh a nih chuan amah rawn beiseitu chu engang mi nge a nih a, an chhungkaw dinhmunte amah rawn betute hnenah chuan a lo zawt ang.

Chutia nupui atan a amah rawn beiseitu chanchin a hriat a, a ni pawhin pasal atan a lo duh ve a nih chuan, “In la rawn leng leh dawn nia,” a ti a, a ṭhi emaw, a ruakawm (kawnghren) emaw puan emaw, athil roh tak tlangval chu a pe anga, tlangval pawh in nula chu engemaw a pe ve bawk ang. Chutianga an inthlah liam chuan an va kal leh chuan tlangval chuan ṭhian engemaw zat hruaiin zanah an va ru chhuak tawh mai ṭhin a ni. Chu chu an nupui ruk dan a ni.

Tin, tlangval ṭhenkhat chu an nu leh pa te’n nupui an zawn sakte pawh an awm bawk. Chutia nu leh pa te’n an fapa nupui tur an zawn sak chuan, mo tura an duh nu leh pate hnenah chuan zu emaw, thingpui leh chhang emaw kengin an fapa nupui tur chu an va dil ṭhin. Nula nu leh pa te’n rem an lo tih chuan inhaul nan tlangval nu leh pa te chuan nula nu leh pa te chu bawng pakhat leh Tk 15 – 30 an pe ṭhin. Mahse, chu bawng an pek chu an fanu chu an fapa in nupui a an neih tawh hnuah an la let leh ṭhin. Chu chu ‘Dawngpün chia’ an ti.

Nu leh pa te hriatpui leh inbiak remna a innei nimahse, an chhungkaw khawsak a har sa deuh a, chhun a lawi an tlin lo a nih chuan zanah pawh an lawi mai ṭhin a, chu chu ‘Uar mang’ an ti. Tlangvalin nu leh pa te hriatpui leh inbiak remna a innei ni mahsela zanah a ṭhian ṭhahnem tak te nena a nupui an va hruai chu, “Cham lo masih” an ti a, nupui ru kan tih ang tho hi a ni.

Tlangvalin chutia nupui a ruk chuan in an thlen veleh a nupui leh a nupui rawn thlahtu nula pathum, pali te’nen chuan an in a kimma an tihah chuan an awmtir nghal a, khua a var ve leh an mo lawmna tur bawng emaw vawk emaw an talh nghal ṭhin. Nu leh pa inberem lova nupui an ruka a nupui lam chhung te’n an fanu/farnu rutu chu an duh loh chuan nula chhungte (ṭhenkhat) chuan an fanu chu an rawn um in an hruai haw leh ṭhin.

Mo neitu lamin mo lawmna ran an lo talh tawh hnu a an mo chu a chhungte lamin kimma (Pindan chhungril) a awm chu an rawn hruai haw leh a nih chuan mo lawm nan a an ran talh man zawng zawng chu hmeichhe chhung te’n an chawi vek tur a ni.

    1. Pakla purkung ni mang (Chhun lawi I):

Hetiang a inneihna hi nu leh pa te inbiak remna a nupui pasal neihna ropui ber a ni a, chuvangin chhunah an lawi ṭhin. Hetiang a inneihna hi an ti ropui hle ṭhin. Hetiang a inneihna hi a hautak em a vangin tunlai chuan hetiang a innei hi an awm meuhtawh lo.

Mipa lamte mo hruai tura an kal chuan an inpuahchah nasa hle ṭhin. Anupui turte khua amite ei khawp tur ar tam tak ken a ngaih a vangin mo hruai tura an mi sawmte chuan mo neitute lam ṭanpui nan ar pakhat tal chu an keng ve ṭhin. Ar tam tak, fei, diar, puanhlap leh zu te kengin a pui a nawi in a khua deuhthaw in mo hruai turin an kal ṭhin.

Mo turte khua an va thlen chuan an mo turte inah chuan an thleng khawm vek a, khaw chhung a nu ho chuan mo hruai tura rawn kalte ei atan chaw leh chawhmeh an thlen naah chuan an va dah ṭhin. Mikhual te’n chaw an ei kham vek tawh chuan an ar rawn ken chu khaw chhunga mite ei atan an puah nghal mawlh mawlh a, mo chhungte pawh chuan mo hruaitu te ei a tan vawk pa sutnghak chu an lo phet thlu ve nghal bawk ṭhin.

Mo hruai turte an thlen ni a ṭang chuan a khawi lam ve ve pawh ruai eitur siam buaiin an buai nghal mum mum mai a, ei tura an peih fel hnu chuan mo hruai tur, mipa lam chhungte chu chaw an ei hmasak tir ṭhin.

Hetiang a an inneihnaah hian nupa/chhungkhat a insiam tum-e ti lo chuan intih mualpho an tum tawn ngar ngar ni te hian a hriat a. Miria te hian anmahni hnam dan a ruai an ṭheh hian an pute leh an makpate chu sa bel khat a mi an ineiṭawm thiang lova, an pute lam chuan an makpate rawn ken arsa an ei a nga, an makpate lam chuan an pu te vawksa an ei vethung tur a ni.

An pu/makpate lamin chawhmeh an siam sak chu, “Kan pu/makpa te’n kan ei khawp chawhmeh pawh min hlui thei lo, an nep lutuk, kei ni chuan an ei sen loh kan hlui a nia,” ti a vuivai an duh a vangin chaw ei laite hian chaw ei siamsaktu te hriat tham si loh hian, “Chaw kha ei lovin a sa ringawt kha ei rawh u, chaw ei kham hma a sa ei zawh vek tur,” tiin chaw ei laiin an infuaih nasa thei ṭhin khawp mai. Mo chhungte lam chuan Arsa an ei a vangin a ei tam theih hle a, moneitu lam vethung chuan vawksa an ei a vangin vawk thau tak sa phei chu ei tam a har khawp mai. Hetiang a, anmahni Mire dan anga ruai an thehna sa hi “Chan ngar” an ti.

Hetiang inneihna ruaiah hian ṭhenkhat phei chu indawi an ching a, mo chhungte lamin moneitute lam dawi an ching zawk ṭhin. Chutianga inneihna ruaiṭheh remchanna a lo hmanga chu ruai ṭheh ve an hmelmate lo dawi ching pawh an awm bawk. Ruaiṭhehna a an indawi chuan an dawi a chuan a sa ei chu a kawchhungah sa hring hlar tak angin a awm anga, chu chu engtin emaw an luak chhuak lo a nih chuan a lo ṭhang lian zel anga, mihring kawchhungah chuan zungte a kaih a nga, a hmulte a lo awm a nga, chu mihring chu a thi mai tawh dawn a ni. Chutiang a kawchhung a lo awm chu “Chapang” an ti.

Ṭum khat pawh kan khaw nula khaw danga pasal nei kan thlah ṭumin kan haw hnu chuan nula pasal nei a pa chu a pum a na vak mai a, vanneih a siamin mi in an dawina sa hrang hlar tak mai chu a luak chhuak hlauh mai a, luak chhuak lo se chuan a ni pawh chu a thi maithei a ni.

Mo hruai tura va kalte chuan mo pu (a nu pa/nuṭa ‘tutma’ an ti) ei atan ahran in ar an talh sak a, an puah in an chhum sak vek a nga, chu chu mo pu chuan a mahin a ei zo vek tur a ni. Chu ar puah dan leh chum dan chuan awmzia a nei em em a, a puahtu leh a chhumtu te chuan an puah dan leh chum danah an tihsual a a ei tu turin a hriat chuan chu an arsa chhum chu an paih vek anga, adang atharin an chum ṭha leh tur a ni. Sawiselna tur engmah a awm tur a ni lo.

Hetia an makpa in a pu arsa ei tur a chhum lai hian ven ngun a ngai hle ṭhin a, mo chhungte lam chuan an makpa chu an tih mualpho theihna turin a rukin arsa belah chuan arhmul emaw lo thlak an tum ṭalh ṭhin a ni. Achang chuan mo neitu te lamin chaw an ei lai in mo chhungte lam chuan thukah hmarhcha ro an lo rawh a nga, mo hruaitute lam chu hahchhiau chuah chuah chungin hnap tawlh pur leh mittui tla pur chungin chaw an ei a ngai ṭhin a ni. Hetih lai hian an fiamthu a sang thei khawp mai.Mo hruaitu te’n mo hruai a an haw dawn chuan mo nu leh pa te chuan an vawkpa sutnghak puah sa, an makpate lamin apum an zawn haw tur an buatsaih a nga, chubakah vawk dang arpai a an pai haw tur an buatsaih leh bawk a nga, chu an vawk pa lian tak tual a an talh chu mo neitu te lamin mo in luhkapui a chan turin an luhkapui sang angreng tak, thing pum reh leihlawnah chuan tlangval chakin a pu chho ngei tur a ni. Tlangval chak mang lo chuan chutiang vawk lian chu put tum ve ngawt chi a ni lo.

Vawk a pum a zawn haw tur chu an puah zawh hnu in a pum chhungah chuan an khua an thlen hun a an channa tur chemte, chemte a bil hun a an tah hriam leh na tur lungtât lian tawk leh aṭām turte nen an dah a nga, a pum chu an ṭhui ping leh bawk ang. A zawnna tur raw pum lian el awl chu aputna lai chu an tichik hrup a nga, chu an tih chik hrupah chuan hmarhcha dendip tihhuh an chulh leh vek a nga, vawk puahsa chu azawnna a ṭanga chung lam a ke akhup a ṭanga chung lam lawr chho vawuhin an ṭawn ang a, chumi a ke lawrah chuan maupum, vawk hnung lam leh hma lam ke lawr chho-ah chuan an ṭawn lawr vawoh bawk ang.

An vawk zawn chu kal zawnga inthen nawlh nawlh thei turin an siam a nga, chu an vawk zawn chu phuai-in an chei buai bawk ṭhin. Vawk dang an talh pawh chu lian tak takin an chan a nga, arpaiah an dah a, chu arpâi pàina hrui chu tetakte fip deuhin hrui an hrual anga, chu an hrui hrualah leh vawksa pàina arpâi pang zawng zawngah chuan hmarhcha dendip an tih huh chu an tât leh vek bawk.

Chu vawk pum zawn tura an buatsaih chu mo neitu lam tlangval chak, ‘kei’ inti chin mi pahnihin luhkapui aṭang chuan inchhungah an zawn lut anga, zawn pahin chhuat lai a zupui hlum bel a an dah chu an khàwn a nga, chutih lai chuan ṭhenkhat chuan an vawk zawn ke chung lam a maupum tawng khat vel an tihlawrah chuan an vawk zawn chu inthên turin an lo kawih her sak a, (chutih hun lai chuan atu a te mah chu kawr ha awm hek suh) chutia an vawk zawn a in thên chuan an vawk zawnna maupum an koki zawna an ti chik hrup chuan an koki a ti pilh phung mai a, chu mi pilhah chuan an hmarhcha dendip tih huh an tah chu a lut a, tawrhchhel a ngai ṭhin khawp ang.

Chutia vawk zawntu te’n zupui an khàwn hnu chuan in a ṭang chuan vawk zawn chung a mo hruai a an haw dawn chuan an kaiṭen/sumhmun a ṭanga chhukna leihlawn sang angreng tak thingpum reh, an leihlawn dawh chu vawk pum puah sa zawn chung chuan harsa ti takin an zawh thla a, tual an thlen chuan mo chhungte lam; mo thlahtu te chuan, “Ka ke a na, ka kal thei lo,” ti in an lo tlu der ṭhin a, mo neitute lamin zu an pek hma chu an tho duh ṭhin lo va, chutiang chuan khaw chhung an kal pelh hma chu mo chhungte lam chuan zu an ngen reng ṭhin a, chutia an din laite chuan an vawkzawn inthên tur chuan mau lawrah chuan an lo kuai thên leh lawp ṭhin a. chutia an vawk zawn a thên chuan azawnna mau hriamin an koki a ti pilh a, chumi pilh a hmarhcha thak lut chu azawntu te chuan na an ti thei em em mai a. Khaw pelh hma zawng chu vawk zawntute chu inchhawk theih a ni bawk si lova, an na ti lutuk chu an ṭhen tlawk tlawk ṭhin a ni.

Vawksa arpâi a pàite pawh kha an vawksa pài a rih nen; apâina hrui chu te tak te a hrual fip tlat a ni bawk si a, bâkah apàina hrui leh arpâi pangah chuan hmarhcha tài rawt dip tih huh chu an tât vek bawk nen, an ni pawh an nuamsa chuang lo. Khaw hran a mi deuh vek nupui pasal a innei an ni in an khaw kar kawng a chhia in a chhuk chho thei bawk si nen, kawr ha bawk si lova ni sensa hnuaiah thil rit tak pai leh zawn chungin hmarhcha thak nen a inchiahpiah reng chunga chu tianga han kal nilen chu rapthlak tak hi a ni ringawt mai. Lamhla tak a mo hruaitu te chu thi lova ruangin in an thleng hi a ni ringawt ṭhin. Hetih lai hian an fiamthu a sang mah mah a ni. Fiam thu nge intih hreawm duh vang hrim hrim thih pawh hriat a har khawp mai.

In an thlen chuan an vawksa hawn chu an chan a, maipawl nen an chum a, mo rawn thlahtu te ei tur ar an talh ve bawk a, hlim takin khua a mi zawng zawngte nen Mo lawmna ruai chu an ei ho leh ṭhin.

Mo thlahtute an haw dawn chuan Mo leh Moneitu chu chhuat laiah an ṭhut tir a, mo thlahtu te zing a upate chuan an fanu mi in a mo tur chu thu ṭha tinreng an hrilh a; chutianga thu ṭha hrilh tur chuan upa, ṭawngkam thiam deuhte an hruai ṭhin. Hetiang hian an fate chu thu an hrilh ṭhin, “Hei, tunah chuan pasal i nei tawh a, i pasal nu leh pa te chu i nu leh pa an ni tawh a, kawng engkimah i zahin an thu i zawm ṭhin tur a ni.

I pasal a khawi lo vakchhuak a lo haw in tlaiah pawh a ni thei, zanlai leh khaw var lam pawh a ni thei, ṭha tak leh hlim takin i lo be ṭhin tur a ni. A ṭhiante pawh a rawn hruai thei a, a ṭhiante chu ṭha takin ei in i lo buatsaih sak in an mutna i lo siam sak ṭhin tur a ni,” tih te leh thu ṭha dang tam tak an hrilh bawk a ni. Mo neitu nu leh pa te hnenah pawh, “Hei, tunah chuan in fa a ni tawh a, hau a ngaih chuan lo hau in zilh angai chuan in lo zilh ṭhin dawn nia,” ti in an chah thlap bawk ṭhin.

Mo thlahtu tam zawkte an haw hnu chuan mo ṭhian, nulate chu ni thum, ni li vel an la cham hnufual ṭhin. Chutia mo ṭhiante an cham chhung zawng chuan mo neitu (Nupui neitu ber) chu in dangah a riak daih ṭhin a, khaw chhung tlangval leh khaw ṭhenawm (an khaw hnai deuh a mite) tlangvalte chuan chutia mo leh aṭhiante pindan chhungril a inkhung tlatte chu a chhun azan in an rim ṭhin a, chutia an rim lai chuan an mutpui theih theih mutpui chu an hnial lo bawk. (Hei hi anmahni ṭawngka ngei aṭang a ka hriat a ni)

Mire hmeichhiate hi an mo thar lai chuan an zakzum em em ṭhin a, hun rei tak chhung chu chaw kawpuar pawh in an ei ngam ṭhin lo. Miria te hian ‘Nupa’ tih hi ‘Ta far’ (Nuta leh Farnu) ti pawh in an sawi bawk ṭhin. Miria te hi atlangpuiin inngaizawng a innei ai chuan nupui pasal neih hun intih tawh a vang leh nupui pasal neih duh tawh a vanga innei an ni tlangpui a, chuvangin zan a inhruaite ṭhenkhat phei chuan anmahni thlahtu an hawn vek tawh hnuah an pasalte hmel hre chiang ve chauhte pawh an awm ṭhin. Miriate hian inneih an awlsam ang bawk hian ṭhenkhat chuan inṭhen pawh an awlsam leh hle bawk.

    1. Pakla purkung ni mang (Chhun lawi II):

Hei pawh hi a asawngbawl dan chu a chunga mi nen khian a inang renga, a danglamna chu vawk puahsa an zawn hi vawk pahnih a ni tur a ni. He inneih dan hi chu mi neinung tak takte chauh in an ti thei a ni, hetianga inneihna-ah hi chuan zu leh sa chu duhthusam in a tam a ni. Tunlaiah chuan mi an lo changkan deuh tawh a vangin chhun lawi pawh ni se hmarhcha thak a intih hreawm leh vawk puahsa zawn chu an ti tawh meuh lo.

    1. Sutawng (Kutsuih):

Miria te’n nupui pasal an inneih a kut an insuih dan chu mo neitu leh a chhungte zawng zawng ban ding lamah la dum emaw, la sen emaw, la var emaw an hreng vek anga, chu an la hrenah chuan an inneih lai a ramhuai hnena an inthawina vawk beng dinglam them an ṭawn tel vek tur a ni. Inneih tirtu hi an puithiam a ni a, puithiam a awm remchan loh chuan an khua a upate pawhin an inneih tir thei a ni.

Nupa tuak tharte an ban a la an hren lai hian buhfai, aieng nen a rawt dip an chalah an hnawih vek bawk tur a ni. Puithiam chuan zu hmuam chungin sawhthing a seh a nga, an sam a dawm in tlem tlem in a zu hmuam chu a phuh zel anga, “Sawksawrawk, a ngho thla de ang, a ngal hmul rawpui hung ang,” a ti bawk ang. Chutia a tih chhan chu ramhuai in an ni(nupa thar) chu hlau rawhse a tih vang a ni.

    1. Man hlan.

Miria te nupui man hi a tam hle a, abehbawm insensona tur pawh atam hle a ni. An inneih hian nupui man hi an pe nghal tlangpui a, mahse an man hi a tam tham deuh a vangin ṭhenkhat chuan an pe tlak nghal mai lova, ṭhenkhat phei chu rei tak man pe lote pawh an awm bawk. Mahse, nupui man hi a bat ral theih lova, mi a nupui a thihni thlenga a nupui man a pe tlak lo a nih chuan a nupui ruang awmlai ngei in a nupui nu leh pa emaw, a nupui nuṭa te’n emaw anupui man chu an rawn la anga, pek tur a nei lo a nih chuan a chipui te’n a nupui man an pek sak ṭhin a ni.

An nupui man a tan chuan hmanlai British hun lai a India pawisa Silver ngei kha an la hmang a, chu an nupui man sawi nan chuan, ‘Buksung’ an ti a, (nupui man tihna a ni) Buk sungah chuan Silver tangka 100 a ni a, heibakah hian ‘Chumüa’ Silver tangka 5 leh fei, a pu, (mo nu pa/nuṭa) an pe ang a, a pu chuan a makpa chu diar var tawng sawm a sei a khim tir ve ang. Tunlaiah chuan ‘Chumüa’ hi Silver pawisa sawm la te pawh an awm. Mo chu a bengverh a peh, Panchhia a nei, nau a lo chhiat tawh emaw chuan ‘Chumüa’ chu an la thiang lo a ni.

Miria te hian sârthi an sawngbawl dan a dang a, Sâr thi chu a thi a ni tih an hriat ve leh vawk an chhun hlum nghal a, chu an vawk chhunhlum chu ei lovin an paih nghal tur a ni. Ruang an hal emaw phum emaw hnu chuan vawk thisen a inthen thianghlimna neiin an inthawi leh ṭhin. Vawk a an inthawi hnu chuan sâr thi chhungte inah chuan tupawh in an ei in an in thiang tawh a ni.

An khua a an chipuite leh khaw dang a an chipui leh khaw danga mithi chhungte zinga pasal nei a awm emaw pawh ni se, an chhung/chipui zinga mi raicheh a thi a awm a ni tih an hriat chuan raicheh a thi chu an vui hma chu chaw chauh tihloh chu engmah an in in an ei thiang lo a ni.

Mithi chu vui tura an zawn chhuah chuan kawngkapuiah an zawn chhuak thiang lova, sâr thi angin an Kimma hnung lam bang satthla in chuta ṭang chuan ruang chu an zawn chhuak tur a ni. Mithi chu an vui hnu chuan vawkin an inthawi leh tur a ni.

    1. Rawmuak (Ruang lam) :

Hmanlai chuan Miriate hian ruang an lam hual ṭhin a, chu chu ‘Rawmuak’ an ti. Ruang an lam hual hun chhung hi zan hnih emaw, zan thum emaw a ni ṭhin. Ruang chu ding lam zawngin an lam hual a, hetia ruang an lam hual lai hian kawngka bul aṭangin mi pakhat in khuang a lo beng ṭhin.

Ruang lam hualtu te chuan lam kual pah chuan thil tum rik an nei a, chung an rimawi tumte chu Khuang, Darmang, Tu (tuithawl hmanga siam), te a ni. Ruang chu kuanga an dah hma in kuang chu an inthawihna vawk thisen an thlawr a, atah pawh an tât bawk ṭhin, chumi hnu in kuangah an dah a, kuang chu chhuatlaiah an dah a chu chu an lam hual ṭhin a ni.

Arpa an talh a, engmah pawlh lovin an chhum a, a sa tlemte chu mithi chu an pe ṭhin. Mithi chaw an pek chu mithi lu zawnah an dah ṭhin. Mithi tan vawk an talh a, chumi an vawk tal hrang khat chu sumhmunah an paih a, mithi chu nithum chhung nitin chaw vawi thum an pe ṭhin. Mithi chu chaw an pek a piangin vawk pakhat leh arpa pakhat an talh ziah ṭhin. Mahse, ṭhenkhat chuan mithi chaw an pek a piangin vawk leh arpa chu an talh zel thei lova, chuvangin an chhumsa chu mithi chaw an pek dawn a piangin an lum mai ṭhin. Mithi chu chaw an pek dawn a piangin an lam hual hmasa ṭhin.

  1. Bawng/Sial chhun :

Bawng/sial chhun hi Miriate ruaiah chuan a ropui ber a ni a, a hautak hle bawk. Bawng an chhun nachhan hi an kût atan ni lovin damlo ramhuai in a tih dam a tan a ramhuai hnen a an inthawihna a ni.

An dam loh chuan ramhuai puithian emaw, aien thiam emaw hnenah an kal a, puithiam/aientu chuan an dam theih naturin bawng/sial//lawi etc… a inthawih tura a lo tih avang a hetiang a inthawih hi an ni. Ṭumkhatah bawng pahnih an chhun chang pawh a awm ṭhin. An puithiam in ai a lo en dan chu chi hnih a awm a, pakhat chu ‘Hawra’ an ti a, pakhat chu, ‘Howngpui’ an ti.

Hawra a ai an en dan chu; Darmang a an vuakna lai, a tle lai takah hian puithiam chuan tel a hnawih a, chumi a tel hnawih a en tur chuan midangin an lo hmuhpui theih loh nan puan in a inkhuh tlat ṭhin a ni. A en dan dang leh chu aien thiam chuan aklutzungpui tinah tel a hnawih a, chumi a kutzungpui a tel a hnawihah chuan ai chu a en ṭhin a ni. “Midang chuan lo en ve reng mahse an hmu ve thei lo a ni,” an ti. Ai entu chuan a duh duh sawi mahse midangin an hmuhpui vet lat si loh a vangin aientu sawi ang a piang chu an ring em em zel mai a ni.

Howngpui hmang a ai en dan chu lasai ngul hmangin a ni a. Aientu chuan lasai ngul chu a chelh a, ahan chhamphual a, eng ran hmangin nge an inthawina tur, ran hming ahan chham fuh chuan lasai ngul chu a lo inthên vak mai a, achang phei chuan aientu ban pawh chu a khur dur dur hial ṭhin.

Hetiang a bawng, sial leh lawite an chhun hi an inthawina a nih rualin an hausdakna lan tirna pawh a ni tel bawk. Hetih hun lai hian ran lian bawnga an inthawi bâkah ran te vawk, ui leh ar te pawh inthawina tesepah an hmang tam khawp mai.     1. Uariak (Zanriak):

Aientu in bawng puitlin pakhat emaw, pahnih emaw a inthawi tura aien tirtu, damlo chhungte chu a lo hrilh chuan chu inthawina hautak tak mai a inthawih thei tur chuan an inbuatsaih nghal ang a, a hautak deuh a vangin ṭhenkhat chuan bawng chu an chhun nghal mai thei lova, mahse ramhuai hnenah puithiam kaltlanga bawng chhun tura an lo intiam tawh a vangin damlo chu a dam daih tawh hnuah pawh bawng chu an chhun ngei ṭhin.

Bawng an chhun dawn chuan hmun dang danga awm an chhungte pawh an inchah khawm vek ṭhin. Chung; hmun dang aṭang a rawn kal, an chhungte chuan an inkungkaih dan a zirin; aṭhen in vawksa an rawn keng a, a ṭhen in ar an rawn pai far bawk ṭhin. Chung an mikhualte chu an haw lamah pawh an inkungkaih dan a zirin aṭhen in bawngsa, vawksa leh ar te bawng chhuntu te chuan an pai haw tir leh vek ṭhin.Bawng an chhun hma chuan an bawng chhun tur dahna ‘Riiawng krowng’ an tih chu an siam a, bawng leng tawk chiah in thing leh mau in an hung a, bawng chu bawk thei lo turin an siam bawk a. Chutah chuan bawng thlunna tur thing mal tiat vel an phun a, bawng pakhatah thing pakhat zel an phun a, chutiang thing an phun chu ‘Limpu’ an ti a, ‘Turtut’ ti pawhin an sawi bawk. ‘Turtut’ tih chu, “Abul tihngheh” tihna a ni.

Limpu bulah chuan lung an phun a, chu an lungphun chu ‘Huaâi’ an ti. Chu lung an phun chu an bawngchhun hriat rengna lung phun a ni. An lungphun hi a lian hran lova, engmah an ziak hran lo bawk. An bawng thlunna chung chu an dawh duai a, ṭhenkhat chuan achhawngin an dawh bawk. Chumi an dawh chu kurkum leh ziahphuai rawng hnawihin achung lamah thing suih zum rawng sen leh a pawl a hnawih an ti lawr fur bawk. An thil dawh chu an chei nasa thei hle ṭhin. Chu an thil dawh chu ‘Riiang krowng’ an ti. Chutiang a an bawng chhunna tur an siam hna chu, ‘Sawtna pumpau sip’ an ti.

An bawng chhun tur an lam hualna tur sir velah chuan zupui bel (hlum bel) hunna tur an siam thluah a, an zupui bel hunna tur pawh chu an thiam ang tawkin mau, changel leh thangṭung hnah leh ziahphuai rawng hnawih in an chei bawk. Zu bel khat zel chu a viltu an awm zel a, chuta zu chu nula leh tlangvalin an khàwn deuh ber a, upa-te leh a duh apiangin an khàwn thei tho bawk a ni.

An bawng chhun tur chu a chhuntu te in lam hawi zawngin an thlung ṭhin. An bawng chhun tur chu chutia mi zawng zawng hmuh theih a khawlai a an hung chhung a an thlung chu a zak ang tih an hlauh a vangin a zah loh nan ti in puanpui tharin an khuh ṭhin. Bawng chhuntu te sumhmun zawn dithlifarfem tlakna-ah chuan rawpui sei tak a ler kuai thla nguih tham hi an phun a, a ler uai thla-ah chuan vapual bu lem hnanga an siam chu an khai a, a kul laiah takah chuan vapual thlawklai lem hnanga an siam chu an dah bawk a, chu rua chu ziahphuai rawng a chuan an chei buai bawk a ni.

Bawng an chhun lai a hetiang a rua sang tak a an phun, an chei buai mai bawk hi ‘Dawnglu kauma’ an ti. Hetiang a Miria te’n bawng an chhun a pianga Dawnglu kauma an siam ziah na chhan chu an thawnthuah; Hmanlai hian pa pakhat hian fapa pahnih a nei a, a nupui chu a thi a, a nupui thih hnu chuan nupui dang a nei leh a, chu a nupui thar chuan a pasal fate pahnih chu a tiduhdah thei em em mai a. Apasal chu a fate pahnih that turin a ti a, mahse an pa chuan afate thah mai chu ṭha a tih loh a vangin ramhnuai hmun hla takah a kalpui a.

Chutah chuan hnuchhiah in a hawn san tadaih mai a, Afate pahnih chu ramhnuaiah chuan rilṭam in an vak an vak ta mai a. Nakinah chuan thing kung a vapual bu hi an hmu a, a u zawk chu vapual tui chu lak tumin thingkungah chuan a lawn a, vapual tui chu a la a, pakhat chu a ei a, pakhat chu a nau tan ken thlak sak a tum a, mahse a kut pahnih chu thingkung aṭang a chhuk nan chuan a hman a ngaih a vangin vapual tui chu a hmuam a.

Thingkung aṭang a chhuk tum a a ṭanna lamah a vapual tui hmuam chu a dawlh palh ta daih mai a, chutia vapual tui a dawlh chuan a u chu vapualah a chang dawn ta mai a. Vapual a achan dawn chuan a nau hnenah chuan tihian thu a chah a, “Ka nau, vapual tui kha ka dawlh palh hlauh mai a, chuvang chuan vapualah ka chang dawn tlat mai, vapual a ka chan chuan ka thlawhna apiangah min zui zel la khaw chhungah ka hruai lut ang che.

Nakinah i lo hausa hle ang a, chutih hunah chuan bawng te i la chhun a nga, bawng i chhun hunah chuan sumhmun lam dithlifarfem bulah rua sang tak i phun a nga, hnangin vapual lem i siam a nga, a bu lem nen, min hriat reng nan raw lerah chuan i lo dah ṭhin dawn nia,” ti a a lo chah a vangin a u hriat reng nan a u in a chah chu a dah ta zel a, chu chu tun thleng hian an la ching chhunzawm ta zel a ni.

Bawng chhuntu te hian an bawng chhun ruaiah chuan anmahni leh ramhuai inkar a palai, bawng pakhatah mi pahnih zel an nei tur a ni a. Inthawitu puithiam hran a awm bawk ṭhin. Chutiang a palai chu ‘Riamang’ an ti a. Puithiam chu ‘Uamow’ an ti. Palaite chuan puithiamte chu an pui ṭhin. Chung an palaite chuan bawng chhunna a lo kal mikhualte chu an lo chiauau in zu a awm leh awm loh te an ngaihven reng tur a ni. Bawng chhuntu te chu an buai em ṭhin a vangin chung palai ho te chuan a ṭul ang angin bawng chhuntu te chhung chu an lo pui ṭhin, an ṭangkai thei khawp mai.

Bawng chhuntu te chhung leh ramhuai inkar a palaite chuan bawng chhuntu te in an lo thlen aṭangin bawng chhuntu te chhungkaw zing a mi, mi pahnih nen chawhmeh, antui, hrui chi reng reng, entirnan, mai, behlawi etc…. te chu an ei thiang lova, rakzu pawh an in ve tur a ni lo. Inthawina sa pawh an ei ve tur a ni lo bawk. Kuva pawh an ei thiang lo.(panhnah hi hrui nei chi a nih vang a ni). Athur chi reng reng an ei tur a ni lo bawk. Chutiang chuan ni sarih an in serh tur a ni.

Bawng chhuntu te in chhuat laiah chuan zupui bel hnih an hung reng a, a viltu chuan a ṭhut chilh chawt mai ṭhin. Chu an zu chu ‘Pawitang iu’ an ti. Tar leh upate chuan dawhkan hniamte “Pawitang” an tihah chuan ti ti pah in zu an in reng ṭhin a.

Zan dar kua velah chuan puithiamte leh upate chu an bawng chhun tur chu an lam hual ṭhin a. Lam tura in a ṭanga an chhuah hma chuan zupui an khàwn phawt a, chu chu “Tarowng iu” an ti. Chumi hnu chuan Puithiam leh upate lam hun a an tum bik ṭhin; an rimawi tum chung chuan tual zawl a bawng an chhun tur an thlunna lam chu an pan ṭhin.

An lam ṭan dawn chuan pawn a zupui an dah chu bawng chhun tute chhungkua a ṭangin an dâwt a, lem lovin an hmuam a, chumi bul a hnah a an dah, buhfai leh aiduchhe zailep chu la in an sumhmun dithlifarfem bul a raw sang tak an phunna Dawnglu kauma-ah chuan an zu hmuam chu phuh in an thil lak chu an va dah hmasa te te a, chumi hnu chuan chutiang bawk chuan an bawng chhun tur chu an zu hmuam chu phuh in buhfai leh aiduchhe zailep chu bawng bulah chuan an va dah leh a, chumi hnu chuan an bawng chhun tur chu an lam hual ṭhin.

Tar leh upate’n an bawng chhun tur an lam hual

Hetia tar leh upa leh pavalai te’n an bawng chhun tur an lam hual hian pitarte tihloh chuan an tumphung chu an keng deuh vek ṭhin. Ṭhenkhat chuan zu um te pawh an keng bawk. Nau paw pah a lam lawp lawp tawkte pawh an awm bawk. An lam dan chu a sawtin an sawt deuh awoh awoh a. Lam pah chuan an tum phung chu an tum ṭhin. Hetia upa te’n bawng an lam hual lai a an hla sak hi tunlaiah chuan upate pawh thiam an awm tawh loh a vangin a thluk ringawtin an sa mai ṭhin.

Athluk a an sak dan chu “Haw he ü, haw ü, haw he haw ü haw ü,” ti in an sa mai a, a hla thu hi bung dangah kan tar lang ang. Anmahni pawhin nuam an ti thei ṭhin khawp mai, an ke per tan tan pah in, “Anuam lutuk, lei pawhin min dawl dawn hleinem le,” te an ti vel ṭhin. Hetia upate an lam lai hian a khat tawkin darkhuang an lo tum ri hluah hluah ṭhin. Upate chuan an bawng chhun tur chu vawi riat an hel tur a ni.

Hetia ṭhalaite an lam hian mipa te’n rimawi (Plung) an tum a, hmalam pan a sawt ouh ouh pah in an lam a, hmeichhiate chu mipate hmachhawn zawng in Themthainu ang a hnungtawlh pah zelin an lam a, Mizo buhchil ang deuh a sawt chungin an hnungtawlh ṭhin.

An nula lam erawh han en mai chuan lam dan chi dang em em hmuh tur a awm hran in a lang lo.

Hetia an lam hian ṭhalai rawn lam te chu khaw khat mai nilovin khaw dang dangte pawh an rawn lam ve ṭhin a, khaw neitu te an lam hmasa ber a chumi hnu chuan khaw dang aṭang a rawn kalte chu an lam chhuak ve ṭhin. Pawl khat an lam chuan an rimawi chu a ri a mawi in a ropui em em mai a, mahse khaw dang dang an rawn lam tawh chuan an rimawi (Plung) ri chuan mumal pawh anei thei tawh lova, a ri nuak nuak tawh mai ṭhin.

Chu Lawilo dawrpui a an bazar dan chu; nula te’n tlangval an duh a piang an bânah emaw an kawrah emaw an man a, dawr a an thil zawrh, chhang, sabawn sahriak leh thil dangte an lei tir a, ṭhenkhat chuan an duh duh an la a, chumi man chu tlangvalin an pek a ngai ṭhin a ni. Tlangval ṭhenkhat te chuan an leisak mai duh ṭhin loh a vangin nula in a thil duh leitir tura an inphihna lamah mipa ṭhenkhat chu kawrkilhte a tla phung a, an kawr ipte te a peh phung ṭhin. Hetih hun lai hian nu leh pa te pawh an bang bik chuang lo. Tlangval hmelṭha deuhte phei chuan mantu an ngah thei hle ṭhin.

Tlangvalte chuan hetih hun lai a nulate’n an man hun a an thil ngen leisak theihloh hlau in hun engemaw ti a ṭang khan pawisa an lo khawl ve ṭhin a ni. Anmahni sawi da nin nulate tan a an insen-na chu a thlawn a ni vek bik lo va, a hmun ah bill an la thei ṭhin a, bill an hmu mai lo a nih pawh in, chung an bill lak loh a te hnenah chuan, “Chawfun kan in dah lawk a ni mai,” an ti a, chu vangin chutih hun lai a hmeichhe lak a sum sen chu an ui bik lo a ni. Hetiang a hmeichhia in mipa thil a lei tir hi hetiang ruaiah chauh nilovin ruai puitham deuh leh sakhaw thil a an puipunna ah pawh an ching tho a ni. Khawvar zu an in zawh hnu chuan zan a upa hovin an bawng chhun tur an lam hual ang khan an bawng chhung tur chu upa hovin vawi riat an lamhual leh a, chumi hnu in nula leh tlangval te’n an rimawi nen chuan an lam hual leh ṭhin. Mizo te’n, “Ṭhian sauitan” kan tih ang deuhin nghet tak a ṭhian leh unau insiam dan an nei a, an ni chu chhechham in ṭhian/unau-ah an insiam ṭhin. Hetiang a ṭhian/unau a insiamte chu an inṭhian/unau dan a nghet em em ṭhin a ni. Ṭhian/unau a insiamte chauh nilovin an chipuite ve ve thleng pawhin nupui pasalah an innei thiang tawh lo a ni. Innei lo awm pawh ni se an pawt ṭhen leh ngei nghei ṭhin, inneih luih chu thihna emaw, chhungkaw din a tluan lohna ah an ngai a ni.

Chhehham a ṭhian/unau a an insiam dan chu he tiang hian a ni: Ṭhian/unau a siamtu zawk chuan bawng a talh a, bawng kăl, apumpui, arilpui leh arilfang, achuap, athin leh a sa tlem chu thil dang engmah telh lovin a chhum a, ṭhian/unau a asiam leh ruai ṭheh tura a in a awm zawng zawngte nen chuan zupui bel khat a mi an khàwn tur in zupui bel chu chhuat laiah a dah ang a. Chumi hnu chuan ṭhian/unau a a siam chu a ko anga, a hnenah chuan, “He zupui hi ṭhian/unau a kan insiamna zupui a ni a, nang han khàwn hmasa rawh le,” a ti a nga, a ṭhian/unau a a siam chu a khawn hmasak tir ang.

Chutia a ṭhian/unai in zupui a khawn chhung chuan chu a sa chhum chu chaw nen a hlawm ang a, aṭhian/unau in zupui chu a khawn zawh chuan chaw leh sa a hlawm chu a ṭhian/unau chu a barh ang a, barh pah in, “Hei ṭhian/unauah ka siam ta che a, thing a ka tlak chuan ka tlakna thing chu i kit a nga, tui a ka tlak chuan ka tlakna tui chu i theh kang vek tur a ni. Mei a ka kan chuan ka kanna mei chu i leih mit a nga, ka chanchin a chhia a ṭha, mi in an sawi i hriat chuan min hrilh nghal zel tur a ni. Chutia i tih loh chuan he ka chaw barh che hi i tan thihna turah chang rawh se,” a ti ṭhin. Chutia a tih zawh chuan a ṭhian/unau a asiam chuan chutiang bawk chuan a ti ve leh tur a ni.

Ṭhian/unau a siamtu chuan a ṭhian/unau a asiam nen a an inhriat reng nan Silver tangka 30 a ṭhian/unau a a siam chu a pe ang a, a bawng talh sa chu hmun thum a ṭhen a hmun hnih a pe bawk tur a ni. Chu a sa pek chu, angumruh, amei tel in, a bawp pahnih leh a dar pakhat, a ir leh a vun ṭhenkhat a pe tel bawk tur a ni.

Ṭhian/unau a asiam chuan ṭhian/unau a siamtu chu abawp pakhat chu a pe let ve leh tur a ni. Ṭhian/unau a siamtu chuan ar pakhat a talh leh a nga ṭhian/unau a asiam nen chuan hmun khatah chawhlui an kil dun tur a ni. Chuta ṭang chuan ṭhian/unau an nih tawh a vangin an chipuite ve ve chu nupui pasalah an innei thei tawh lovang.

Ṭhian/unau a asiàm chuan engtik hunah emaw ṭhian/unau a siamtu in a tih ang chiah chuan a ti ve leh ngei tur a ni. Mahse, ṭhian/unau a siam hmasatu ai in silveer tangka khat in a ṭhian/unau chu a pe tam zawk tur a ni. Chutiang a a tih ve hma a a lo thi a nih pawh in a fapa talin chutiang chuan a ti ve tur a ni.

  1. Awi U Pa (Nu leh Pa chawimawina).

Miriate hian an nu leh pa te dam lai ngei in anmahni hring a an lo enkawl puitlin a vanga lawmthu sawina leh an nu leh pa te chawimawina; nu leh pa hmangaihna hria leh a tlinte chuan sial pahnih leh bawng leh vawkte talhin ruai ropui tak an buatsaih ṭhin.

Chutah chuan hmun danga an chhung khat leh unaute leh an ṭhian ṭhate an chah khawm chiam ṭhin. Chung an michahte chuan achahtu te pek tur ar leh fei an keng vek a, an va kalna tur kawtchhuah a an thleng tawh a ni tih hriat nan halpuah hal tur an keng tel bawk ṭhin. Chumi ruai a an mi sawmte chuan khua an rawn thlen dawn in kawtchhuah an rawn thleng tawh a ni tih hriat nan an halpuah rawn ken chu an hal a, ṭhenkhat chuan silaite pawh an kap ri dum dum bawk ṭhin.

Kawtchhuahah chuan an ar rawn ken hmul te chu an pawt thla nuai a nga, a sawmtu te lam chuan an mi sawmte an rawn thleng tawh tih an hriat chuan a hmuak turin zu um leh diar var tawng nga ai tawi lo nen an lo hmuak a nga, kawtchhuah an thlen chuan halpuah an hal a nga, mikhualte chu zu an in tir a, an diar ken chu an khim tir bawk ṭhin.

Nu leh pa chawimawitu chuan dawhkan hniamte ‘pawitang’ an tihah a nu leh pa chu chawhlui a buatsaih sak ang a, a nu leh pa te ṭhutna turah chuan an puanpui thar hlak a phah sak bawk ang. A nu leh pa te ei tur chuan sa chi hrang hrang a hlui a nga, a nu leh pa chu puan thar leh pawisa te a pe a nga, a pa chu diarvar a khim tir bawk ang.

A nu leh pa in chaw an ei dawn chuan an kut a sil sak bawk ang. A nu leh pa te chu zu no hnih, no thum in a thlit sak bawk ang. Zu no a nu leh pa te pek pah chuan, “Ka Nu, ka Pa, ka sèn têt lai aṭangin min enkawl a, ka zun, ka ek zawng zawng thianfai in min enkawl a, ka dam lohin ka damna turin theihtawp inchhuah a, tunah chuan hei, ka lo puitling ve ta a, hetiang a ka lo puitlin theihna chhan chu nangmahni vang liau liau a ni. In thin ka lo tihrim leh in pawi ka lo sawi te min ngaidam ula, in damchhung chuan ka theih ang angin ka ṭanpui reng ang che u,” an ti ṭhin. Hetiang a an nu leh pa te an ti lai han hmuh hian keini Mizo te hian kan unau Miriate hi hemi kawngah hi chuan kan tluk lo hle in ka hria, a ropui in hnuk a ulh hial zawk ṭhin a ni.

Chutia a nu leh pa te chaw ei tura an ṭhutna a an ṭhut lai chuan midang chuan nu leh pa te chu zu tlem te te in an thlit sak in an lo pe ṭheuh bawk a, chung lai vel a awmte pawh chuan zu an inthlit in induhsak nan zu an in intir liam liam ṭhin.

A nu leh pa chawimawitu chuan a mikhual sawm te chu a ran talh sa chu ṭhahnem tak a hawn tir ṭhin. Chhechham kher lovin chung a ran talh sa te hmang chuan ṭhin te pawh a siam bawk ṭhin. Chu ruaiah chuan sial sa, bawng sa an ei nasat theih em a vangin “Bawng sa ei ruai ‘Chia nga chak pawi’ ” an ti bawk.

  1. Chamleng.
    1. Chhungkhat inkan : “Chamleng” an tih hi ahuhova sa nen a chhungkhat inkan a ni a, Hetiang hi tunhma chuan Mizo zingah pawh a awm thu Mizo upa pakhat in min hrilh a, mahse ka phak tawk a Mizo History ka zirna leh lehkhabu hrang hrang ka chhiar tawhah chuan hetiang inziakna engmah ka la hmu lo a ni.

Miria te’n hetiang a ruai nen meuh va chhungkhat an inkan ṭhin hi an mâkpa te’n an puzawn (nupui pa) te lam an kan na a ni tlangpui. An nuṭa te’n an farnu hetiang a an kan chang leh an farnu te’n an nuṭate hetiang a an kan chang pawh a awm ve bawk. Hetiang a chhungkhat an inkan hun hi favang awllen lai a ni tlangpui. Ahautak ṭhin em a vangin an chhungte an va kan turte chu thâwtlâwk hmasa lo chuan hetiang hian an kan mai mai ngai lo.

An va kan turte an va thâwtlâwk a, anni lam pawh an lo inpeih tawh a nih chuan a puzawnte kan tur chuan an khua a mi (achipuite) atam theiang ber; a puzawn te a kan na a kalpui turin a sawm a nga, ava kan tur a puzawn te khaw ei khawp tur ar, arpāi in an pài a nga, a puzawnte khaw lamah chuan apui a nawi in an va kal a.

A puzawnte khua an va thlen chuan an ar ken chu a puzawnte khaw ei turin an puah nghal char char a, apuzawnte lam pawh chuan an makpate, anmahni rawn tlawh a rawn kalte zawng zawng ei tur vawk an talh ve nghal a nga, ei theih tur in a chhum ve nghal char char bawk a, mahse an thlen hlim chuan khua a nu ho chuan an mikhualte ei tur chu mikhual thlen khawmna-ah chuan an neih ang ang chaw leh chawhmeh an va dah khawm ṭhin.

Hetia an chhungkhatte tlawh (Cham leng) a an ruai buatsaih dan chu nupui pasal an inneih lai a an ruai siam dan nen a inang deuh a, mahse hetih hunlai hi chuan an nupui pasal neih lai ang chuan dan a tam lo vethung, hetia an mâkpa te leh an puzawnte an ruai sa ei a inang lo hi chhungkhat inkungkaih dan hriatna-ah an ngai a, an ni chuan “Chàn ngar” an ti. Heta an len hian an puzawnte khuaah chuan nikhat an cham a, chutia an cham chhung chuan hlim takin zu te in hovin upa hote chu zan rei tak tak thleng an ti ti ho ṭhin.

An mâkpate an haw dawn chuan an mâkpa chuan a puzawn chu ar a talh leh ang a, chu a arsa chhumah chuan engmah a telh tur a ni lo. An haw dawn zan chuan an puzawnte leh an mâkpate chu hmunkhatah an ṭhu hova hlim takin zu an in ho ṭhin. A puzawn chuan an mâkpa leh an mâkpa lam a upa rawn kalte chu diar var an khim tir ṭhin a, an mâkpa te haw dawn tukah chuan an puzawnte lam chuan an mâkpate hawn tur vawk an talh a, vawksa chu lian pui puiin an chan a, arpāi in an pài haw tir ṭhin, khua an thlen hun a a khua a ei khawp tura an hawn tir a ni.

Keini Mizo te pawh hian kan unau Miriate ang em hian chuan ti ve lem lo mah ila an tih dan hi entawn ve atan a ṭha khawp mai. Vawikhat chu ka pate unau zing a upa ber, Ka Pa Hluna chuan, “Kan tu leh fate pawh an inhre lo mai dawn, hmun hran hranah kan awm a, keini upahote pawhin kan tu leh fate pawh kan hre tawh lova, chhungkhat chaw ei hova in hriat tawn kan ngai e,” ti in Aizawl a ṭangin an chhungkua in Hnahthialah an rawn kal a.

Vawksa, Bawngsa leh sa dang pawh an lei ṭeuh a, Hnahthial veng hrang hrang a kan chhungte inko khawm in kan inah chawhlui kan kil hova, chutia kan chhungkhatte kan han in hmuh khawm chuan hmun hrang hrang a kan chhungkhatte, keini naupangte lam chu kan lo inhre lo hle mai a, upa ho lahin keini naupangte chu tu fate nge kan nih lah min hre bawk hek lo.

Chutia chaw kan ei ho aṭang chuan chhungkhat kan ni tih pawh lo inhre miah lo chu chhung khat kan ni tih leh kan inkungkaih dante kan han inhria chu nuam kan ti in kan hlim tlang khawp mai. khatiang khan a khattawkin ti thei ṭhin ila chuan chhungkhat kan inkungkaih dan a ṭha reng thei dawn a ni.

Kha mi tum a chhungkhat kan inhriat bâk kha chu chaw kan ei ho leh theih tawh loh a vang leh kan hnathawhte a dan vangin hmun hran hranah kan lo awm darh bawk nen, kan tu leh fate chu an inhlat tulh tulh tawh a, chhung khat kan nih pawh kan inhre tawh lo mai dawn ni in a lang a, a thei chuan hetiang a, chhung lehkhatte chaw ei ho hi nei thei ṭhin ila a va ṭha dawn em. Tunlaiah chhungkaw khawsakte a lo in ang lovin rethei leh hausa inkar a hla tulh tulh a, chhungkhat khawsak harsa zawk leh khawsa thei zawkte inkar a zau tulh tulh mai thei dawn a ni.

Kan unau Miriate ang hian chhungkhat inkan ve ṭhin ila, chawhluite kil ho ṭhin ila chhungkhat kan inkungkaihna a lo nghet zel a nga, chhungkhat a khawsa thei leh khawsak harsa zawkte pawh kan inkungkaihna a lo ṭha in atheite’n kan chhungkhat ṭanpui ngai zawkte pawh hmangaihna nen kan inṭanpui thei tawh in chhungkhat chu hmangaihna in kan insuih zawm reng thei dawn a ni.

    1. Damlo kan : Hei hi chu in dang, khaw dang a an nu leh pa emaw, an pi leh pu te damlo na zual deuh emaw, na rei takte emaw an inkanna a ni. Damlo kan nan chuan ar an keng ṭhin a, ar keng kher lo pawh a inkàn pawh an awm bawk. Damlo zual, dam chhuak tur a an rin lohte an kan chuan ar an keng ngei ṭhin. An damlo kan chu na zual hle pawh ni se, an ngaihsak a, an hmangaih a ni tih lantir nan an ar ken chu talh in damlo chu tlem tal an ei tir ṭhin. A haw dawn in damlo kantu chu engmah hawn tir kher a ngai lo. Nu leh pa emaw Pi leh pute emaw damlo zual tak, dam chhuak tawh lo tura an ngaih ar keng a an kan chu “Taawi” an ti.
  1. Beng verh : Mipa emaw hmeichhia emaw pawh ni se an fate beng verh hi an hnam kalphung a nih avangin an ngaipawimawh em em mai a. An fapa upa ber beng verh lai phei chuan ran an talh ngei ngei ṭhin. An fanute leh an fapa naupang lamte chu an beng verh na ran talh tur an neih loh chuan mifate bengverh ran talh denchhenin an beng an lo verh ve mai ṭhin.

Fate beng verh hun hi kum bithliah hun a awm hran lova, duhtawk a an kal theih tawh a ṭangin a remchan hunah an beng chu an verh mai ṭhin. An beng verhna hmanrua chu; hmawlh an suih zum a, chu an hmawlh suih zum chuan beng chu an verh a, chu an beng verhna hmawlh chu beh nghal mai theih turin an ulhtliah na tur an rek nghal thlap ṭhin. Beng an verhna lai turah chuan bal zailep leh buhfai an bel hmasa a, a verhtu chuan eng eng emaw a han chhamphual a, chumi hnu chuan beng chu an verh ṭhin a ni.

  1. Chamung pawk pawi (Buh thar hlan kût): Hei hi an buh thar ranhuai hnen a an hlanna kût a ni. An lo aṭangin buhvui pakhat emaw, pahnih, pathum emaw an rawn hawn a, chu chu an kang rova, chumi an kan ro sa chu an inthawina sa nen tualah an theh darh a, zu pawh tualah chuan an theh bawk ṭhin. Chutia an tih chuan ramhuai, lei chunga thuneitu (Krawngma namma nam) peah an inngai a ni. Chutiang bawk chuan an inthawina sa leh an buh kan ro chu van lamah an vawrh leh a, chu chu boruak chung a thuneitu ramhuai (Malimala nam) an tih chu peah an inngai a ni.

Miriate hian van boruak leh ruahte hian huai neiah an ngai a, chungte pual chuan inthawihna an hlan ṭhin. Tin, lei lam malsawmna pe theitu ramhuai pawh awmah an ngai a, tuifinriat leh lungpuite, thing lian pui puite pawh hian huai an nei vekah an ngai bawk. Chungte tan pawh chuan inthawina an hlan vek ṭhin. An nun hi ramhuai hlauhna leh hlauh tlawnna hian a khat a ni ber mai.

  1. Chamawi (Buh thar lawmna kût) : He kût hi buh an chil zawh a an inthawihna a ni a, lo lam a an thil serh tawpna a ni bawk. An lo a buh in an phurh thlen zawh vek hnuah ahuhovin inah an inthawih ṭhin. An inthawih ni chu Pathian ni emaw, nilai ni emaw a ni ṭhin. Miriate hian an hnathawhna hmanrua zawng zawng hian huai neiah an ngaih vek a vangin an hnathawhna hmanraw hming kimin inthawina an hlan vek ṭhin. He an kût hi an tan chuan ni hlimawm tak a ni a, chhung tinte chu vawk leh ar te talh in chaw eiah te an insawm ṭhin a, hlim takin hun an hmang ṭhin.

Changkran kût hmang tur a nula leh tlangval kalte

  1. Changkran pawi (Kum thar lawm kût) : Changkran pawi hi an kût zinga pawimawh tak a ni a, Budish sakhaw kum thar a ni a, anni pawh hian kum tharah an pawm ve a ni. Miriate pawh hi ramhuai be mai nimahse, Mawk ho nen a inawmpawlh an nih vangte pawh a ni mai thei, “Budish kan ni,” an ti ve a, he an kumthar pawh hi an ngaipawimawh khawp mai. Mire khua-ah hian Budish milem pathian biakna in a awm ve nual a, chung an milem pathian biak in enkawltu zingah chuan anmahni Miria pawh an awm ve nual a ni. An kumthar niah chuan Krama sakhaw hruaitu te pawh Budhis milem pathian biakinah chuan a phusa pawl tak te an ni bawk.

Hemi ni hian hmun hla tak tak a ṭangin an milem pathian biakin awmna-ah apui a nawi in an kal khawm chiam ṭhin a, Budish sakhaw zuitu tak nilo mahse Miriate hian huau huau an ngainat em a vangin kal khawm an tam hle ṭhin a ni. Hemi hun hi Miria te chuan nikhat chiah an hmang ṭhin a, Mawk Budish te chuan ni sarihte an hmang ṭhin. Hemi ni pawh hian an tihpui dang ang bawkin nula te’n tlangvalte chu thil leitir turin an pawt a, nu te pawh an bang bik chuang lo.

Nula in thil lei tir turin tlangval a pawt lai

Pawisa kheltute chu a hma ni a ṭangin an lo kal khawm a, pawisa khelhna chi hrang hrang leh zu zuartute an fuan khawm a, lawilo dawrpui hi a ni ringawt mai ṭhin. An mire infiamna sukpaih (Taket) an tih pawh hemi niah hian hmuh tur a awm bawk ṭhin.

Kum khat chhung a an pathian lim an dah chu hemi hunah hian an bual ṭhin a, a bualna tui chu tinreng damdawiah ṭha in an ngai a, chuvang chuan an dam nan an in ṭhin a, inahte pawh an hawn bawk ṭhin. Miṭhenkhat chuan hetih hun hian an milem pathian inah chuan chaw leh chawhmeh fun an dah ṭhin a, chung an chaw leh chawhmeh fun aṭang chuan an milem pathian chu tlem tlem in an pe ṭhin a, abâk chu a duh a piang ei tur a an tih a ni.

Coconut rahah hian hmawlh an vit phun ṭeuh a, chutah chuan pawisa leh lehkhamawi an tar ṭeuh a, chu chu an milem pathian hnenah an va hlan ṭhin. Mi engemawzat chuan an milem pathian hmaah chuan agarbati chhi in bungbu kawt kawt in chibai an buk ṭhin. Chung an milem pathian chibai lo buktute chu an milem pathian enkawltute chuan mal an lo sawm sak ṭhin.

  1. Kua khang (Khaw serh/hung) : Khaw serh hi an kût zingah chuan pawimawh pawl tak a ni. Hemi ṭum hian chhàngte dêngin vawk leh arte talh in chhungkhat leh ṭhenawm khawvengte chaw eiah te an insawm ṭhin a, an hlim thei ṭhin khawp mai.

Khua an hung dan chu an la kaih, lazai in an khua chu an zam hual vek a, ramhuai hnenah an inthawih ṭhin. Hetih hunlai hian an khua aṭangin tumah an chhuak tur a ni lova, tumah an rawn lut tur a ni bawk hek lo. Tumphung tumin emaw bengchheng an siam tur pawh a ni hek lo. An chhuak lui emaw, an lo lut lui emaw, tumphung hmang a bengchheng siam an awm a nih chuan an inthawina chu ramhuai in a pawm lova, a bul aṭang a inthawih ṭhat leh a ngai a, chu an inthawihna a an ran talh man chu an dan bawhchhetu chuan a chawi vek tur a ni.

Hetia khua an hung hi Pathian ni emaw Nilai ni emaw a ni ṭhin. Hetia khua an serh a an hung hi ni khat chauh a ni a, hemi nikhat chhung chu khua a mite pawh tuikhur thleng bâk tumah an chhuak tur a ni lo. Chu hun chu, “Uangdir” an ti. Atukah pawh chawhma chu khua a mite chu khawiah mah an la chhuak tur a ni lo. Atuk chawhma an la serh chu, “Uang uowi” an ti. Tlai lamah chuan an duhna hmun apiangah an chhuakvak thei tawh a ni.

Hetiang a khaw serh/hung hi kum khatah vawi hnih an nei ṭhin a, mahse an khuaah natna leh thihna a tam lutuk a nih a aien thiam in natna leh thihna ata an him theihna tur atan a an khua chu hung/serh tura atih chuan kum laklawhah pawh khua chu an serh/hung bawk ṭhin. Kum khat chung a an khaw serh/hung pangngai vawi hnih chu ‘Satma khang’ (Ṭhal khaw serh/hung) leh ‘Siadawng khang’ (Fur khaw serh/hung) te an ni.

    1. Satma khang (Ṭhal khaw serh/hung) : Hei hi ṭhal lai a khaw serh/hung a ni a, ahun hi an lo vah hma deuh January/February thla ah a ni. An lo vahna a tawhsual tawk lo leh an inhliam loh nan a ramhuai hnen a an inthawihna a ni.
    2. Siadawng khang (Fur khaw serh/hung) : Ahun lai chu fur June/July thlaah a ni ṭhin. Buh leh bal a malsawmna an dawn theih nan a ramhuai hnen a an inthawihna a ni.

Mire ram a Missionnary rawngbawl hna thawk tura kei, mi tling lo leh thiamna lamah pawh rual ban zo lo tak mai Lalpa’n min han thlang ve tlat mai hi a mak ka ti in a ropui ka ti a. 1. Thuhril leh zirtir : Lalpa rawngbawlnaah hian khawi hmunah pawh Thuhril leh zirtir hi a pawimawh em em a. Mire ram a rawng kan bawlnaah pawh chu chu kan hna hmasa a ni a, tun thleng hian he hna hi kan la thlahthlam ngai lo a ni.

Mire ram a ka awm tirh khan kan unau Miria te chu taksa leh thlarauah an la hniam em em mai a, chuvangin an hnen a thuhril leh zirtirna pek chu ka huphurhin chu hna chu thawh thuai thuai ṭul ka ti em em mai bawk a, chuvang chuan an ṭawng chu theih tawpin ka zir a, Lalpa ṭanpuina in kum hnih ka awm chuan Mire ṭawngin Pathian thu ka sawi thei a, nuamti takin Mire ṭawngin ka ṭawngṭai thei bawk a ni. Kum li ka awm chuan an ṭawngin Zirṭan bu, Mire ṭawng Grammar bu, hlabu, leh Kristian vanram awngzawh bute ka siam in ka letlin thei a ni. Lalpa chu fakin awm rawh se.

Bangladesh-ah hian ka awmna Bandarban District-ah deuh chauh hian Miriate hi an awm a, Bandarban District zau zawng hi 4502 Sq Km’s a zau a ni a, Miriate chu a tlangpuiin chhim lam Mire tlangpui (Chimbuk range) Matamahuri, Marancha hung leh Bawm ram chhim lamahte an awm a, chungah chuan bial pawh nei hran lem lovin Chanchinṭha hril in ka vak chiam mai ṭhin a, Matamuhuri lam Burma ram ri thlengin biakinte pawh kan nei ve nual mai a.

Ka sawi tawh ang khan Miriate hi chu inkungkainna ngaipawimawh mi an ni a, Mizo ka nihna nilovin Pastor Chownglowka fapa (Miria) ka nihna chu ka hmang ṭangkai hle a, ka kalna khuate chu eng hnamte nge an nih ka hrechiang hmasa a, ka mi kawmate chu engtia koh tur nge ka hriat hmasak leh a ngai a. Ka papui, ka pate, ka pu, ka ni, ka ṭang (pate farnu ni-te pasal), leh ka u emaw unaupa (Miria te chu an inkungkaihnaah inkoh dan keini Mizote ai chuan an ngah fe zawk a ni) tiin emaw kovin, thlawhhma lamte leh an dam ṭhat leh ṭhat lohte zawt a sawi puiin zawi zawiin Pathian thu ka sawipui chauh ṭhin a, chuti chuan inpawh takin kan inkawm thei ṭhin a ni.

Miriate hi an la hnufual em a vangin hnam dang lak a ṭang hian an inlahrang em em a, chuvang chuan hnam dang thusawi chu han zawm tinah an tin ṭhin lo ti ila kan sawi sual tampui awm love. Chuvangin a nih ka ring, Mire ram ka zu thlen thleng khan an chhehvelah hnam dang changkang zawkte lo awmin kan unau Bawm phei chuan an hnenah chanchinṭha an lo hril thin a, Kristiante pawh engemawzat lo siam tawh mahse an incheina, an ṭawng zahmawh tak tak leh an khawsak phung an la thlak chuang meuh lo a ni.

Kei pawh ka incheina an mahni anga ka la inthlak hma leh an ṭawng ka la thiam chian hma chuan chanchinṭha hrila ka zin changte hian ṭhen khat chu nutling patlingte pawh min hmuh chuan an tlanchhiat dawr dawr chang leh keimah zawkin ka hlauh a ka tlanchhiat changte pawh a awm ṭhin a ni.

Pathian thu sawi a ka vah velnaah hian kan veng nula U, B.N-i (Lalbiaknii) ṭingṭangte min pek leh Arth & Culture Didector Pi Bawihchhingpuii Hand Mike (aurinnate) min pek chu mipui ka hipna atan a hmanraw ṭangkai tak mai a ni a, ka ṭingṭang ri leh aurinna ri an hriat chuan ka zai ngaithla tur leh ka ṭingṭang perh en tur chuan mi tam tak tak an rawn pungkhawm ṭhin a. Tin, milem Pathian thu sawina atan a remchangin lian pui pui a ka ziak hmangte chuan ka zir tir bawk ṭhin.

Hetia chanchinṭha hril a ka vah hlan hian ka nupui Zopuii chu a lo hah em em ṭhin a. Keima insak ve, lian leh ṭha tawk a ka sak chu kum khat pawh kan luah hma in CFB President Pu Rualthanmawia chuan Empu khaw chim chhuah lam a naupang za ngawt leng tura a sak Hostel lian leh seipui mai khaw pawna awmah chuan awm turin min ti a, chu Hostel lianpui mai piah lawkah chuan School atan a a tih Bawngin tia lek School in te tek te hi a awm bawk a. Chu a Hostel sakah chuan naupang awm tur an awm loh avangin a rawp angreng hle tawh a, chutah chuan kan awm ta a, thli atleh dawn a a lan chuan khawchhungah tlanchhiat zel a ngai bawk a.

Ka khualzin chhung chuan ka nupui duhtak leh ka hmangaih em em ka fanu kum khat pawh la tling lote chu kan awmna chu ramhnuai a nih avangin an lo awm ngam ṭhin si lova, chutih lai chuan Army te’n kawngpui an siam a vangin an teivâk reng mai bawk si a. Mire ho lah chhunah chuan an feh vekin tar leh naupang bâk inah an awm ngai bawk silo, bâkah an in chu thim in a ṭawp em a vangin Mire in bathlarah chuan nau paw chungin nileng ṭhak a lo din changte pawh a awm ṭhin a, zanah chuan kan khaw nula ṭhenkhatin an riahpui ṭhin a. chutih lai chuan Tk 1200 ka hlawh ve a, mahse hlawh hmuh loh thla a tam thei ṭhin khawp mai. Han hmuh chang pawhin hmuh loh thla kha chu hmuh loh thla a ni tawp zel mai bawk a.

Chuvangin kan ei leh in tur kan thawhchhuah ve angai a, lo leh huante ka siam bawk a, ka nupui u leh nau te’n min rawn pui ṭhin a vangin kei chu ka zin thei zawk chauh ṭhin a ni. Lalpa’n Bangladesh ami Bawm nupuiah min pe chu ka rilru tak hian ka lawm em em ṭhin a, a chhan tamtakte zinga pakhat chu chawhmeh ṭha han hmeh ve tur in a leina tur pawisa kan neih loh a vangin ramhnuai a hnim hnah; (eng hnah hnahte nge pawh ka hre lo) ka nupui chuan a rawn la a, chung a rawn lâkte chu chawhmeh antuiah a han siam a, tui ti takin kan ei thei zel a, chhuan chhama kan awm leh lek chang lah a tam, mahse ka bulah phun nawi miah lo leh lungawi takin a awm zel thei hi ka nupui chungah leh hetiang nupui ṭha min petu Lalpa chungah ka lawm em em thin a ni.

Ka zin chhuah loh chang pawhin ka nupui leh fanaute chu ka awp lum hman lo em em mai a, kan ei tur ka thawhchhuah angai a, kan khaw ṭhalaite chu hla ka zir tir a, ziak leh chhiar ka zir tir reng ṭhin bawk a, mite zirtir tur atan milem ka ziak in ka typewriter upa tawh tak chuan Mizo hla Mire ṭawng a lehlin leh lehkhabu dang dang Mire ṭawnga buatsaihin hun ka hmang reng ṭhin bawk a, kan in luah lah chu ramhnuai a nih bakah a chhuat chu lei a ni a, rul leh tit te an rawn lut renga, thosi leh vaihmite a tam bawk si, a chung rangva siling awmlo a nih avangin ṭhalah chuan a lum thei em em mai bawk a, fanu leh fapa kan neih ve diah duah tawh hnu pawh chuan kan ei-in tur inzawn chawp nen, chanchinṭha hril a vahchhuah reng ngai bawk si.

Chutiang khawp a amah ngaihsak hman lo leh hreawm a ka awmpui chung chuan ka nupui Zopuii chu a vui ngai lo reng reng a, hreawm ti hmel pawh a pu ngai bawk lo. Hreawm atih ber chu kan khuaah hian dawrte a awm ve a, fate chhang leh chhithlum han lei sakna fahran pawh kan nei lo chu hreawm a tih thu a sawi ve ṭhin.

Khaw hrang hrangah kalin Pathian thu ka sawi a, Kristiante an lo awm chuan ziak leh chhiar ka zirtir nghal zel bawk a, a thiam fal deuh bikte chu ka awmloh hlan a zirtirtu a tan ka ruat zel bawk a. Kan khaw nulate chu Action Songs te ka zirtir a, ṭhalai ho nen ahuhovin khaw dangah rawngbawlin kan chhuah chang pawh a awm ṭhin a, bung hmasa lam a kan sawi tawh ang khan, kan khua chu incheina lamah pawh hma kan sawn chak hle a, chu chu khaw dangah pawh a kangkai ve zel a ni.

Chu kan awmna Hostel ruak awngrawp tak daipawn a awmah chuan kum thum kan awm hnu chuan Hostel chu chhuahsan a khaw chhunga awm ve chu Pu Rualthanmawia chu kan dil a, mahse Pu Rualthanmawia chuan, “Nangni in in luah loh chuan a chhe mai anga sin,” atih tlat avang chuan khaw chhuga kan insawn chu a phal lova. Chu Hostel sak dan chu, a ban kha thingṭawl a zawm tur a tih a nih lain asatute’n a ban thing vek kha tan in an zawm leh a, chu an zawmna chu lei hrulah a awm si a, a zawmna laiah chuan tlumpiin a ei chhe zo tawh si a, thli a tleh chuan tlu rup mai thei a ni renga.

Pu Rualthanmawia chuan Bandarban-ah Seminer turin min kova, kan khaw pa ho pawh an kal ve nual a, chung kan khaw pa ho chuan khawchhug a in kan sak a kan awm ve theih nan Pu Rualthanmawia hnenah chuan min dil sak chiam avangin khaw chug a in kan sak a kan awm ve chu min phal sak ta hram a. Khaw chhungah chuan in te takte in kawngpui bulah, ka pa Pastor Chownglowk-a te in bulah, maurapin in ka sa ve a, kan in sawn chiah chu chu Hostel awngrawp tak mai pawh chu a tlu ve nghal ta a ni.

Miria te chu tlawmngaihnate zirtir in kan ṭhalai bember hlo thlo hlei thei lo, mi hlo thlawh zawh hun a zo ve thei lote chu kan pui bawk ṭhin a, kan pawisa thawh khawmte chu mi harsate leh ṭanpui ngaite ṭanpui nante kan hmang bawk ṭhin a ni. Kum 1992 khan kan unau Bawm ECC khawmpui, kan awmna aṭang a hla angreng tak Ruangsuri Mumthar khuaah kan khaw ṭhalai mi engemaw zat kan kal a, Actionsong’s hmanga rawngbawlna kan neih pawh mite’n an lawm tak meuh a ni.

Miria te hian ṭawng zahmawh buru tak tak sawi hi ahmei a pain an serh lovin an uar em em a, chumai nilovin an hmingah pawh hmeichhia chu hmeichhe zahmawh hming, mipa chu mipa zahmawh hming leh thil dang pawh hming chhe pui pui hminga in phuah/koh an uar em em a, chungte chu kan innêl a, kan inhmangaih tawh chinah chuan theih tawpin bansan aṭul ziate ka zirtir ṭhin a. An nula leh tlangval inrim dan khaw danga zan dar sawmpahnih hnu lam a an inrim dan leh an nungchang mawi lo leh inthlahdah em emte chu a ṭhat loh ziate leh thil dang tamtak zirtir an ngai a, ka theih ang tawk in ka zirtir ṭhin a, apawn a ṭang nilova achhung a ṭanga ka zirtir a vang leh ka hmangaih a, an ni pawhin min hmangaih bawk a vangin ka han hau deuh bawrh bawrh chang pawh chuan ka chungah an lungawi lo chuang lova, ziak leh chhiar ka zirtir lai phei chuan ka ṭhin a lo chhe ve bawk nen; ṭap zawih zawih a ka hau changte pawh a awm ṭhin a ni.

Khawi ram leh khawi hnam zingah pawh chanchinṭha hrilin zirtir ila chung kan hrilh leh kan zirtir a te hnam chu kan hmangaih em em tur a ni a, enganga hnam mawl pawhin hmangaihna chu an hre thiam in an tan pawh anmahni hmangaihtu lo hmangaih let ve chu thil har a ni lova, awlsam takin an lo hmangaih ve nghal mai thei a ni. Chutiang a inkungkaihna nghet leh ṭha a kan hrilh a kan zirtir a nih chuan a pawn a tangin kan zirtir a nilova, achhung a ṭangin kan zirtir tihna ni.

A biktakin hnam mawlte zingah chuan apawn a ṭanga zirtir chu an hrehiam har em em in an ṭi tlat ṭhin a, a chhung a ṭanga zirtir erawh chuan kan thu hrilh leh zirtirte chu awlsam takin an hrethiam a, harsa ti lo takin an lo pawm mai thei a ni tih keima tawng ngei a ṭangin ka hre chiang a ni.

Seminer leh Treining pek : Akhat tawkin kan Mission a thawktu leh Kohhran hruaitute chauh nilovin Mire Kristian Kohhran hruaitu zawng zawngte ko khawm in Pathian thu zirna Seminer ka neihpui ṭhin a, ni khat, ni hnihte hun kan hmang ṭhin a, a nuam kan tiin kan hlawkpui thei ṭhin khawp mai. Keima tan tak pawh a ṭangkai khawp mai. Hetiang a Jubject hrang hrang zirtir tur hian kei pawh lehkha ka chhiar nasa in ka inbuatsaih nasat ṭhin em avang in.

Hetia Mire ṭawng vek a Subject hrang hrang zirtir tur a tan a ka buatsaih hi Kohhran hrang hrang leh Mire ram a Mire ṭawng a Lalpa rawngbawltu te’n anmahni Kohhran ṭheuhah an hman ṭangkai lehzual theih nan a bu pawhin ka siam bawk a ni.

2. Leterature lam a hmalakna : Kan unau Miria te taksa leh thlarau lama hma an lo sawn zel theih nan Literature lama hmalâk sak hi a ngai em em a, eng chen nge Lalpa’n Mire ramah min awmtir dawn ka hre si lova. Chuvangin Lalpa’n Mire ṭawng thiamna min pek hi ka awmloh hnu thleng pawh a Miria te’n an ṭangkai pui zel theih nan Leterature lam a hma lâk hi ṭha in ka hria a. Mire ram a ka awm mek lai pawhin mipakhat ve mai ka nih a vangin khawi kipah ka kalin ka awm thei si lova.

Mire tan lehkhabu ka buatsaih sak theih chuan ka kal theih loh leh ka awm lohna hmun amite hnenah pawh lehkhabu hmang chuan rawng ka bawl zel thei dawn tho a ni tihte ka ngaihtuah a, ṭha dawn hle in ka hria a. Lehkhabu siam tur chuan lehkha chhutna khawl leh lehkhabu siamna sum leh paite ka mamawh dawn a ni tih ka hria a, mahse chu chu ka tih theih loh thil a nih a vangin Lalpa mawhphurhah ka dah a.

Miriate chu ziak leh chhiar kan zirtir a, achhiar tur lehkhabu kan pek leh si loh chuan kan zirtir hahna chuan awmzia anei tlem dawn a ni tihte ka ngaihtuah bawk a. Chutia ka ngaihtuahna chu Lalpa’n a lo hria a ni ang, a mawhphurhna chu rang takin a rawn hlen ta mai a. Ka la hmelhriat ngai loh leh min hretu ni pawh a ka hriat loh, Malumghat Baptist Association Mission a an hotupa America sap Herold Abarson chuan lehkha min rawn thawn a, alehkha thawnah chuan Typewriter min rawn pek thu a lo inziak a, rei rial lovin kan tlangval Mensin chu Malumghat-ah chuan kan tir nghal a Typewriter ṭha tak German siam Pu Herold-a chuan min rawn pe nawlh mai a. Chu chuan ka lo suangtuah ṭhin hna chu ka thawk ṭan thei ta a ni. Lalpa chu fakin awm rawh se. Herold Abarson leh kei

Pu Herold an Typewriter min pek hian Mire tan lehkhabu engemaw zat ka chhu a, ka hman rim em avangin ka Typewriter pawh chu a tar in a hate (achhutna) a lo bal nual ta a, achhutna bal lai thir phekte a han chhut te chuan kut a na thei hle mai a, chuvangin Lalpa hnenah Computer ka dil ta a, “Lalpa, ka hnathawhna Typewriter min pek hi a tar tawh lutuk a, a chhutna lai pawh a bal nual tawh mai a, Computer min pe tawh mai teh, i rawng bawl lo tute zawk pawhin an nei a ni a, kei chuan i rawngbawl nan ka mamawh takmeuh a ni. Mahse, Computer khawih chu ka thiam lova, thiam turin ka zir bawk lo, min pe a nih chuan a thiamna nen min pe nghal dawn nia,” tiin ngenngawl tak in ka dil a.

Kum 2005 chuan kan Field Director Rev Lalhmunsanga chu Bangladesh ah a awm theih tak loh avangin a haw alo ngai a, a Computer kawl chu kan hotute’n Pu Sangtea hnenah chuan, “Computer kha chu Lura hman atan hnuchhiah mai rawh,” an ti a, Computer Desktop chu min hnuchhiah sak ta ruau mai a, kan khuaah Carrent a la awm loh a vangin Bandarban a ka Pi Lalzovi w/o Taia chuan, “Kan inah dah la i hnathawhna atan room pakhat ka pe ang che, hna i thawh chhung chu kan ei ang ang i ei in i in ve mai dawn nia,” a ti a.

Bandarban a ka Pi Lalzovi te inah chuan ka Computer chu ka dah a, an inah chuan hna chu ka thawk ṭhin a, achang chuan kâr khatte, kâr hnihte hnathawkin ka awm ṭhin a, ka awm chhung zawng chuan inthlahrung miah lovin ka Pi Lalzovi te hnenah chuan ka ei in ka in a, ka Pi hian min hmangaih em avangin a tu leh fate zawng zawng pawhin min hmangaih veka. Hnathawka Bandarban a ka cham chhung chuan tha ka âm thei em em a, zingah ka thova Breakfats ka ei zawh hnu aṭang chuan ka hna chu ka ṭan nghal char char a, chhunah chaw kan ei a, zan dar kua velah chaw kan ei leh a, zan dar sawmpahnih thleng hna ka thawk leh ṭhin a.

Chutiang a ka thawh rim em avang leh ka Pi te ei in (vai ei ang) chu ka pum in a lo mil vak lo nen; ka hriselna chuan kum khat pawh ka thawh hma chuan a tuar ta tlat mai a. Lalpa hnenah, “Lalpa, Computer Desktop min kawl tir reng chuan ka dam rei lo tawp ang, hna thawhtur lah ka ngah si a, Laptop min pe zawk mai rawh, Laptop chu kan in a ka Solar in ka hmang thei ang,” ka ti a.

Chumi kum chuan Mizoram ah ka han chho leh a, kan Field Director lo ni tawh, Pu Sangtea chu ka ngaih em avangin Lawngtlai bul Thingkah a a awm chu ka va tlawh a, Pu Sangte a chuan, “Hei, ka Laptop hi Amongphru in Bangkok aṭangin Secondhand min rawn lei sak a ni a, Secondhand mah ni se Japan siam a ni a, aṭha em em a, mahse achhiat palh chuan heta siam a buaithlak dawn a vangin i Dasktop kawl lai kha hotu te hnenah hralh dil la, a daih bâk chu Office ah pawisa dil la ka Laptop hi lei mai rawh,” a lo ti a.

Asawi ang chuan kan hotute chu ka dil a, kan hotute chuan min lo phal sak a, Pu Sangtea Laptop chu Lalpa’n min pe leh ta a ni. Lalpa hi chu amite tan thil ṭha hi a ui ngai lo tak tak a ni. Chu laptop chuan hna tam tak kan inchhungkhur aṭang ngei-in nuam takin ka thawk thei ta a. Kum 2007 khan Fuarlogth turin Mizoramah kan chhungkua in kan chho dawn a, kan chùan-na Jeep chu mi kan tam em avangin a chhungah ka leng ve lova a chungah ka ṭhu a, Bandarban thlen dawn chuan kawng kawiah motor a tlan chak lai tak in kan Bag chu a tla dawn a, kan bag dawm ka va tum chu (kawng kawi lai taka lo ni a) motor chung aṭang chuan kawngsir lei char laiah motor chuan min paih thla ṭhuai mai a, lei ka thlen lai pawh ka hre hman lo.

Ka nupui leh fanaute chuan motor chhung aṭang chuan, “Apa a tla,” an ti a, an râk chul mai a, motor chu a piah deuh daihah chuan a ding ta a. Keichu ka thova ka inbeng thawk thawk a, motor lam pan chuan ka kal ve leh mai a. Engmah nâ ka nei lova, Bandarban a ka Pi Lalzovi te in kan va thlen chuan ka Leptop chu ka han en nak chuan a lo keh vek mai a. Pawi ka tiin ka ui em em mai a, “Kei chuan engtikahmah Laptop thar chu ka lei leh thei dawn si lova, hotute hnenah pawh dil leh mah ila Laptop thar leina chu min pe leh dawn tawh si lova, hna thawh tur lah chu ka la ngah bawk si a,” ti in ka ngaihtuah a. Ka ngaihtuahna a buai zova, ka â muih hian ka inhria a, chaw ei nachang pawh ka hre lova.

Ka nupui leh ka fate chuan, “Mitam tak motor chung a ṭanga an tlakin natakin an inhliam a, ṭhenkhat chu an thi a, (kan line ah hian motor chung a ṭanga tla hi mi pathum lai an thi tawh a) nang chu i engmah hliam lovin Pathian in a veng che a, Laptop chu a pe leh em em ang che,” min han tih chuan ka harh huai hian ka hria a. Chuta ṭang chuan ka hlim ve leh in ka nunna Lalpa’n min zuahsak avang chuan lawm nachangte ka hre ve ta chauh a ni.

Mizoram kan thleng a, he ka lehkha bu, ‘Ka Hringnun leh Miria te Khawvel’ bu hi Mualchin Printing Press neitu M.C.Awia’n (Pu Awitea) kei in a chengkhat pawh in sengso lovin ama sumin abu min chhuah saka, kan Fuarlogth naah kan Relist nghal a, chu ka lehkhabu man pawh chu Pu Awitea chuan, “Chengkhat pawh ka la dawn lo, i mamawh lei nan i hmang dawn nia,” min ti a, chu ka lehkhabu man chuan ka nupui leh fate sawi ang ngei chuan Laptop thar leh Camera thar thatak ka lei thei ta a ni. Lalpa’n Laptop Secondhand hmang lovin Brandnew ngat min hman tir a lo duh a ni tih ka hre chhuak ta a ni. Kan sawi nawn leh teh ang, Lalpa hi amite tan hian a va han ṭha em, amite tan chuan thil ṭha engmah ui pawh a nei lo a ni. Lalpa chu fakin awm kumkhua rawh se.

Mire ṭawng Grammar bu a lo awm chuan Mire hnam zinga hnamdang (Miria ni lo) rawngbawl a awmte tan pawh Mire ṭawng zirna kawngah nasa takin a pui theih ngei ka ring bawk a ni. Bible lehlin hi mimal a kalpui chi niin ka hre lova, a chhan chu, mimal hming a lan chuan min duhtu tam tak an awm lai in min duh lo a min dodaltu tam tak an awm thei a ni tih chu ka hriat avangin Mire Christian Litteratur Committee (MCLC) din chu ṭulin ka hria a, Dt 14 Oct 2009 khan Mire ram a Kristian Mission hrang hrang a mi hruaitute ka Office-ah ka kokhawm a, Bible lehlin chungchang mai bakah Hla bu leh lehkhabu dang dang siam chungchangah pawh Mire Christian Litterature Committee din chu a ṭul zia ka han sawi chuan an ni pawh chuan ṭha an ti tlang a, hruaitute pawh kan inthlang nghal a.

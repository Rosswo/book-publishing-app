BUNG 21

DAN BAWHCHHIAT A VANGA CHAWI THEIHNATE

Bung hmasa lamah khan Miria te hian inngaihdamna an tlakchham thu kan sawi tawh a, thil an tihsual palh pawh in a phu tawk hremna emaw, chawi tir an ni deuh zel ṭhin a ni. Ṭanpui ngaite an ṭanpui pawh hian thabat ang deuh a ni zawkmah a, entirnan; Damlo zawn emaw, inkang ṭhelhpui leh thil dangah pawh ṭanpuina dawngtu chuan amah ṭanpuitu te chu ruai a buatsaih sak ngei a ngai ṭhin.

Mire ram a kal emaw, awm emaw chuan hetiang hi hriat ngei a ngai a ni. Athuhrimah mi an lo changkang zel a, keini Mizote pawh ram dangah, hnam dang zing a khawsa kan tam ve tulh tulh a, hnamdang zing a kan awm hian kan awmna hnamte kan zir chiang in fimkhur thiam hi a ngai hle a ni. Heng dan bawhchhiat a vanga chawi theihnate phei hi chu kan hriat ngei ngei a ngai a, kan awmna hnamte azir in an hnam dan erawh chu a dang ngei ang. An hnam dan pawh kan zah sak thiam angaihna tam tak a awm ṭhin bawk a ni.

Mire hnam zinga dan bawhchhiat a vanga chawi theihnate chu hengte hi an ni:

  1. Nula leh tlangval chesual lai an hmuh a thubuai an siam chuan tlangval chu vawk an chhun (chawi) tir ṭhin. Chu chu “Iang pak” an ti. Nula chu a lo rai chuan a raitu chuan Tk 5 a chawi tur a ni a, chu chu, “Kan khang dawng” (Hmeichhia in nau a neih hun a chawhmeh a nghei lai a a hman tur a ni).
  2. Mi nu leh pa hmeichhiat mipatna kawng a an chesual lai mi in an hmu a thubuai an siam a nih chuan mipa chu vawk an chhun (Chawi) tir ṭhin. Hei pawh hi “Iang pak” an ti tho. Hmeichhia chu a pasal in a nei duh tawh lo a nih chuan a nupui man chu hmeichhe nu leh pa te’n an pe let leh vek tur a ni.

Mipa chuan a nupui a uire san manah a puzawn chu Tk 70 leh Silver tangka 3 ape a nga, chubakah a puzawn chu ar a talh sak a nga, a pizawn leh a puzawnte ke a dawm in ngaihdam a dil ngei tur a ni.

  1. An pindan, “Kimma” an tihah a neitu phalna la hmasa lova mi a luh chuan pawisa emaw, vawk emaw chawi tir theih a ni. An pindan a aneitu planna lova mi a luh a vawk an chhun tir chuan vawk lung leh a kal chu mautheiah aiduchhe bal eichi nen an chhum a nga, chu chuan an inthawih tur a ni.

A tisualtu chuan, “Ka lo tisual a, he inah hian natna emaw, thil ṭhalo emaw thleng lo turin ka inthawih e,” a ti tur a ni. A vawksa chhumna mau chawng chu a chhumna hmunah nikhat a dah a nga, Tk 70 a chawi bawk tur a ni.

  1. Midang hliam palh in a hliam dan a zirin a hliamtu chuan pawisa a chawi tur a ni.
  2. Hmeichhe phalna lova mipa in a hnute a khawih a hmeichhia chu a thinrim chuan a hnute khawihtu mipa chuan hmeichhia in a phut zat zat pawisa a chawi tur a ni.
  3. Meichher chhi a mi thlam lam a kal a, mi thlam chu a kang a nih chuan meichher chhitu chuan a hal lai hmu ker lo mahse meichher kengtupa chuan vawk emaw, pawisa emaw a chawi tur a ni.
  4. Lo hal vang emaw a khawram (khaw hmun hlui) a kan chuan a ti kangtu chuan vawk a chawi tur a ni.
  5. Lo thlai sengkhawm zawh hma a nula leh tlangval inru mi thlam a an riak, thlam neitu in a hriat chuan nula leh tlangval inru te chuan vawk an chawi tur a ni.
  6. Mi nu, nula ang a chhaih emaw, an lungawi loh zawng a khawih palh emaw chuan a khawih/chhaihtu mipa chuan zu a chawi tur a ni a, chu chu “Pri” an ti. A tisualtu chuan akhawih/chhaih chu a pute lam a nih chuan fei a chawi tel tur a ni a, a tute lam a nih erawh chuan zu bakah diar var tawng nga ai a tawi lo leh silver tangka 15 – 17 a chawi tur a ni.
  7. Mi in nupui a neih lai in a puzawn tan ar a chum sak a nga, a puzawn tan a a ar talh danah emaw, a chum danah emaw a puzawn a lungawi lo a nih chuan a arsa chhum chu a paih vek a nga, a dang a chhum sak leh a nga, a mâkpa chuan a tihsual manah fei pakhat leh zu um khat a chawi bawk tur a ni.
  8. Mi in lo a thlai ramhuai hnen a hlan hma a a lo ei palh emaw, a tihchhiat palh chuan lo neitu in a phut zat ang a piang a chawi tur a ni.
  9. An khaw hung/serh lai a lut emaw, chhuak emaw, bengchheng lo siam emaw chuan khaw hung inthawihna an ran senso, ar emaw vawk emaw a chawi tur a ni.
  10. Kharbari te pindanah an monu lo mutpui emaw sual emaw chuan Silver tangka (lukawlh chuang ngei) a chawi a nga, kharbari chu ar a talh sak a nga, ngaihdam a dil bawk tur a ni.
  11. Mi fanu tlangval in a ru a, nula chhungte chuan an um a, a rutu te’n mo lawmna ran an lo talh tawh a, an fanu chu mo lawmna ran an talh hma a an lak kir leh tur thu sawi lawk si lova an va hruai chhuak leh a nih chuan an fanu rutu te mo lawmna ran talh man chu nula chhungte chuan an chawi vek tur a ni.
  12. Mi in nupui a neih lain a puzawnte lam chuan achipuite leh a mâkpate chipui lam chu tunhma in emaw intihthiamlohna an neih tawh leh neih loh an chhui a nga, anmahni in an hriat mai loh pawh in midang an zawt a nga, engemaw thu hlaah an mâkpate hnam leh anmahni hnam chu intihthiamlohna a lo awm tawh ni a an hriat chuan a mâkpa chu Silver tangka 5 emaw a chawi tir ṭhin. Ṭhenkhat chuan chutiang a ti kher lote pawh an awm bawk.

Hnam dan hi a hmun leh a hun a zir in a danglam ve thei a, hei hi hemi lehkhabu ka ziak lai a Mire hnam zing a an dan bawhchhiat a vang a chawi theihna ṭhenkhat a ni a, ka ziak kim vek lovang tih erawh ka ring a ni.

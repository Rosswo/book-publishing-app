BUNG 1

KA PIAN LEH MURNA

Min hmangaihtu, ka Nu leh ka Pa leh ka pi leh ka pute vangkhua, an lo Lalna ram leh khua leh tui chu Mizoram chhim chhak lam, Tuichang ral, Thingsai khua aṭanga Km 7 a hla, Bualpui ‘H’ a ni a. Kan khua Bualpui ‘H’ chu kum 1968 khan Mizoram buai, sawihkhawm zingah khan tel vein Grouping Center hnai ber Thingsai khaw lam pa nin lungchhe tak leh an hmalamah hun thim tak an hmachhawn dawn a ni tih hre reng chungin Lota nupui anga hnung lam hawi kir pah reng chungin; mangang leh thlaphang em em in, alian ate, mahni zawhtawk ṭheuh phurin Thingsai khaw lam pa nin an kal nguah nguah a.

Tudang ngai bik lova an tuallenna, Bual khawpui ngai leh thlahlel ngawih ngawih leh mahni intodelh a hlim taka an lo lên ve ṭhinna chu chhuahsa nin Thingsai khua chu an han thleng ta a. An khaw ṭhenawm Thingsaiah chuan; khaw ṭhenawm mah nise; an kal dan in azir loh em vangin nidanga an kal ṭhin nen chuan aboruak adang hle ang tih arin mai theih awm e.

Thingsai khua a an awm lai hian eiin achhiat em vang leh aboruak an ngeih loh vangte pawh a ni ang, an inhloh nasa hle nghe nghe a ni. An tan thlawhhma lâk pawh ahautak em em mai a, an khaw ram; Thingsai khua a ṭanga mel tam takah lo an va nei ṭhin a, vai sipai ho lah chuan lo a riah an phal bawk silova, lova riah an phal lo mai nilovin an fehte chuan chawfun ken takngial pawh an phal lo lehzel si a, chuvangin chhungkaw tam takin thlawhhma an lâk theih loh a vangin nasa takin ṭam a tlak phah nghe nghe a ni.

Mite chu an rethei em avang leh an ei achhiat em avangin hrisel lohna in a tlakbuak a, tam takin an thih phah a ni. Kuli atam em em reng mai bâkah, damlo leh thei lote pawh vai sipai ho chuan kuli turin an phut lui zel bawk si a; vai camp sak leh camp kulh sak hnatlang an ko reng mai bawk a, rinhlelh thu mai maiah vai sipaite chuan mitam tak an vua in an hrem vak vak mai bawka, mipa puiling vuak tuar lo leh hrem tuar lo an awmlo hial awm e.

Rambuai avanga Bualpui ‘H’ khua in an tawrh dan sawi vek seng lo mah ila a tlangpui chu hetiang hi a ni:

  1. Bualpui khua, Thingsai khua a sawikhawm hun 4th April 1968.
  2. Khaw hal hun 15th April 1968.
  3. Khaw haltu : Assam Rifle Capt Negi leh ahote.
  4. In kâng zawng zawng zât : In 102.
  5. Khaw hàla thil kângte ahunlai pawisa hlutna a chhutin Rs 3,00,000/-(Nuai thum) hu vel niin an ngâi.
  6. Bualpui khawmi ram tana nun hlante.
    1. Pu Hranglinga. Silchar jelah vuakhlum a ni.
    2. Rualthanga.
    3. Thanghrima
    4. Thangruaia.
    5. Hmingthanzuala te an ni.
  7. Bualpui khaw hlui a let leh hun: Kum 1973. Vai sipaiin an awp zui leh nghal.

Bualpui khua hian rambuai avangin an tuar nasa em em a, chuvangin zirna lamah pawh nasa takin an tlakchhiat phah a, lehkhathiam pawh an chhuah tlem phah niin a lang,

Mizoram buai chu a zual tulh tulh a vangin Mizoram pum mai chu hun thimin a tuam chhah tulh tulh mai a, Mizoram hmun hrang hrangah ṭàm, hri leh harsatna chi hrang hrang a vangin mite chu an rum tlawk tlawk mai a, Cerfew rei deuh deuh puan a ni reng mai bawka, puitling chin tan kuli, achhun aza nin a awm reng bawksi nen.

Chutia Mizoram thim chhah takin atuam lai mek chuan Thingsai khaw veng pakhat ‘Pemveng’ an tih a ka Pu Tintlunga te inah chuan tukhaw hriat lovin April 18, 1968 chawhnu lam chuan mimang piangin he ka khualzin kawng bumboh leh chhuk cho deuh mai zawh ṭan turin khawvel êng ka lo hmu ve ta a ni.

LAL HEMPHUNGA BUALPUI

Ka Nu leh Pa leh ka pi leh pute vangkhua hian chhiatna rapthlak tak lo tawk tawh mahse anmahni venga an tuallai lènna a tana vangkhua lo duan sak a, a thilsiam mawi leh duhawm, nungchate tualchaina leh làm an lo thaina hnum, vau, ṭuah, fâr leh tlaizawng thing mawi tinreng vulna hmun, an khaw hming a tana hming ropui tak leh an LAL zaidam leh Pathian Hmangaihna tuipui burtu Hemphunga hming chawi a ‘LAL HEMPHUNGA BUALPUI’ ti a, Ahming Maka, Remruttua’n a lo ruat sak tawh chu tuma tih bo rual lohvin tun thleng hian ṭhan chhoh lam a la pan zel a ni. He khaw hming awmze thuk tak nei a lo phuah saktu leh tun thlenga venga enkawl tlattu LALPA chu fakin awm mawlh rawh se.

He khua BUALPUI hian a hming alo put dan leh a khaw thleng a rem em em dan hi tlemin han sawi hlek ila: He khua aṭanga chhim lam hla vaklovah hian ‘Sabualkàwn’ a awm a, chu sabualkàwnah chuan sa chitin reng mai hian inbual nan an hmanga, zawng leh ngau leh sava chitin reng mai an awm bawka, chung nungcha chitin reng maite chuan chung lai a ramngaw dur khung maiahte chuan nunchan zirin làm an lo thai tawh ṭhin a.

Tin, a khua hi chhim leh hmar zawnga khaw dung awm a ni a, khaw lu lamah leh khaw mawng lamah hian mual pawng rem ṭha êm èm mai a awma, a chhim lama tlang awm chu a hniam deuh zawka; mahse, amual alian in rem êm èm mai a, ram buai lai hun engemaw chen phei kha chuan vai sipai hovin an luah nghe nghe a ni. Tunah chuan in a awm vek tawh. Kan naupan lai chuan chu tlang velah chuan pa chitin reng mai, Papâr, pasawntlung, palengsen, palengvâr leh pauithinte kan veh ṭhin a, iptepui âng hmawk hmawkin pa chitin reng mai chu kan hawn ṭhin a, pauithin phei chu kan beng thawk thawka, “A hmin tawh” kan ti a, kan bâr tawn tawn a, tui kan ti thei ṭhin khawp mai. Hemi tlang chhuah lampangah hian ‘Fâr tlang’ a awm leh a, chu tlangah chuan fâr thing a tam êm èm mai a, thli in a tih tiahte chu kan phura, kan khua chuan zanah far thing fim chu khawnvarah kan hmang ṭhin, mahse akhu a ung duh khawp mai.

Hmar lam tlang chu a sang zawka, a lei chu a ṭha em em mai a, hmanlai chuan chu tlang ami chu belvawtu te’n bel vuakna a tan lei an lâk ṭhin a vangin ‘Bellei tlang’ an ti a, kan khaw dâi velah hian thei chi hrang hrang a tam êm èm mai a, khawchhung lam a tlang rawn pharh thla, hmun zawl thlen hma chuan in lên thumin akal thla a. Hmar thlang lam Thingsai lam kawtchhuahah kawtthler phei nuam takin a awm leh bawk a. Tunah chuan Bellei tlangah pawh in tam tak a awm tawh.

Chhim lam tlang leh hmar lam tlang inkâr chu awih zâl,(awih tlan) in sak rem êm èm si hian a awm a, lo fing hnih awm vel hian zawl duai mai a, chùan chung leh chùan hnuai hian a awm a, chùan chung zawkah chuan in lên hnih in a kala, mual zau tak a awm bawk a, chumi mualah chuan kan naupan laichuan Maruati pa te bawng pasen leh bawng pavar an insi ṭhin a, akhua in kan en luih luih ṭhin a, a ennawm thei ṭhin khawp mai. Chung kawtthler nuam leh mual zau takah chuan tlai atanga zan thim; pafa hmei hai thleng hian naupang kan pawnto luai luai ṭhin a, nuam tak a ni.

Chhuah lam chùan hnuaiah chuan Primary School a awm a, kàwn zawl zau tak, chhim lam tlang leh hmar lam tlang a ṭanga bawlhhlawh hnawm tinreng arawn len tlak tlin khawmna a nih a vangin lei a ṭha em em renga, kum tin akhua in kàwnah chuan anṭam hmun kan siam ṭhin a, anṭam a ṭha duh em em a, anṭam rêm lamah chuan naupang ho chuan anṭam kung bul kawm ṭha tak takte chu khelkawm lemah kan la a, chu chuan hawki te pawh kan khel lem ṭhin a, anṭam huan hungna palte chuan bûkte kan sa a, a chhun a zanin chu kan bûkteah chuan hlim takin hun kan hmang ṭhin a.

Thlasik alo nih tawh chuan chu kàwn zawlah leh kan in chungahte hian vur hi a khawn iaih uaih ṭhin a, kàwn chùan hnuai zawk zawlah chuan vûr a tlak chhah deuh ṭhin a vangin naupangte chuan raw phel in vûr kan dawh ṭhin a, zing khuante vaivikah kan vûr dawh chu la in kan inah kan ṭhian zaho chuan zing khaw vawt em emah vûr chu kan thial ri ràwp rawp ṭhin a, a chang leh kan in zakzeh siak bawk ṭhin. Khang hun lai khan pheikhawk bun tur pawh kan la nei silova, zing khuante vaivikah, vûr zingah kan pên thawih thawih a, vawt kha kan hre lo nge, vawt kha engahmah kan ngailo zawk tih pawh ka hre thiam tawh love. Chu mi kàwnah chuan sephungte kan lai ṭhin a, kan inthlang kawngah sephung chu kan insiktir ṭhin a, “Ui êk leh mihring êk ami sephung chu a huai duh,” te kan la ti tak deuh deuh a.

Ka Pu (ka Nu pa) min hrih dan chuan, “Hmanlai chuan chu kàwnah chuan chhim lam tlang leh hmar lam tlang aṭanga fur laia tuihawk rawn luang chu rei tak thlengin atling pung ṭhin a, chutah chuan ramsa chitin rengte hi an rawn inbual ṭhin a ni,” a ti.

Christmas rim alo nam tawh chuan Bualpui khaw dâiah chuan tlangsam pâr leh tlaizawng pâr a vul chek chuk ṭhin a, tlaizawng pâr tlan savate chu sairawkherh hmang thiam deuhte chuan chăngin ipte âng hmawk hmawkin sava chi hrang hrang an rawn àk haw lawih ṭhin a ni. Chung tlaizawng pâr te chuan Biakin kan chei chuai ṭhin.

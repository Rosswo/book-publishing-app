BUNG 24

AN SAKHUATE

  1. Budhist sakhua :

Kan unai Miria te hi keini Mizo te ang bawk a ramhuai hlauh vanga thing bul, lung bul leh ramhuai awmna ni a an hriat apiang a inthawina hlan chawt mai ṭhin a ni a. Mahse, an thlang tlak lai in Budhist sakhaw betu Mawk ho nen khawimaw lai laiah an lo inawmpawlh tawh a vangte pawh a ni maithei, Budhist sakhaw zuituah an inchhal ve tlat mai a ni.

Mahse Miria Budhist a inchhal atamzawkte hi chuan Budhist sakhua hi hriat chian em em pawh an tum chuang lova, chuti vakin an ngaihsak em em hek lo. Ramhuai biak hi an dah pawimawh em em ber mai hian a lang. Budhist sakhaw hunpui “Changkran pawi” an tih pawh Mawk ho in ni 7 lai an hman lai in Miria te chuan ni khat chauh an hmang a, atamzawk chu chawhnu lamah chuan an haw duak ṭhin a, mi tam zawk phei chuan sakhaw kûtpui an hmang a ni tih pawh an inhre hran chuang lova, nawmsip bawlna leh huau huau satliah-ah an ngai ni in a lang mah zawk.

Mithi thlarau an ngaihdanah chuan Budhist rin dan ‘Pian nawn’ na hi an ring em em mai a. Ṭhenkhat phei chuan an ring Advance leh lutuk a, tar tawh tak, ṭhenkhatte chu an thih hma hma in an faah rawn piang nawn lehah an ngai hial a ni. Hei hi kan khua a putar, Pu Klanguai a a thih hma in kan khaw bul Kurang khua a naupiang pakhat chu kan khua a Pu Klanguaia a rawn piang a ngai in an fa hmingah chuan an Pu Klanguaia hming chu an lo phuah hmanhmawh a ni.

An fa rawn piangte hi engemaw a vanga an ṭah vak chuan “Awn ming” an ti a, hming a ngen ni in an ngaih a vangin ramhuai puithiam hnenah an va kal a, ramhuai puithiam chuan ai a lo en sak a, ai a en na hming chu “Howngpui” an ti. A ai en dan chu, ramhuai puithiam chuan lasai ngul hi ahruiah a chelh a, tichuan mithi tawh hming a sawi dun dun a, a hming tur tak a rawn sawi a nih chuan lasai ngul chu a rawn inthên dawrh dawrh a, lasai ngul vauntu pawh chu a khur thei hial ṭhin. Chu hming chu naute hming a an han phuah chuan naute ṭap pawh chu a bang nghal hmiah zel e an ti.

Milem pathian an be lai

Mi an thih hian an hmaiah emaw, an chalah emaw belmang an tât ṭhin a, chu chu chhinchhiahna “Mâtlawi” an ti a, naute a lo piang a, kutchuk a lo awm hian athih lai a an chhinchhiahna ‘Mâtlawi’ a rawn pu niah an ngai. Mithi chu a dam lai a miṭha chu miṭha leh hausa-ah rawn piang a, mi sual chu misualah emaw, ramsa emaw ranah emaw rawn piang nawn leh turah an ngai. Ṭhenkhat chuan mithite thlarau hi khawiah emaw awmkhawmah an ngai a, ṭhenkhat chuan khawvelah hian vak vel mai mai a ngaite pawh an awm bawk.

Budish puithiam (miria)

Hnam tin mai hian Pathian hi engemaw ti hriat hriat hi chuan kan hre vek mai a, a zawn pawh kan zawng ṭheuh bawk. Miria te pawh hian Pathian hi chu an hre em em a, Pathian hming pawh an lam zing hle ṭhin. Mahse Pathian hnen a kalna kawng an hriat loh a vangin an hriatna kawng kawngah an kal ve mai ni in ka hria. Pathian hnen thlen theihna kawng dik an hriat loh a vangin kal ngaihna an hre lo a, chuvangin setana (ramhuai) hi an tlawn ringawt ni ber in a lang.

Mire hnam zingah hian Budhist bante(Puithiam) pa hnih khat chu an awm ve tawh. Kan sawi tawh ang khan Miria te hian ramhuai hi an hlau em em mai a, chuvangin ramhuai awmna ni a an ngaih a piangah chuan inthawina an hlan chawt zel mai ṭhin. Mithi an hlauh ṭhin em a vangin an chhungkaw zinga mi pawh thi se ṭhenkhat chuan an hlau tho ṭhin.

Hetia ramhuai an hlauh em vangte pawh a ni mahna, ramhuai thawm sawi tur leh thlahrang sawi tur an hre thei khawp mai. Ramhuai pawh hian a hnen a an inthawi dan a dik loh chuan zahngaihna leh khawngaihna nei miah lovin chawplehchihin a tisualtu chu a tihlum mai ni in thenkhat chuan an sawi.

  1. Krama sakhua Krama sakhaw tihchhuaktu Menleh

Krama sakhua hi anmahni Mire tlangval Menleh tihchhuah a ni a. Menleh hi kum 1965 khan a piang a, a pa chu Mensing a ni a, a nu chu Tumte a ni. A ni hi an unau zinga a upa ber a ni a, a unaute chu Kirown leh Menia te an ni. Menleh hi khawlai fa a ni a, Menleh nu hian raipuar chungin pasal a nei a, a pasal Mensing inah Menleh hi a piang a ni.

Bung hmasa lamah kan sawi tawh ang khan Miria te hian sawn an pâi chuan an fapai chu an nu leh pa te in a hrin ai chuan pasal neih thuai a, an pasalte in a hrin chu an tih dan phung a ni a, mipate pawhin anmahni fa ni kher lo pawh hmeichhia rai thei tih chiangsa neih chu an hnualsuat miah lo mai ni lovin nau nei thei tih a a chian sa a vangin an duh zawk emaw tih mai tur a ni tih kan sawi tawh kha.

Menleh-a hi kum 13 mi a nih in Bandarban khawpui bul lawk Sualok a Murung Complex-ah chuan aṭhiante chu sikul an kal a, a ni pawh chuan aṭhiante nen a sikul kal chu a chak ve thei em em ṭhin a, mahse a nu leh pa te chuan a sikul kal chu an phal tlat lo mai a. Atawpah chuan a nu leh pa te phal lo chung chu sikul-ah chuan a kal ve ta a, an khua aṭangin Sualok a an sikul kalna chu a hla vak lova.

Apa phal lo chung sikul a kal luih a vang chuan a pa chu Menleh chungah chuan a thintim em em mai a, a tuithlar hial nghe nghe a. Mahse Menleh chuan lehkha chu a zir lui zel thova. A sikul kalnaah te chuan naupang ṭha tak a ni a, midang ṭanpuite chu nuam a ti hle ṭhin a, thil lem han ziak neuh neuhte hi nuam a ti hle bawk, keimah ngei pawhin a sava lem ziahte pawh, aṭhianpa kan khua Empu a mi Ruiianga hnen a ṭangin ka hmu ve a ni.

Pawl ruk a nih chuan a mizia chu a lo dang ta a, amah chauhva thil ziah neuh neuh chu a ching ta em em mai a, chung a thil ziakte chu Mire hawrawp a lo ni a, achang leh a ṭhian te’n an en dik pui a, tun a kan Pastor nita Iowngrung-a phei chuan a hawrawp siamah hian a pui nasa hle ni in Iowngrung chuan a sawi.

Kum 1985 chuan aṭhiante rin phak bâk in a lo danglam a, sakhua hial a lo siamchhuak a, chu a sakhaw siamchhuah chu, ‘Krama di’ ti in a vuah a ni. Hetia a sikul kal lai a sakhaw thar a siamchhuah thu leh Mire hawrawp a siamchhuah thu a sawi hian ṭhenkhat in an ngaih thutak em em lai in ṭhenkhat chuan an ngai thutak em em lem lova.

Kum 1986 aṭang chuan a sakhaw thar siamchhuah leh Mire hawrawp a siamchhuah chu mite zirtir turin an sikul a ṭang chuan a chhuak a, “Keini Miria te hi Kristian-ah pawh kan awm tak tak thei lova, Budhist tak tak lah kan ni thei hek lo. Eng sakhua mah bulfuk taka biak leh zui kan neih loh a vangin kan tan chuan chhandamna hmuh a har em em a, chuvang chuan kan hnam chhandam turin Pathian in kei hi min rawn tir a ni. Hman a Pathian in hnam tinte tan a hawrawp a sem lai a kan hawrawp bawng in min ei sak kha ka hnenah Pathian in min pe let leh a, chuvangin bawng chhun a ngai tawh lovang……..,” ti in a sakhaw hmuhchhuah thar chu a chipui Miriate hnenah chuan a han zir tir ṭan ta a.

Mi tam tak chuan an lo la thutak em em mai a, chutiangte a nih tak a vangin a apa chuan Menleh chu a fa ang a a lâk luh leh theih nan an hnam dan in vawk a chhun a, tichuan Menleh chu an inah a pa fapa angin a haw leh thei ta a. (Mire danah tuithlar tawh fa a la lut leh tur chuan vawk chhun a ngai a) Mite chuan chu sakhaw thar chu an bawh ruih ruih ta mai a, Pathian in a pek, an hawrawp bawng in a ei tak; Menleh hmanga Pathian in a rawn pe leh ni a an hriatna chuan chu sakhaw thar ‘Krama di’ sakhua leh Menleh chhandamtu a an rinna chu a tih ngheh phah hle mai a. Kar loah chu sakhaw thar chuan Miriate awmna tawh phawt chu a fan chhuak ta mai a, a zuitute pawh an pung chak em em a ni.

Hemi kum 1986 hian a sakhaw siamchhuah thar chu zirtir in a zin kual a, Burma ah a chho nghal a, Burma a achhoh hma in, “Ni chhuahna lamah ka kal dawn a, kumnga hnuah ka lo kir leh a nga, keimah min ringtute chu ka rawn chhandam vek ang,” a ti a. Tichuan Burma ah chuan a chho ta a, tun thleng hian a lo let leh tawh lo a ni.

Menleh akalbo hnu hian a sakhaw thar siam Krama sakhaw hruaitute chuan Menleh sawi angin thu tam tak an phuah belh a, kan Bible a thu ṭha tak tak; Isua sawite, Pawla sawi te hi an copy a Menleh sawi ang mai in an sakhaw bu-ah te an ziak a, tin sakhaw chitin mai hi an ringin an la kual a, chung an lak zingah chuan Hindu Sakhaw thawnthu Rama leh Sita thawn thu phei chu an sakhua emaw tih mai turin an sakhaw kûtpui leh hun pawimawhahte chuan an sakhaw hruaitu te’n tlaivar ṭhak ṭhakin an sawi in a ngaithlatu te pawhin an ngaithla peih a, a film pawh an chhuah nasa thei khawp mai.

Budhist sakhua lah ni se, Changkran, a Budhist ho in an pathian lim chibai bukna-ah lah, milem pathian chibai buk chu a phur phur an ni in an sakhaw hruaitu te lah Budhist bante (puithiam) ang hrim in an milem pathian bulah an tâp chawt a, milem pathian chibai buktute malsawmna lo pek chu a phur phurah an la ṭang zui lehnghal.

Inthawihna lah ni se, Budhist a an awm lai a an inthawihna chu “Changnat” an ti a, mahse Krama sakhua a an awm chuan ramhuai hnen a inthawihna tho ni si in a hming an thlak a, “Baingnia” an ti thung a, ‘baingnia’ tih hi kan awmna lam vai ṭawng a ni a, ‘changnat’ tih nen thuhmun tho a ni. Ramhuai hnen a inthawina tho a ni. A hming an ti danglam ringawt a ni.

Kristian tihdan lak lamah ni se, kan sawi tawh ang khan kan Bible a mi thu ṭha tak tak la in an sakhaw buah Menleh ziak angin an ziak a, Johan 3:19 “…eng khawvelah a lo kal a,, nimahsela mi te’n eng aiin thim an duh zawk…..” tih te leh Johana 1:11 “Amite hnenah a lo kal a, ama mi te’n an lo lawm si lo.” tih te leh a dangte pawh an sakhaw bu-ah an ziak nual a ni. Menleh ama mite chhandam turin pathian in a rawn tir a, mahse tam tak in an rin ve loh a vangin he thu hi Menleh-a’n ama chungchang asawina angin an ziak a ni. Pathian ni an serh ve a, nilai zan leh inrin ni zan pawh an inkhawm ve a, nilai zan phei chuan nupa pawh nupa nun hman thiang loah an ngai a ni.

Ṭum khat chu an sakhaw hruaitu pakhat in Bible chhiar a chak thu sawi in Bible min dil a, pek ngei ṭha ka ti in a remchan hun a ka pek tur thu ka sawi a, kan Evangelist pakhat chuan, “Pe reng reng suh, an ni hi chu Baibul pek a ṭha miah lo a nia, a chhung thu ṭha ṭha hi an ru chhuak a, an Krama sakhaw hmuchhuaktu Menleh sawi angin an ziak zel a,” a ti a, ka pe duh ta lo a ni.

Menleh-a hawrawp siam chu anmahni Krama sakhaw hawrawp bikah an ngai a, han tlangzarh vak pawh an phal lo a, mahse Mire hawrsawp siam chhuah a nih a vangin Mire hnam zinga lehkha thiam deuh leh Mire hnam Leader te chuan ‘Mire hawrawp’ ti a Miria zawng zawng zir theih a siam an duh hle a, Sorkar leh N.G.O. hrang hrang pawhin an ṭanpui viau na in Krama sakhua a chhuah tantirh a an zir uar viau ang kha chuan mi te’n an ngaihsak lutuk vak tawh lo.

Ṭhenkhat in min hrilh dan chuan, “Menleh awm lai kha chuan Krama hawrawp hi kan zir rei ngai lova, reilote kan zir chuan kan thiam zung zung zel mai a ni,” an ti. Menleh chungchangah hian a mah ringtute chuan achanchin mak tak tak sawi tur an hre tam khawp mai. Krama sakhaw zuitu a an hruaitute chuan an chhandamtu in, “Kum nga hnuah ka rawn let leh a nga, min ringtute chu ka rawn chhandam vek ang,” a ti a.

Mahse tun thleng a a lo la rawn lan si loh a vangin a awmna tur ni a an ngaihte leh thil dang tam tak pawh an zeldin na leh an rindante chu an mi leh sa te hnem nan an thutak sawi hmiah hmiah mai a, an mi leh sa te lah chuan thil nithei a nih leh nihloh lam ngaihtuah miah lovin an sakhaw hruaitute sawi a nih chuan dawt ngialngan tak tak pawh an awih em em zel mai lehnghal a. Hmelma pa setana hian chuan kan unau Miriate hi chu a vahan bum kowi nasa tehlul em!!

An sakhaw hruaitute chuan an mite hnenah chuan hetiang te hian an sawi a, “Tunah hian Menleh chu khawvel a tlang sang berah pathian hnen a chakna dil a ṭawngṭai in a awm a, a ṭawngṭaina tlang san zawng chu ni nen a inchen a, chutah chuan pangpar a vul chuk a,” ti te in an mite an hrilh in an mitthla dante chu a lem mawi fahran lem lo hian an ziak kur ngiai nguai a ni.

Menleh-a chu khawchhak lam aṭangin sakawr var chunga chuangin a rawn kal a nga, a rualin mi pahnih sakawr chung a chuang bawkin an rawn kal ve a nga, mahse, amah chu hriat theihna riau awm turin an sawi. Arawn kal hun chuan khawvelah indopui a lo thleng a nga, Menleh chuan khawvel a hnam zawng zawng chu a hneh vek a nga, khawvelah hian mi tlemte an dam ang.

Menleh a rawn kal hun chuan hmanlai a mithi tawh, thingah leh lung a chang tawhte chu mihringah an chang leh vak a nga, chungte chu anmahni lam ṭang vek turin an sawi. Chuvang chuan lui a an kalte hian lung chung a ṭhut emaw, thawmhnaw dah emaw an duh lo, mihring a lo chang leh tura an ngaih vang a ni.

Menleha’n asakhaw zuitute a zirtir dan chu; Ramhuai hnenah inthawih loh tur. Inthawihna sa ei loh tur. Pathian ni serh tur. Pathian niah chuan ran pawh thah emaw hralh loh tur. Pathian ni zan, Nilai zan leh Inrinni zanahte chuan nupa pawh nupa nun hman loh tur. Zu in loh tur. Thawmhnaw inbel lova awm loh tur. Hmeichhia pawh pawnfen fual fen a mipa pawh lawnggi var veng a puan var bah tawh tur. Mipa chuan sam chhipah zial vek tur.

Heng lo pawh hi thil tam tak a zirtir a, a awm tawh loh hnu in an sakhaw hruaitute chuan an sakhaw dan leh dunte chu an tidang lam zing thei hle mai a. Menleh chu a awm tawh bawk si lova, an sakhaw hruaitute chuan an dan sawi leh an rin dan pawh a dang nuk hlawm mai. He sakhua-ah hian Miria nilo Khumi hnamte pawh an awm ve bawk.

Hetia Mire sakhaw thar a lo chhuah lai hian Kristian Missionary te’n Mire ram an chhuahsan duak lai tak a ni a. Krama sakhua a lo chhuah hma hi chuan NEIG Missionary te leh Voluntary Missionary te an lo awm tawh ṭhin a. Miria te chuan Kristian chu an lo hmelhriat ve nial nual tawh a. Hetia Krama sakhua a lo chhuah hian Mire ram-ah chanchinṭha hriltu hun rei tak an awm tawh loh a vangin an nun chu a ruakin an khawhar lai tak a ni a.

Ramhuai hnen a inthawih an lo ning em em tawh bawk si a, an nun in Pathian an zawn a an nun ruah lai tak chuan hmelmapa Setana chuan Miria te thinlung ruakah chuan dik awm tak a lang, mahse a tawp thihna nisi in a bum thlu ta a ni. Hmelmapa Setana chuan Mire ho te awlsam tak a a bumna thang a an awh theih nan Mire hawrawp, hnam dang ṭawng ziah fiah chu sawi loh, an ṭawng pawh ziak fiah vek hleihtheih loh a pe tel ngei bawk a.

Chu sakhaw rin dan leh inzirtir dante, sakhua a lo chhuah dante, eng thlarau berin nge thawk tih pawh hre lo leh ngaihtuah thiam lote pawhin Menleha’n hawrawp a han siam chhuak hrim hrim mai pawh chu mak an ti in chu sakhua chu an rin phah em em a ni. Menleh chanchin te chu “An ti” tih ringawt hian mak tak takin an sawi ṭhin a. “Mire sakhua a ni,” an han tih ringawt pawh chuan an bàwr nasa in sakhaw thar leh hawrawp an nei chu an lawm mai nilovin an inchhuang zawk emaw tih mai tur a ni.

Chu sakhaw thar chu Kristian zirtirna zulzui a nih a vangin an hnen a Pathian thu sawi pawh a har em em mai a, han sawi pawh ni ila, “Krama leh Kristian te chu kan inang reng a lawm, Menleh pawh Isua ang tho a lawm,” an ti a. Krama sakhaw hruaitu te an ṭawngṭai chuan rulpui tar dakamang (Dragon) hming lam lo chuan an ṭawngṭai an tawp thei lo a ni.

An sakhaw hunpui te lah chu lawilo dawrpui ang mai in pawisa khelh, zu zawrh leh nula leh tlangvalte nuam tawlna hmun hi a ni hawt mai a. An pathian thu sawi leh an khawmpui han hmuh chuan an lainat awmin hmelmapa setana sual zia leh fin vervek zia hi a lang chiang hle a ni. Hetiang tak a Setana’n a abum tawh te Krista hnen a hruai chhuak turin Lalpa’n mi ti mauh si a. A va han harsa ṭhin tak em.

Menleh hian a sakhaw thar chanchin mite hnen a sawi tur a a zin chhuak chu Burma a Mire ho awmna te a thleng a, Burma a Mire ho te pawh chuan an lo lawm em em a, sakhaw thar an nei te, hawrawp thar an neite chu an lawm em em a. Menleh chuan Burma a amah ringtu, a sakhua zuitute zingah chuan hruaitute a ruat a, chu a hruaitu ruat chu Menlung a ni a. Menleh chu chumi hmun aṭang pawh chuan a chhuak leh a.

Kum nga hnuah amah ringtute chhandam turin a rawn kir leh tur thu leh a rawn kal dawn chuan khawvelah indona a thlen dawn thu te, chu indonaah chuan mitam tak an thih dawn thu te a sawi a vangin Burma a Krama sakhaw hruaitu atan a a ruat Menlung chuan, “Menleh a rawn kal dawn hnai tawh a, khawvel a buai dawn a, chuvang chuan tar ho chu in dam reng chuan a buai thlak,” ti in tar 13 lai mai chu a that a, chung a tar thahna hmun chu, Burma Rakhaing (Mawk) State a Mire khua Ramdawng khua ah tar 2. Plaksai khua ah tar 4, Naingsik khuaah tar 5, Tawngsawng kriah tar 2 a that a ni. Amah Menlung pawh chu tar a thah chiam a vang chuan Lawnghina’n a kap hlum ve nghal a ni.

Menlunga’n tar athah chiam a vangin chung a tar thah chhungte leh mi engemaw zat chu Bangladesh ah an rawn tlanchhia a, chung rawn tlanchhia te nen chuan Bangladesh Burma ram dep lam Bawrawbiti lam a Pathian thu sawi a kan kal lai in kan in hmu a, an ni chuan Menlung chan chin leh Menleh thih dante hetiang hian min hrilh a, “Krama sakhaw tichhuaktu Menleh kan khua a a rawn kal lai in a thu sawi chu kan awih em em a, keini Miria te chu Krama sakhua ah chuan kan awm a, Menlunga chu Krama sakhua a hruaituah a dah a……..Menleh chu hmun dangah a kal leh a, chuta a kalna ah chuan Burma sipai te’n zin theihna tur phalna lehkha (Permition) an lo dil a.

A ni chuan, ‘Pathian ka ni a, ka mi te zawng zawng chhandamtu tur ka ni a, ka kal zel a ngai,’ a ti a, Burma sipaite chuan, ‘Phalna lehkha tel lova kal luih i tum a nih chuan kan kap mai ang che, a kal theih loh,’ an ti a. A ni chuan, “pathian ka ni a, min kap mah ula nithum hnuah ka lo tho leh thovang,’ a ti a. Menleh chu Burma sipai chuan an kap a, a thi a, an camp thlangah an phum a, chuta ṭang chuan a tho leh tawh lo a ni ang, khawiah mah amah hmu an awm tawh lo a ni,” ti in min hrilh. Krama sakhaw hmuchhuaktu Menleh a thih dan min hrilhtu zing a a hming ka la hriat chu Kawngbawr Taisang hnam, Hungkut khua a mi a ni. A ni hian Menlunga’n hian tar 13 laia athah bâkah piangsual te pawh a thah thu min hrilh bawk.

Krama sakhaw zuitu zingah hian Menlung bâkah eng thlarau emaw chang a chanchin maksak leh nuihzat thlak tak tak sawi tur nei an awm nual bawk a. Chung an chanchinah chuan thil mak angreng tak tak leh nuihzat thlak tak tak pawh a awm nual bawk. Chungte chu a ruhlang rawt a sawi dawn chuan sakhuana lam thilte a nih a vangin sawi vek kher chu a ṭha lo zawk a ka rin avangin ka sawi vek loh a pawi lo zawk ang.

Krama sakhaw zuitu zinga chanchin neitham leh maksak zualte:

  1. Sowidowi Lawngmadi : A ni hi Krama sakhaw ti chhuaktu Menleh tih lohah chuan pathian ang hial a an biak leh chibai an buk ṭhin a ni a. Hetia Krama sakhaw zuitu te’n pathian ang hial a an ngaih a an hmuhna a pianga ṭhingṭhi a, bungbu kawt kawt a chibai an buk ṭhin a vang hian amah pawh hi pathian ang hialin a inngai tawh ni in a lang. A ni pawh hian a mah chibai buktute chu a kut pakhat emaw, pahnih emaw phar chhuakin mal a lo sawm zel ṭhin. Ram hnim te hi mal a sawm chuan damdawiah chang vek thei-in an ngai a ni.

A awmna hi ka awmna Empu a ṭanga hla vak lo Hawncha khua a ni a, an khu a Pathian thu sawi a ka kal hi chuan an sakhaw chanchin zir chian ka duh a vangin ka va tlawh ve ṭhin a, “Unaupa,” ti in kan inko ṭhin a. Vawikhat chu Sowidowia hian, “Unaupa, India Thailand ah min hruai teh, kei chu motor ka chuang ve ngai lova, thlawhna in min hruai a ngai ang, min hruai a nih chuan mal ka sawm ang che nga, mal ka sawm che chuan ram khat lal i ni dawn a ni,” min ti a. kei chuan, “Unaupa, Eng vangin nge Thailand-ah chuan kal i duh a?” ka ti a.

  1. Paubawng mala (Nula Chamleh)

Kum 1998 Changkran (an kum thar lawm ni) ah a ṭhiante nen Rumthu khua aṭangin Changkran kûtpui an hmanna ṭhinna Ngaringchah khua ah chuan an kal ve a, an haw lam a an khua an thlen dawn chuan a ni chu ramhnuai-ah a kal a, a ṭhiante chuan an hawsan a. A ni chu amahin ramhnuaiah zan khat a riak a. Atuk a khua a rawn haw chuan thlarau pawlna a chang a, pawisa puk a hmuh thute a sawi a.

Chuta ṭang chuan a khattawk in nikhaw hre lovin a awm ṭhin a. Chutia nikhaw hre lova a awm a lo harh chuan a thlarau chu vanah a han chho ṭhin a, Menleh a hmuh thute a sawi ṭhin a, chutiang a a awm chuan arsi nula hovin làm an lo zirtir ṭhin a, chu làm chu Krama sakhua hian lam an neih loh a vanga an sakhaw làm a nih thute a sawi a. Chuvangin Krama sakhaw zuitu nula leh tlangval te chu lam a zirtir ṭhin a. Krama sakhaw zuitute chuan an ngaisang em em mai a.

Krama sakhaw hmuchhuaktu Menleh farnu angin a tir chuan a insawi a, Menleh-a nu leh pa awmna Kruknua khua-ah chuan a zu kal a, Menleh-a nu leh pa hnenah chuan Menleh farnu a nih thu leh Menleh awm lai a zawhte dum an neih tawh thute a sawi a, Menleh-a nu leh pa chu, “Ka nu, ka pa,” ti in a kova. Menleh a nu leh pa te hnen a a awm lai khan zawhte dum an neih thute a sawi a, a sawi ang chuan Menleh-a, a nu leh pa te hnen a a awm lai chuan zawhte dum chu an lo nei tak tak a ni awm e. Chuta ṭang phei chuan mite chuan Chamleh thu sawi apiang chu an ring em em vek tawh mai a. Menleh-a nu leh pa te pawh chuan an fanu angin an lo pawm a. Ahming thar a tan, “Paubawng Mala” ti in hming thar mawi tak an lo phuah sak a, chu hming awmzia chu, “Pangpar zeh mawi lanu” tihna a ni.

Chuta ṭang chuan khaw tinah a lamin, làmte a zirtir ṭhin a. (kei pawh in a lam lai chu ka va en ve nghe nghe a) Alam dan chuan mu leh mal a neih loh em a vangin mite chuan han zir ve ṭhin mahse an lam rual thei ngai reng reng lo mai a, lam zirtute chu a lam ang a an lam thiam loh a vangin ṭawng zahmawh pui pui in a hrawk hrep ṭhin a, mahse tumah an thinrim ngam ngai lo.

Paubawng Mala chu a hmelṭha in apian a nalh a, ṭawngkam athiam em em bawk a, zai pawh athiam in a aw pawh a ṭha phian a, changkran pawi (Kum thar kût) te hian a zai nilen ṭhak chang te a awm thin, mipui tam takin a zai an ngaithla ṭhuap ṭhin. Mi hip theihna riau pawh a nei emaw tih tur hial a ni. Chutia a rawn lar chhuah chuan Krama zuitu pawh an pun phah in an sakhaw thar chuan a ṭhanduan phah hle mai a.

Akalna apiangah apui a nawi in an zui duah duah reng mai a, kawng a a kalte hian a hnungah chiah chuan an sakhaw hruaitu te’n an zui a, chumi hnuah midangin an rawn zui leh a, a kal kawi leh an kawi ve zel a, an inzui kawi nguah nguah a, Paubawng Mala zuitute chu an intlar thui thei ṭhin khawp mai. An sakhaw biakin thar hawnna apiangahte chuan lam turin an sawm ṭhin.

Tunah chuan Krama sakhua pawhin rawih lam a pan in Krama sakhaw zuitute aṭang pawhin mi engemaw zat chu Kristian-ah an rawn lut tawh a, abik takin thalaite zingah Kristian a rawn lut an tam tawh hle a ni. Ṭhenkhat chu an hnu ngai, a angial angan a ramhuai biakna lamah an kir leh nual tawh bawk a ni. An sakhua hian innghahna bulfuk tak nei lovin sakhaw hrang hrang an pawt lawr a, bâkah an sakhaw hruaitu ṭhen khatte sumah leh hmeichhiat mipatna lamah an hmingchhiat ṭhin a vangte in tam tak chu he sakhaw zuitute hi an beidawng tawh a ni.

Heng kan unau hmelmapa Setana bum mek, hmelmapa Setana’n kawng dik zawh ni ang a a inngaih tir a, a zial ât tlat te hi Lalpa’n chhanchhuah a duh a, an ni chhanchhuak tur hian kei ni Zofate hi Lalpa’n min duh a ni. An hnen a kal in emaw, ṭawngṭai na in emaw, thilpek emaw talin kan unau Miriate saltan na a ta hruaichhuak turin ṭan kan lâk ṭheuh hi a va han ṭul tak em.

BUNG 19

RAMHUAI NI A AN NGAIH LEH AN NIHNATE

Kan unau Miriate hian ramhuai hi an hlau em em mai a, an nun hi ramhuai hlauhna chhum thim chhah takin a khuh lúk hi a ni ber e. An khawngaihthlak in chhanchhuah thuai thuai an ngai tak zet a ni. Ramhuai ni a an ngaih leh huai nei ni a an ngaih pawh a tam hle a. Hlauh tur an ngah em a vangin an nun a zalen lo em em a. Ramhuai ni a an ngaih hming nei bikte kan han tar lang a nga, a hming an phuah sen loh, “Chumi huai, khami huai,” an tih chu kan ziak lang vek seng dawn lo a ni.

  1. Chowngchawi : Mizo-in ‘Lasi’ kan tih ang deuh hi a ni a, mihringte a zawl ṭhin a, hmeichhiat mipatnarte pawh hmanpui ṭhin ni in an sawi. A mi zawlte chuan a lung an tihawi loh chuan tihhlum mai pawh a hmang ni in an sawi. Chu a zawlte chuan an Chowngchawi zawl tan chuan kum khatah vawikhat bawng chhun in Chowngchawi hnen a inthawihna an hlan ṭhin tur a ni.

Ramhuai dangte angin thiltihtheihna a nei lo a ni ang, chuvangin a zawl a te awlsam tak a malsawm theihna a neih loh a vangin mi hmuh loh lai in a zawl a te ri a mi buh a ruk sakin a zawl a te buh dahnaah a dah ṭhin a vangin Chowngchawi zawlte nen a inri emaw, an lo ep a lo neih chu tumah in an duh ṭhin lo.

A pian hmang ni a an sawi dan chu, mihring hmel ang tho an pu a, khamahte an cheng ṭhin ni in an sawi. Achang chuan an bawng chhunnaah te pawh an lo tel ve ṭhin a, an bawng chhunna a an lo tel ve chuan an kham tur a an ngaih a ruai sa chu a zo duak duak a, an kham ta ṭhin lo a ni. Chowngchawi zawltu in a zawl Chowngchawi tan a bawng a chhun chuan Chowngchawi chu ti hian hla in a ko ṭhin:

Uadi ü, ua da òw,

Uadi saiche rawng phua towm ü, towm ü,

Uadi saiche rawng phua towm ü, towm ü, ti in.

  1. Uimukmama : Hei hi Mizo in Phung kan tih kha a ni a, Apianhmang chu hmeichhia ang, hmui zum rut leh lian tak a ni. Mit lian tak a ni bawk a, a ni hian mihringte a tih buai dan chu, Mi mut lai in a lo hmet ṭhin a, chung a mi hmeh a te chu “Maimi zial” kan tih ang hian an awm ṭhin a, engemaw chang phei chuan mi kawchhungah te pawh lut ṭhin ni in an ngai.
  2. Chungkruh : Khawhring kan tih ang hi a ni a, “Langke chungkru” an ti deuh kher ṭhin, chu chu “Bawm Khawhring” tihna a ni. Bawm ho zingah khawhring hi atam bik an ti a. Khawhring nei a an ngaihte chu an haw em em a, tihhlum ngei an duh a, an tihhlum tak sawi tur pawh an awm nual reng a ni.
  3. Saingphak : He ramhuai hi mi seh ṭhin a an ngaih a ni a, mi tihthaih pawh a ching niin an ngai. Dawithiam te’n dawi in an ko chhuak ṭhin a, an mi huat zawngte an seh hlum tir ṭhin.
  4. Sitsi : Hei hi mi in a huat zawng nghaisa tur leh ti hlum tura ramhuai an khawi ṭhin a ni a. Dawi lehkha hmangin hetiang ramhuai hi an khawi ṭhin. Mi dawi nan hian hawrawp hman bik an nei a, chu an hawrawp hman ṭhin chu Kawl hawrawp hi a ni. He ramhuai hi dawi lehkha kawltu hnen apiangah a awm ṭhin. He ramhuai khawitu ni a an hriat pawh hi an haw em em a, tih hlum ngei an duh ṭhin.
  1. Măt : Hei pawh hi Lasi kan tih nen a inang deuh a, ramsa zawng zawng enkawltuah an ngai. An pianhmang chu mihring ang tho a ni a, mahse mihring ai chuan a te zawk daih a ni. Ramsa lian deuhte chu a zui ṭhin a, engemaw chang phei chuan sapeltu te’n a kehniak an hmuh ṭhin thu an sawi. Engemaw chang phei chuan thangawkte a lo ru ṭhin a, achang chuan ram kalte hi a hruaibo ṭhin nite in an sawi.
  2. Mururowng : Heng ramhuaite hi mihring angin khua te an nei ve a, achang chuan bawngte pawh an chhun ve ni in an sawi. Mihring mithmuhah an inlar ngai vak lova, an bawng chhun a an lam leh an hlim thawmte, an naupang rak ri chel chulte chu hriat tur a awm ṭhin.

Miria, bawng chhun a lam tur te’n an tumphung an indaih lo a nih chuan Mururowng ho hnenah inthawihna hlan in chung an thil mamawh chu Mururowngte hnenah an hawh thei nite pawh in an sawi. Mihring an tibuai ve ngai lova, achang chuan an bawng chhunna a puithiam hna thawk turin mihring puithiamte an rawn sawm chang a awm e an ti. Achang chuan bazarahte pawh an kal ve nite pawh in an sawi.

  1. Philu : Hei hi chu ramhuai lian leh koham tak, mihringte pawh ei ṭhin ni in an ngai a, tuifinriatah a cheng deuh ber in an ngai.
  2. Barua :Hei pawh hi dawi leh dawi lehkha hmang a mihringin an khawi chi ni in an ngai a, dawi hmang a ramhuai khawi chi-ah chuan a chak ber ni in an ngai.
  3. Malimala : Hei hi boruak huai a ni a, thli leh, khawchhiat, khawṭhat leh khawkhenah thu nei in an ngai a, a hnenah an inthawih ṭhin.
  4. Krawngma namma nam : Hei hi lei lam malsawmna pe ṭhin tuah leh anchhia pawh lawh theituah an ngai a, a hnenah inthawihna an hlan ṭhin.
  5. Taping kuila : Hei hi tap huai ni in an ngai a, a lan dan chu uipa ang hi a ni. Nau then bawm a nau inthente hi a lo tihṭhaih ṭhin ni in an ngai. Naupang kaihte hi a tihah an ngai a ni.
  6. Dawngtua namma : An in kailawn hi thing pum rek dawh a ni ṭhin a, chu an in a kailawn pawh chuan huai neiah an ngai a, hei hi chu leihlawn huai chu a ni. Mi a tibuai hran lova, achang chuan a inlar ṭhin a, a lan dan chu pitar sam tuak var vek tawh ang hi a ni.
  7. Pungdaka : Hei hi ramhuai zawng zawng a chak berah an ngai.
  8. Phuk : Mizovin “Tulum” kan tih ang hi a ni a, mithi tawh kawchhung a lut in mithi tawh chu a thawh tir leh a, engemaw chang phei chuan mithi tawh ruang hmang chuan mihring la damlaite a ei ṭhin ni in an ngai. Mihring dam laite chu chakna pawh a pe thei ni in an ngai bawk.
  9. Mihring a ramhuai pasarihte : Mihring pakhat zelah hian ramhuai pasarih zel awmah an ngai a, chung ramhuai te chuan mihringte chu karkhat zel an inven chhawk ni in an ngai a. Chung ramhuai pasarih-te zing a pakhat “Ruhu” an tih chuan ni a chang ve lova, chutia a chùan tak a vang chuan mihring vengtu a piang hnenah chuan a lo tel ve zel ṭhin a, chung ramhuai zingah chuan aṭha pakhat chiah a awm a, chu chu “Lümsawilami” an ti. Adang zawng chu thlarau sual vek an ni.

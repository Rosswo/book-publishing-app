Chittagong a damdawi in a ka awm chhung khan thi in ka luak reng a, Doctor te’n en na chi hrang hrang a min en pawhin ka natna chu an hre thei si lova. Farukah chuan kan cham deuh reng a. Ka fanu Mazuali leh a hming phuahtu, a Pi Zawni

Hnahthial a kan awm lai ni 7 April 1994 Ninga ni zing tukṭhuan ei lai a ṭangin ka nupui Zopuii chuan nau a vei ṭan a, Hospital-ah kan tlan chho nghal a, tlai lam dar 5:04 chuan Lalpa’n ka fa tir a tan fanu duhawm leh hmelṭha tak min pe a. Ka nupui Zopuii chuan, “Kan fanu hming chu a Pi Zawni hian phuah rawh se, a ni hi a zaidam em em a, a hmel a ṭha bawk a, alo len hunah pawh a Pi Zawni achhun ngei theih nan,” a ti a. Dt 11 April 1994 Thawhṭan ni zingah ka Nu Zawni chuan ka fanu hming a tan Lalhmangaihzualzeli ti in a sa a. Ka nau te’n, “Ka nu, i hming sak hi a sei lutuk a ni, ‘zel’ tih hi paih mai rawh,” an ti a, ka nu chuan, “Paih love, Lalpa hi kan hmangaih zual zel tur a lawm,” a ti a, a phuah ang chuan a pu hlen ta a ni.

Hnahthial a kan awm lai hian kan fanu Lalhmangaihzualzeli (Mazuali) chu Pathian hnenah kan hlan nghal a, a hlantu Pastor Chalhmingliana a ni. Kan nauhlan lai a nau hlan ve hi an tam hle a, Gospel Cente’nary kum leh Mizo a aw b Cente’nary kum a ni bawk a, kan rual a nau hlan vete nau hmingah chuan Cente’nary a tel nawk hlawm mai.

Hetia Mizoram a kan cham chhung hian Mire hnamin an hnam thlarau bo chhanchhuak tura Mizoram an rawn auhna chuan mite thinlungah nasa takin hna a thawk a, BCM pawh chuan Lalpa kohnaah ngai in an ngaipawimawh em em a ni. BCM Assembly-ah hial lo sawi a, Mire ram a Lalpa rawngbawl chu ṭha an lo ti tlang a, Mire ram a thawh danturah CFB President Rev RT Mawia biak a, a ṭul chuan thawhho pawh pawi an ti lova, Pu Mawia chu rawn be turin kan haw thlak rualin Missionary Lalthawmliana rawn tirh chu an tum ta a ni.

Ka natna Aizawl Civil Hospital a ka inentir chuan TB te a lo intan tir hi a lo ni a. Lalpa zarah ka natna pawhin min kian san tawh a, Mizoramah thla kua zet kan cham hnu in min entu Doctor pawhin, “In haw thei tawh ang, mahse damdawi kha kum khat chhung chu i ei tlingtla ngei ngei tur a ni,” a ti a. Ka fanu chu thla ruk mi lek a nih lai in khaw lum em em lain, hmun lum lehzualah, Lalpa’n awm tura min ruatna hmun Bangladesh Mire ram lam pan chuan kan inpaw thla ve ta hnak hnak a. Kan rual hian BCM in Mire ram a thawh dan tur ah CFB President Rev RT Mawia rawn be tura an mi tirh, Missionary Lalthawmliana chu a rawn kal ve a.

Bangladesh a kal tur chuan Pukzing lamah Sajek a lut thla turin kan kal a, khua a lum em em mai a, kawng lah chu a hnim bawk si a, kan thuam phur Chakma pahnih lah chu kir leh hman an duh avangin an kal chak em em mai a, keini chuan kawng kan hriat siloh a vangin kan thuam phurte chu kan umphak loh chuan kawng kan bo mai kan hlau bawk si a. Chuvangin kan um hlawk hlawk reng mai a, kan chau em em mai a, Sajek New Lawngkawr kan thlen dawn chuan ruahpui vanawnin min nan hrep lehnghal a.

New Lawngkawr ah chuan U Tlangmawia teinah kan thleng a, an ni chuan min lo duat em em mai a, vawk lian zet mai hi min lo talh a, ni thum kan cham a, chutia kan cham chhung chuan kalkawng a khaw lum em em a hah tak a kan kal a vang leh ruappui vanawn in min nan a vangte pawh a ni ang; kan fanu chu thih ngamin a na ta mai a, New Lawngkawr chu Bangladesh thingtlang khaw chengker tak mai a nih a vangin damdawi han lei mai tur lah a awm bawk silova, kan thla aphang tak zet a ni. U Tlangmawia nau Nghakthanga chu tlawmngai in New Lawngkawr a ṭanga nikhat kal zet Tuisen bazar a mi damdawi min zu lei sak a, a ni chu keimahni a vangin a hmanhmawh em avangin a rawn lehlang hman a ni. Kan fanu a dam theihna turin Nghaka chu ava han hah dawn tehlul em.

Kan fanu pawh chu a zia ve leh ta a, Lalpa hruaina in tluang takin Rangamati chu kan zu thleng leh a, hetih lai hian BCM in an biak duh Pu Rualthanmawia chu Rangamati a a awm lai a lo ni hlauh mai a. BCM palai Thawma’n asawi duhte sawi in inkawm na hun ṭha chu an nei thei ta a. Mahse, CFB President chuan thu tawp a sawipui duh tatlat lo mai a, “Han haw leh la, in hotute Dhaka-ah ka hnenah rawn kal rawh se, an rawn kal thei dawn lo a nih chuan min rawn hrilh se la, Calcutta-ah kan inhmu mai dawn nia,” a ti a, Thawma pawh chu a haw chho ve leh ta mai a.

Thawma chuan BCM hotu te hnen a thil awmzia a han hrilh chuan, BCM hotu te –President Rev Raltawnga leh AGS Rev B.Thangchina te chu CFB President Rev RT Mawia hnen a Mire ram rawngbawl dan tur sawipui tur chuan Dhaka a rawn kal an tum a, mahse Culcatta an thlen hnu in engemaw rokhawlhna a awm a vangin Dhaka-ah chuan an kal thei ta lova, Culcatta a ṭang chuan engemaw rokhawlhna a awm avang a Dhaka a an kal theih loh thu leh a theih chuan amah chu rawn kal a Culcutta a in be mai turin Rev RT Mawia chu an phone a, mahse Rev RT Mawia chu Culcutta ah chuan a kal thei leh si lova, an inbe thei lo hlen ta a ni. Chutia an biak tur an biak theih tak lovah chuan BCM hotu te chu Mizoramah an haw leh a, Mire ram a thawhna tur kawng dapin BCM hotute chuan CFB President Rev RT Mawia chu vawi thum lai lehkha an thawn leh a, mahse BCM hotute chuan chhan na vawikhat pawh an hmuh loh avangin Mire ram a thawh dan kawng chu tihngaihna awm ta vak lo ni in a lang leh ta a ni.

Mire ram hi setana kulhpui a ni a, hmelmapa setana chuan a kulhpui ṭhiat a Pathian ram din tur chuan a phal lo em em a ni. Tunhma NEIG Mission hun lai a Mire ho an sak zia te, Voluntary Missionary hun lai a rawngbawlna a sak zia leh Mire ram tak a an zuk luh thlak hleihtheih loh dante leh kei ni AFC Mission Board ngei pawh beidawng a an lo ṭilh zia leh an mi tirhte pawh pakhat mai bâk chu an kir leh vek dante, tunhnu a Mire ram a Mission hrang hrang a awm hnuah pawh thawktu ṭhenkhat chu Pathian ram ti zautu ai a Pathian ram zauna kawng daltu an nih zawk zia zawng zawngte leh tumna tak tak tel lo chuan awlâi takin Mire ramah hian a luh mai theih dawn loh ziate kan hotute hnenah ka sawi ṭhin a.

Kan hotute pawh chuan a tihngaihna tak an hriat loh avangin Mire ram a thawh tumna pawh chu a reh leh ta vang vang rih a. Kum 2000 a lo inher chhuah chuan CFB Mission chuan Mire ram chu hnuchhawnin Bawm ram lam a hawi ta zawk a, Chutia CFB Mission in Mire ram a a rawngbawlna a chawlhsan tak hnu chuan thil ṭul avangin Mizoramah ka chhuak chho a, kan khua Hnahthial ka han thlen chuan kan Kohhran hruaitute chuan kan rawngbawl dante min zawt a, chutia kan ti ti naah chuan Mission ṭanpuitu kan neih tawh loh thute ka sawi a, chu chuan kan Kohhran hruaitute beng a verh hle mai a. Local Church Committee naahte an lo sawi a, a remchan chuan kan Local Church chuan min rawih an duh thute relin a remchan dawn leh dawn loh BCM Head Quarter ah phone in an lo zu zawt a.

Achhan chu Mizoram aṭangin Field dang angin Missionary an in tir thla ve zung zung thei dawn lova, ka thawhpuite an la ve lo a nih chuan Mire ramah keimah chauh chuan engmah ka ni dawn chuang lova, BCM Mission hming a chhiat phah mai mai zawk dawn a ni tihte ka sawi ve nual bawk a. Rev Sangvela leh Rev Ramdinmawia Empu khua-ah

Tichuan ngaihṭha takin Mire ram lam pan chuan ka haw thla leh ta vang vang a. Kan khua ka thlen chuan kan Kohhrante chu thil awmdan kimchang takin ka han hrilh a, ṭhenkhat an lawm hle laiin ṭhen khat chuan rin harsa an la ti hle bawk a. Keini chu, “Mizoram Baptist nen kan inzawm tawh dawn a vangin kan Kohhran hmingah pawh Baptist Church of ‘Maruchah’ “Miria” (BCM) kan intih atha ang,” ka ti a. An ni pawhin tha min tihpui a. Hun a kal zel a, Baptist Church of Mizoram (BCM) chuan an thutiam ang ngei in ni 31 May 2000 Nilaini chuan mi pathum : Promotor R.Zaichhuana, Pu R.Thianghlima Zotlang Mission Committee Member leh Pu Lalngurliana Thenhlum Kohhran Secretary te chu an rawn thleng a. Kan Kohhran chuan kan lo lawm hle mai a, anni pathum chuan rei deuh cham a Mire khaw dang pawh tlawh kual an tum a, mahse anzing a mi Pu R.Thianghlima chu a khua asik ta tlat mai a, hmun dangah pawh kal thei lovin kan khua Empu aṭang chuan an haw leh ta a ni.

Mizoram a ṭanga rawn kal, kan hotute an haw hnu chuan thil ṭul a vangin Mizoram-ah ka kal leh a, hemi ṭum a Mizoram a ka kal hian BCM Head Quarter ka tlawh a, Mire ram a rawngbawlna bulṭan nan kei, Bible Thuthlunghlui lettu, Pastor Chownglowk, Evangelist pahnih leh Evangelist Teacher pakhat te hlawh min rawn pe nghal a, kan lawm takmeuh a ni.

BCM Mission chuan chak zawkin Mire ram rawngbawlnaah hma a la chho zel a. Ni 9 February 2002 Inrinni chuan BCM Associate General Secretary i/c Mission & Evangelism Rev T.C. Laltlawmlova leh Promotor R.Zaichhuana leh Audio Visual – a thawktu Pu Ruata Kawlni te’n min rawn tlawh leh a. Chutah chuan a rammi thawktu min pek belh leh a, kan Hostel naupangte pawh engemawzat min chawm sak bawk a, Hostel cook hlawh pawh min lo pek sak bawk a ni. Heng kan thawktute hlawh min rawn pek sak bâkah pawh hian sum sen ngaihna a piangah a ṭul angin hma an rawn la ta zel a ni. A ṭul angin kan hotute chuan min rawn tlawh reng bawk.

Kum 2004 aṭang phei chuan Field Director fel tak, Bangla thiam em em, kan dinhmun min hrethiam em em tu ni bawk Rev Lalhmunsanga min rawn pe a, a ni chu Chittagongah a awm a, a awmna aṭang chuan min rawn tlawh reng ṭhin a. Mahse kan Field Director chuan Bangladesh a awmreng turin Visa a neih theih tawh loh avang in kum 2005 December thla khan Mizoram lamah min haw san leh a, kei chu Field Incharge in min dah ta a ni.

Kan Field Director Rev Lalhmunsanga chuan Baptist Church of Maruchah (BCM) te hmalam hun min thlirpui a, kan tlin vang nilovin, kan mamawh a vang leh kan inzir chhoh zel nan Baptist Church of Maruchah (BCM) chu Assembly te min siampui a, Kohhran inkaihhruaina bu-te min siampui bawk a, tun thleng hian kum tin BCM rorelna sang ber Assembly chu kan la nei ziah ṭhin a ni.

Kan Kohhran-te hian Pastor niturin min duh thu hi an sawi reng ṭhin a, vawi tam tak Pastor hna thawk turin min ngen ṭhin a, mahse kei chuan, “Pastor ka nih kher a ngai hleinem, ka thiamna pawhin a tlin lova,” ka ti a. Mahse kum 2002 a Mizoram a ṭanga kan hotu BCM AGS Rev T.C. Laltlawmlova te rawn kal khan helam (Mire Baptist Kohhran) a kan Kohhrante chuan kan hotu rawn kalte hnenah chuan keimah Pastor atan a min duh thu leh aṭul zia leh Pastor ka nih thei danturte an lo zawt in an lo sawipui a, kan Pu chuan, “Chutiang tak a in duh a in mamawh a nih chuan Pastor-ah lo la ula, ramthim in la ni a, keini pawh tunhmate chuan Tirhkoh aṭangin aṭul angin Pastor-ah kan in hlangkai zel mai a lawm, amârawh chu keini chuan kan nemnghet thei lovang a, in tih tih in lo ti ula, keinin kan lo hriatpui dawn che u nia,” an ti a.

2004-2005 a kan Field Director Rev Lalhmunsanga te chhungkua

Chutia kan hotu berin a tih takah chuan, ka duh emaw, ka duh lo emaw ngaipawimawh tawh lovin, aṭulna avang zawkin kan Kohhran chuan ni 10 November 2003 Pathian ni a Baptist Church of Maruchah (BCM) khawmpui rorel chuan, “Evan Rosanglura Fanai hi kan Pastor tur a kan duh leh kan mamawh avangin Pastor a la nemngheh tur a tan Probatiobnary Pastorah kan ruat e,” ti in Pro Pastor nihna chu min pe ta a. Ka hnial thei lo na in ka hming bul a Pro-Pastor tih ka han dah ve tur chu ka inthlahrungin ka zak ru hle mai a. Bangladesh ah chuan a zia tak a la awm a, Mizoram a ka kal hunah Pastor atan a min hmuh tlin dawn loh tur ziate ka ngaihtuah in ka zam hle mai a.

Chutia keima lam in en a ka insit em em lai chuan kan Kohhramte chuan, “Kei nin Pastor atan kan duh che a, i duh leh i dil a vanga Kohhran in Pastor a tan ruat che kan nilova, Pastor a tan kan mamawh em vang zawk che a ruat che kan ni, khawi hmunah pawh Pastor i nihna hi zak lovin a ṭul chuan i sawi zel zawk tur a ni, i zah avang a i inzêp a nih chuan Pastor a tana ruattu che Kohhran leh Pathian i zahpui tihna a ni dawn a ni,” min ti a. An sawi ang chuan Pathian leh a Kohhran te’n min ruat ka nih a vangin Pro-Pastor ka nih thu chu a ṭul angin ka sawiin ka lehkhabu ziak leh ka Article ziakahte pawh Pro Pastor tih ka ziak ve ta kalh zel a.

Hun a lo kal zel a Head Quarter lam a kan hotute pawh an lo inthlak ve zel bawk a, kan hotu thenkhat chuan, “Nangchu kan milehsa i ni a, kan milehsate reng reng chu tunlaiah chuan B.D pass phawt loh chuan Pastor a nih ngawt theih loh, chuvangin Pro Pastor inti tawh lo la a ṭha ang, kan Missionary te chu a ṭul angin serh leh sang khawih theina kan pe ṭhin a, serh leh sang khawih theihna kan pe zawk dawn che nia,” min ti a.

Chutia kan hotu te’n min tih takah chuan lawm takin ka pawm a, kan hotute in a min tih dan chu kan Kohhranah ka sawi a, an ni pawh chuan a tihngaihna tak an hre bik chuang lova, “Kan tan chuan Mizoram a B.D sawm rual ai chuan i hlu fe zawk a sin mawle, mahse chutia hotu te’n an tih chuan an thu chu i ngai chang phawt ang u,” an ti a, kan hotute thu chu kan ngaichang ta a.

Athuhrimah ka dinhmun hi mihringte mithmuhah chuan a khawlhkham ve angreng khawp mai a, kan hotu te’n Pathian thu zir leh tur a min tih lahin Field chhuahsan a kum thum, kum li B.D. han zir leh tur chuan ka milehsate chutiang chen chen chhuahsan theih dinhmunah an ding si lova, ka awm loh hlan a min thlaktu tur lah an awm bawk si lo. Kan hotute hnenah chuan keiin a B.D. ka zir leh ai chuan nakinah ka milehsate aṭang a zir thei an awm hun a min sponsor sak zawk turin ka ngen a, ka ngenna pawh chu min pawm sak nghe nghe a ni.

Kei chu Lalpa’n arammi ni turin min dah a, mahse Pathian khawngaihna zarah BCM chuan an Missionary ah min ngai nghet tlat bawk si a.

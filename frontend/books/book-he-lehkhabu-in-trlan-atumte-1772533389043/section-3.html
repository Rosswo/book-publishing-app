BUALPUI ‘H’ KHUA A LO INDIN ṬAN DAN

Tun a an khaw luah lai, an la luah hma chuan tuna an khaw luah lai aṭanga chhim lam km 3 vel a hla Bunghmun velah sawn khaw sa in an lo awm tawh a, mahse chu khua chu thli a nat em a vangin chhuhsan a khaw thar kài zâi an rêl a. An khaw thar kàina tur hmun en tur chuan an Lal khawnbawl ka Pu Ngunlinga (Ka Nu pa) ho chuan mi engemaw zat; an khaw luah lai a ṭanga hmar lam panin Arpa hluisen pawm chungin an inzui chhuak dam dam a, an khua aṭanga Km 3 vel an kal hnu chuan hmun pakhat; khua atana rem êm èm mai, hmar lam tlang lian tak, inphah chho duai mai leh chhim lam tlang lian lutuk lo rawn thluang thla ve duai bawk, chu tlang pahnih rawn inpharh thla duai kâra lo fing nih zet tur zawl duai mai; chu hmun zawl pawh chu chùan chung leh chùan hnuai ala awm lehnghal a, achùan hnuai zawk phei chu ramsa leh nungcha tinrengte’n lamtual an lo thàina leh nunchan an lo zirna hmun a ni a.

Lal khawnbawl leh a ṭhuihhruate chuan chu hmun an va thlen chuan atlang leh amualin an lung adum tlang bawk a ni ang; an Arpa hluisen pawm chu beiseina sang tak nen kàwn zawl chùan chung zawk, tlak lam sira khiang thingbul lianpui maiah chuan an thlung ta ràn mai a. An Arpa hluisen thlun pawh chuan apute lungrualpui-in, “Khaw thar, kan khaw luah lai aia hmun rem, boruak ṭha leh nuam zawk kan thleng ta e, hetah hian khawthar chu kan kâi ngei tur a ni,” ti niawm hrimin tui har leh har loh pawh ngaihtuah lêk lo chuan akhuang ta malh malh mai a. Mizo pi pute chuan, “Ar khuan tamna chu hmun ṭha a ni ngai e, hmun ṭha loah chuan ar pawh akhuang duh lo,” an lo ti reng a.

An Arpa hluisen pawhin alo lungrualpui takah chuan an tui tlan tur hla deuh pawh pawisa lo chuan chulai hmun chu an Lalpa Hemphunga hova an tual lènna atan an Ordain nghal hmiah mai a. Phur leh hlim tlang tak chuan an Lalpa hnena report pek hlan nghakhlel tak chuan an haw leh ta a.

An Lalpa leh an khualehtuite chuan khawthar kàina tur zawng tura Lal khawnbawl leh aṭhuihruai kalte chu nghakhlel takin an lo thlir ve reng bawka. Hmun thar ena kal te’n khua an lo thlen chuan khua a lo awm, an Lalpa leh a upate hnenah chuan thil awmdan chu kimchang tak a an han sawi chuan an Lal leh khaw mipuite chu an phur tlang hle mai a. Kum 1940 – 1942 chuan khawthar chu an kâi ta muah muah mai a.

Atir chuan hmar lam tlang, khua a tâna rem em em maiah chuan an awm a, chuta ṭang chuan an in zâr pharh thla zel a, chhim lam tlang thleng chuan an inzâr pharh chho ta zela, tunah phei chuan hmar lam tlangah chuan Middle School leh Higth School-te a awm tawh a, mimal in pawh tam tak a awm tawh bawk. Kawn chùan hnuai zawk chu anṭam hmun mai nitawh lovin Primary School te awmin Field (Khel mual) zau tak leh Community Hall lian tak nen, mimal in pawh a awm nual tawh bawk.

He khua hi mikual hian nuam an ti thei êm em a, “An duh airei an cham ziah ṭhin e,” an tia lawm. Akhaw thleng anawm mai bâkah ami chengte an hawihhawmin chhungkhat vek an ni emaw tih maitur an ni. Chuvang tepawh chu a ni mahna, mikhualin nuam an tih em em ṭhin ni. Tunah phei chuan khuamite leh sorkar ṭanrualna avangin khaw chhungah tui an la ve tawh a, khua ati nuam lehzual khawp mai.

Aram chin chu; chhak lamah Mianmar, Mianmar leh Mizoram inrina-ah chuan Mizoram a sangha tui berte rûn nuam Ṭiau lui chak takin a luang vut vut renga, Bualpui khua aṭang chuan Mianmar a Thau tlang, tlang lianpui a lang lurh mai bawk a, Mianmar a Bualpui khaw hnaih lam a khaw awmte chu Ṭiauchhun (Ruahbuk anti bawk) te, Bungtlang leh Bungkhua te, Thau khua pawh ahan hla vak lo. Heng khuate nen pawh hian an intlawhpawh êm èm reng a ni. Bualpui ram chhung Ṭiau kamah hian chinai lung pawh tam tham tak a awm nghe nghe a ni. Ṭiau kamah chuan nul pawh an siam ṭhin.

1TUI

Chhim lamah khawchhak Tuipui (Kolodyne) aluang dam duai mai bawk a, Ṭiau lui chak taka chhim zawnga luang leh khawchhak tuipui (Kolodyne) dam duai-a hmar zawnga rawn luang nen chuan Saisih chhuah bul, Bualpui ‘H’ khaw ramah chuan an intawk a, tunhma chuan fur tui len lai a luipui leh luipui an han insu chu ahmutu te’n a ropui zia leh a hmuhnawm zia hahîpin an sawi ṭhin. Ṭhenkhat sawi dan phei chuan, “Fur tui len lai a luipui pahnih an han insu ṭhin mai chuh!! Sang tak tak thlengin an inbuan kang úr ùr ṭhin a. Chumi en chuan mitam tak an kal ṭhin a, chumi en tute chu nupa tuak sarih an inṭhen phah a, nupa tuak sarih an inneih phah a ni,” an ti. Chhim lam tuipui ràl a an khaw ṭhenawmte chu Muallianpui leh Lungpuitlangte leh Lungleng khua te an ni. Heng khua-te nen pawh hian an inpawh in nupui pasal hialte pawh an innei tawn ṭhin a ni. Tlem a hla deuhah ‘S’ Vanlaiphai leh Darzo, Sangau, Archhuang leh khaw tam tak a awm bawk.

Hmar thlang lam a an khaw ṭhenawm Thingsai khua hi Rorehlova fapa naupang ber Khawtindâla’n kum 1860 vel khan a lo din tawh a. Chutih lai chuan Thingsai khuaah hian in zathum (300) an awm tawh lain Bualpui ‘H’ khua hi chu tlai khawhnu daih a din a ni a. A chhan chu; Fânai Lal ropui ber Rorehlova fate pathum zing a a upa pahnihte,Aithangvunga leh Khawtinkuala an thih hnuin Khawtindâla hian a unau fate zawng zawng a kawi khawmin hmun hrang hrangah a pêm kualpui reng hnuin Thingsaiah hian khaw nghet siam in an awm ta a. Thingsai a ṭang chuan kum 1871-1872 khan Muallianpui- ah an chhuk leh a.

Khawtinkuala fate, Hnochhuma, Pahnuna leh Lalthawmate’n Darzo tlang, Muallianpui leh Lungleng tlangte an awp a vangin Khawtindâla leh a hote in zathum vel chu Thingsai a lêt leh in kum 1884 khan Thingsai khua hi an luah leh a. Chutih lai chuan Pahnuna fapa Dokâpa chu Bualpuiah khua siamin a han kâi zawk a, mahse kum khat emaw lek chauh a han awm a, Lunglengah khua a siam leh a, a Pa Pahnuna’n Muallianpui khua chu a ṭhut a. Hnochhuma’n Darzo tlang a awp a. Lungleng Lal Dokâpa chuan kum 1897 khan Muallianpui a awm ho in sawmthum vel chu Lungphun a Darbilhi hnen a khaw thar kâi tur a awmte zingah dawi thiam awm nia an hriat a vangin, (Chutih lai chuan a Pa Pahnuna a thi tawh a. Muallianpui chu Hnochhuma nupui Pi Darbilhi hovin an luah a.

Muallianpui a ṭang hian Darbilhi hote chu Darzo ram Lungphunah an kâi leh a, kâi băng in sawmthum vel Muallianpui a la awmte zingah dawithiam rawt a ni. Kum 1897-ah Dokâpa chuan Muallianpui chu a awp ve leh thung ani) dawithiam a an ngaihte chu Dokâpa hian a rawt ta a. Dawithiam a an ngaihte chu zânah an hnukchhuak a, tualah an vawhlum ta hlawm a. Chutia Dokapa’n mi a thah a vang chuan Lunglei Bawrhsap in Dokâpa chu a man a, Thingsai Lal Hleibawrha pawh dawithiam that zing a inhnamhnawihna awm a hriat anih a vangin an man ve a, an pahnih chuan Chittagong Jail-ah an tan tir ta a, Chittagong Jail chhunghah Dokâpa chu a thi ta a ni.

Chutia Dokâpa Jail a thih tâkah chuan Lunglei Bawrhsap chuan, “Dokâpa hian fapa a nei em?” tiin a zawt a. A nupui Pi Lawngcheri chuan fapa Sangburha a nei reng chungin an man ve mai ang tih a hlauh a vangin, “Nei lo,” ti in a chhang a. Chutia Dokâpa’n ro khawmtu tur fapa a nei lo ti a Bawrhsap in a hriat tak a vang chuan Dokâpa nau Hemphunga chu Dokâpa khua Bualpui chu a pe ta a. Hemphunga chuan a u Dokâpa nupui thihsan Pi Lawngcheri chu nupui a nei in a rochun a. Kum 1898 chuan Bualpui tlangah an kâi ta a. Sangburha lo puitlin hnu chuan a nu in a pa ang in an man ve palh a nga, a thi ve palh ang tih hlauh a vang a afapa neih a zep chu hre thiam lovin; a nu hnenah chuan, “Ka Pate Hemphunga neih i tum vangin, ‘Dokâpa’n fapa a nei lo,’ i ti mai mai a ni,” ti in a thinrim a, a nu nen chuan an intithiam lo nghe nghe a ni.

Dokâpa hian fapa 1 leh fanu 2 a nei a, chungte chu : 1. Sangburha. 2. Aimawii. 3. Bualtawni te an ni. Sangburha hian fa 2 a nei hman a. Chungte chu : Thanzauva leh Lalhleii te an ni. Dokâpa fapa a ro khawmtu dik tak leh Bualpui tlang changtu turin a chan tak si lovah chuan Dokâpa fapa Sangburha chu a lungawi thei lova, Bualpui khaw tlang sang lai takah chuan veng hran siamin a kâi ta a. In sawmthum dawn ṭhelh a kâipui a. A Pate Hemphunga nen chuan Lal an inchuh ta a, mahse, a tirah a nu in fapa nei lo ang a a lo sawi tawh a vangin Hemphunga chu Dokâpa khua ṭhut tur a Bawrhsap in a lo tih ngheh tawh a vangin Sangburha chuan thiam a chang ta lova, Sangburha chuan a veng chu thla thum chhung a chhuahsan a Lal Hemphunga khua a fin lo a nih chuan a khua chu hàl vek turin Bawrhsap chuan thu a pe ta a. Sangburha chuan thinrim ṭeuh chungin Lal Hemphunga khua chu a fin thla ta a ni. Sangburha chu kum 1927 khan pavalai chauhin a thi ta a ni.

Lal Hemphunga khua Bualpui chu hmar lamah chuan Thingsai khua nen an inri ve thung a, Thingsai khua bâkah Cherhlun, Ngharchhip, Tarpho leh Khawhri-te nen pawh an intlawhpawh ṭha khawp mai. Thingsai khua nen a an inrina lai chu tunhma chuan Sechhun kawnah a ni a.

Thingsai khua nen a an inrina lai chu tunhma chuan Khawte kawn hi a ni a, Bualpui ‘H’ Lal Hemphunga’n Thingsai khaw Lal ram a phel lai a Thingsai khaw Lal chu Hleibawrha a nih a rinawm. Thingsai khua pawh hi an Lal chu Lal Hemphhunga-te unau Fânai ve tho a ni a, Thingsai khua hi chu hetih hun lai hian in 300 lai an ni a, Bualpui ‘H’ khua chu a tir chuan in sawmsarih vel an ni a, mahse Bungtlang, Țiauchhun, Sailam Zophai leh Muallianpui lamahte a hlawm hlawm a an pêm a vangin in sawmthum emaw vel chauh an awm tawh a.

Thingsai khua hi an khaw ram chhim lam leh tlâk lam ram chu khàm chhengchhia a tam em a vang leh hmar lam ram chu Țhaitui a ṭanga hmar lam zawng chu Cherhlun ram deuh vek a nih bawk a vangin lo a neih tlak a awm meuh lova, chhuah lam deuh chauh lo a neih tlak ram ṭha a ni a. An khuaah chuan mihring pawh Bualpui ‘H’ ai chuan a let engemawzatin an tam zawk a. An ram chu lo neihna an indaih loh em a vangin kum 1970, 1982 khan Khawhri ram Paawh ram chu lo-a tân an lo hawh tawh a, kum 1974 khan Bualpui ‘H’ ram Kawnpui ram pawh lo a tân an lo hawh tawh bawk a ni. (Hetia Khawhri ram Paawh ram leh Bualpui ‘H’ ram Kawnpui ram lo a an neih/hawh hi Bualpui Lal Hemphunga’n a ram a sahthlak/a pek ṭhen tawh hnu a ni) Thingsai khaw Lal chuan an khaw ram an indaih loh em a vangin Bualpui khaw Lal Hemphunga hnenah a ram phel ṭhen tur a dil turin a khawnbawl leh a upa mi sawm lai hruai in Thingsai leh Bualpui ‘H’ khaw ramri Khawtekawn, tun a Upa tui an tihah hian an kal a, Bualpui ‘H’ Lal Pu Hemphunga pawhin a khawnbawl Upa min mi pathum : Pu Tumkhenga, Pu Hranglinga leh Pu Țialkunga te hruai in an kal ve bawk a, ram riah chuan an inbe ta a.

Thingsai Lal chuan Bualpui Lal chu u leh naute an nih a vang a khawngiahna dil in, Bualpui ram phel ṭhen turin a dil a. Atir chuan an inbe rem mai thei lo a ni maithei, an inbiak lai in, Thingsai Lal Upa pakhat Pu Vanzinga chuan ṭawng a lo thlahdah deuh va, “Thu a kan inberem thei lo a nih chuan tharum pawh kan thawh a ni mai ang chu,” a ti a, Bualpui ‘H’ khaw Lal Upa Pu Țialkunga chuan, “Chutia tharum kan thawh dawn chuan a ṭha alawm, kha ka chempui hi là la, ka hngawng hi an sat bung rawh le, i ngam lo a nih chuan keiman i nghawng ka sat ang,” a ti a, a iptepui a a chempui chu phawrh in Thingsai khaw Lal Upa Vanzinga chu a pe a, a nghawng chu a dawh ta mai a, mahse Pu Vanzinga chuan Pu Țialkunga nghawng chu a sat ngam lova, chumi hnu chuan Pu Țialkunga chuan, a chempui chu a kut a chil chhak pharh pharh in nghet tak a hum in, “Anih leh ka nghawng i sat ngam lo a nih chuan i nghawng han dawh rawh leh kei chuan i nghawng chu ka chhawk ngam khawp mai, ka’n chhawk hmawk ange,” a ti a. Mahse, Pu Vanzinga chuan a nghawng chu a dawh ngam ta lova.

Chu tia Pu Țialkunga’n Thingsai khaw pa a tih chuan Thingsai khaw Lal in a rawn hruaite chu an relh ru zauh zauh a, a mi leh sate chu an relh bo zo dawn a a hriat a vangin, Thingsai Lal chuan, “E… ti chuan a ṭha dawn love, kan ṭin phawt a nga, ni dangah kan la sawi ṭha leh dawn nia,” a ti a, an inṭhen ta rih a. Ni dangah chuan an inbe leh a, Lal Hemphunga chuan, “E… a ni alawm, u leh nau inkârah nangin chaw i nghei a kei in chaw ka bâr bik chu rem ka ti love,” ti in, a tir chuan Sechhun kawn thlengin Lal Hemphunga chuan a phel a, chuti chung pawhin Thingsai chuan an la indaih loh em a vangin Thingsai Lal chuan a dil belh leh a, tun a kan ramriah hian kan Lal leh a Upate chuan Thingsai Lal chu an pe leh ta a ni. Kan sawi tawh ang khan Thingsai chuan an ram an indaih loh em a vangin kan khaw ram Kawnpui ram leh Khawhri ram an hawh leh a la ngai fo a ni.

Lal Hemphunga chu Pathian hmangaihna âtchilh bur mi a ni a, a khua leh tuite pawh a hmangaih em em a, Lunglei a Mizoram Lal ho an Committee ṭum pawhin Lal ho chuan Lal lehzualna an rel a, chuta an thu rel ṭhenkhat chu, “Mi in nupui a neih in a pasalin a mutpui hma in a nupui chu Lal in a mutpui hmasa tur a ni, Lal fathang leh Lal sachhiahte a ai tam in an khua leh tui te’n Lal an pe tur a ni,” (Hei hi upa engemaw zat hnen aṭang a ka hriat a ni) tihte an rel avangin kan Lal Hemphunga chuan a duh loh em a vangin Lal ban hun hma in Lal a ṭangin a ban phah ta a ni. Hmanlai Lal-te an LAL tehlul nen, a Lalna bansan duh hial khawp a Mizoram Lalte rorelna chu a khua leh tuite phur rit belhchhah lehzual zawng a an rel avangin a khua leh tuite phur rit belhchhah lehzual a duh lo hle a ni tih a hriat theih in kan Lalpa rilru ṭhat zia leh hmangaihna a ngah zia a hriat theih mai awm e.

Heti khawp a kan Lal hi Lal ṭha leh zaidam, hmangaihna ngah bawksi ani chung in Țhangthar engmah hre lo te’n, “Lal â leh Lal mawl” ti in kei, Lal fapa upaber Thanlinga tupa hmuh hmasak ber beng hriatah ngei pawh kan Lal Hemphunga hi an sawi chhe bawrh bawrh ṭhin a, a hunlai thil thleng an thlirpui thiam loh vang leh kan Lalpa hmangaihna ngahzia an hriat loh vang mai ni in a lang. Hetiang a an Lal zahna nei lo leh ngainep tak a ṭawngtute hian anmahni mawl zia ti langin mahni mawng hlim nen an lem chuang lo a ni.

Kan Lal Hemphunga hi mi harsatna hrethiam tak leh mahni ṭanghma sial lo tak mi a nih a vangin tuna an inrina ramri kawn thleng hian a pe mai a ni. Thingsai khua pawhin Bualpui khaw Lal Hemphunga’n u nau an nih a vanga a hmangaihna a tih lanna leh a ṭhatna zarah ram an lo neih zau phah duai a ni tih hi ṭhangtharte hian an hriat a ngai a ni. Hmanlai a ṭang tawhin ram zimte inchuh a vanga ram leh ram indona hial a chhuah ṭhin lai a hetiang a Bualpui khaw Lalpa in Thingsai khaw tan a Bualpui khaw ram, ram zau tak mai a pek a vang hian Thingsai khua chu Bualpui khaw chung a ṭhat chhuah leh lawmthu sawi chu an tihmâkmawh a ni. A ram pek lah hi ram tlemte a ni lova, ram zau tak a ni lehnghal a.

Bualpui khaw Lal Hemphunga tihloh chuan eng khaw Lal leh ram lal mahin an ram phèl ṭhen turin mittui nen dil pawh ni se khâp khat pawh an pek a rinawm loh. Ṭhenkhat phei chuan Lal Hemphunga hi an dem in “Lal â tak a ni, mahni ramte kaw dang pek ṭhen ṭeuhte chu,” an ti a. Mahse Lal Hemphunga chuan a chipui, a unaute, a khaw ṭhenawmte mangan-na leh harsatna a en liam mai theih loh vangin an ram chu a sah thlak ṭhen sak a ni zawk. Kan Lal Upa leh Lal khawnbawlte chuan kan Lal Hemphunga hi Lal â an ti ngai lova, “Lal fing leh hmangaihna ngah tak a ni, Lunglei a ṭanga Bawrhsap a rawn kal pawhin Thingsai Lalin a tihlawm loh lain kan Lal Hemphunga chuan a lo ti lawm thiam em em a, chuvangin Sap chuan, “I duh chin ram ka pe ang che khawi chin nge ka pek ang che sawi rawh,” a tih lai pawh in, Lal Hemphunga chuan, “Thingsai Lal ram ka pek chin hi chu ka la lêt tawh lovang, ka u naute chaw nghei a an awm lai a kei in chaw lo bâr bik hi ka duh lo,” a ti a ni.

Thingsai khaw mi, tunlai ṭhangthar ṭhenkhat-te chuan an chung a Bualpui khaw Lal ṭhatnate hi an theihnghilh zo ta ni in a lang. Mahse, khawvel awm chhung chuan Thingsai khua hian anmahni khawngaih leh unau an nih a vangte leh anmahni hmangaihna a vang a ram zau tak petu hi ṭhangthar lo la awm lehzel tur thleng pawhin an theihnghilh ngai tur a ni lo mai nilovin Bualpui Lal ṭhatna chu ṭhatna vêkin an rul leh tur ani tih an hriat hi ka duh takmeuh a ni. Lalhemphunga hian a ât vanga a ram hi zau tak pe phal a ni lova, a chipui, a unau, a khaw ṭhenawmte a khawngaih leh a hmangaih vang zawk a pe a ni tih an hriat reng a ṭul takmeuh a ni.

Bualpui khua an Lal Hemphunga hova an rawn luah leh hian a tir chuan hmar lam tlang, khua a tâna rem em em maiah chuan an awm a, chuta an awm lai chuan an Lal Hemphunga in chu tun a Pu Muanchhunga in leh Pu Kiaubila in inkârah hian a awm a. chuta ṭang chuan an in zâr pharh thla zel a, chhim lam tlâng thleng chuan an inzâr pharh chho ta zel a. Chhim lam tlang “Sangburha veng” an tih chu Lal Hemphunga chuan a fapa upa ber Thanlinga chu a pe a. Ram buai hma kha chuan Veng chhak atangin tuna veng thlang a Pu Sawiṭhuama in awmna thleng hian an inzâr thla a.

Ka Pa Hminga (F.Lalhmingthanga) sawi dan chuan, “Ram buai hma khan; “Sangburha vêng, tun a Higth School tlang hi Bualpui Lal Hemphunga fapa, ka Pa Thanlingate ram a ni a, ka u F.Lalhluna leh Tiniângite inneih kum 1954 khan Sangburha vêng a kan hmunah chuan lo kan nei a, buh pawh kawt 80 lai kan nei nghe nghe a ni. (buh kawt 80 tharna hmun chu a zim chhe lo ang tih a rin theih awm e) ka Pa Thanlinga’n Pu Zawngruma chu kan khaw thir deng a nih a vangin a duhsak a, sipai awm lai a Helipet chhim thlang lam, tun a tawngtaina tlang hmar thlang lamah khian thingpui chin nân ram a phel ve hlek a, Pu Zawngruma te chuan chu Lal Hemphunga fapa ka Pa Thanlinga ram phelah chuan thingpui bul tam vak lo a ching ve a.

Chu mi kan ramah chuan Theihai pawhte pawh kan phun a ni,” a ti. Tun thlengin chu an theihai phun chu hmuh tur a la awm a ni. Mahse, Lunglei D.C a ṭangin Pu Zawngruma tu leh fate leh Pu Thanlinga tu leh fate chuan Land Rance (Componsation) an la tawh nge nghe a ni. Pu Thanlinga fapa F.Lalhmingthanga (Ka Pa Hminga) chuan, “Land Rance (Componsation) kan la tawh a, kan hmun chu khaw mi te’n in duh ang angin in lo hmang dawn nia,” ti in khua a mite hnenah a hlan fel tawh a ni.

Pu F.Lalhmingthanga hi Bualpui Lal tupa a ni bawk a, a ti ṭha tak meuh a ni. Chu lai hmun chu ram buai hnu lawk a ṭang chuan Sipai in an rawn luah zui a. Mahse, Mizoram State kan nih hnu khan Sipai chu an chhuak a, tunah chuan Middle School leh Higth School te a awm tawh a, mimal in pawh tam tak a awm tawh bawk. Khaw chhung kâwn chùan hnuai zawk chu kan naupan lai chuan anṭam hmunah an hmang ṭhin a, mahse tunah chuan anṭam hmun mai nitawh lovin Primary School te awmin Field (Khel mual) zau tak leh Community Hall lian tak nen, mimal in pawh a awm nual tawh bawk.

He khua hi mikual hian nuam an ti thei êm êm a, “An duh ai rei an châm ziah ṭhin e,” an tia lawm. A khaw thleng a nawm mai bâkah a mi chengte an hawihhawmin chhungkhat vek an ni emaw tih maitur an ni. Chuvangte pawh chu a ni mahna, mikhualin nuam an tih êm êm ṭhin ni. Tunah phei chuan khua mite leh sorkar ṭanrualna a vangin khaw chhungah tui an la ve tawh a, (Hemi ka ziak lai hi chuan an tui lakna a ṭhat loh a vangin tui chu duh angin an hnianghnar lo hle a, tui hnianghnar zawk a an neih theih nan ṭhan an la ve mek) kawlphetha pawh a awm tawh a. Khaw laiah Boarder Road kawng zau tak a kal tlang ruah a, khua pawh a ti mawi in a ti nuam khawp mai. In leh lo pawh ṭha tak tak a awm ve nual tawh bawk.

Rambuai hma phei kha chuan Bualpui khua hi khaw nuam tak ni a sawi a ni ṭhin a, (tunah pawh khaw nuam tak chu a la ni reng thova) an Lalpa Hemphunga hi a History tam tak sawi tur a awm em em lo na in a Lal chhungin a khua leh tuite chunga lalhrawt mi a ni lovin akhua leh tuite hmangaih tak mi a ni a, Mizo Lal; Lal dang hovin an Lal lehzualna dan tur an rel lai pawh khan arilrem zawng a ni riau lova, chuvangin Mizoram a politic arawn luh ṭantirh khan Mizo Union leh Zalen pawlte a awma, Lal hote an lal dan a dik tawh loh a vangin mipui tam takin Lal neih an duh tawh lova, chuvangin an lal reng thei tawh dawn lo a ni tih Lal Hemphunga chuan a hriat avang leh Mizo lalte lal dan dik tawh lova a hriat a vangin Lal bàn duh pawl; Mizo Union-ah alut ta daih mai a. Chuvang chuan mitam tak chuan, Lal nisi Lal bàn duh pawla a awm chu hrethiam lovin, “Lal â” an ti hial reng a ni.

Mahse alo rin lawk ang ngeiin kum 1954 August thla chuan Mizorama Lalte chu bàn an lo ni ta ngei reng a ni. Tunhnuah chuan mite’n, “Lal Hemphunga kha a lo â lo tawp mai, politic kal vel dan a hre thiam in a lo fing zawk a ni,” an ti tawh zawk a ni. A diktak chuan Politic kal vel a hriatthiam vang pawh nichiah lovin Lunglei a Lal hoin ro an rel laiin an rorel danah khua lehtuite phurrit lak kian sak lam aia an phurrit belhchhah lehzual tura Lal ho rorelna chu a pawmpui loh vang zawk a Lal bàn duh lama ṭang a ni zawkmah.

Lal Hemphunga hi Lal ngilnei leh zaidam tak mai a ni a, a khua lehtuite chawhhlim a thiam ṭhin zia sawi tur an hre tam hle a ni. Pathian hmangaihna thu hi a âtchilh bur maia, a sawi reng ṭhin bawk a, “Inkhawm laite pawh hian mi thusawi lai pawh ni se, ‘Ngawi teh u, thu ka sawi ve lawk dawn e, Pathian hian min hmangaih em em a, keini pawh inhmangaih dial dial tur kan ni,’ tiin ṭap zawih zawihin a sawi ṭhin a ni,” an ti.

“Vawikhat chu Thingsai bunghmun a inkhawmpui kan neih laiin inkhawmpui ṭiak tukah, khawfing chah dawnin Pathian in Pathian fa ka nih min hriat tira, ka lawm lutuk chu ka mittui atla nasa hle mai a, ka lukham pawh a huh vek a ni,” tiin a fapa Sangduna hnenah a sawi. Lal Hemphunga farnu Vanhnuaichhingi chuan Pathian thu a han awi ve chuan an thul mawnga a dahṭhat Ṭing (laduang chum dumna) chu la chhuakin, “Hei hi diklo taka ka neih a ni a,” tiin an kawt laiah ahal hluah hluah e an ti.

Kristian an nih hnu in he khuaah hian vawi tam tak Pathian thlarau thianghlim pawlna changin harhna in vawi tam tak alo nuai tawh ṭhin a. An khaw chhehvel Mizoram leh Burma-ahte Kohhran engemaw zat an lo phun tawh nghe nghe a ni. Ṭum khat phei chu hlo thlawh hun laiin hlo thlo thei lo lêkin Pathian fak leh chawimawi-in hun an hmanga, an han feh dawn ṭhina, lo thleng loin kawtchhuah aṭangin Pathian fak leh chawimawiin inlamah an haw leh ṭhin a, nileng leh zankhua in Pathian hming fak leh chawimawiin an zaikhawm ṭhin a ni. Chumi kum chuan an khaw ṭhenawm Thingsai leh Cherhlun in an hlo chu an lo thlawh sak a ni. Pastor M.Vailingan’n Bualpui ‘H’ in an Kohhran chanchin ziak tura an ngenna anga Bualpui ‘H’ Kohhran chanchin a ziaknaah hetiang hian a sawi :

“Kum 1935 Zoram hlim (harhna tiin an vuah talo) vawi 4-na a ṭang erawh kha chuan kan Kohhran-ah hlimna nasa tak alo thleng ve ta in Thingsai leh Cherhlun lamte nen a ‘Inchin harhna’ ti in an inkalpawh hlawm ṭhin a, lo lam pawhchhiat thlengin hlimna chu a chawl hleithei lova, mite chu zaikhawm leh lam chu an hnapui ber a ni ta mai a. Chung an hlimna rawn chhim chuan Thingsai khua aṭang leh Cherhlun khua aṭang chuan mi engemawzat an lo kal a, Bualpui lo pawh chu ahohovin pawhchhiat runpui an nei nghe nghe a ni.

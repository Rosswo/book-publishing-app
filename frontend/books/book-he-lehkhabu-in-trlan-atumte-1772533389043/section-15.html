PU BUANGA CHHANNA:

Patlaia

Ka nau duh tak,

I thu min zawh chu ka hriat ang angin ka han chhang ang .

Bawm ho awm dan hi i sawi angin Mizoram kan lo luh tirh ang deuh tak khan ka lo hria a. Anmahni vei a i rilru mangan ziate pawh keimah ang kha i ni bawkin ka hria a ni. Zorama kan luh tirh chuan pa te hian Kristian nihna lam hi an ngâisâng lo em em a,. An dan pangngai an la ngaina em em a ni. Kristian ni tur hian an lang lo ve. Tunlai thangtharte hian hmanlai Zo hnam mawlzia hi an hrechiang lo va, tunlai hian Isua Krista hming avangin awihlote hnenah pawh awmdan danglam daih a ni tawh.

Hmanlai Zo hnam awmzia kha ulh tak leh la thianghlim lo tak chu Lalpa Isua hmingin Thlarau Thianghlim tanpuinain, beidawng hauh lovin ka beisei ṭhin a ni. Tin, Sap kan ni a, hnamdang Zoram amite rilru putzia leh awmdan hi kan hre lo hle si a. Tin kan awmrenga rei tak pawh Pathian hnena mite hruai thei mai loin kan awm a, a chang chuan beidawn thlak hle mai, beidawnna rilrute kan lo pu bawk ṭhin. Mihring ngaih mai chuan kan lo chhuah tirh lai Zo hnam hi Pathian thu hrilh hi a sak a, sawt lo angin a lang a ni.

Paulan, “Thil ṭha tih i ning lovang u khai, kan inthlahdah loh zawngin a hun takah chuan kan seng dawn si a,” a tih angin, hmangasihna a beidawng lovin kan bei kan bei zel a. Tichuan Krista thu nung kan theh chu mite rilru thuk lamah chuan a lo dah ta zawk a ni. Paula chuan, “Nau vei angin ka vei che u,” a ti. Nuin nau a vei a, a chângin an chhiat a, a piangsual thei bawk a, nimahsela, lawmna lam hi a ngah zawk fo thin.

Chutichuan, nang pawhin tunah hian Bawm ho hi na takin i vei tih ka hriatpui che a. A changin keini ang bawk chuan tih rual loh beidawn thlak takin i hria ang. Nimahsela, keini Sap ai hian Zo hnahthlak hian heng in vel amite rilru put zia hi in hmu awl thei a, an thlek zia in hre thuai thei. Keini Sapte ai chuan awlsam takin mite Pathian lamah in hruai thei zawk a ni. Theihtawpin Lalpa Isua Krista tan mi tam tak in lo ngam thei ngei ang tih ka ring a ni.

In tan lawmawm ka tih chu mitthite hian thlarau nung an neih tih an hriat hi a ni. Taksa thi mahse, thlarau a nung reng tih an rin hi a ni. An hnenah Krista ram thu thlen a remchangin, in tan a lawmna pakhata ni. A dang chu an Kristianna an ban dawnin nangmahni an dil che u hi; nu leh pa angin an ngai che u a, a lawmawm a ni . Chuti ni lo sela an mahni thuin an bang mai ang.

He Bawm ram hi theihtawpin Krista hmangaihna kengin beidawng lo va bei zui zel a ṭul ni berin ka hria. Rei lo deuh chinah chuan Krista chuan mi tam tak a hnenah a la pakaitir ang.

Zoram a kan thawh dan hetiang hi a ni. A tirin lo vât vât ang kan ni a, chumi hnuah chuan buh chi theh theh lehin chuan, Pathian in a Thlarau Thianghlim ruahpui a rawn surtir a, buh chi theh chu nasa takin a lo tiak a, a lo pung a, a lo hlawk ta a ni. Chutiangin rei fe atan engmah hmu mang lovin thlarau Thianghlim ring ringawtin kan thawk a. Pathian lehkhabu taima takin kan zirtir a, nakinah Lalpa Pathian remtih hun a lo thleng ta a. Kan rin ai a nasain a Thlarau chuan a tifing zo ta.

Chutiangin, nangmahni pawh, lo vât chu inla ni rih a. Thlai chi tuhin Pathian lehkhabu thu hi lo zirtir fo ula. Zoram angin nakinah hrilhfiahtu Thlarau chuan a la rawn thawk leh ang tih ka ring a ni.

Aizawl a kan awm tirh chuan, Isua khenbeh thla hi mite ka entir a, an nuih an nuih mai a. Ka entir duh leh ta ngai lova. Tunah erawh chuan, chumi thla chu mi tam takin tih tak zet a an ngaihhlut a lo ni ta zawk. Chuti angin, tunah engmah lo angin lang mahsela, in chi tuh chu Thlarau vin a to-tir ang. In hnathawh zawng zawng te hretu Isua Krista chuan thihna hlim kawr a mi ên zel turin ṭanpui che u rawh se.

I pu,

Pu Buanga

Upa T.Lalzara

Tlabung

Pastor Patlaia chanchin Upa T.Lalzara Tlabung ziak hi a ma ziak angin ka dah a ni, ama zarah Pastor Patlaia chanchin leh Mire ram a Missionary nitur a a luh kumte kan hriat phah a, Bawm hnam Kristian an nih hma nun leh Kristian an lo nih tan dante kan hriat phah bawk a, Upa T. Lalzara chungah ka lawm takmeuh a ni. Upa T.Lalzara Lalpa’n malsawmin vengin kaihruai zel a a dam khua lo sei zel turin duhsakna ka hlan e.

Bangladesh a kan unau Bawm hnamte hian hmelma pa setana bawih a, thim zinga chanchinṭha hre lova an awm chu kum 1919 khan Mizo Krista pasalṭha Pastor Patlaia te’n Pathian aṭanga thinlung taka hmangaih a veiin harsatna leh hreawm tam tak tuarin chanchinṭha an lo hrilh a, Mizo Krista pasalṭha Missionary-te thawhrimna leh an tawrhnate chu Lalpa’n mal a sawm a, Bawm hnamte chu za-a za Kristian an lo ni ta a ni.

Pathian malsawmna pawh an dawng nasa hle nghe nghe a. Mahse an ni chuan chu chu hre duh lovin sap pa Edwin Rowlands Mizoram a ṭanga Burma kal turin 1918 khan akal kawng a Bawm khaw pakhat-ah ariak a, chu chu Bawm ho chuan an hnen a chanchin ṭha thlentu hmasa ber (Missionary hmasa ber) a puangin sailungvarin a limte an siam a, kum 2018 khan ropui takin Gospel Sentenary an lawm nghe nghe a ni. An Gospel Sentenary a tel turin sap pawh an sawm nual a, kei pawh a bul a ṭanga a tawp thlengin ka awm ve a, anmahni thim ata êng a hruaichhuaktu, Mizo Missionary-te hming reng an lam ri lova. Bandarban a Pu Thanzama’n (Mizo) Bawm hnam thim ata eng a hruaichhuaktu leh mi engemaw zat te’n chanchinṭha a vanga Bawm ram a an lu an lo phum tawh ziate a sawi hnuah Rev, Paksim (Bawm) in an Mizo Missionary te chanchin tlemte arawn sawi bâk chu an mahni thim ata êng a hruai chhuaktu an Missionary Mizo chanchin sawi chhuaktu reng an awm lo kha a mak ka ti in ka rilru a lo na ngawih ngawih a, Pathian rilru phei chu engang takin na ang maw. Khang Missionary-te kha Lalpa ngaiha milian an ni si a.

Bangladesh a Bawm hnam hian an Missionary, anmahni thim ata êng a hruai chhuaktu Mizote chawimawi leh hriat reng ai chuan hman a an Missionary Pastor H.Dala’n Pathian thu zirna alo din tawh, ama hming chawi a Haudal Baible Seminary tia an lo dah tawh pawh a tu leh faten ahming ama ziakdan anga ‘Haudala’ ti a dah tur a an ngen avangin ahming pum tibo in anmahni hmangaih em emtu an Missionary Pastor Haudala hming chu an thai bo leh ta ni.

Bawm ṭhalaite mi thenkhat chu India-ah Pathian thu an zir a, an ram a an rawn haw chuan sap te dawrin Kohhran thar an din a, tunah hian Bangladesh a Bawm khua chu Biakin in a khat a ni ringawt mai, inkhawm tur mihring awm mumal silovin. Ring lo te pawh in Bawm hnam tlemte zing a Kohhran pawl hrang (Biakin) tam lutuk chu mak an ti hial a ni. Hnam tlemte zingah Kohhran pawl hrang atam em a vangin a eng kohhran mah chak lovin sum pathianna bawih-ah an tang zota. Eng tikah tak Bangladesh a kan unau Bawm hnamte hi harh chhuak ang maw?

Hetia kan unau Bawm chanchin ka ziak duah nachhan chu an ni hi Mizo hnahthlak an ni a, Mizo Missionary hmasate a ṭanga chanchin ṭha êng a lo awm a Kristian lo ni ta an nih a vang leh Bawm ram a Missionary hmasa Pastor Patlaia chanchin kan ziak ta sa sa a, chuvangin thil nih phung leh awm dan ka han ziak lang a ni e.

Pastor Chownglowk

Apa Nüdowi hi an khua Kharbari a ni a, a ni Chownglowk hi an unau sawm leh pakhat zinga a sawmna a ni a. Kum 1928 January thla-ah apiang a, kum 11-mi a nihin Mire ram NEIG sikulah lehkha a zir a, kum 1942-ah Ma nipur State a NEIG Mission sikulah lehkha a zir leh a. Heta lehkha a zir lai hian Pachhunga nen an inkawmna aṭangin Thlarau lam a piantharna a chang a.

A lehkha zir lai hian Kristian a nih a vangin a chhungte’n an ngaihsak duh lova, hreawm a ti viau na in Kristian a nih tawh avangin a chhungte ngaihsak lohna pawh chu chhel takin a tuar zel a. Lalpa ṭanpuina in a zirlaiah pawh atluang hle a ni.

British leh Japan indo buai a vangin kum 1943-ah Bawm ram Pankhiang khua-ah Primary a zir zawm a, Primary a zir zawh hnu in Churochanpur-ah Junior High School a kal chhunzawm leh a, chuta lehkha a zir lai chuan an zirtirtu Rev Lalthanliana’n an ram a haw leh a a chipuite hnen a Lalpa rawng a bawl theih nan a chipui, Miria te inchei dan a inchei chu ṭha zawk dawn in a hrilh a; bâkah, chutiang a Mire inchei a a inchei loh chuan nupui pawh a hmu thei dawn lo a ni tih a hre nghal bawk a. Chutia lehkha a la zir lai chuan a sam a zuah a, a chhipah a zial ngoh mai a ni.

Junior High School a zawh hnu chuan an ramah a haw leh a, nupui nei in a nun chu a lo inthlahdah deuh a ni ang, ringlomi ang main a tlusawp ta hle mai a, a nupuite ṭhen in nupui dangte a nei a, ramhuai puithiamah hialah a ṭang leh ta mai a. Mahse Lalpa mihriat, piangthar tawh leh a chipuite zinga Lanpa’n a rawngbawl tura a belruat a nih a vangin Lalpa’n a tlukna a ṭang chuan a kaitho leh a. Mire hnam zinga Lalpa rawngbawltu ṭangkai tak leh Kohhran ban pawimawh tak a lo ni leh ta a ni.

A u Angka ang main huan thlai siam lamah a tui em em mai a, huan thlai siam turin midangte pawh a zirtir in a ṭanpui nasa hle ṭhin a. Kohhran pawh an lo pung zel a. Chutih hun lai chuan NEIG Mission a awm tawh loh avangin Mission hrang hrang a bel kual a, mahse eng Mission mahin a duh angin Mire Kristiante chu an enkawl thei lova, chuvangin kum 1978 khan rinna a anmahni hringtu Mizo te bawk chu anmahni enkawl tura sawm turin Mizo ramah a kal a.

Chutih hun lai a Zoram Baptist Mission (Z.B.M) hruaitu Rev C.L. Hminga a rawn bia a, mahse chutih lai chu Mizoram chu rambuai a la nih a vang leh Mire ram chu Bangladesh a awm a nih a vangte, Chakma hel ho avangin ram buai an ni ve tho si a, kawng a la inhawng thei rih lova. Mahse, Miria te’n an mamawh em em; NEIG Mission hun lai a Mire ṭawnga an lo dah tawh Johana bu mal chu Z.B.M chuan emergency in J.B.Printing Press-ah Copy sangkhat lai an lo chhut sak a.

Aizawl lamah Pastor pakhat (he Pastor hi a hming kan hre lo) in copy sanghnih lai a lo siamsak ve bawk a, Chu chu Presbyterian Kohhran in Mire ram a phur thla turin Lunglei thleng an rawn dah bawk a. Hla Pastor H.Dala te lo lehlin, Evan Lalṭhuama’n a bu a a buatsaih pawh bu tam tak an chhut sak bawk a. An mamawh em em Chanchinṭha Johana bu mal leh hlabu; hla 75 awmna chu Lalpa’n Mizo te hmangin Miria te a pe leh ta. Lalpa chu fakin awm rawh se.

Pastor Chownglowka’n Beiseina sang tak nen a Miria te thlarau bo chhanchhuak tur leh thlarau lam a an puitlin ve theih nan a anmahni enkawl tura Mizo Kristian te a sawmna chu ahlawhtlin tak loh a vangin a beidawng em em a, a mah alo upa tawh bawk nen, chakna reng a nei tawh lova. chutih lai-in Mizo-te a auh aṭanga a kum 13-naah Lalpa’n kei achhiahhlawh tling lo tak mai hi Mire ramah chuan min tir thla a ni.

Pastor Chownglowka’n an hnam thlarau bo chhanchhuak a enkawl tura a lo bei sei em em Mizo min han hmu chu a hlim em em mai a, Kohhran angin Mizo te’n Mire ramah hma la thei rih lo mah ila a beiseina Lalpa’n ati hlawhtling ngei dawn in a hre tlat a ni. Kum sawm chhung tukhaw hriat lova mahni a ei inzawn chawp a ka awm hnu in kum 2000 Jun thla aṭang chuan Baptist Church of Mizoram (B.C.M) chuan Mire ram chu Field a neihin hma a rawn la ṭan ta a, Pastor Chownglowka’n a auh aṭang a kum 22-naah Lalpa’n kei, achhiahhlawh tling lo tak hmang hian a rawn hlawhtlin tir ta a ni. BCM-in Mire ram chu Field ang a hma an rawn lak tantirh lai hian Pastor Chownglowka chu kum 72 lai a upa ni tawh.

Pastor Chownglowka hian Mire Bible Thuthlung thar a letling a, Thuthlunghlui lehlin lai chuan kan thawk dun a, alo upa tawhin a hriselna a lo ṭha lo zel a, duh angin rawngbawlna lamah a inhmang hlei thei tawh lo hle mai a, natna khum a a awm lain min kova, ka hnenah chuan, “Ka fapa, ka hna chhunzawm zel tur leh Mire hnamte hmangaih tak a lo enkawl zel turin ka ring che a, nang i awm a vang in thlamuang takin ka kal thei dawn ta e. Ka Almera kha hawng la ka Certificate te leh ka lehkhabu pawimawh zawng zawng chu la vek la, ka Bible lehlin lai pawh la vek rawh, nangman i lo chhunzawm dawn nia,” min ti a.

Kei chuan a sawi ang chuan ka la vek a, a hnu lawk chuan kei chu Office thin tul pawimawh a vangin Mizoram-ah ka kal a, Mizoram a ka awm chhungin kum 84-mi niin, ni 13 April 2013 chuan a rawng a bawlsak ṭhin Lalpa hnenah chawl hlen tawh turin Lalpa’n a hruai haw ta a ni. Ka Pa Pastor Chownglowka thih thu Lunglei a ka awm lai in ka han hria chu; ka awm lohva min lo kalsan chu pawi ka ti em em mai a, a ni pawhin a thih dawn darker thlengin min lo lam lawm lawm chungin mual min lo liam san ta a ni.

Mahse, Lalpa’n Pastor Chownglowk hi chawimawi a lo duh zawk a ni. Pastor Chownglowk vuina inkhawm chu Lunglei a ka awmna Pu Lalhmangaiha (Mangaiha) te in, Lunglei Moria-ah kan nei a, BCM hruaitu lian mi engemaw zat leh Lunglei veng hrang hrang a Kohhran hruaitu te leh midang tam tak, Mangaiha te in lian ve angreng tak mai, a chhungah pawh leng lovin an kawt laiahte an la ṭhu hmur mai a. Chuti tak mai a ka Pa Pastor Chownglowk BCM Hruaitu te leh veng hrang hrang Kohhran hruaitu leh mipui tam tak in Lunglei khawpui aṭanga a ruang thlahna inkhawm kan han nei thei chu ropui ka ti lutuk leh ka lawm lutuk chu ka hnuk a ulh zawk a ni.

Mire ram a an in a a ruang thlahnaah pawh Mire Kristian tam takte an rawn kal khawm bâkah Bawm ram aṭangin Kohhran hruaitu leh Kohhran-mi tam takin a ruang chu ropui takin an thlah liam bawk a ni. Pastor Chownglowk hi Lalpa’n a va chawimawi nasa tehlul em. He lehkhabu a NEIG Mission chanchin leh Miriate khawvel ka ziaknaah pawh Pastor Chownglawk, mifing tak mai hnen aṭang hian a tam zawk chu thu chiang ka hriat phah in ka ziak theih phah a ni. Achungah ka lawm tak meuh a ni.

  1. VOLUNTARY MISSIONARY-TE CHANCHIN.

N.E.I.G. Mission awm tawh loh hnu chuan rambuaite a lo ni bawk nen, Mire ramah chuan Mission mumal awm lovin Lalpa rawngbawltute an reh ṭhuap mai a. Chutih lai chuan Mire Kristian tlemte lo awm tawhte pawh chu an thamral ta emaw tih mai turin chakna reng an nei tawh lova. Chutih lai chuan Mizoram pawh rambuai a lo ni ve leh zel bawk si a. Mizo mipuite chuan an unau Miria te thlarau bo chu an lainatin an vei em em reng a, mahse eng Mission mahin Miriate thlarau bo hruai chhuak turin hma an la thei bawk si lo.

Chuvangin an unau Miria te thlarau chu aboral mek a ni tih hre reng chung chuan mahni inlumah an thut reng theih loh a vangin Miria te thlarau boral mekte hruai chhuak turin mahni inchawm chawpin Voluntary Missionary mi engemaw zat Mizoram aṭangin an liam thla a, chung Voluntary Missionary te chu kan hriat thei chinte lo tar lang ila, tihsual palh leh kan tarlan hmaihte pawh an awm mai thei, kan tihsual palhte a awm a nih chuan khawngaih taka min hrilh turin chhiartu te ka ngen a che u. Pu Tanmanga leh Machhini (Mapuii) te chu Sangha luiral tlak lam Mire talangpui a Empu khua-ah kum khat an awm a, an awm chhung hian Pathian in a veng tlat a, engemaw ti a Mizoram a an chhuah hlan in Bangladesh Army te chuan Empu khua ah Mizo an awm tih an lo hria a, Empu khua Kharbari leh Pastor Chownglowk bâkah mi pathum, anvai in mi panga an man a, mahse Mizo hnam sipai MNF te’n an chhanchhuak leh ta a ni. Mapuii leh Pu Tanmanga pawh chu Mire ramah an rawn kir leh tawh lo.

Mizoram a ṭanga Voluntary Missionary te chu Mire ramah an luh thlak theih loh a, Bawm ram a Mire awm hote hnen a rawng an bawl lai chuan Bangladesh a Bawm ṭhenkhat te chuan Mire ramah Mission puitu pawh nei mumal lovin rawng an lo bawl ve bawk a. Mire tlangpui, Matamuhuri leh Marancha hungh velah anmahni kotu Lalpa rinchha nin ṭhahnemngai takin an Lalpa tan an lo thawk a. Lalpa hnenah Miria te thlarau bo tam tak hruaiin Biakin pawh engemaw zat an lo din hial nghe nghe a.

Mahse, Mission ṭanpuitu mumal an neih loh avang leh Miria te thlarau lam a an ṭhanlen theihna atan a an mamawh phuhruk saktu an neih loh a vangte in anni pawh chu an duh ang a thawk reng thei lovin, Mire ring thar, rinna kawng a ṭiak tir te te, la enkawl ngai em emte chu enkawl zui zel thei lovin an kir san leh vek ta mai a, chuvang chuan Mire Kristian te chu ṭhang thei lovin an awm a, tam tak chu hnim hlingnei zing a tla ang mai in an nun dan hlui chuan a chim ral leh ta a ni.

Heng ka han tarlan bâkah pawh hian Mire ram a Kohhran chawm nilova Bawm ram aṭang a Voluntary Missionary ka ziah hmaih palh an lo la awm a nih chuan, chhiartu duhtak, chung ka ziak lan hmaihte chu khawngaih taka min rawn hrilh turin ka ngen a che.

Heng kan han tar lan tak Voluntary Missionary te hi an awm hunchhung ka hriat theih loh a vangin an awm hunchhung kum ka rawn tar lang thei lo a ni. Tin, heng mite bâkah awmchilh lem lova Mire ram a rawngbawl a kal ṭhin, kan unau Bawm hnam zingah mi engemaw zat an awm ngei ang tih ka ring bawk a ni.

Heng Bawm ram a ṭanga Voluntary Missionary te hi Mire ṭawng thiam tak tak tepawh an awm nual a, chungte chuan hlate lo lehlin sakin a aw b te an lo zirtir ṭhin a, Mizo lengkhawm zai hla pawh Mire Kristian ṭhenkhatte chuan an lo thiam em em a ni.

Heng kan sawi takte, Lalpa tan a ṭhahnemngai tak leh rim tak a lo thawktu te’n harsatna tam tak a vanga an ram a an haw leh tak hnu a ṭanga kum engemawzat liam hnuin tun hun hnuhnung lamah hian Lalpa’n kei achhiahhlawh tling lo tak mai hi Miriate thlarau bo hruai chhuak leh chawm puitling turin Mire ramah min tira, keimah mai chuan thawh theih ka neih tlem em a vangin kum sanghnih hnu lam aṭang chuan Baptist Church of Mizoram leh Mission dang dang Lalpa’n a rawn tir bawk a ni.

Hmelma pa Setana, Diabawla ni bawk hian Miria te taksa leh thlarau boral tur chhanchhuak a Krista hnen a hruai thlenna dodal turin theihtawp a chhuah a, a dodal theih natur a nih phawt chuan eng hmanrua pawh a hmang duh a, a hmang mek zel bawk, Miriate thlarau bo chhanchhuah in an awm mek a ni tih a hriatin, “Tlangsamah ramhuai a pawng tal e,” an tih ang mai in kan hmelma pa hi a pawngtal mek a ni.

Engang pawhin hmelma pa hian min bei mahse Lalpa kan lamah aṭang a, Lalpa Kohhran te hian kan unau Miria, satana bawi a tâng mekte chhanchhuah hna hi beidawn san mai lovin, chhel leh huai takin i bei zel ang u. Indona chu Lalpa indona a ni a, huai tak leh chhel taka kan bei zel chuan hnehna kan chang ngei ang, hnehtu Pathian vangin.

4. Christian Fellowship Of Bangladesh (C.F.B) chanchin.

C.F.B. Mission President chu Rev Rualthanmawia Chhakchhuak a ni a, a ni hi S.Koria leh America a ṭanga Sponsor nei a Mire ram leh hmun dang dang a Lalpa rawngbawlna hna thawktu a ni a, Mire ram bik hi kan ziah lan duh a nih a vangin Mire ram a thawh dan kan ziak dawn a ni.

Mire tlangpui, Pawpla Headman khuaah ni 7 February 1989 khan ruai ropui taka buatsaih in Mire Kristian te a ko khawm a, chutah chuan Mire Christian Church (M.C.C) a din pui a, MCC kaltlang chuan Mire ho zingah chuan Lalpa rawngbawl hna kum 1990 ah a thawk ṭan nghal a ni. Pu Rualthanmawia chuan Mire ram a Lalpa rawngbawl hna tihtakzet a a thawk dawn a an hriat a vangin Mission dang dang a awmte pawh chu Mission pakhat a awm chuan ṭha zawk dawn a an hriat a vangin MCC ah chuan an awm tlang ta dial a.

Chu Mire Kohhran thar MCC chu enkawl turin Dt 7 February 1989 chuan hruaitute an inthlang nghal a.

He an Kohhran thar din, Mire Christian Church (MCC) hian Pu Rualthanmawia Kohhran din Christian Fellowship of Bangladesh (CFB) chu a zawm nghal a, chu Kohhran a thawktute hlawh pawh chu CFB chuan a peksak vek a ni. Mahse, MCC chu anmahni chhungkuaah buaina neuh neuh a awm a vangin heng an hruaitu thlante hian kum khat pawh an hruai hma in an ṭilh san leh ta deuh vek a, mahse chu an Kohhran thar din chu CFB hnuaiah a la kal ve zel a.

CFB chuan Mire ram a thawktu pawh a ruai tam hman hle a, mahse ṭhenkhat te chu reilote kum khat pawh tling lo an thawk a, an ban san leh a, ṭhenkhat chu hlawh a pe tlem em em a, a pek a te pawh regular tak a pek mumal a nei meuh lo. Ṭhenkhatte phei chu thlakhatah Tk 500 chauh pe a a rawihte pawh an awm.

Pu Rualthanmawia chuan Mire ram a ka awm thu a lo hriat chuan kum 1992 kumtir lamah Dhaka a a hnen a kal turin min chah a, a hnen a ka va kal chuan Pu Rualthanmawia chuan, “Lalpa rawngbawl in Empu khuah harsa takin i awm tih ka lo hria a, a remchan chuan ṭanpui che ka duh a ni,” a ti a. Kei chuan, “Pu Mawi, min ṭanpui i duh a ka lawm takmeuh a ni, mahse Mizoram a kan hotute ka biak ve phawt a ngai ang,” ka ti a, a ni pawh chuan a remti a, Mizoram a kalna tur Tk 500 min pe a, Mizoram a kan hotute hmu tur chuan ka kal ta nal nal a.

Mizoram ka han thlen a kan Team, Abassador for Christ Team Speaker Upa K.Lawmsanga chu thilawmzia ka han hrilh chuan a ni chuan, “Chutia CFB Mission in a lo ṭanpui theih che chuan a ṭha a lawm. Pathian remruat a nih dawn chu, keini lahin duhangin kan ṭanpui thei mang si lo che a, mahse keini lam pawhin ṭawngṭaina leh thilpekin kan theih ang tawk chuan kan ṭanpui ve reng thovang che, kan thlahthlam dawn chuang lo che a ni tih i hre reng dawn nia,” a ti a. Phur takin Bangladesh-ah chuan ka kir leh a Dhaka a Pu Rualthanmawia hnenah chuan va kal leh in thilawmzia chu ka va hrilh a, CFB Mission chuan ni 22 Feb 1992 aṭang chuan min ṭanpui a, thla khatah cheng 1200 min pe ve a, ka lawm khawp mai.

CFB Mission in Mire ram a a thawh tak tak hun chhung chu kum 1990 – 1999 a ni a, mahse kum 2000 hnu lam a la awmte hi CFB President Rev R.T. Mawia anmahni Sponsor leh tura an ngen avang a hlawh a pek leh te a ni. Mire ram rawngbawlna chawlhsan a tum hnuah mi tlemte in anmahni ṭanpui tura an ngen tlat a vangin hemi lehkhabu ka ziah lai thleng hian mi 2/3 te hlawh chu a la pe a ni.

BUNG 10

REPORT HLIMAWM MIZORAMAH

Ka Pu Lalhmachhuana’n Bangladesh khualehtui nihna Identity Card min siam sak hnu chuan Bangladesh Mire ramah zalen takin Lalpa rawng kan bawl thei tawh dawn a ni tih Mizoram a thlen chu thil ṭul hmasa ber ni a ka hriat a vangin Mizoram lam pa nin ka kal leh ta vang vang a. Lalpa hruaina in tluang takin Vaṭhuampui chu ka han thleng leh a. Hetih lai hian kan Team, Ambassador For Christ Team-te chu Speakek Upa K.Lawmsanga hova Vaṭhuampui a rawngbawlna an neih lai alo ni a. Repor hlimawm: Bangladesh; Mire ramah zalen takin Lalpa rawng kan bawl theih tawh tur thu ka han sawi chuan kan Speaker Upa K.Lawmsanga leh a Team-te chu an lawm em em a, Vaṭhuampui khua mite pawhin min lawmpui em em bawk. Pathian hnenah lawm thu sawi-in kan ṭawngṭai mum mum ta mai a, a vahan hlimawm tehlul em. Vaṭhuampui a rawngbawlna kan neih zawh hnu chuan Damddep-ah Camp kan nei a, chumi hnu in M.Kawnpuiah te, Diltlang-ah te rawngbawlna kan nei chho zel a, tichuan Hnahthial chu tluang tak leh hlim takin kan thleng chho ta a.

14AFC Team

Hnahthial kan han thlen a Mire ramah zalen takin rawng kan bawl thei tawh dawn a ni tih thu ka han sawi chuan mite chuan mak an ti in min lawmpui hle mai a, min ṭawngṭai saktute pawh an lawmin an phur phah sawt in ka hria. Kan Unit Ṭ.K.P. te paw’n an ṭawngṭaina Lalpa’n a tih hlawhtlin sak ngei a ni tih an hriatin an lawm ve hle a. Ka chhungte, ka laina hnai leh ka hmelhriat ṭhate pawh an thaw a veng huai in an lawm ve em em a ni.

Hnahthial a ka cham chhung hian kan Unit Ṭ.K.P te’n Leite Unit Ṭ.K.P kan tlawh a, Hun neitu ṭhenkhatte engemaw rokhawlhna a vanga an rawn tel theih loh avangin kan hotu te’n min duhsak a, ahun khatna min kian a, a bâk chu kan hotu te’n han hmang zawk se, kei chu thu ngaithlatuah ṭang zawk ila, tia ka ngaituah lai-in hun neitu apiang te’n an hun chu min kian ta zel mai a, han hnial pawhin min duhsakna chu ka hnial hneh silova, thusawi tura inbuatsaihna nei miah lo leh chawpchih tak chuan lawm tak leh huphurh takin Sermon hun zawng zawng chu a bul a ṭanga atawp thlengin ka hmang ta vek mai a. Achang phei chuan kan hotu te’n hmang ve tawh zawk se ka tih avangin pulpit bulah, thusawitu koh dawn ṭep thlengtein kan han inhnawn leh rih changte pawh a awm hial a. Thusawi hun chu Speaker ang hrim in ka hmang ta vek a.

Hemi ṭum hian Lalpa’n ka thusawite mal a sawm a, Pathian thlarau pawlna changin hlim tak leh kham lo takin hun chu kan hmang zo ta a ni. Kei tehlul, kan hotu fel tak tak leh thusawi thiam tak tak kâr a Pathian leh kan hotu-te duhsakna changa hun ka han hmang chu ropui ka ti in ka lawm em em a ni. Lalpa hi chu mi hnuaihnung zawkte chawikangtu hi ava han ni chiang em. Hemi ṭum hi chuan Hnahthial a ka cham chhung pawhin hlim takin hun ka hmang ve thei ta. Lalpa chu fakin awm mawlh rawh se.
